## Layout (Wolf Talons)

1. **Open-hand assault rig** — **not** a hand-replacement talon. Palm and five fingers remain **fully articulated** (no power-fist bucket). Bolt trigger, ***Spina Cineris*** thumb access, and gauntlet maintenance remain possible without stripping the rig.

2. **Knuckle-coupled blades (Wolverine / Space Wolves geometry)** — three short energized claws per hand mounted **over** the hand at **knuckle / back-of-hand couplers**. Blades **extend** for strike bursts and **retract** flush against the vambrace when disengaged. Fingers stay **free** beneath the field — Einar finishes what the claws open. Field color: **red** (not blue).

3. **Purification / remake note (canon intent)** — original ship-origin claws taken from **Kzorn’s Maw** during capture; after recovery **purified and re-wrought** under **Kadmos** with *Reclusiam* oversight (**Varro** / clergy) into Castra Vetera interface standards as **Wolf Talons**. Silhouette retained; Brass/warp contamination **denied**.

**Gauntlet paint in example:** Mephiston Red on **gauntlets only** (including fingers) — *Amissi* / Einar paint lock; vambrace stays Mechanicus Standard Grey.
