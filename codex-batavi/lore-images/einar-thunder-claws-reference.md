## Layout (Wolf Talons)

1. **Angel’s Talons pattern (common Imperial)** — energized blades seated on **wrist / lower-gauntlet housings** and projecting forward. This is the Armourium layout most Astartes Chapters field as lightning claws — **not** a knuckle-coupled Wolverine rig and **not** a hand-replacement “talon-hand” where blades are finger phalanges.

2. **Batavi remake (not BA copy)** — after recovery, **Kadmos** with *Reclusiam* oversight (**Varro** / clergy) **purified and re-wrought** the ship-origin claws into Castra Vetera interface standards as **Wolf Talons**. Silhouette stays Imperial wrist-blade; paint, seals, and field color read **Batavi** (Mephiston Red gauntlets; **red** energy field). No Sanguinary filigree, no jewelled BA heraldry.

3. **Purification note (canon intent)** — original claws taken from **Kzorn’s Maw** during capture; Brass/warp contamination **denied** in the remake. Name remains **Wolf Talons**; pattern class remains **Angel’s Talons** (Imperial common).

**Gauntlet paint in example:** Mephiston Red on **gauntlets only** (including fingers) — *Amissi* / Einar paint lock; vambrace stays Mechanicus Standard Grey.
