## Layout (Dreadwolf Claws)

1. **Dreadwolf Claws** — lightning claw pattern executed as a **talon-hand**: the weapon is a hand itself, not a dorsal-blade gauntlet. The energized talons are the **second phalanx** of each finger, yielding a bestial “predator hand” silhouette.

2. **Grip and articulation** — full **palm + thumb** grip is retained (no power-fist bucket). The claws read as “finger-bones turned to blades,” but the hand can still seize, climb, and control.

3. **Purification / remake note (canon intent)** — original ship-origin claw-hands were taken from **Kzorn’s Maw**; after recovery they were **purified and re-wrought** under **Kadmos** with *Reclusiam* oversight (**Varro** / clergy) into Castra Vetera interface standards while retaining the predatory silhouette.

**Forearm in example:** Red vambrace shows *Amissi* **red limb** mark (arms + legs below knee on full suit).
