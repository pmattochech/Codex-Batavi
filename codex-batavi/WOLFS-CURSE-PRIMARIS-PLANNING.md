## 1. Strategic intent (two-era model)

**Owner sequencing (planning lock):** Era **B** begins **shortly after** crucible war **victory** — “win the war, then the vaults open.” **Not** a long peace or a separate epoch decades later; exact **in-universe gap** (weeks vs few years) is **TBD at write**.

| Era | Force composition | Narrative job |
|-----|-------------------|---------------|
| **A — Founding / crucible war** | **Firstboot** majority | *Codex* breaks; **Furor / Exanimus / Versibar** math; **tiny survivor pool**. |
| **B — Reinforcement (contiguous)** | **Primaris** inducted (Rubicon and/or new plate) | Rebuild **mass** right after the wall holds; **Tyvar / Indomitus / Throne** logic explains **why relief is timed to that window**, not a random later century. |

**Design line:** *The Rubicon does not grant innocence. It buys mass. The first invoice is Furor.*

## 2. Reinforcement cohorts (policy sketch)

| Cohort | Curse state (typical) | Rubicon / Primaris | Rationale |
|--------|----------------------|--------------------|-----------|
| **Clean enough Firstborn** | Pre-**Furor** or stable subclinical | **Approved** | Surgery + recovery = **trauma spike** that often **registers first Furor** — price of new bodies. |
| **Furor locked / chronic** | **Furor** managed or oscillating | **Contraindicated** or trial-only | Implant cascade risks **advancing** pathology. |
| **Stabilized Exanimus** | **Red hands** protocol | **Not required** for “power” | Claws + predatory bandwidth already buy **edge** vs line Primaris in **niche** (burst, close friction); maintenance is **Apothecarion + *Axiom*** tax. |
| **Advanced / total progression** | Near- or post-**Execratio** risk, unstable Versibar-adjacent | **Incompatible** or **fatal** | Risk **accelerates** ratchet; *Medicinae* black-tag. |

**Guardrail:** **Exanimus > line Primaris** only where doc already supports **edge** (threat read, claws, fixation): **burst / friction niche**, not “better at everything.”

## 3. Wolf’s Curse — operational upsides vs costs (from current encyclopedia)

Summarized from [`projection-aurea-wolfs-curse.md`](biological-encyclopedia-bestiary/projection-aurea-wolfs-curse.md) for **writer-facing** tradeoffs.

### Furor (*irritable exhaustion*)

| Upside | Cost |
|--------|------|
| Still **tactically viable**; *Axiom* can **contain**. | **Continuous** monitoring; instability **managed**, not cured. |
| **Type III (Duty)** engraved: **locks** vs explodes — can **look** stable. | Types I/II: **pride / fear** amplify; fragment under failure. |
| | **Threshold stripe** — peer and self never forget the state. |

### Exanimus (*face of friction*)

| Upside | Cost |
|--------|------|
| **Early predatory recognition** — faster threat loop. | **Misreads** diplomacy / deceit unless *Axiom* reins it. |
| **Objective fixation** — grinds problems until solved. | **Won’t disengage** when orders demand; fixation **days** without override. |
| **Vexillatio territorial threshold** — instant protect reflex for sector/brothers. | **Acts before justification** post-hoc. |
| Combat: **high-efficiency predatory focus**; claws **pierce ceramite** (sustained). | **Post-combat** = **highest instability**; **trimming / pain**; *Axiom* = **continuous work**. |
| Veterans: **functional respect** for reads forged under cost. | **Red hands** — visible forever. |

### Execratio (Versibar)

| Upside (for *enemy’s* problem) | Cost (Chapter) |
|---------------------------------|----------------|
| Absurd **speed / mass** in corridors. | **Irrational**; **no** chain; friendly **catastrophe**. |
| *Adversarii* **area denial** doctrine (locked). | Testament / Cells / execution **only** sane outcomes; **Alpha** exception is **singular**. |

## 4. Line Primaris vs Exanimus-positioned (comparison)

| Axis | Line Primaris (curse absent or pre-Furor) | Exanimus-positioned |
|------|-------------------------------------------|----------------------|
| **Thickness** | *Codex*-shaped **mass**, logistics scale. | **Edge** — predatory bandwidth, close-work **ceiling**. |
| **Cognition** | Standard OODA. | **Split stack** + **post-fight** danger window. |
| **Sustainability** | Replenishment **scales**. | **Apothecarion + doctrine** **tax**; fewer bodies hold long. |
| **Peer read** | “Reinforcement.” | **Red hands** — **reverence + dread**. |

**Summary:** *Primaris buys thickness; Exanimus buys edge — the bill comes due every hour after the shooting stops.*

## 5. Triumvirate — **not** the common marine rule (psychological divergence)

**Shared tier:** Founding exposure / gene-seed stress **ballpark** with Alaric; **not** “weaker curse lottery.”

**Why Execratio eats Alaric first (planning framing):** **Patriarch / pack** — **emotional coupling** to **every brother’s** wound and **curse echo**; **brothers’ Furor** **resonates** in him → ratchet toward **Execratio** when **everything fails at once** (existing file: *Exanimus holds when Axiom is practiced*).

| Figure | Mind–curse relation (planning) | Containment mechanic |
|--------|--------------------------------|----------------------|
| **Varro** | **Accepted** the beast; **tamed** it. | **Integration** — spike is **named**, **aimed**, **ritualized**. |
| **Drusus** | Beast = **noise** beside data. | **Clinical dissociation** — phenomenon **logged**, not **become**. |
| **Alaric** | **Carries the pack’s suffering.** | *Axiom* + duty **until** contextual collapse → **031**. |

**Merge note:** Encyclopedia already places **Varro and Drusus** in **stabilized Exanimus** (claws, maintenance). This planning frame explains **stability** and **why Alaric crosses to Execratio** without claiming they **avoid Exanimus biology** — unless a future retcon removes their Exanimus stigmata.
