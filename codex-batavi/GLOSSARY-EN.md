Common pattern: **perpetual Deathwatch** with scheduled returns for **brief** + **Rites of Participation and Regulation**; also rim / **Halo Stars** kill-teams (often **Shadows Cohort**). Etymology: **White Scars** exchange (Chogoris **off-pack** predator analogy). See [`steppenwolf-doctrine.md`](personae-command-index/doctrine-and-organs/steppenwolf-doctrine.md). |
| **Deathwatch** | Ordo Xenos **kill-teams**; Batavi treat as **mission creditor** — rotations, *Medicinae* gate, **tiered disclosure** ([`deathwatch-and-batavorum.md`](political-relations/deathwatch-and-batavorum.md)). **Default Batavi livery on loan:** **Watch black** with **Chapter insignia on the shoulder pauldron**. |

Haft **relay**. |
