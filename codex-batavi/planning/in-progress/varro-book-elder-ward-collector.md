## 3. Beat spine (locked intent)

1. **Surprise intrusion** — Chapter does **not** know Trazyn is coming. Local fortress habits + compartmentalization respond; not full Chapter mobilization.
2. **Varro + Librarian at Black Cells** — Librarian present to **file the mind** and **calm / comfort** the elder ward (not to bait Trazyn).
3. **Intellectual / moral contest** — primarily **Varro** (heavy, theological, debt). Librarian **truthful**, not manipulative: acknowledges what Trazyn’s preservation *does* well; names the failure: museum = eternal object; Batavi custody = **possibility** of meaningful death / wall duty under *Adversarii*.
4. **Extraction conflict** — Librarian’s **anchoring** (Batavi doctrine: maximize local reality coherence / stabilize unstable psychic imprint on the worldline) **changes variables** in Necron translocation equation — does **not** brute-force overpower Necron tech. Capture envelope widens; Varro + Librarian + ward displace to wilderness relay. Trazyn may remain and pursue.
5. **Hunt** — ward loose, not lucid; settlements at risk. Shared immediate objective (contain) with incompatible endings (collect vs responsibility).
6. **Physical close** — Varro destroys Trazyn’s **committed surrogate** (*Iron Verdict* / Sentence). Tactical win; collector survives elsewhere.
7. **Return** — confession beat Varro ↔ Alaric (see §5).
8. **Testament reform** — doctrine for pre-Testament / term-expired Iron Prison cases (see §4).
9. **Funeral / terminal rite** — Alaric meets elder ward as brother; Alpha challenge / Death of the Wolf geometry adapted for one who never chose.
10. **Name elevation** — Strategium threshold / lintel (see §6).
