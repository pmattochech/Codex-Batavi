## 3. Density / five movements (locked intent)

Novel is **dense**: shame and comfort work **before** the collector; hunt is middle, not the whole book.

### I. The Weight (ends **before** Gideon enters the Cells with Varro)

**Cutoff lock:** Part I closes after the **archive conversation with Otho** (Gideon introduced / tasked). **First cell session with Gideon = start of Part II** (or immediate bridge), not Part I.

- Year: **146.M42**.
- Varro’s private vigils over Atreus; shame and weight; symbol vs man.
- Contrast Hell Cells (punishment) vs Black Cells (failure dressed as duty).
- ***Reclusiam* / Bram:** ~**109**; ~**100 years** under wing since childhood. Line (lock):

— Today is a colder one, my lord?

- **Einar:** only via **recent visit** Varro paid the *Amissi* / Dreadwolf — mention that adds weight to an already heavy burden; no Einar co-plot.
- Decision: presence is not enough → Varro asks **Otho** for help.
- **Venue:** **archives** (not Cells, not Kitchen).
- Otho introduces **Gideon**; mandate locked: Gideon is to **measure the size of the turmoil in Atreus’s mind** (ego/mind filing; *Apothecarion*-adjacent talent; anchoring later). Comfort/calm are tools toward that measure — not the Part I on-page cell work yet.
- Drusus: slate/edge only if needed; not a third archive voice unless useful.
- **Confidence chain (book-scale):** Bram = finite mortal latch → (after Aethelgard) Gideon = Astartes private counselor.

### I‑PLAN. Part I planning prose (staging — not chronicle body)

**Open.** Noviomagus *Reclusiam* cold. Varro has come from a **recent visit** to Einar / the *Amissi* watch — the living leash that still answers. The visit did not lighten him. It reminded him that some brothers can still be held in motion, and one brother in the Black Cells has been held only in place since **Malakor’s end**.

**Vigil texture.** He does not preach to Atreus. He inventories: grille, pheromone warning, the absence of a Testament that did not yet exist when Atreus fell in the **final moments of the Valerian Heresy**, while Alaric’s own hour on that world was breaking open. Varro’s shame is not that Atreus became Versibar. It is that the Chapter learned how to name choices afterward (**145**) and left this one outside the naming — a debt older than the paperwork.

**Hell Cells vs Black Cells (brief).** A punished brother in Hell Cells still has a sentence with an edge. Atreus has duration without consent. The Judex feels the difference as geometry, not sermon.

**Bram.** The old mortal — child of the crucible’s end, century of ash and silence — does not ask for the face. He reads the other silence.

— Today is a colder one, my lord?

Varro does not deny it. Denial would be theater. He lets the question stand as proof that the symbol has weather, and Bram has always been allowed to notice.

**Turn.** Presence has failed as medicine. Varro will not perform failure alone as if it were doctrine. He goes to **Otho** in the **archives**.

**Archive.** Haruspicium language, not comfort. Varro states the need without begging: Atreus’s mind is a storm the Judex can stand beside but cannot map. Otho does not offer mysticism. He offers a tool — **Gideon** — trained to read ego-turmoil size, to file what can be filed, to work the border where *Librarium* and *Apothecarion* already share telemetry habits. Mandate spoken plain: **learn how large the turmoil is**. Not “heal the Versibar.” Not yet “free him.” Measure.

**Close of Part I.** Gideon is named and tasked. The Cells wait. Part I ends on the threshold of that work — Varro still the jailer who has asked for a second pair of eyes, Bram’s colder day unanswered in words, Einar’s recent visit still a weight on the same spine.

### II. The Theft (Black Cells) — **measure + teaching, then Trazyn only at the end**

**Cutoff lock:** Part II ends at **displacement onto Aethelgard** (hunt begins = **Part III**). Trazyn is the **closing intrusion**, not the middle of the part.

- Dense cell work first: talk, failed reach, Varro teaching the never-chose.
- Gideon **speaks the name Atreus** once as a reach → **agitation** (lesson paid in storm).
- Surprise Trazyn **only after** that weight is earned.
- Anchoring + envelope → Aethelgard.

### II‑PLAN. Part II planning prose (staging — not chronicle body)

**Span.** Not a thin raid vignette. Part II spends its bulk on **reaching and teaching**. The collector arrives late, like a knife after the confession has already started.

**Descent.** Black Cells. Varro leads Gideon into Atreus’s weather: red lumens, pheromone warning, grille geometry. Drusus on slate or edge — biology certified, soul-debt not his to narrate. Bram stays above.

---

**Block A — First measures (talk before miracle).**  
Gideon works the mandate: **size the turmoil**. He asks Varro what the Judex already knows — not for gossip, for Grid. Varro answers in inventory, then — rare — in story.

He teaches what the paperwork cannot: brothers who reached Execratio **before Testament (145)**; Castellan-kept wards; the gap between Hell Cells (punishment with an edge) and Black Cells (duration without consent). He places **Atreus** inside that class: fell in the **final moments of the Valerian Heresy / Malakor**, same season Alaric’s own hour broke open; never signed because the choice did not yet exist as law; held while the Chapter learned how to name futures for others.

Gideon listens as *Librarium* — files, does not comfort. Tiny early gains: impulse maps, storm-width estimates, the ugly proof that the cage still contains **suffering-shaped** fragments, not blank hunger alone. Varro hears that proof as indictment of his own decades of presence-without-ending.

---

**Block B — The name (reach that fails).**  
Against caution — or because measure requires a spike — Gideon tries to **reach** the brother by speaking the true name:

— Atreus.

Agitation. Storm spike. Grille violence. The name does not restore; it **hurts**. Varro does not strike Gideon for the attempt — the Judex asked for measure, and measure sometimes costs. He corrects the method, not the courage: identity scraps are not levers; they are wounds. The session ends with a clearer bound on turmoil size and a colder rule: **do not spend the name for hope.** (Later, correcting Trazyn with the name is different — that is refusal of specimen-language when the ward cannot be used as a switch.)

Varro may speak more of Atreus **after** the spike, when the beast has receded: who he was in brief strokes only (no omniscient biography) — enough that Gideon understands the jailer is not housing a trophy, and enough that the reader feels the century of unfinished debt.

---

**Block C — Second / third sittings (weight, not montage).**  
More talk. Gideon adjusts: comfort without name-keys; ego-file from the outside; size the storm by what settles when Death Wolf presence (Varro) stands in frame. Failures remain loud. Gains stay small and clinical. Between sittings, Varro’s shame is sharper because the map exists: he can no longer pretend Atreus is only a closed problem. The never-chose are not abstract — they have a face of storm and a name that must not be used as bait.

Optional thin thread: mention of other pre-Testament wards still held, or already ended — Atreus as the longest surviving of that injustice — so Gideon sees policy, not only one tragedy.

---

**Block D — End of Part II: Trazyn.**  
Only when the teaching and the failed reach have landed does the theft begin. Impossible displacement, collection commit, surrogate. Debate — now with earned context: Trazyn offers forever-shelf; Gideon can answer from measured turmoil (forever would freeze suffering, not silence it); Varro answers from debt. Extraction fires against Gideon’s seated anchor → envelope widens → **Aethelgard**.

**Close.** Open air. Atreus loose. Hunt clock starts (≤ ~6 h). Part III.

### III. Free roam + Hunt — split **IIIA (Atreus POV)** / **IIIB (Varro + Gideon)**

**Clock:** entire free + hunt envelope **≤ ~6 h** or Aethelgard faces planetwide major crisis.  
**Cutoff:** Part III ends when the trail is fully read and the hunters are closing — **before** the final set-piece with Trazyn’s forces and Atreus’s death (**Part IV**).

**Reading order (lock intent):** **IIIA then IIIB** (sequential). Reader lives the free storm first; then pays for it in Judex eyes. (Intercut later only if prose demands.)

### IIIA‑PLAN. Atreus POV — free roam (staging — not chronicle body)

**Tone lock (absolute for IIIA prose later):**
- **Sensations first**, then acts. Cold wind. Grass under feet. Mud on hands. Smell of blood → **wrong geometry** waking.
- Voice always **caveman-adjacent**, **very simple** — short scraps, almost no grammar elegance (see §4). Never lucid memoir. Never sermon.
- Twin track in the skull: faint veto / sorrow / “not me” **vs** Versibar scream — *more death, more blood, more meat* — always louder after each kill.

**Open — land / body.**  
Aethelgard. Wind cold on fur. Grass on feet. Mud on hands. Sky big. Not cage. Free. Then blood-smell on the air — not his yet — and the storm inside **tilts**.

**Prey ladder (locked order):**

1. **Salt deer (elk-adjacent), already bleeding.** Two (or a couple) fighting each other — wounded, easy wrong-prey. Kill. Devour **hearts** — **each beast has three**. Versibar howls *more*. Small veto dies under meat.

2. **Batav (the wolf — not Batavi the marine).** He hears a batav move near. Kill. **Sorrow** — he was among the **first** who saw the Chapter become **Batavi**; the wolf is pack-memory, not stranger meat. Sorrow does not stop the scream for more.

3. **Village.** Whole settlement. Afterward: disgust. *This wrong. This not me.* Versibar does not care. *More. More. More.*

4. **Quinta neophyte (Joris)** — on a **probation / trial** Quinta employs. Kill. Eat flesh. Eat hearts. Then the glimpse: he **cries** for what he has become — fragment grief, not restored personhood. Versibar still screams under the crying.

**Close of IIIA.** Crying and hunger in the same skull. Open sky. Part IIIB will find the trail he does not name as crime.

**Lexicon note for writers:** **batav** = Chapter wolf; **Batavi** = Astartes of the Chapter. Do not conflate in Atreus scraps or narrator gloss.

### IIIB‑PLAN. Varro + Gideon — the hunt (staging — not chronicle body)

**Tone lock:** Moral mirror of IIIA. Atreus *did*; Varro *reads the bill*.  
- Varro: Judex-spare inventory and weight (not caveman, not sermon).  
- Gideon: measure and consequence.  
- **Do not rush.** Each station of the trail is a full beat with its own coin. Montage to Joris is forbidden in planning and in prose.

**Open — clock and mission.**  
Same ≤6 h envelope, other eyes. Displacement wreckage; Aethelgard trial weather; no Cell geometry. Mission split in silence: **contain / recover the brother** vs Trazyn **collect** (collector signature may grow on the board; **full clash = Part IV only**). Gideon is still under Otho’s mandate — size the turmoil — but the storm is **moving**.

---

**Station 1 — Salt deer wreckage (proof, not climax).**  
Three-heart butchery. Fighting-pair already wounded, then finished.  
- **Gideon:** first bound on Versibar feed-pattern *in motion* — not random predation; ritualized hunger (hearts). Files it.  
- **Varro:** proof the cage failed and the brother is free enough to *eat as Execratio*. Omen, not yet the worst bill.  
- **Talk (thin):** pattern confirmed; next vector guessed (warm blood, settlement noise, trial lanes). No absolution.

**Spend time here.** Smell, mud, opened chests, the wrong neatness of three hearts taken. Let the reader arrive from IIIA’s sensation into IIIB’s accountability without skipping.

---

**Station 2 — Batav (the wolf).**  
Pack corpse.  
- **Varro pays the deep cut:** pack-warmth; Atreus among those who saw the Chapter *become* Batavi; wolf is not stranger meat. Silence changes quality — the silence Bram would have named colder.  
- **Gideon:** witnesses that change without filing it for ranks; learns the jailer’s weather by standing in it.  
- **Talk (needed):** one or two strokes only of who Atreus *was* — enough that the wolf’s death is not anonymous fauna. Not a biography dump. Gideon may ask; Varro answers in inventory that hurts.

**Do not leave this station early.** The batav is the bridge between beast-trail and Chapter-debt.

---

**Station 3 — Village.**  
Whole settlement spent.  
- **Varro:** Shared Sweat inverted — the Wall’s reason for existing, eaten by the Wall’s unfinished brother. Disgust that rhymes Atreus’s *not me*, from the other side of the grille.  
- **Gideon:** impulse to stop, seal, count, bury — **clock forbids** full mercy theater. Measure the slaughter as turmoil expressed at population scale.  
- **Talk (needed):** Gideon presses whether ending Atreus now is still “brother.” Varro does not answer cleanly. The question stays open into Station 4.  
- Optional pressure: distant vox / Quinta trial traffic / civilian survivors — interrupt, don’t resolve.

**Stay with the village.** Bodies, ash, the time they *don’t* have. Rushing here cheapens Joris.

---

**Station 4 — Joris (Quinta probation trial).**  
Named. Unfinished plate. Flesh and hearts taken — trail matches IIIA.  
- **Varro’s fear proper:** not death — the Wall’s past eating the Wall’s future; century of cage and comfort-work and delay made this hour possible. Anguish, responsibility, worry.  
- **Gideon:** witness only. Does not absolve. Does not file the jailer-read.  
- **Name discipline:** Gideon almost spends **Atreus** again (reach / report / grief). **Varro stops it** — name is wound; the trail is already loud enough; they will not bait the storm for feeling.  
- Mark what can be marked for later Frontier Wolf / Quinta accounting. **No full rite** — clock.

**This station is the moral peak of IIIB.** Give it room: finding, recognizing neophyte marks / trial gear, the eaten hearts, Varro’s stillness, Gideon’s refusal to pretend this is only a specimen event.

---

**Push — after Joris, still not Part IV.**  
They hunt forward. Sparse talk: next hunger vector (more trials, more settlement, sea-edge). Trazyn’s phase-wrongness louder on the board — pressure, pursuit math, not the set-piece. Varro may speak one line of debt without sermon. Gideon offers no comfort that would close the wound.

**Close of IIIB.** Contact imminent — Atreus located or about to be; collector forces on the same ground. Fear and measure both unpaid. **Part IV** = fight beside Gideon, Varro’s stroke, rise / *We hold*, collector’s scrap.

**Anti-rush checklist (writers):** if any station is only one paragraph of “they found X and moved on,” expand. IIIA bought complicity in sensation; IIIB must buy time in responsibility.

### IV. Aethelgard close — fight, stroke, *We hold*, collector scrap

**Cutoff lock:** Part IV is the **Aethelgard terminus** of the hunt. Ends with Atreus dead, Trazyn denied the living prize (but not empty-handed), Varro and Gideon alone with the private truth. **Part V** = return, Quinta funeral, Triumvirate law.

### IV‑PLAN. Part IV planning prose (staging — not chronicle body)

**Tone:** Not parade victory. Clock almost spent. Joris unpaid. Collector and jailer on the same ground. Gideon must see **Varro the failure**, not Judex theater.

---

**Block A — Contact / three-way geometry.**  
Atreus located. Trazyn’s forces commit to recover the specimen before the Batavi end him or the clock makes the world unrecoverable. Varro + Gideon fight **side by side** — *Iron Verdict* / Absolution and Gideon’s measure-war (anchor, denial of clean phase lanes), not duel glory. Atreus is storm between them: sometimes obstacle, sometimes prize the collector still reaches for. Keep the set-piece **clear enough to read**, not so large it swallows the personal stroke.

**Talk (thin, earned):** Trazyn may still speak shelf-mercy; Varro has no sermon left — only refusal. Gideon does not debate ethics mid-swing; he keeps Atreus’s present *real* enough that theft stays expensive.

---

**Block B — The stroke (plea, not sentence).**  
Opening: Atreus spent, pinned, or the envelope of the fight forces a choice — collect vs end.  
**Varro strikes.** Not clean Judex theater. Desperate ask for forgiveness from the brother he caged without consent — jailer, betrayer, keeper of torment. Gideon sees that. Ranks are not here to see it.

---

**Block C — Rise / *We hold* (seconds only).**  
1. Atreus falls.  
2. Rises once — last fragment, **not** restored personhood.  
3. Fist to chest. Mangled mouth almost fails the words: ***We hold***.  
4. Final collapse / death.  

Forgiveness that does **not** heal: enough for Varro to **steel** and carry being who he is; not enough to empty the wound.  
***We hold* secrecy:** ranks **never** learn. Gideon may half-hear; does not publish. Varro is sure.

No second lucid speech. No embrace. No name spent as lever in this hour unless Trazyn forces specimen-language and Varro refuses it again — brief, not a speech.

---

**Block D — Collector’s exit (bitter scrap).**  
Living Versibar denied. Surrogate may be destroyed or withdrawn. Trazyn leaves with **blood and/or tissue + data/telemetry**, and a collector’s lesson: shelving this thing and later **freeing** it (museum attack, contingency) could threaten the **entire collection** worse than invaders — hazard, not safe trophy. He is not humiliated into reform; he is informed. Varro does not credit him as teacher.

---

**Close of Part IV.** Aethelgard quieting. Atreus ended. Joris still a bill for Part V. Gideon and Varro share a private ledger: the stroke was not Judex; the brother answered anyway. Confidence chain completes its wartime half — Gideon will become the rare counselor; Bram remains the mortal latch at home. Part V opens on return and the Quinta funeral.

**Anti-dilution checklist:** if the Necron fight outscales the stroke + *We hold*, cut the fight; the personal terminus is the point of *Keeper of Torment*.
