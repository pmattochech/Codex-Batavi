## 1. Frame — Noviomagus as provincial capital

**Noviomagus Prime** is **not** Holy Terra — it is the closest thing the subsector has to a **provincial capital**: Imperial forms arrive intact (Administratum registers, Ministorum chapels where soot allows, High Gothic in law), while **local identity** remains **forge-first**.

Citizens think in **shifts**, **district stamps**, **yield**, and **rain schedules**. The Throne is honored when **the line holds** and the **tithe seal** reads clean.

**Scale & role (from atlas fork):** ~**120 billion** in stacked hives; **civilized forge world** — arms, armor, throughput. Direct Chapter administrative shadow.

**Economy-ecology:** **1.12G**, hollowed crust, adamantium ribs, **acid Black Rain**. Life is **rust management**: filters, gutters, reclaim vats.

**Porta Nihili flavor:** Faith is **throughput theology**. Saints are **forge masters whose dies never wandered**. Sin is **unlogged scrap** and **missed torque**. Chapels exist; workers often prefer **the stamp booth** — *“God saw the seal.”*

**Already-filed social hooks (atlas):** district dynasties (e.g. Guild of the Lower Gutter, Third Shift Concord), **civil ID plaque** cult (birth iron), **elevator castes** between hive stacks, **Solis-tier** bridge households. Fork energy: **provincial Roman** — High Gothic law, local forge cant; urban *limitanei* who built the wall themselves.

**Shards already on atlas (keep):** *rain-closed face*; *gear prayer* (three knuckle taps on a duty belt — later borrowed into Batavi habit); *black tooth* (honest corrosion as seniority).

## 2. Basal creed — faith of work (Porta Nihili accent on Noviomagus)

* **The Emperor is paid in labor.** Completed shift, logged manifest, child registered to quota, fuse replaced before the hive browns out — prayers with receipts.
* **Speech is cheap; seals are holy.** Ecstatic preachers who cannot audit a cargo slate = entertainment or suspect.
* **Silence can be offering** when networks are sacred (relay silence, listen-only watches) — Men of Iron hangover generalized into habit.
* Mortals remain **Ecclesiarchy-baptized** on record. Accent is frontier: God loves **the useful hand**.
* Chapter faith note for contrast only: Batavi honor Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight — **not** Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Population still uses Imperial chapel language; the **feel** is work-first.

## 3. Three classes (draft lock candidate)

Working labels: **Rabble** · **Common population** · **Upper class**.  
(Not Administratum legal castes — anthropological strata for fiction.)

### 3.1 The Rabble

**Who:** Undercroft reclaim crews, gutter runners, unstamped scrap-sorters, rain-blind beggars between elevator rights, Black-Rain lungers who sell filter time by the hour.

**Mood landing:**

| Premise | How it functions |
|---------|------------------|
| Duty without audience | Survival *is* the shift. Lowest drains held because if reclaim fails, the stacks above drown in rust first — geometry, not patriotism |
| Forgotten, not faithless | Never on the ledger worth remembering → **pre-forgotten**. Oath = don’t steal a brother’s filter; don’t leave a body in open rain |
| Weathered service | Faith = hands that still move. Ministorum chalk-saints feel like someone else’s furniture |
| Loss as weather | Missing people are weather reports. Names stop at the district lip. Grief short, practical, sometimes cruel — then back to the vat |

**Sound:** Short sentences. Dark humor. Proto–*We hold* as “we don’t leave the pipe.” Occasional bitter pride when a Batavi shadow passes and they pretend not to watch.

---

### 3.2 The common population

**Who:** Shift guilds, middle-stack district dynasties, stamp clerks, forge hands with civil ID plaques, Third Shift Concord types — spine of throughput theology.

**Mood landing:**

| Premise | How it functions |
|---------|------------------|
| Duty without audience | The seal *is* the prayer. Yield must read clean; the Wall above must not starve for shells. Terra’s applause is not in the contract |
| Forgotten, not faithless | Know they are Nihilus-edge. Still baptize, still stamp births in iron, still name the Emperor — in **forge cant**, not cathedral ecstasy |
| Weathered service | Virtue = logged torque, on-time shift, rain schedule kept. Sin = unlogged scrap, missed quota, empty speech. Stamp booth over chapel when holiness needs a receipt |
| Loss as weather | Convoy silence, Wall-name rumors, cousins who never returned from levy — filed next to rust. Melancholy as discipline, not theater |

**Sound:** Exhausted loyalty. *Rain-closed face.* Gear-prayer taps. Soft contempt for loud Imperial missionaries; quiet respect for anyone (mortal or Astartes) who **shares sweat** without speeches.

---

### 3.3 The upper class

**Who:** Stack-elevator magnates, forge-house elders, Administratum liaison blood, Ministorum chapter-house patrons, and **lower-upper bridge houses** — people who can buy cleaner air and cleaner seals.

**Solis family (lock):** **Lower upper class.** Bridge household that can enter forge-cant rooms and Chapter-adjacent civil rooms without holding peak elevator rights. Soft power = Shared Sweat / kitchen continuity and quiet *face-hold* — still upper, not mid-common.

**Mood landing:**

| Premise | How it functions |
|---------|------------------|
| Duty without audience | Audience is **local** (Chapter shadow, tithe audit, rival houses), not Terra. Imperial form kept because **form keeps the forge legal**. Holding face is a kind of Wall |
| Forgotten, not faithless | Forgetting felt as **status anxiety**: provincial capital of a cut mesh. Over-invest in seals, saints’ windows, “correct” devotion so outsiders cannot call them fringe barbarians |
| Weathered service | Keep the machine fed — labor quotas, ammo contracts, rain infrastructure. Charity = rationed usefulness (filter endowments, widow guilds), not ecstasy. Fear useless piety as much as heresy |
| Loss as weather | Name losses in marble and ledgers — still treat them as **cost**. House continuity absorbs Guard sons, drowned districts, Wall-fusion stories from below |

**Sound:** Cooler, longer sentences; forge-cant under High Gothic. Resent rabble noise; envy Batavi certainty. Some quietly prefer Chapter silence to Ministorum thunder — not love, **recognition** that the Wall also needs no audience. Lower-upper / Solis band: quieter than magnates; less auctioned piety.

## 4. Class shards (draft lock candidate)

One **slang** / **small rite** / **taboo** per class. Atlas-wide shards (*rain-closed face*, *black tooth*) still apply as mesh color; class shards below are stratum-specific.

### 4.1 Rabble

| Kind | Shard | Note |
|------|-------|------|
| **Slang** | *filter-hour* | Unit of life; “owe me two filter-hours” = real debt |
| **Rite** | *pipe watch* | Before a reclaim dive: one hand on the pipe, one on a brother’s shoulder; no words if the rain is loud |
| **Taboo** | Body in **open Black Rain** | Worse than theft — the rain “eats names” |

### 4.2 Common

| Kind | Shard | Note |
|------|-------|------|
| **Slang** | *seal-clean* | Morally right *and* administratively safe; “that’s not seal-clean” ends arguments |
| **Rite** | *gear prayer* | Three knuckle taps on the duty belt at shift start (already on atlas; **common default**) |
| **Taboo** | **Unlogged scrap** | Stealing from the Throne’s yield — worse gossip than chapel absence |

### 4.3 Upper (incl. lower-upper / Solis band)

| Kind | Shard | Note |
|------|-------|------|
| **Slang** | *face-hold* | Keeping Imperial form when the mesh feels forgotten; Solis houses do this quietly, without auctioning piety |
| **Rite** | *window stamp* | Touch the house seal (or chapel lintel) once when leaving for Chapter/civil rooms — continuity, not spectacle |
| **Taboo** | **Loud devotion** that can’t survive an audit | Empty sermonizing that shames the forge; lower-upper especially hates looking like fringe barbarians to Batavi or Administratum guests |

**Solis color:** lean *window stamp* + *face-hold*; Shared Sweat / kitchen continuity as soft power inside the lower-upper band.
