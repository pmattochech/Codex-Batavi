## 1. Mood lock (use this)

Castra Vetera mesh people — **especially before** firm Imperial reconquest / Cohors Batavorum ratification — feel like:

* **Duty without audience.** They hold because the line must hold, not because Terra is watching.
* **Forgotten, not faithless.** Oath and toil remain; recognition does not.
* **Weathered service.** Faith is shift, seal, and rain schedule — *we serve because we must*.
* **Loss as ordinary weather.** Hidden tombs, missing convoys, names that never reach a remembrancer — grief filed as logistics.

**Lyric beats → mesh echo (author only):**

| External lyric beat | Castra Vetera mortal register |
|---------------------|-------------------------------|
| Hold unflinching / duty-bound | Proto–*We hold* in worker and auxilia mouths |
| Forgotten / cursed by the past | Insula cut by ribbon + fringe; Grey Hounds / bottle centuries |
| Hidden tomb / all gone | Walls, pyres, names fused where no clerk came |
| Serve because we must | Basal creed of work ([`porta-nihili-and-basal-stratum.md`](../atlas-and-topography/cultures/porta-nihili-and-basal-stratum.md) §2) |

**Do not:** paste lyrics into `codex-batavi/` as in-world hymns; claim Rogue Trader / Zarkov continuity; replace Batavi *Axiom* with Ministorum ecstasy.

**Do:** let pre-reconquest and deep-hive POV sound like **exhausted loyalty** — iron still standing after the paperwork died.
