## 1) What “Legatus Council” means here

Three distinct rooms — do not collapse them in prose:

| Body | When | Who | Output |
|------|------|-----|--------|
| **Legatus Ring** (standing) | scheduled / crisis convocation | Legati **I–IX** (+ **Radulf** witness weight when convened) | operational consensus on **Vexillatio** geometry, rotation, and **Tier I–II** readiness — not terminal cull authorization alone |
| **Old Voices sealed session** | inversion-risk recognition | **Varro**, **Drusus**, **Corbec**, **Armin** (+ observers: **Kadmos**, **Otho**, **Tyvar**) | redundancy **proposal** and custody sketch — see chronicle |
| **Black chamber ratification** | post-mechanics lock | **Varro**, **Drusus**, **Tyvar** + **assigned Legatus signatories** | wax seals on **Axiom Lupus** + custody annex — see chronicle |

Planning rule: the **Legatus Council** is the **Ring** plus the **ratification signatory set**. The Old Voices session is the **genesis argument**, not the permanent voting chamber.

**Excluded from Legatus Council vote weight:** **10th Vexillatio (*Decima*)** — Triumvirate authority applies directly; **Kadmos** and **Otho** hold **Tier B** execution custody, not Ring seats.

## 5) Consensus mechanics (not head-count democracy)

The Ring does **not** use simple majority vote on sealed doctrine.

**Working model:**

1. **Proposal block** — typically **Corbec + Armin** (Old Voices genesis); any Legatus may add **amendment slices** scoped to their theater.
2. **Clinical lock** — **Drusus** marks biologically incoherent options dead (capture at inversion scale, etc.).
3. **Judex lock** — **Varro** marks legally incoherent options dead (rank exception, grief reinterpretation).
4. **Threshold readiness** — **Tyvar** marks delay drift; **Radulf** marks honor-weight insults that would break Ring compliance later.
5. **Consensus declaration** — recorded when **no Tier A holder** files written dissent within the sealed window **and** Triumvirate dual seal attaches.

**Dissent handling:**

- **Recorded dissent** stays in **Vermilion** annex — not open *vox*.
- Dissent does **not** grant veto on Tier III if **Trigger Group C** fires; it grants **audit liability** post-event.
- Persistent dissent without alternative geometry → **theater substitution** (§6) for that Vexillatio’s Tier A seat.

**Signatory order (black chamber — planning default):** Varro → Drusus → Tyvar → **Corbec** → **Armin** → remaining Tier A Legati in **Vexillatio number order** (II → IX, skipping vacant).

## 6) Fallback procedures

### A. Ring deadlock (no consensus before crisis)

- **Varro + Drusus** may attach **interim continuity schedule** for Tier I–II only.
- **Armin** may demand **validation lane** (`09-war-game-black-mirror`, `10-proof-of-chain`) before Tier III text is executable.
- **Tyvar** may flag **Custodes readiness** independent of Ring comfort — does not authorize cull alone.

### B. Tier A Legatus casualty or unreachable (warp-leg / siege isolation)

| Vacant seat | First substitute | Second substitute |
|-------------|------------------|-------------------|
| *Prima* (Corbec) | *Secunda* Legatus (**Kessler**) | *Quinta* (**Armin**) |
| *Quinta* (Armin) | *Prima* (**Corbec**) | *Tertia* (**Brach**) |
| Other I–IX | Adjacent **theater-linked** Legatus per *Strategium* substitution map | **Radulf** witness until re-seat |

Substitution inherits **Tier A** custody **minus** kill-route segments the substitute has not been briefed on — **Drusus** must certify briefing completeness or downgrade to **Tier I–II** authority only.

### C. Triumvirate partial casualty

- **Varro only:** Judex lock holds; **Drusus** co-seal required for matrix rewrite; **Otho** holds Blindgate continuity.
- **Drusus only:** clinical lock frozen at last hash; **Varro** + **Tyvar** may execute Tier II firewall; Tier III requires **dual Tier A Legatus** confirm + **Tyvar** readiness.
- **Both Varro and Drusus unavailable:** **Tyvar** + **Radulf** + **Corbec** convoke **emergency Ring**; **Kadmos/Otho** Tier B only until Tier A reconstituted.

### D. Castellan present but non-responsive (Silent Vigil core case)

- Ring routes orders through **Legatus substitution chain** documented in `10-proof-of-chain`.
- **Alaric** retains **objective-only** awareness; Ring must **not** disclose executor choreography in his presence.
- **Martha’s kitchen** is **not** a council venue — Denial Protocol and domestic frame unchanged.

### E. Post-event rollback

- Temporary Tier B elevations revoked after stabilization (`access-matrix` rollback event).
- Ring convenes **audit session** — dissent file opened if Tier III was **not** fired but Tier II was.
