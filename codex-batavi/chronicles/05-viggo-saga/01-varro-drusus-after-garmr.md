# [CHRONICLE] AFTER GARMR — VARRO AND DRUSUS

**Classification:** *Reclusiam* Vermilion / *Apothecarion* sealed concordance  
**When:** **~055.M42** (immediately after [`varro-anima-speculum.md`](varro-anima-speculum.md))  
**Where:** Noviomagus Prime — *Reclusiam* sub-vault, adjoining *Apothecarion* corridor  
**Who:** **Varro** (Judex), **Drusus** (Chief Apothecary), subject of concern **Alaric von Helis**  
**Cross-references:** [`garmr-witness-dravic.md`](garmr-witness-dravic.md) · [`viggo-saga.md`](viggo-saga.md)

---

The sub-vault had no decoration and no mercy. Steel walls drank sound, and the lamps kept the same cold color from shift-start to shift-end, as if time itself were under discipline.

Varro waited in full plate, cowl low, red lenses fixed on nothing visible. Dravic had left minutes earlier. His words had not.

The door cycled once. Drusus entered without escort, white surgical arm reflecting lumen in hard lines.

— You called outside schedule — Drusus said.

— Correct.

Drusus stopped two meters from Varro and held that distance, the same way one holds a safe distance from an open machine.

— The sergeant?

— Stable enough for line duty.

— Good.

Varro’s lenses shifted by one degree.

— Not good enough.

Drusus did not answer immediately. He brought up a slate, then lowered it again without reading from it.

— You want verbal before numbers.

— Yes.

Drusus gave him what he asked.

— Garmr’s Shelf confirms abbreviated *rubor thermicus* recurrence under stacked pressure. Forced helm-loss by hostile strike. Predatory window then self-recapture at non-combatant trigger. Post-event command delay. Emotional burden high. Functional output preserved.

Varro’s voice remained flat.

— Say it in one sentence.

— It happened again.

The steel between them seemed to tighten.

— And now there is a pup in the iron chassis — Varro said. — A live variable where armor should be certainty.

— Collar niche, not core cavity — Drusus corrected.

— It is still a variable.

— Yes.

Drusus looked down the corridor toward the closed chamber where Alaric should have already reported and had not.

— You think this is no longer only thermal.

— It was never only thermal.

Drusus gave a short nod.

— Clinical concerns, then. One: recurrence probability rises if command latency increases after a window. Two: self-hatred masquerading as restraint can produce non-response at command level. Three: niche carry introduces additional tactical vulnerability and decision drag.

Varro completed the list without looking at him.

— Four: line witnesses absorb fear debt and convert it into doctrine if we leave a vacuum.

— Agreed.

Silence sat with them like a third officer.

When Varro spoke again, his tone did not rise, but the air changed around it.

— This is not indictment of Alaric.

Drusus met the red lenses.

— It is preservation of Alaric.

— And of the Chapter that hangs from him.

Drusus unfolded the slate now and keyed a formal channel.

— Triumvirate summons template?

— Full seal. No advisory language.

— Attendance mandatory.

— Attendance mandatory — Varro repeated.

Drusus entered the final line and held his thumb over the confirmation rune.

— We are not punishing him.

Varro answered without pause.

— We are refusing to lose him.

Drusus sent the summons.

The confirmation tone was small and mechanical. In that chamber it sounded like a verdict.

Neither moved.

Neither needed to say what both already understood: if Alaric crossed this threshold and stood where required, the fracture remained a fracture. If he did not, fracture became policy.

Varro turned toward the inner door.

— We wait one cycle.

Drusus closed the slate.

— And if he does not come.

Varro’s helm tilted fractionally, the gesture of a man deciding where to place a blade.

— Then we go to the palace.
