## III. PROOF (WEIGHT)

Proof, in the tongue the Wall offers *tourists*, is borrowed finery. It is lifted from chapels and from the theater of the *court-martial* — clean lines for a *scroll* a *Crown* can sign without blushing, as if ink need never know blood. *Terra* wants evidence you can file in a tidy *font*; a stamp the Administratum can love without owning a throat. The *Strategium* does not start with a verdict, it starts with a pan. *Weight* is the cheap word *trend* misnames, and the honest one *gravity* refuses to. It arrives as a relay of small ugly signals: the *auspex* that should wobble, the *Munitorum* hour that should lose an argument to bone, the guest *vox* that should crack once if the line is still *mortal* the ordinary way. When all of that *stays* flat, proof ceases to be a caption and becomes a *pressure* a *Limes* gut names the way a bore names a *rivet* — honesty before any *blessing* gilds the number, and honesty before a *Vermilion* *noun* wets a sealed *annex* the way blood wets a seal, on the day *Munitorum* *green* stops reading as a benediction and reads as a civil fiction a tithe *row* can still file as orderly *loss*.

*Magos-auxiliary Henrik Soll* had not screamed into the *Strategium*. A proud Chapter *chatters*, and a proud line leaves fingerprints in the noosphere, little sins a shell can trade. The *Ash* line on the *vox* that morning was flat the way a chart is flat after someone irons it — too polite, too complete, a courtesy with no price in public, and therefore no truth the *Mechanicus* can fold into a plan. His log was not a siren, not a halt order, not a theater staged for a distant *Terra* clerk’s comfort. It was **PATTERN FLAT (NONCONCLUSIVE / MONITOR)**: a lean admission that *flat* is not the same as *innocent*, and that a hull can grow a civil shape when a crew rehearses silence the way a billet rehearses *drill* — *throughput* in the voice, a *Munitorum* stamp in the *manners*. Soll nudged the shell with a heavier *feather* on the *scale*, because a *Magos* who shouts *wolf* early is a *Magos* the *Strategium* stops *hearing* on the *one* day a *wolf* is *real* enough to weigh a forgeworld in teeth, and a forgeworld that misreads *flat* can pay for the misread in *vapor* a tithe *row* will still file as *orderly* *loss*.

Brother *Apothecary Thess* and the guest, *Carn* had *not* turned a med-bay *annex* *into* a *scene* *Terra* *could* *crown* in a *single* pict *crawl*. The *Limes* *tithe* of *continuity* — *vials*, *ritual*, the clumsy *proof* a *wandering* *line* is *still* a *Chapter* and *not* a *pirate* pantomime — *wants* a *tolerance* a *Crown* can *tour* in a *tidy* *font* before it tithes a truth a guest is still eager to *hide* behind a *courtesy* *scan* first. Thess whispered a *relay* a billet *learns* to *hate* — because a bell is *how* a *clean* guest *turns* to *collateral* in a *ledger* that had printed *continuity* as a blessing: **CARN, exit, rehearsed, pair with Soll** — *chain* *and* *not* *verdict*; a *door* *kept* *boring* a *minute* *longer* in *Limes* *Munitorum* *civil* *cloth*.

In the *Strategium* shell, **Varro** and **Drusus** did not *debate*; they *weighed* Thess, Carn, and Soll in one *pan* before the heap earned the cheap word *verdict*. A *wall* is *geometry* before *blessing*; a *Limes* courtesy is a *cordon* in *bored* clothing *until* the *day* bares *teeth* and a *Limes* hour *names* a *hatch* *wrong*.

— Carn is breathing *fear* dressed as *courtesy*, — Drusus said on a tight vox, — the scan is *courtesy* because Thess *made* it *courtesy*.

— I will not waste a captive mind on a guest I can drown in *ink*, — Varro returned — *yet* the *ink* is *already* *wet*.

A relay crossed to the *Fifth*; the **Legatus of the *Quinta*** received no theatrics, only a *weight* *tick* and a *Strategium* *request* to *hold* *Vexillatio* lanes *uncrossed* until a Castellan *closed* a *mouth* *or* *opened* a *bore*.

*A* *sealed* *packet* *moved* *on* the *Falange* of *Nine* *mesh* — *three* *by* *three*, a *tourism* of *grids* *Crown* *chancelleries* *love* *on* parchments *Noviomagus* *billets* *as* *a* *throat* the *Munitorum* *row* *must* *count* *honestly* *before* a *Vermilion* *annex* *nicks* a *Limes* *tongue* and *Crown* *arithmetic* *learns* a *Limes* *gut* the way a *bore* *keeps* a *rivet* *tight* *until* a *seal* *fails* *or* a *fist* *tightens* *first*.
