## I. TEXTURE

The *Limes* did not ask the Wall to love its guests. It asked the Wall to **feed** them without feeding the void between stars, because the void is not a metaphor in Castra Vetera: it is a *mouth* you pay in fuel, time, and blood, and it does not *tithe* in fair proportions. *Treaty mouth* is the polite name for a sentence the Administratum can stamp without blushing: *if your seal is in order, we will extend credit you may hate needing.* *Treaty gut* is the ruder lesson, the one the dock teaches with smells: the frontier learns which stomachs can take a stranger’s hunger without *cramping* the line, and it learns it by watching men choose between being *courteous* and being *true*, and by discovering, again and again, that on a *Limes* day *courtesy* is the faster route to a throat you can *measure* later.

A *Limes* year is *bored* the way a forge is bored: not sleepy, not kind. Bored the way a blade is bored into a scabbard with no play. The Wall’s hospitality is a *tolerance* you can *file* as law if you are the kind of officer who needs law to *excuse* what your gut already *knows*. The Batavi do not mistake *tolerance* for *friendship*; they mistake it for *room* — room to feed a guest, room to watch a guest, room to *close* a guest if the guest confuses *room* for *safety*.

That morning the high dock wore the *green* fiction of a routine day. Batches and stamps and the small, consoling lie that *today* is like *yesterday* because the auspex says *green* and the *Munitorum* hour says *on schedule* and the *Armourium* — which calls all of that *throughput* — smiles in the private language of tonnage. Four friendly Chapter signatures in two decades, twice that many *Munitorum* trains, and a whole taxonomy of *almost*-legal void traffic the Chapter shelves as *Limes* *tolerance*; the dock hands learn the difference between a *visitor* and a *predator* the way a billet learns the difference between a *drill* and a *choir*: by *pressure*, not *names*.

When the **Ash Standard** made approach, the sheet *said* *legal* — margin, tithe, gene-seed still *Imperial-shaped* enough to pass a first gate. The ship’s story was *elsewhere* in the Imperium, written in burn scoring and *empty* *ready* *berths* the manifest did not *own*. The Chapter had bled somewhere else. It wanted **bolts**, **promethium**, a hold that would not *ask* for stories. Ordinary hungers, until you noticed the *manner* of the wanting, and the manner *stank* — not the stink of rot, not yet; the stink of *order* *too* *complete*, a ship that *bowed* at every line as if the deck were a *confessional* and the Wall were a *priest* with a *stamp* instead of a *soul*.

**Mira Isten** had been barge factor on a civil writ for thirty-two years. A late pallet was *usually* grief in work-order clothing, not *malice*. The Ash Standard’s lines were *too* clean, seal by seal, a smile that had not *earned* trust. Guest serfs did not *have* to *avoid* a dock hand’s eyes; they *did* anyway, glances hunting *exits* before they hunted *freight*, as if the deck were a *stage* with the fire just off the boards, *always* *just* *off* the boards, *always* *polite*.

**Henn Yarrow** still believed politeness *meant* safety. He leaned in.

— The vox is *very* polite today, — he said — I almost thanked it back.

Isten did not look up from her margin.

— Politeness is free, — she said — honesty is expensive. A man who overpays in politeness is *smuggling* a second *invoice*.

— That is a little *grim* for a manifest, — Yarrow tried.

— *Grim* is what a manifest looks like *right* before it *lies*, — Isten answered.

Yarrow laughed, because humans do, then *stopped* because Isten had not *joined*.

Alaric was not looking at a pict. He was watching a *lattice* of requests arrive too fast, too *polite*, stacked in the submissive grammar of a man who expected an *audit* in his sleep. A crusade fleet *barks*; men who think the frontier owes a bow *drawl*; these packets *kneeled*, and kneeling is a posture you can *train* a bureaucracy to *love* if the bureaucracy is hungry enough. The *Strategium* shell offered the cheap word *anxiety*; the Castellan wrote only this, because writing more would be a kind of *mercy* he was not in the business of *selling* to a line that had not *earned* *mercy* yet: *they are not negotiating with us. They are negotiating with what chases them.* He let the *first* hour go to the people a mistake could *kill* — or who could die for being *right* too *early* and too *loud*. A *Limes* day is not a *debate*; it is a cordon staged as courtesy until it is not, and the difference is not *tone* but *which* serfs *live* if a *hatch* breathes wrong and the *Strategium* must pick between a polite stamp and a *closed* *mouth*.

Deeper in the noosphere, **Magos-auxiliary Henrik Soll** held a *skin* the Mechanicus wanted smooth. On guest days, what *gnawed* him was the *silence* where chatter should have been. A proud Chapter *chatters* — a serf joke, a bungle, a Techmarine’s sour aside, a lie you can *use* because it is *human* *enough* to *fold* into a plan. The Ash *line* on the vox was *flat* the way a chart is flat *after* someone *ironed* it, and Soll had seen that *shape* before, in another *system*, on another *day* that had ended with *Vermilion* words in a file nobody *read* for *pleasure*.

He logged **PATTERN FLAT (NONCONCLUSIVE / MONITOR)**. He nudged a *lean* weight to the *Strategium* shell — not a siren, not a *halt* order, only a heavier feather on scales already tipping, because a Magos who *screams* wolf early is a Magos the *Strategium* soon stops hearing even when the wolf is real *enough* to weigh a forgeworld in *teeth*, and a forgeworld that *misreads* a guest’s *silence* can pay for that misread in *vapor* and *tithe* the *Munitorum* will still file as *orderly* *loss*.

**Brother-Sergeant Varik Orest** of the Fifth *Vexilla* QVINTA *liked* a rude guest, the way a man *likes* a problem he can *name* without having to *love* the problem’s face. Rudeness is *honest*; you can *plan* for *honest*; *courtesy* that dried *his* mouth was a different kind of *weather*, and the weather *wrong* in a *Limes* hour where a sergeant’s *job* is to keep a deck from mistaking a *Munitorum* “green” for a *benediction* just because a *Munitorum* lens is trained to *love* a civil stamp more than a true breath. The high dock, that morning, still carried the *bored* light the auspex would file as routine, the *bored* *Munitorum* hour the *Armourium* would file as *throughput*, and Orest did not trade in those fictions; he *measured* weight and *measured* eyes.

His second, **Brother Morne**, murmured that the Ash *moved* as if the deck behind them had *teeth*, and Orest grunted that *naming* it thins the *wall* before a *Limes* hour distills *fear* into a *file* a register can *tithe* without blushing.

A guest sergeant with a *clipped* nameplate — **Cray** — *nodded* the way a man nods to *furniture*, then *over-corrected* to *deference* as *if* he *were* spending *coins* of *it* on a *minute* he *did* *not* *own*.

Orest returned the *nod* one *millimeter* *slow*, the smallest *insult* a *sergeant* can *pay* in *public*.

*Cray* had not *angered*; *Cray* *braced* for *delay* the way a man *braces* for a *minute* that will *cost* a *throat* — not the *stance* a *cargo* quarrel earns.

The *Limes* tithe of *continuity* — vials, ritual, the clumsy proof a wandering line is still a Chapter and not a pirate pantomime — brought **Brother Apothecary Thess** and a *guest* patient, **Carn**, into a small med-bay annexe, a room sized so a *storm* in a *chest*, if it *came*, could still *read* *like* a *storm* and not *theft* dressed *as* routine. Carn’s hands stayed steady in the *wrong* way, *rehearsed* in the *dark* of a *hold*; Thess’s auspex purred in *tolerance*, and the purr made Thess uneasy, because a *Medicae* *can* file a number called *tolerance* long before a soul admits what the number is hiding.

— A deeper pass is *courtesy*, not an order, — Thess said, — if you want *certainty* for your file.

Carn’s eyes flicked the *door* first, before the *lens*. Eager to be *underway* fit him like a bandage on a mouth that would not admit a wound, because a wound is a *story* and a guest does not always get to tell stories on a *Limes* day. Thess *whispered* up the line, in a dialect a billet learns to *hate*, because a bell is *how* a *clean* guest can turn to *collateral* in a ledger that had printed *continuity* as a blessing when it had never *meant* *pity*: **CARN, exit, rehearsed, pair with Soll**.

**Techmarine Daros** held a pump seal long enough to shame a *Munitorum* stopwatch. The work-order *wanted* the *early* blend, the *early* bay, and hatches *civil* in the invoice, as if a *hatch* *owed* politeness to a *pump* before the *pump* *owed* honesty to a *line* that keeps a *void* out and a *breath* in. A labor-serf on the spar joked once that the guest *Geller* field was *suspiciously* *stable*. Daros did *not* smile. Stability is not purity; it is a shape *you* file *until* it *stops* *lying* to a hand *already* *done* with *merciful* *Limes* *tongue* and *hungry* for a *verdict* in *Limes* *gut* instead.

In the *Strategium* shell, **Varro** and **Drusus** did not *debate*; they *weighed* Thess, Carn, and Soll in one *pan* before the heap earned the cheap word *verdict*. A *wall* is *geometry* before *blessing*; a *Limes* courtesy is a *cordon* in *bored* clothing *until* the *day* bares *teeth* and a *Limes* hour *names* a *hatch* *wrong*.

— Carn is breathing *fear* dressed as *courtesy*, — Drusus said on a tight vox, — the scan is *courtesy* because Thess *made* it *courtesy*.

— I will not waste a captive mind on a guest I can drown in *ink*, — Varro returned — *yet* the *ink* is *already* *wet*.

A relay crossed to the *Fifth*; the **Legatus of the QVINTA** received no theatrics, only a *weight* *tick* and a *Strategium* *request* to *hold* *Vexilla* lanes *uncrossed* until a Castellan *closed* a *mouth* *or* *opened* a *bore*.

**Serun Orach** of the *Ash Standard* met Alaric in an antechamber sized for a billeting *throat*, not a throne room. Noviomagus does not *worship* a guest: it *measures* a guest, and a measurement is a colder intimacy than a confessional, because a confessional still feints toward a *soul* while a *Limes* gut feints first toward *bore* and clearance, toward the honest geometry of a throat that either fits a seal or does not. The light on Noviomagus that morning looked *bored* the way a forge looks bored when a workpiece is neither a victory nor a failure yet, only a piece a hammer must fit before it earns a *name*.

— You are *efficient*, — Alaric said — and that is not flattery. Efficient is a machine’s pin, not a blessing.

A breath, the *Wall*’s private grammar in a *Limes* year, nothing wasted on a stranger’s *vanity*.

— Eager is *geometry*, — he said. — A throat is a *gate*. A rivet is a *gaze*.

— Walk with me, — he said at the mouth of the ferrocrete spine to the forges — we will keep the talk in logistics *until* it *cleans* *or* *bites*.

The Batavi behind him did not perform *drama*. A gentle order in a *Limes* year is still a cordon in the *lungs*, pressure without pantomime, a billet learning a *drill* that has stopped being a show and become the *shape* of a day. You pass a *gate* with your *mouth* closed and your *hands* where a *sergeant* can *see* them, because the Wall is not a theater.

For a guest who *borrows* *time* from a *calendar* the Tides tangle, Noviomagus *offers* a single, sour *romance* — *courtesy* *until* it is *not*, a cordon in *plain* cloth, a *throat* *measured* the way a forge measures a *bore* before it names a tool or a lesson.

## II. BAIT (TIME)

They walked the long gantry that locked the void dock to the forges, a spine of mesh and handrail that looked like a parade if you were the kind of officer who mistook *length* for *respite*. Foreign captains always did. A long walk was a gift the Wall gave without meaning kindness: time to compose a face, time to invent a reason, time to convince yourself the next hatch would not be a *throat*. Alaric did not turn to face Orach yet. He let the man take the view the way a guest takes a compliment — grateful on the surface, *hungry* beneath. A crane sang under load. Somewhere below, a *Munitorum* voice argued with a *Machine God* hour and lost, the way *Munitorum* voices always lost on days the auspex said *green* and the *bone* said *not yet*.

The gantry *knew* their weight, which was the joke Noviomagus told without laughing. Everything in this *mesh* pretended to weigh only tonnage until the scale *named* a different debt — tithe, *reputation*, *fear* — on the day routine stopped being *civil* enough to file. Alaric kept his stride even, the Castellan’s *politeness* as *geometry* rather than *mercy*. He was not giving Orach a *tour*; he was giving him a *line* to walk, and a line in Castra Vetera is a *mouth* before it is a *path*.

— This region is a calendar liar, — he said. — The Tides do not forgive navigation by habit. A ship can sit in our yards while your crew *feels* a month, and a dispatch from Segmentum command can arrive with a date-stamp that *already* mourned you. Warp transit here is hostile to the idea that *now* is one thing for everyone, and a man who clings to a single *now* will find that *now* has teeth.

He let the words sit without looking back. A Castellan’s voice on a *Limes* day should sound like a *weather* report, not a *warning*. Warnings invite argument; *weather* only tells you what to *wear*.

— Worse for those who do not know the routes, — he continued. — You can jump out one gate and find the Imperium’s clock has *slipped* — decades, sometimes *centuries* — and the scroll you called *news* in one hall is already a *eulogy* in another. That is why we keep local ledgers. That is why *arrival* is a *rubric* and not a *story*. A *story* can be *smuggled*; a *rubric* can only be *filed*.

He let the pause bite, not for *drama* but for *room*. Room is where a man fidgets, and a fidget is a cheap map of what he is carrying, even when he is careful not to *confess*.

Orach’s boots found the seam in the grating a half-second *late*, as if he were bracing for a deck that tilted in a place that *should* not *tilt*. A small *tell*: the kind a sergeant *loves* on a *Limes* day, because a *tell* pays like *currency* before it pays like *sin*.

— We keep our time *clean*, — Alaric said, still not turning. — *Clean* does not mean *fair*. It means *legible* to a *pump*, a *seal*, a *bore*: *hardware* that does not argue in *metaphor*.

The guest officer groped for a civil answer, the way men grope for a fire *exit* in a room that smells *wrong*. Alaric did *not* help. Help *belonged* to a different *aisle* of the *Munitorum*; *bait* was the cast a Castellan used *when* the *Strategium* needed a tongue to open *before* a *knife* did *first* work *on* a *pump* *that* refused *poetry* and *tithe* in *ink* instead.

— It must be a *relief*, — he *added* — to resupply *where* the *rest* of the *Imperium* *can* *barely* *hear* *you*. On a *tired* *ear* his *tone* *did* what *Limes* *courtesy* *does* *best*: *kindness* on the *surface* for a *stranger* *still* *braced* for a *Limes* *day* to *bite* *if* a *hatch* *breathes* *wrong*.

A *beat*. Forge *wind* *rose*, *hot* and *bored*, the *Instrumentum* *breeze* *that* *never* *flatters* a *guest* *because* *flattery* is *lubricant* and *Noviomagus* *prefers* *grit*.

— Out *here*, — *Alaric* *said* — *silence* is *not* *treason*. *It* is *weather*. *Weather* is *what* *chases* *you* *without* *wanting* a *blessed* *name* *first* *while* a *hull* *still* *owes* a *throat* a *tithe* *row* a *Munitorum* *clerk* *can* *file* *as* *orderly* *if* *courtesy* *was* *all* *that* *moved* *on* the *day*.

He heard *Orach* *exhale* — *too* *controlled* to be *relief*, *too* *soft* to be *parade* *discipline*; *a* *man* *braced* for *pursuit* *who* *had* *mistaken* *breeze* for *mercy* for *half* a *second* and *then* *remembered* *mercy* is *a* *receipt* and a *Munitorum* *row* *that* *will* *not* *blush* for *anyone*.

Orach *took* the *civil* *lane*.

— Weather I can *navigate*, — he *said* — *if* the *chart* is *honest*.

— *An* *honest* *chart*, — *Alaric* *returned* — *is* a *cruel* *chart* *in* *this* *mesh*. *Honesty* is *not* *kindness*. *Kindness* is *what* *we* *sell* *tourists* *when* *we* *want* *money* and *not* *blood*.

The *Castellan* *still* *did* *not* *turn*. *Eye* *contact* is *a* *coin* *a* *Limes* *day* *rations* *tightly*; a *back* *kept* to *a* *guest* *can* *read* *as* *courtesy* *or* *as* *insult*, and *Alaric* *gave* *courtesy* the *way* a *sergeant* *gives* a *cordon* *not* *theater* *so* a *tongue* *stayed* *open* a *minute* *longer* in *a* *ledger* *that* *still* *pretends* a *Limes* *breeze* is *only* *weather* and *not* a *bore* *tightening* *first*.

— You are *not* a *tourist*, — he said, softer. — You are a *hull* *with* a *habit* of *bowing* *on* *time* — *civil* *as* a *Munitorum* *stamp* in a *tongue* *that* *measures* *throats* *before* *it* *blesses* *them*.

The *line* *stung* because it *sounded* like *praise*.

Orach’s *breath* *hitched* *once* — *small* *enough* to *deny* *later* in *any* *debrief* *that* *still* *wants* *courtesy* to *read* *as* the *only* *motion* a *Limes* *year* *admits* *on* *paper* *before* a *Vermilion* *noun* *touches* an *annex* *first*.

*Alaric* *filed* it — *surveyor’s* *work*, *not* *friendship*.

*He* *kept* *walking*. *The* *forges* *unrolled* *ahead* in *a* *bored* *heat* *poured* *without* *theater*; *Noviomagus* *wore* a *fence* a *predator* *learns* *by* *heat* *before* a *keeper* *trades* a *name* the *way* a *bore* *keeps* a *rivet* *tight* *until* a *seal* *fails* *or* a *fist* *tightens* *first*.

## III. PROOF (WEIGHT)

Proof, in the tongue the Wall offers *tourists*, is borrowed finery. It is lifted from chapels and from the theater of the *court-martial* — clean lines for a *scroll* a *Crown* can sign without blushing, as if ink need never know blood. *Terra* wants evidence you can file in a tidy *font*; a stamp the Administratum can love without owning a throat. The *Strategium* does not start with a verdict, it starts with a pan. *Weight* is the cheap word *trend* misnames, and the honest one *gravity* refuses to. It arrives as a relay of small ugly signals: the *auspex* that should wobble, the *Munitorum* hour that should lose an argument to bone, the guest *vox* that should crack once if the line is still *mortal* the ordinary way. When all of that *stays* flat, proof ceases to be a caption and becomes a *pressure* a *Limes* gut names the way a bore names a *rivet* — honesty before any *blessing* gilds the number, and honesty before a *Vermilion* *noun* wets a sealed *annex* the way blood wets a seal, on the day *Munitorum* *green* stops reading as a benediction and reads as a civil fiction a tithe *row* can still file as orderly *loss*.

*The* *Limes* *civil* *hour* *poured* *enough* *gravity* * — * *Noviomagus* *bored* *light* *without* *theater*; a *Castellan* *kept* *walking* to a *gallery* *over* a *caldron* *floor* *where* a *Munitorum* *row* *breathed* *courtesy* *a* *minute* *longer* and a *hull* *faced* a *pump* *that* *refused* *to* *lie* *in* *poetry* *first* *.*

## IV. TALK (NOT COUNSEL)

They stopped where the forges returned heat to the air the way a bored blade returns warmth to a palm: honest, without flattery, without absolution. Below, the *caldron* floor moved in its own slow weather — cranes, sparks, the bass note of hammers kept off camera by distance but never off the bone. Up here the Administratum could have staged a postcard for a billeting *year*: *tolerance* in good light, throughput filed as a *Munitorum* hour, *Armourium* arithmetic smiling in the private language of tonnage. The railing carried vibration you could mistake for life if you had never watched a dock learn the difference between *green* on an auspex and truth in *Limes* gut. Alaric did not lean out for the view. He used the pause the way a sergeant uses a cordon — not to rest, to read a throat while it still believed it was only breathing weather.

The Batavi escort stayed where escorts stay on guest days: near enough to be friction, far enough not to be theater. Their presence was not *security* staged for a register; it was weight on a scale Orach could feel without needing to count faces. Beyond the mesh, somewhere in the *Falange* of *Nine* grind of grids and seals, relays still crawled — **PATTERN FLAT**, **CARN** paired with **Soll**, *NONA answering names without drama — facts that did not belong in this conversation and therefore shaped it anyway, the way tide shapes a harbor even when captains speak only of paperwork.

**Serun Orach** was a mouth lent a rank, not a king. A mouth can remain civil the way a stamp remains civil until the debt behind it is named. He opened with gratitude because gratitude is cheap armor: throughput, prompt berths, the professionalism of Noviomagus dockhands — each sentence accurate enough to pass a clerk’s skim and hollow enough to hide what accuracy was for. Alaric answered in monosyllables where monosyllables still counted as courtesy. He did not offer encouragement. Encouragement is what you give when you want a man to believe tomorrow will forgive today; on a *Limes* day tomorrow is another jurisdiction’s fiction.

Orach tried the safer lane — schedule, blend, promethium curves — language that behaves on invoices. When that lane stayed open too long he tried the other lane guests reach for when invoices stop comforting them: brothers, strain, the moral fatigue of crusade. He did not quote scripture; he did not need to. The register alone was chaplain-coded, comfort offered as hygiene, soul bundled with logistics as if the Wall were a chaplainry with a *Munitorum* stamp for absolution-by-rubric.

Alaric let the small talk finish because interruption is a kindness tourism likes, and because on a *Limes* day you buy minutes with silence before you spend them in ink. When the guest’s voice finally hunted an empty place to land, the Castellan gave him none.

— I do not need a chaplain’s comfort, — he said, low, dry as a work-order. — I need a hull to stop lying to a pump.

The words did not accuse Orach of heresy. They named a different failure — the kind a *Munitorum* lens files as routine until a seal says otherwise in *Limes* gut. **Techmarine Daros** and his stopwatch were not in the gallery; they did not need to be. The shame of the line was not mystical. It was mechanical. A pump does not confess. A pump fails, or it holds. The *Strategium* had already named the shape above them all: courtesy is a cordon in plain cloth until a hatch breathes wrong and *geometry* replaces *blessing*.

Counsel, if Orach had earned it, would have arrived elsewhere — with ink that cost captive minds, with rites that cost time the guest did not own. The Castellan offered neither duel nor blessing. He offered a straight edge in *Limes* tongue: bore, clearance, throat measured before any *Crown* deputy gets to call the hour *orderly* on parchment. That is not cruelty. Cruelty would have been pretending the mesh could still pretend.

Orach’s jaw tightened — the small tell a sergeant pays for before *Vermilion* nouns whet in a sealed annex. He had wanted a ruler he could quote back to his men as proof the Wall still *spoke their language*. What he received was a ruler that measured gaps: where tolerances ended, where *civil* silence stopped being weather and became a hull’s habit of bowing on time.

Talk is not counsel. Counsel is mercy in registry dress — the comfort *Terra*’s chancelleries sell to hulls a pict cannot be made to bleed. Talk is what you spend when you still hope a breeze is only *weather*, not the leading edge of a cordon the *Strategium* will name before a hatch earns a *Vermilion* noun. The mesh does not argue in metaphor; it tightens.

Nothing in that gallery sealed the hour. A gallery is a trap for anyone who mistakes a *Limes* breeze for absolution and bored light for the peace Noviomagus sells tourists when it wants tonnage and not a mouth that opens too honest a minute too early. Below, the *caldron* kept its bored theater of labor — honest sweat, honest sparks — while above it the fiction of *routine* grew thinner with every polite sentence still standing. The next motion would not be a sermon. It would be *pressure*: door logic, throat logic, the Wall remembering it was never a door.

## V. CORDON

The cordon began without a speech.

It began with **Brother-Sergeant Varik Orest** feeling the deck change under his boots.

He did not need auspex to tell him. The high dock had a voice when it was being honest: a low, constant vibration in the plating, the kind you stop hearing after ten years of duty until the day it shifts by a fraction and your body hears it before your mind does. Orest had learned to love that fraction. Fractions were what kept hatches from becoming mouths.

The rail beside him trembled with the same bored power it always carried — cranes dragging tonnage, forges breathing heat through ducts — but the traffic around it started to align, the way a formation aligns when the drill stops being for show. Lanes that had crossed each other all morning like casual conversation stopped crossing. Men took half-steps, then found themselves *already* in the right place, as if the dock had always been that strict and their earlier ease had been the lie.

Orest checked the guests first. He did not look for weapons. He looked for impatience.

**Cray** had been the impatient one since the first nod — nameplate clipped, manners over-corrected. Cray’s helm turned toward the refuel boom and away again, a small mechanical dart of attention that meant *exit* before it meant *task*. The Ash did not move like dockhands. They moved like men who had rehearsed a route in darkness.

Orest’s vox crackled with the kind of order the QVINTA preferred: no sermon, no flourish, only a line made audible.

— Lanes remain uncrossed. — He did not raise his voice. He did not need to. — Anyone who crosses buys the correction.

Brother Morne, his second, shifted a half-step to cover a service hatch Orest had not looked at. Morne’s boltgun stayed low. Threat, in a *Limes* hour, is most useful when it is not performed.

— They’re watching the wrong things, — Morne murmured.

Orest did not answer at once. He watched Cray’s posture, the way the guest sergeant tried to stand *civil* while his muscles tried to stand *ready*.

— Good, — Orest said. — Let them.

He tasted the day’s logic without naming it. The *Strategium* did not send poetry; it sent bias. Bias meant someone upstairs had put weight on a pan.

Across the deck, **Silence** arrived as the absence of extra words.

You did not see Silence as a squad the way you see DECIMA. You noticed that open vox channels thinned to work-order grammar, that dockhands stopped saying each other’s names, that a joke died in a throat before it became sound. You noticed that the polite noise of a guest ship stopped being flattering and started being *flat* in a way that felt wrong under the skin.

Orest felt it as a tightening in his own voice. He found himself speaking in shorter phrases. Even his breaths seemed counted, as if the air were being measured for contamination.

— Pattern? — Morne asked.

Orest could not see the noosphere. He could only feel its discipline settle.

— Pattern is someone else’s hunt, — he replied. — Ours is the thresholds.

**Shadow** took the thresholds.

Not in the melodramatic sense outsiders like to imagine — steel ghosts, theatrical darkness. Shadow was simpler: the places a hull forgets it is a hull. Service ways. Maintenance hatches. The junction where a conduit run made a blind angle. The thin door that exists only so a Tech-adept can reach a valve and never so a guest can *leave* when leaving becomes inconvenient.

A Shadow Cohort brother — Phobos silhouette, paint drinking light — appeared at Orest’s left without sound. His helm stayed canted toward the Ash, never quite pointing, never quite accusing. His voice came on a tight line.

— They rehearsed the aft access to the coolant manifold. Twice. — A pause. — They think it’s an exit.

Orest did not ask how Shadow knew. Knowing was Shadow’s trade. Orest’s trade was making knowledge into geometry.

— Close it, — he said.

— Already closed, — the Shadow brother replied.

For a half-second Orest felt something close to satisfaction — then crushed it. Satisfaction is for men who believe the day is decided. A *Limes* day is decided only when the last hatch that matters has stayed shut.

He watched Cray again.

Cray’s helm snapped toward the coolant manifold corridor as if he had heard the door close through steel. Then he corrected, smoothing it into a casual scan, like a dock guard checking traffic. A dock guard checks traffic when he is bored. Cray checked traffic because the traffic had changed and he needed it to change back.

Orest moved one meter — not toward Cray, not toward a weapon, but toward the line that made Cray’s preferred path expensive. It was a small movement. It made the deck feel narrower.

— Sergeant, — Orest called, and he made the word sound like an invoice. — Where are you going.

Cray’s response came too quickly.

— Clearance review, — he said.

Orest let the lie hang in the air until it began to look like what it was.

— Clearance is ours, — he said. — You wait.

That was how the cordon worked at the level guests could understand: not an accusation, a pause forced into their schedule. A schedule is a throat. The Wall had started to close it.

Somewhere above, the order that mattered did not sound like an order.

It sounded like **Alaric von Helis** speaking a sentence that did not need emotion to carry weight.

— Silence. Shadow. DECIMA. — The Castellan’s voice on the secure channel was as dry as a work-order. — Hold.

Orest did not feel inspired. He felt *aligned*. Inspiration is a mortal luxury. Alignment is what keeps transhuman bodies from becoming myths.

Then DECIMA arrived where guests could not pretend they had misunderstood.

The justice vexillum did not come with a choir. It came with the rhythm of boots that did not hurry. A bearer line moved like a bore being set: one step, one breath, each footfall a quiet stamp on the deck’s authority. The cloth itself looked wrong in dock light — too stark, too judicial — like a sentence carried in fabric so it could be seen from any angle.

Orest had seen banners. He had seen honor cloth. This was neither. This was doctrine made visible for one purpose: so no officer could later claim he had not known what kind of day it had been.

Morne’s gaze tracked the vexillum for a moment.

— Malakor precedent, — Morne said, very softly.

Orest did not answer. He felt the old memory of how quickly a day becomes a file.

He watched the Ash react.

Some of them held still, the way trained men hold still when they realize motion will be counted. Others fidgeted inside armor, micro-shifts that broadcasted where they wished the exits still were. Cray’s hands curled and uncurled once.

Orest spoke again, not shouting, not performing.

— You see it, — he said. — Good. Then you understand the lanes.

He could have said: *who may pass, who may not.* He could have said: *treaty mouth has ended; treaty gut names the price.* Those would have been narrator sentences. In a novel, men do not speak like doctrine scrolls. They speak like men who have learned doctrine in blood.

So he said only:

— Stop.

And the Ash stopped.

The high dock sealed to the void without romance.

Clamps cycled in a rhythm that belonged to machinery, not morale. Sirens came as short blats and clipped acknowledgments, swallowed by procedure before panic could grow teeth. Air leaned — so subtly a mortal might not notice, so clearly an Astartes inner ear registered differential before thought caught up. The auspex stopped selling routine and started selling hull integrity: the only green that matters when outside is not weather but hunger with no clerk.

For a moment the world narrowed to seals and breath.

Orest listened for the one sound he feared most: a hatch attempting to open under pressure.

Nothing.

Silence.

Not comfort. Not counsel. A cordon’s silence.

In it, Orest realized why the day still felt like a novel when it was being lived and like a report when it was remembered: because living is made of seconds you cannot summarize without lying.

He held the line anyway.

Because holding is what remains when blessing is gone.

What followed would not stay in the Chapter’s hull alone. Pressure seeks a file *Terra* can sign without blushing — but that is treaty blood, and treaty blood belongs to the next ledger.

## VI. SPOILS (TREATY BLOOD)

The vox taught ugly words in polite voices.

**Brother-Sergeant Varik Orest** heard *treaty blood* for the first time that day and hated it instantly — not because it sounded false, but because it sounded *accurate*. Accuracy on a dock is worse than melodrama. Melodrama at least admits it wants your throat.

— Repeat, — he said to his bead, — translate that into something a sergeant can obey.

The reply came lean, stripped to triage grammar.

— Spoilage outbound. Witness intact. Do not *interpret* on open air.

Orest’s jaw tightened anyway. Interpretation was how men stayed human. Obedience was how they stayed alive.

He moved with the convoy corridor anyway, boots finding the same half-rhythm they had found during the cordon: not parade, not panic — *bias*. The Fifth’s lanes were still uncrossed in spirit if not always in paint, and now they had a new job: not to *win* a fight, but to keep a dock from confusing *payment* with *panic* when payment arrived dressed as freight.

Brother Morne walked on his left, boltgun low, eyes on the Ash serfs’ hands the way a man watches a dog that has not bitten yet but has learned the taste of meat.

— They’re listening for drama, — Morne murmured.

— Then we give them work orders, — Orest returned.

A crane sang. A seal hissed. Somewhere a servitor counted stamps with the devotion of a cantor.

Orest tasted *promethium* cold in the back of his throat — lines the guest Chapter had wanted *filled*, not *read*. He thought, unbidden, of bolts stacked like arguments: honest salvage argued in metal, lies argued in *manners*. Then he killed the thought, because thoughts like that became speeches, and speeches became leaks.

Another vox line opened — tight, clinical, Apothecary-clean.

**Brother Apothecary Thess** did not waste warmth.

— **CARN** is moving on my chain, — Thess said. — Keep your lanes boring. Boredom is mercy today.

Orest answered without flourish.

— Understood.

He did not ask what Thess saw in a guest’s blood. He did not want Thess’s eyes to become his imagination.

Then **Magos-auxiliary Henrik Soll** spoke, voice flat as an ironed chart.

— **PATTERN FLAT** persists, — Soll said. — Log it. Do not dramatize it.

Orest almost laughed. Almost. The laugh died because Soll was right: drama is how *bureaucracies* pretend they did not see the wolf until it was too late.

— Copy, — Orest said.

---

**Kadmos**, Master of the Forge, did not watch the dock drama. He watched metal tell truth.

Under his mecadendrites, the **Ash Standard** hull ceased to be a story about a wandering Chapter and became *mass*: tonnage, stress fractures, heat scars, places where armor had been repaired too cleanly, too often, like a man smoothing a lie until the lie becomes a texture.

A Techmarine from the tally tried to save time. Time, on Noviomagus, is always trying to save itself at the expense of honesty.

— Master, variance is inside tolerance, — the Techmarine said.

Kadmos did not look up.

— Tolerance is what liars buy, — he said. — Weigh it again.

— Sir—

— *Again.*

The scales obeyed. Machines liked Kadmos more than men did. Machines did not flatter themselves that mercy could be measured in *feelings*.

When the second pass landed, the Techmarine’s voice went quieter.

— It moved, — he admitted.

Kadmos allowed himself one nod.

— Then route it, — he said. — Noviomagus eats steel. It does not eat *titles*.

He split the hull the way a sawline splits shame from usable plate — not because he enjoyed cruelty, but because *geometry* is what remains when you strip *blessing* off a hull and discover whether the skeleton still deserves a *name* or only a *bin*.

Steel first. Story second. Story last.

---

Out on the void cradle, the transfer wore the boredom of extreme hazard — tractor fields laid like reins, Geller thresholds discussed in the dull tones men use when they refuse to romanticize the warp’s appetite.

Orest watched from the right distance: close enough to see, far enough not to become a tourist.

The **Minotaurs** arrived without needing Noviomagus to love them.

Their ships did not *announce* themselves with poetry. They occulted stars the way a hammer occults a nail until the nail understands it is not the sky’s equal. At their head — **Asterion Moloc** — the void learned a simpler grammar: not *brotherhood*, *vector*. Not *help*, *receipt*.

Orest felt the old instinct to hate another Chapter’s arrogance. He strangled it. Hatred is a luxury when you are holding a lane open so your own Chapter’s *Vermilion* nouns do not spill into a *Munitorum* *year* that still wants *green*.

He thought, privately, the sentence he would never say on open vox:

*Treaty blood is debt paid in things so Terra can pretend it paid in law.*

Then he filed it the only way a sergeant can file anything: by keeping his mouth shut and his sector clean.

---

Later — minutes, hours; time on a dock becomes elastic when seals start eating sound — Orest caught two fragments on a secure channel not meant for his ears, meant for the air anyway, because secure channels leak when pressure rises.

**Varro**, dry as ash:

— Witness stays *witness*. Do not sell it as mercy.

**Drusus**, colder:

— Biology stays numbered.

Orest did not pray. He did not interpret. He listened to the cranes and the stamps and the steady insult of machinery doing its job.

What left Noviomagus in the Minotaurs’ shadow was not “help.” It was *payment* shaped like assistance — *treaty blood* carried in an Imperial fist so the **Cohors Batavorum** could keep its own confessions where confessions belong: behind seals, in **Vermilion** annex gravity the frontier feels even when it does not read the pages.

Seventy-two hours had not elapsed yet. The **Ash Standard** still existed on paper long enough for ink to *dry* into the shape *Terra* prefers. What would eat that paper would not arrive as Noviomagus theater — not another gallery, not another polite *Limes* minute. It would arrive as *bureaucracy* does when it finally stops flirting with *throughput* and admits *loss* is an *asset* it knows how to *liquidate* — quiet as a scalpel, *regular* as a crown’s breath, cruel only if you mistake *regular* for *innocent*.

## VII. SEVENTY-TWO (STRIKE)

Seventy-two hours later, Noviomagus tried to pretend nothing had happened.

That was the first lie the day told — because lies always start comfortable.

**Brother-Sergeant Varik Orest** stood in the same sector he had held during spoilage and felt the wrongness of *routine* returning too fast, like skin closing over a splinter before the wound has been taught honesty.

Brother Morne leaned on the rail beside him, casual in posture, not casual in eyes.

— Different silence, — Morne said.

Orest grunted agreement.

— Same dock.

On the tally slate in Orest’s gauntlet, the **Ash Standard** entry was simply gone.

Not crossed out. Not flagged. Not explained. *Gone*, the way a missing tooth feels when your tongue finds absence where a name used to live.

— Query it again, — Orest said.

Morne’s fingers tapped.

— Same null, — Morne answered. — Like it never berthed.

Orest tasted old anger and swallowed it. Anger was for men who thought the Imperium owed them continuity. The Imperium owed nothing. It *leased* names.

He forced his voice back into work-order grammar, because grammar is hocw you keep a sergeant from becoming a mourner.

— Log the sector clear, — he told Morne. — No commentary.

Morne almost smiled. Almost.

— Aye, — Morne said. — *Boring* it is.

---

The strike did not arrive at Noviomagus with banners.

It arrived as a change in what the air was allowed to remember.

**Brother Apothecary Thess** spoke on the tight line first — voice stripped to custody, not comfort.

— **CARN** is filed, — Thess said. — Stop treating him like a story. He is a chain now.

Orest answered the way you answer weather.

— Copy.

Then **Magos-auxiliary Henrik Soll**, flat as an ironed chart.

— **RESOLVED**, — Soll said. — Do not dramatize it.

Orest’s throat tightened anyway.

— Define *resolved*, — he said, knowing it was a stupid question.

Soll did not indulge stupid questions. Soll indulged *precision*.

— It means the *Administratum* can breathe, — Soll replied. — And you can keep holding a dock.

The line closed.

Orest stared at the blank slate a heartbeat longer than discipline strictly allowed.

*Resolved*, he thought, is what they call it when a clerk does not have to hate you to erase you. Hatred would have been *attention*. Attention is expensive. Silence is cheaper — and silence dresses wounds as *healed* until a crown’s breath can stay *regular*.

He hated that thought too.

He filed it the only way a sercgeant can file anything.

---

**Kadmos** did not come to the high dock to offer comfort.

He came because forges always notice when the world’s weight redistributes, the way a crane notices when a load shifts.

Orest saw him once at the edge of the spoilage lane — bronze arm catching dock light, mecadendrites folded like tired predators, eyes on nothing in particular because Kadmos’s attention was never *on* things; it was *through* them.

Kadmos did not nod to Orest like a brother. He nodded like an acknowledgement of torque.

— Back to work, sergeant, — Kadmos said.

— Aye, — Orest answered.

It was not friendship. It was permission to stop being a witness and start being a wall again.

---

Later, when the mortals began their dishonest work — smoothing spikes into anecdotes because anecdotes are cheaper than fear — Orest heard **Henn Yarrow** try a joke two bays over and heard **Mira Isten** shut it down with one flat sentence.

He did not turn to watch. Some lessons belong to humans.

He kept his eyes on the hatches anyway.

---

**Silence** and **Shadow** did not celebrate on an open channel.

They did what they do after a cordon hardens: they stripped drama from the noosphere until only *routing* remained — clean enough for *NONA to answer without theater, lean enough for logs to stop pretending *flat* was a compliment.

**Varro** and **Drusus** did not argue the strike in public.

Public is where *Terra* sells *mercy* as *font*.

Private is where the Triumvirate weighs what *mercy* would cost the gate if it were allowed to become habit.

Orest caught only the edge of it once — two words leaking through static like splinters:

— Witness holds.

— Biology holds.

That was enough theology for a sergeant.

---

The **Minotaurs** did not need thanks.

Thanks would have been another story.

They had already taken their payment in motion — mess converted into file-friendly *velocity*, *treaty blood* laundered into a shape chancelleries can lift without blushing.

What remained on Noviomagus was not relief.

Relief is a tourist emotion.

What remained was *regular*: schedules re-knotted, margins re-drawn, the high dock allowed to pretend it had always been a place where nothing interesting ever happened.

---

A *Limes* year is bored because boredom keeps men from worshiping their own fear.

But *Limes* gut remembers.

It remembers a pump that lied to a throat it did not own. It remembers how quickly *guest* becomes *weight* when the Wall stops pretending to be a door. It remembers that *geometry* is what remains when *blessing* is gone — because *geometry* does not comfort, does not forgive, does not forget.

It only holds.

And on the seventy-third hour, the **Ash Standard** was still gone in every register that mattered — while a crown’s breath stayed *regular*, and Castra Vetera stayed *indispensable*, and the void stayed hungry, and the Wall did not bend in the only way that counts out here: by *holding*.
