Rain had learned new cadence over wreck camps — less weather than **percussion**: boots on duckboards hammered over vein-mud, recovery teams shouting across salvage lanes, cough of Thunderhawk warming where Chapter pretended sky could still be owned after CV y0 had taught them otherwise.

Somewhere behind that noise, **needle-bridge** they had burned still cooled, and twenty-one names remained **absence** arranged like lesson Torven’s squad had been forced to teach without speaking.

**Maela Thornwake** lived in cage of escort and auspex, still selling silence as professionalism while Kadmos’s clerks copied her remaining braid bytes into ledgers labeled **EVIDENCE / PROVISIONAL ROUTE / FATAL INGRESS**. She was not forgiven. She was **useful**, which in Imperium was often same shape as mercy with worse interest.

**Drusus** filed what Apothecarion would later cite as *primum incertum* — first Castra Vetera document that ended without genus. **Event origin: undetermined.** He did not rush conclusion. Rush would have been lie dressed as duty.

Forty-one pages followed — particulate densities from Torven’s classroom of glass and tide, oscillation harmonics in bridge resonance, saline intrusion into ceramite joints where mud pretended polite, thermal ghosts along gallery that insisted bodies had *been* and were *not*. Column stayed blank under *assailant biologics* because auspex, when asked to commit, returned only machine equivalent of swallowed tongue. Machine spirit was not humble. It was **frightened**.

— What it names — Varro murmured, standing close enough that his *Codex* death-skull reflected Drusus’s helm-lamps as twin moons drowned at birth — it must answer for.

Drusus did not look up.

— Then let it be silent.

— Silence is its own naming — Varro said.

Strategium’s first reply arrived as **compression** — Navy liaison demanding actionable genus before escort rotations could be renegotiated, as if naming monster were same as owning it. Drusus forwarded sheet of numbers without single adjective, because adjectives were how staff turned fear into **story** and stories into budgets misallocated toward ghosts. He attached Torven’s bridge harmonics anyway — not as poetry, as **constraint**: if enemy would not be named, environment could still be forced to testify.

Down the marsh channel, PDF outpost at **Sallow Morrow** squatted where Administratum planners drew neat icon and tide drew sneer — fifty-seven souls in lighthouse citadel of flakrock and prayer, tasked to watch channel no trade consortium had profited from in two generations.

It had ceased to exist between *vox*-checks **04** and **06** with insulting neatness of ledger line closed by someone who did not sweat. Not taken. Not breached. Not stormed. *Expired.*

When Batavi came — Alaric leading, Kadmos map-bound, Varro’s censers swinging like pendulums that refused to tick — they found **boots**. Boots only, as if bodies had been edited out of history by editor who respected footwear.

They stood in pairs along spiral gallery: PDF-issue, salt-bleached, mud-rimmed at soles. Laces tied in regulation knots — square, joyless geometry taught on drill yards a thousand worlds away. No Needles fisher had ever tied knot like that.

Kadmos swore once, cleanly, officer-true. Word died as if gallery inhaled it. Alaric stared down phantom ranks. Helm’s lamps swept pairs and found nothing else: no smear of exit, no bolt splash, no xenos trophy hung in theatrical contempt. Only *order* — second time since fall enemy had left instruction instead of corpses.

They pulled roster from drowned locker — Administratum splendor reduced to pulp, fifty-seven names bleeding blue ink into brine until only nine remained legible enough to **matter**. Someone had tried to dry pages on chapel rail; faith’s heat warped signatures into smears no court would admit.

Kadmos treated lighthouse as kill-box that had already fired: measured sightlines from spiral gallery to lantern housing as if sniper might argue geometry with them later, then dismissed own math with disgusted twitch because enemy had proven it preferred **arrangement** to bullets.

Varro walked paired boots once — slow — reading them not as footwear but as **steps** in liturgy performed by absent feet.

At the gallery’s rim he did not shout.

— What did your priests say — he asked, not shouted, of embassy that had followed them from wreck field.

**Iria** stepped from stairwell’s mouth, crab-shell cloak rattling like small jaws. She had yielded her name the way sharp stones yield blood: slowly, pressed hard.

— Our priests say tide remembers footprints — she answered, voice flat with fatigue older than her years. — Your priests say Emperor counts souls. Here, both may be true — and both may be **hungry**.

**Delta cordon** map — still theoretical on vellum — already wore coffee rings and thumb-smudges where officers tried to pin Iria’s tide-marks onto Imperial grids and discovered grids were wrong species of truth.

Auxilia engineers hammered duckboard over sucking mud while arguing drainage in voices that carried like small ordnance. In that chorus, war’s romance tried to reassert itself as **work**, because work was easier to salute than disappearance.

Alaric let work continue without blessing it on open channel. Blessing too early turned competence into **expectation**, and expectation was cousin to prediction.

Varro moved to seal threshold — **Binding of Threshold**, not full liturgy, only proto-rite: salt-ash and murmured law meant to make house *deaf* to listening that wore no ears.

Lay preacher blocked him. Man wore barnacle-scars across tonsure like second crown harvested from deep. Hands shook. Spine did not.

— This house is not yours to seal — he said. — It is ours to *hear*.

Varro tilted skull-mask. Candle-light — ridiculous, precious — ran along bone enamel.

— Then hear this: silence saves.

— Silence is how they learn your rhythm — preacher replied, and words fell into Drusus’s ears like diagnosis he had not yet earned permission to write.

Chill followed — not temperature; *recognition*. Drusus’s helm sensors caught micro-vibration in deck: footsteps **almost** real, cadence almost Batavi, then gone when he turned. Only boots. Pairs like teeth.

Drusus logged deck whisper as *mechanical uncertainty* on form that had no box for it and set junior tech to phase-match footfalls against Batavi gait libraries. Match climbed to eighty-one percent before collapsing into static, which was worse than zero: eighty-one suggested mimicry, zero suggested malfunction, and both implied observer who understood *enough* to flirt.

— Flirtation without consent is still violence — Varro said, almost too soft for *vox* capture.

Drusus almost answered *I know* before discipline swallowed pride. Above, rain practiced percussion on roof built to survive storms and not built to survive **witness**.

From landing platform, new voice rode wind — cog-scrubbed, eager. **Genetor Threx** of ***Lex Ignis*** advanced with retinue of Skitarii hunters in red chrome, auspex packs humming hymns to acquisition. Threx’s optics whirred with particular hunger paperwork fails to hide.

— Site classification: high-yield biological substrate — Threx intoned. — Tactical sampling authority under Treaty Appendix —

— Denied — Alaric said, not raising voice, only lowering world’s volume around it.

Threx’s *vox* fuzzed indignation.

— Castellan, mud carries epithelial traces beyond —

— Mud carries **ours** — Alaric replied. — You will not scrape chapel gallery for trophies while PDF ghosts still stand in boots. Collect your data from **air** and **water** until I say rotal. Test me and I will have your leash shortened in orbit.

Threx tilted head — calculation, not submission.

— Compliance logged under protest. Opportunity deferred is opportunity compounded.

Drusus watched Threx watch boots. **Corpse politics** had arrived early.

— Take swabs — Drusus told Genetor quietly — concession as trap. — **Swabs only.** Anything that requires cutting boots from this floor waits on my ink and Castellan’s seal.

Threx’s smile was augmetal.

— Generous, Apothecary.

— It is *containment* — Drusus said. He already foresaw next fight — chain of custody spelled in Navy ink, sanctified recovery spelled in Mechanicus red — two liturgies trying to eat same evidence while ghosts still stood in paired boots like congregation waiting for hymn nobody dared begin.

On the landing pad’s heat-sheen, Threx’s Skitarii mirrors caught lantern-flame and fractured it into red petals — pretty, wrong — while quartermaster’s team argued weight limits on flak-board destined for perimeter that could be obsolete before dawn.

Alaric watched without intervening. Intervening would have stolen dignity of solving **one** problem enemy had not authored. He updated rally doctrine in own cipher — **no route twice**, **no prayer on schedule** — and passed it to officers by mouth because data trails were lessons too, and lesson this week was that channels which felt too **clean** felt watched.

That night Alaric consolidated patrols into webs *Codex* would have called paranoid — with cause. He erased predictable timing. He forbade same route twice. He rotated rally points until officers swore they were fighting **maps**, not mud. He turned war into improvisation — not genius glittering, but endurance dressed as invention.

He forbade synchronized lantern checks. Forbade cathedral tone of boots landing together on metal stairs at shift change. Forbade references to *usual sweep* until language itself felt starved of habit — brutal, but habit was **frequency** you broadcast to any listener patient enough to harvest it.

Officers grumbled Castellan was asking them to fight cartography. They obeyed anyway, because grumble was also habit, and they could not afford it. Hunters punished prediction anyway.

When dawn still pretended mercy, **Blue-Echo** had been name from before CV y1 learned humility: kill-mark on Stormbird’s flank, joke about auspex returns, then private tally of minutes survived inside wrong weather.

Before they lifted for sweep that would cost Rhylan, Alaric gave them no sermon — only sequence: if arrangement replaced violence, answer with motion that could not be filed; if silence replaced motion, break silence with purpose, not panic; if mud offered **hands**, treat them as traps until proven otherwise.

Drusus stood on embarkation lip and watched filtration loads spike in micro-rhythms that looked almost like **language**, then refused to write *language* in ink because ink was how you taught archive to hallucinate.

Varro traced short blessing on hull metal — not for machine, for men who still believed blessings were kind of **armor** — and let gesture end before it became theater.

Blue-Echo returned — not screaming triumph, not trailing wounded brothers like badges. They returned **minus** Brother Rhylan as one returns from temple with space beside you that used to hold voice.

Rain had begun again over the promontory — not cleansing, **accounting** — washing mud into channels until channels looked like veins the planet kept for its own filing. **Kessler**, not yet assigned to Alaric’s cordon but already learning Aethelgard’s arithmetic, counted auxilia mouths at the landing lip because counting was the only vaccine that had never lied to her yet.

On Rhylan’s pack: single bolt shell, stood upright on primer as if balanced by patient hands. Casing wore spiral of mud — too regular for wind, too fine for fingers *human*. Kadmos’s voice went tight.

— Assessment.

Drusus inhaled. Air tasted of salt and iron and something else — almost ozone, almost *curiosity*.

— Undetermined — he said. He felt word land in mouth and **stay**. It would become habit. That night, it was only ash.

Alaric looked at shell spiral and did not touch it.

— Triple the watch — he said. — Change watch’s **heartbeat**. If it has learned us, we learn ourselves again from zero.

He named no successor for Rhylan’s place in line yet. Naming too quickly would have stitched loss into **policy**, and policy was how families learned to stop grieving on schedule.

Kadmos posted countersigns that changed at random minute-offsets until even sergeants laughed once — one laugh, then silence — because laughter was another rhythm, and rhythm was currency.

**Drusus** sealed Rhylan’s auspex trace under *undetermined; do not merge with Sallow Morrow boot harmonics prematurely* — sequence, not prophecy — and felt eighty-one percent mimicry climb in his buffer like a flirtation that understood *enough* to hurt.

Rain had not stopped when **Iria**, shadow at gallery’s rim, whispered — too quietly for most, not for Astartes ears:

— They are not teaching you yet. They are **measuring** how fast you learn.

Her cloak’s shells clicked once, sound too small to be threat, too deliberate to be accident: tide-marking in miniature.

Rain struck lighthouse roof like knuckles. Oil from repair torch drifted sideways in gust and painted brief false constellations on wet stone — pretty, irrelevant, universe’s habit of offering beauty when what you needed was **signal**.

Somewhere between boots and bolt-shell, crucible advanced one notch — not with clamor, but with **arrangement** — and arrangement, unlike battle noise, carried cleanly through rain.

Chapter filed that truth without applause: **arrangement** was weather you could not salute away — only endure it, outlast it, file it.

Alaric did not say *We hold.* Not yet. Not here. He had learned arrangement left instruction instead of corpses, and instruction was enemy grammar written in paired boots and spiral mud.

Varro filed threshold binding as proto-rite — not victory, **seal attempted**. Drusus filed undetermined and meant it. Kadmos carried cipher scar forward like wound that would not close.

**Maela Thornwake**’s braid bytes still copied into *EVIDENCE / PROVISIONAL ROUTE* — useful, not forgiven — and **Forty-six** void names still sat in Valerius’s ledger like teeth the Chapter could not stop tonguing. Arrangement advanced one notch without clamor: paired boots, spiral mud, undetermined genus, Threx’s appetite deferred not denied.

**Kessler** — not yet on Alaric’s cordon but already learning Aethelgard’s arithmetic — watched the lighthouse gallery from the landing lip and counted auxilia mouths because counting was the only vaccine that had never lied to her yet. **Iria**’s sentence — *both may be hungry* — would travel forward like salt: abrasive, necessary, refusing the Strategium’s hunger for clean genus.

The clerk stamped twice. Quiet lighthouse, loud rain: the arrangement had taught the mesh that paired boots were not poetry but **survival geometry**.
