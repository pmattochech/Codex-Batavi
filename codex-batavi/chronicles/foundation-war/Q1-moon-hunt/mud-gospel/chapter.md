# The Crucible — *Mud Gospel*

**Strategium anchor:** CV y0 (crash ingress; wreck sovereignty on Aethelgard Prime)

**Tenebrarum Ribbon** did not announce itself with theology. It announced itself with **math** — kind that made Navigators go quiet in way priests went quiet when they discovered chapel had been built on fault line.

**Maela Thornwake** had sworn amber braid was still **profitable**. Mechanicus observer on her bridge had sworn auspex ghosts were **within tolerance**. **House Keth-Marrow’s** boy had sworn nothing at all. He only gripped helm rail and watched Immaterium show them corridor that **moved** when they tried to measure it, as if storm were living thing that resented being charted in trader’s ink.

**Alaric von Helis** felt wrongness through *Argent Mandate*’s bones before alarms learned human language. Translation was never gentle. It was always theft — heat stolen, certainty stolen, comforting lie that hull was still same shape on both sides of veil. This theft was **specific**. It had fingerprint of route sold without coffin ledgers that kept Insula’s chart-houses rich and outsiders dead.

Kadmos had warned him on Strategium deck, voice flat with beautiful discipline of I-told-you-so wrapped in duty:

— Castellan, Thornwake’s braid is **provisional**. We are buying rumor with five hundred lives.

— We are buying crucible — Alaric had answered. — Radulf bought same rumor and lives in machine now.

Sentence had aged poorly in hours since.

Fleet **fell** out of warp way tool falls when hand forgets it — not in parade formation, not with layered airspace and *Codex* corridors painted on hololiths, but in **staggered** realspace shock that tore escort frigates into questions void did not answer.

*Calico Mercy*’s drive-flare screamed once across *vox*, mortal sound dressed in machine pain, then clipped to static. Thornwake’s voice fought through on bleeding channel:

— Castellan, braid is **shearing** — I am not —

Channel died with politeness of ledger closed mid-sentence. Somewhere in convoy’s dying geometry, Rogue Trader learned what every trader eventually learned: Imperium forgave profit, but ribbon forgave **nothing**.

Alaric stood on command dais and watched hololith become confession. Nine wounds, nine excuses for war, nine names Strategium would later try to bind into lattice he could not yet see as **single**.

What he saw now was simpler and worse: bruised green sphere veined with tidewater silver, crowned by stone teeth locals would call **Needles** — oath of rock against sky, mouth of weather — and beneath it, **Aethelgard Prime** rising through cloud not as destination chosen but as **punishment** accepted. Planet did not greet them. It **caught** them.

— Impact solution in twelve — Kadmos said. Not prayer. Countdown.

— We are not inserting — Alaric said. — We are **colliding**. Log it under CV annals. Hour zero. No euphemism.

Drusus’s voice entered net without ornament:

— Apothecarion acknowledges **trauma ingress**. Filter strain climbing fleet-wide. Recommend dispersal from flagship spine before hull compromise propagates.

Varro did not speak on open *vox*. He never wasted frequency when silence could carry same weight as sermon. Alaric felt him anyway — pressure at Triumvirate’s edge, *Codex* death-skull helm turned toward hololith as if world below were threshold that required rites before it could be crossed.

*Argent Mandate* hit atmosphere like bell struck wrong. Metal screamed. Gravity remembered hobbies. Somewhere in ship, mortals who had never wanted to learn theology of weight discovered it anyway.

They did not come down on Thunderhawks in hour tide chose to pretend mercy. They came down as **wreckage** learning to impersonate intent.

Lifeboats and assault bays vomited brothers into rain that fell in sheets heavy enough to ring armor like bells. First Astartes boots did not kiss beachhead. They **drowned** to ankle in black water locals called ***vein-mud*** — not quite soil, not quite sea, living paste that drank heat and gave back cold as if offended by idea that anything from sky deserved footing.

Drusus’s tally began before first shot: heart rates, filtration strain, micro-tells of bodies forced to wage geometry in medium designed to swallow footprints and return nothing but suction.

Needles people watched Grey Hounds — *Slate Cohort* in papers no one here would read — with narrow attention of witnesses at trial. Girl in cloak of stitched **crab-shell** raised both hands, palms out: not welcome, not warning. *See me. I will remember what you do.*

Alaric noted her. He noted, too, priest beside her — no Ministorum crozier, only staff wound with fossilized rope and old guilt. Posture delivered translation: *You fell. Mud is heavier than your fall.*

Behind tribal line, auxilia crawled from pods that had landed sideways, faces young with brutal optimism of tithe ledgers printed on worlds where ground stayed ground. Some vanished to knee in first dozen steps. Few, in armor mis-sized by quartermasters who had never stood in this black, went to hip and had to be dragged by sergeants like stubborn cargo — humiliation dressed as rescue.

From low orbit, ***Lex Ignis*** tender *Lumen in Aurum* cracked transmission across Batavi net with courteous violence of second crown:

*Explorator Mandate Seven requests **priority clearance** for coastal anomaly grid Sigma. Auspex suggests subsurface echoes consistent with pre-Imperial strata. Cooperative security envelope recommended. **Compliance is efficiency.***

Kadmos’s jaw tightened.

— They speak as if we are contractors.

— We are wall — Alaric replied. — They are ledger. Walls do not argue with ink. They endure it.

He opened channel. Voice did not rise. It became only reliable frequency in storm.

— Battlegroup *Lex Ignis*, this is Castellan von Helis. You will hold **debris** anchor until my wreck field is sovereign. You will not descend for samples until my mortals are not drowning in policies you did not read. Acknowledge.

Silence — petty, human, dressed in machine hesitation. Then, clipped as stylus snapping:

*Acknowledged, Castellan. Temporal deferment logged. **Threx** out.*

Victory, of a sort. Romance of command often looked like that: not cheer, only smaller cruelty postponed, with interest accumulating somewhere Genetor would later try to collect.

Thornwake survived. Traders often did, because survival was their heresy.

She came in under guard on skiff that smelled of burned drives and expensive fear, warrant-case dented, coat stained with void-slick that was not oil. Navigator boy did not come. Kadmos’s report said boy’s chair on *Mercy* was empty in way chairs became empty when Immaterium took its fee.

Thornwake stood before Alaric in lee of split lander and did not bow deeply enough to be forgiven.

— I sold you braid — she said. — I did not sell you lie.

— You sold us **Aethelgard** at impact speed — Kadmos said.

Thornwake’s eyes were red-rimmed, furious, still calculating.

— Ribbon turned. Chart-houses will tell you I should have known. Chart-houses will sell you same lie tomorrow with seal. Bind me if you must. Kill me later if it helps your ledgers. But move your brothers before mud learns their names.

Alaric looked at her, then at Needles girl, then at auxilia who were already dying for maps that had never been true. He felt Radulf’s distant sentence move in chest like second heartbeat:

*End this search.*

— You will give Kadmos every byte you still have — he told Thornwake. — Then you will wait under Mechanicus eyes until I decide whether traders die for mathematics or only for politics.

By sixth hour of CV y0, mud preached first sermon.

Auxilia’s second wave — three hundred souls sworn to Segmentum’s forgotten appendix — tried to follow Astartes trace across oxbow Needles called ***The Throat***.

Marines had crossed twenty minutes prior with inexorable patience of masses who knew how to distribute weight until mud consented. Auxilia had **maps** from kinder worlds. Throat took them differently.

**Sergeant Kessler** — built like bridge component, voice trained on parade grounds until parade became survival reflex — held line with profanity and example. She chained squads to rescue lines with knots learned from void-work and stubbornness. She sacrificed dignity for footing. She did everything doctrine borrowed from saner theaters, and some things doctrine never wrote down, like grabbing boy’s belt and yanking before pride could drown him.

Mud opened anyway. It did not boil. It did not roar. It simply ceased to pretend solidarity. Men sank. Women vanished to sternum and screamed in voices rain beat into gurgle.

Someone’s lasgun discharged into water — blue snap, useless illumination — and for instant vein-mud showed interior: not depth, but **layers**, as if world stacked swallowed centuries and tasted boots according to appetite.

Drusus moved among dying with restraint that looked like mercy only if you did not know him. To Drusus, mercy was taxonomy.

— Non-combat casualty cluster, The Throat. Environmental failure exacerbated by inadequate load-bearing doctrine. Recommend immediate halt of auxilia movement pending reassessment.

Alaric turned to Needles interpreters press-ganged into legitimacy by catastrophe. Crab-shell girl stood close enough her eyes reflected helm lamps without flinching.

— You knew — he said.

— We warned — she answered. Gothic clipped, learned from traders rather than priests. — Your papers said *relocate*. Our mouths said *wait*. Papers are louder to soldiers until sky falls. Then mud is louder than all.

Behind her, Kessler hauled boy out by collar. He vomited mud and sea-stink. Perhaps sixteen, cheeks still fat with childhood’s last insult, he looked at towering armor not with gratitude but with blame so pure it bordered on holiness: *you came; you should have known.*

Alaric felt crack — needle-fine — where *Codex* confidence had lived: not in Astartes, but in mortals who had trusted height of allies. *Codex* promised structure. Structure implied predictability. Predictability was kind of love for those who had nothing else. Here, love drowned.

Night did not fall so much as thicken. They established perimeter *Codex* would not have been ashamed to sign — layered kill-boxes, void shields wheezing against humidity, auspex fields crawling with phantom returns.

Varro walked lines and spoke short Rites of Binding to men who flinched at chaplain skull-flesh. He did not ask them to love Emperor. He asked them to hold silence until silence became shelter, because noise is door, and doors are expensive on worlds that listen.

Drusus returned from field tent blooming with bioluminescent fungi Needles cultivated for wounds, gauntlets smeared with chemistry filters would not name cleanly.

— Stress chemistry is climbing — he told command knot. — Not battle-shock. Basal arousal. As if air files us under *prey*.

— Superstition — Kadmos said automatically.

— Superstition is diagnosis we use when we lack taxonomy — Drusus replied, without heat.

Disappearance happened in silence after midnight, when even rain wore itself thin. Third squad, Second Demi-company — twenty-one Astartes under Brother-Sergeant **Torven** — detailed to secure needle-bridge of black volcanic glass spanning tidal gut.

They checked in at intervals. Green icons, then amber, then nothing: no *vox* scream, no las-staccato, only quiet, **wrong** quiet, quiet of courtroom waiting for sentence.

Alaric reached bridge with Varro and Drusus in wake — boots ringing once, then muted as glass drank sound. Site waited like altar poorly consecrated.

Torven’s squad had not been slaughtered in any convention *Codex* recognized. Instead there was **order** — boltguns set beside each other, muzzles down as if in penance; helmets arranged by size; power packs stacked with obsessive neatness. In center lay Torven’s gauntlets, removed, placed palm-down, fingers slightly curved as if resting.

Drusus knelt. Voice, on private channel, carried first honest tremor he had permitted in years.

— This is not retreat. Not capture pattern per conventional doctrine. This is display.

— An offering — Varro finished. — Or a grade.

Alaric did not touch gear. To touch it would have been to accept exam.

Crab-shell girl stood at bridgehead despite orders. Her breath steamed.

— What do your people call this? — Alaric asked quietly.

She swallowed.

— We do not name what hunts. Names make ears.

— It is survival — she answered. — And your giants — without helmets here — they would have heard better, maybe. But you do not take helmets off for mud.

— Your name — Alaric said.

Beat — rain rebuilding overhead.

— **Iria** — she said, way some people said *knife*: small, sharp, a fact.

— Then teach us how to listen — Alaric said.

She flinched, surprised, as mortals always were when mountain asked to learn from ant.

Before they burned bridge to deny unseen passage — before *Lex Ignis* auspex priests argued for sample retrieval and Drusus denied them with voice like scalpel — Alaric addressed Chapter on open channel stripped of ornament.

— There is no glory here yet — he said. — Only weight. We did not take beachhead. We **survived** fall. We failed first covenant mortals make with hope: that help arrives with understanding. Mud took our auxilia because we mistook paper for ground — and because void sold us braid that bent. Now night takes warriors because we mistake readiness for mastery.

Pause. Pause was for breath, for letting every brother hear own lungs and admit they still worked.

— Enemy is not speaking our language. It is speaking ours back — cleaned, arranged, corrected. We will learn its grammar. We will bleed until we do. We will find chart-houses that do not sell **provisional** death.

He did not say *We hold.* That litany belonged to walls fully built. Tonight they were iron in forge, not yet blade.

Somewhere beyond auspex certainty, something studied bridge again and found tableau acceptable. Not victory. Beginning.

Somewhere in wreck field, **Maela Thornwake** watched rain and began, with trader’s patience, to calculate whether Castellan von Helis would kill her before mud did.

**Echo** listened at Needle-bridge for harmonics not yet **tide**-shaped — Triumvirate seal still **narrow** — filing vein-mud as sequence before arrangement taught tide return as flirtation deferred.

**Orwen** copied Q1 opening timestamps without adjectives — ranges, no metaphors — because weight dressed as oracle killed faster than mud gospel remembered in calves.

**Sera** tagged Q1 handshake *monitor only* until Pol seed starved merge columns and Moon Hunt earned attested injury.

Varro filed night under *threshold crossed, not sealed*. Drusus filed stress chemistry under *prey filing — unverified*. Kadmos filed maps under *assume hostile ground until mud proves otherwise*.

Chapter had collided at CV hour zero. Vein-mud had preached. Throat had drowned paper confidence. Needle-bridge had graded them with arrangement.

Mud gospel had begun — not theology, **weight** — and weight would teach grammar before ink learned *Der Batav*, before *We hold* earned speaking, before mirrors and iron men and crowns would ask whether iron could stay.

**Alaric** stood at Needle-bridge lip one minute after twenty-one icons became nothing with order — hinge-work — and did not say *We hold.* Holding, here, was vein-mud preaching weight honest enough to survive when paper confidence drowned in throat geometry arrangement would spend in tide return.

**Valerius** stamped Q1 opening twice — Marine lane beside mortal witness lane — because Moon Hunt would ask for both kinds of solvency and bankrupt anyone who merged them before dog logic taught siege animal flirtation on deck plates.

**Drusus** filed mud gospel under Triumvirate seal — vein-mud as environmental variable, throat geometry as witness route only — and did not brief captains. Briefing would have turned weight into **oracle** before arrangement taught tide return as flirtation deferred.

**Kadmos** posted Needles cordon one meter deeper — geometry as insurance — because walls did not argue with ink and ink still tried to rename drowning as temporal deferment logged.

**Pol** kept monitor seed *sequence only* beside Sera splices until Moon Hunt earned attested injury at needle-bridge.

**Mira** passed wreck wire with tally ink on knuckles — witness without applause — because mud gospel remembered weight in calves before ink learned *Der Batav*.

**Santo** adjusted crate strap at wreck wire and did not look at hololiths naming *provisional* death. Looking would have turned survival into spectacle before arrangement taught paired boots geometry.

Interest collecting teeth before receipts.

Black water. Needle teeth. Wrong quiet.

Always owed.
