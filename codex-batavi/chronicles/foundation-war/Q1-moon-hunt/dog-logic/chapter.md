Chapter had changed watch’s **heartbeat** — routes un-spooled, rally points reborn each night, *vox* discipline tightened until even prayer learned to whisper.

Still Rhylan’s empty rack accused them each dawn. Still bolt shell stood upright on pack no one had yet dared strip for parts, spiral mud drying to memory of fingers too patient to be kind.

They told themselves *discipline* would answer **arrangement**. Until CV y2, most still believed enemy *hunted* — singular appetite, duelist’s sacrament, kind of war knights recognize in mirrors.

**Dog logic** arrived with rain: not one stalker composing tableau, but **pack** bred — or trained — for *clearance*.

Countersigns still shifted at minute-offsets — Alaric’s cipher from Sallow Morrow carried forward like scar — yet CV y2 proved ciphers were not armor, only **delay**: enemy did not need password when it could read **shape** in mud.

Drusus walked past Rhylan’s shell twice daily and did not touch it, because touching would have filed object under *evidence handled*, and evidence handled became evidence argued over by men who had not paid price of standing watch beside absence.

West of Needles’ rain-shadow, **Black Fan** spread: mud flat glazed with oil-sheen from seeps locals burned for night-fires, framed by mangrove roots that rose like **teeth** in triplicate.

Major **Elyan Certo** drew cordon across that fan as if straight lines could shame planet into obedience. Militarum cadre, *coordination liaison* — title that meant *we own bodies you spend* — Certo had voice tuned on parade squares and husband’s grave on world whose name he would not speak aloud.

He believed mortals could be **geometry**. He believed flanking Astartes center was *honor*. Grave lived behind his teeth like second set of orders: if he failed here, he failed twice — once for Segmentum, once for name he would not let mud pronounce.

That private motive did not make him wise. It made him **loud** at wrong frequencies, and loudness on Fan was not bravery but **beacon**.

Oil-sheen caught flame from seeps. Heat lifted in ribbons that fooled auspex into *movement* where nothing moved but chemistry. Techs cursed calibration until Kadmos ordered them to log false positives as **environmental hostility** rather than superstition, because superstition was word officers used when they wanted to sleep.

Rain turned smoke into taste: sweet rot, burned sugar, metal sweat — Black Fan’s palate. Mangrove roots clicked under Marine boots like dice thrown by patient gambler.

— Marines hold the spine — Certo sent over channel Alaric had not blessed and could not silence without snapping treaty that kept three thousand auxilia from becoming three thousand liabilities overnight. — We flank. We **guide** your fire. We show Segmentum we are not cargo.

**Sergeant Kessler** — mud still bleaching flak white in strange patterns — caught his elbow hard enough to hurt man who did not wear plate.

— Sir — she said — Needles say Fan waits. Not like Throat — *different*. Iria says —

— I don’t pay tide-witches to think — Certo snapped, and sentence cut air too clean for apology.

Iria, small against mangrove line, lifted neither hand in omen nor plea. She only **looked** at Alaric once — helmed, immovable — then at mud, as if asking world which hunger would win.

Alaric did not countermand Certo on open channel. Wars of paper kill slower than wars of mud, but they kill all same.

While Certo’s geometry marched onto the Fan, treaty ink still bound him: three thousand auxilia throats attached to logistics tail staff would later describe as city pretending to be convoy, but that precision did not exist yet — only present weight of bodies Segmentum had shipped to world that treated bodies like **floatage**.

Commissariat runners carried stamps. Administratum aides carried clipboards. Both carried same hunger: *proof of useful death*.

Kessler remembered Throat in set of Certo’s shoulders — remembered how paper confidence had drowned boys after sky fell — and tried to put that memory into grip on his elbow without breaking skin. She failed at skin part. She succeeded at memory part. Certo shook her off anyway, because shame and command sometimes share uniform.

He switched to Triumvirate band.

— Kadmos — center mass. Varro — bind panic when it comes. Drusus — **triage permission** for cordon breach; do not lecture me on *purity* if flesh blocks bolts.

— Acknowledged — Drusus said.

— Silence will be weaponized — Varro said.

— I hate politics — Kadmos answered, almost snarl.

— Then love **range** — Alaric said.

Kadmos took center mass literally: fire teams in overlapping arcs that treated mangrove teeth as **cover** only until first flanker proved cover was lie told by roots that moved slower than knees.

Varro moved behind Militarum ranks not to inspire — no time for inspiration, only sequence — but to choke panic at throat: short phrases, single words, *hold*, *mute*, *step*, Chaplain’s voice turned into **brake** on human impulse to scream patterns into rain.

Panic was not cowardice. Panic was **broadcast**, and enemy had already demonstrated it liked listening.

**Kadmos** posted deck cordon one meter deeper — geometry as insurance — because siege animal flirtation required hinge weight before seventy-two taught dishonest arithmetic the sea repeated as shelter integer.

When the first low shapes hit the oil-sheen, enemy did not arrive as hunters **present** themselves — no shimmer of stalker pride, no arranged lesson left for theologians.

Faunal **flankers** hit first: low, slab-muscled shapes auspex mis-filed for precious seconds as *fauna* — Aethelgard’s bitter joke — until they tore through kneecaps and Achilles vectors with bored efficiency of things **handled** on leash.

Then **enforcers** rose from mud that should not have hidden mass: broad silhouettes, breach-weight, movements that owed nothing to honor and everything to *minutes saved*.

They did not collect trophies. They did not line boots. They **removed** problems with industrial contempt of soldiers answering impatient officer thousand years dead.

Overlapping fields of fire — **their** fire — danced with Marine reactions trained for *knightly* violence. Feints drew eyes. Second waves existed to **stampede** mortals into angles where hesitation would kill allies as surely as blades.

Bolters answered in disciplined bursts — *crump-crump-crump* — and each burst carved brief window of heat in oil-sheen air before rain closed it again like lid.

Auspex returns multiplied until techs cursed **mineral ghosts** and sergeants cursed worse. Flamer washed root-line. Flame became orange ribbon, then steam, then smell of cooked vegetal rot that had nothing to do with victory and everything to do with childhood kitchens on dead worlds — sense-memory ambush, kind wars use when they want you human at wrong instant.

Certo’s cordon became **funnel** he had not built and could not close. He died screaming name that was not husband’s — some virtue-title burned into Academy brass — while mud took his chest and something like laughter rode oil-rain without using human throats.

In the minute the Fan spent chewing Certo’s cordon, **Kessler** lived because rage is buoyancy. She dragged boy — sixteen, Administratum dreams still stuck in teeth — when Drusus cleared line in vault obscene with economy: surgical chainblade kiss along flanker spine mid-lunge, weight transfer flawless, anger present only as **precision** if you knew him well enough to look.

Boy’s throat stayed intact. His eyes did not.

In minute after — always minute, never hour — Drusus became machine for staunch and sort: tourniquet phrases, *here*, *not there*, *living left*, *dead right*, because left and right were how you kept triage from becoming philosophy.

Marines who had never liked mortals learned to like them less and move them faster, which was its own brutal mercy.

**Certo**’s virtue-title burned in Kessler’s memory like a second set of orders — husbands, graves, names mud could not pronounce — and she filed it under *living left* without speaking it aloud because speaking would have turned arithmetic into **eulogy** admiralties could sleep on.

**Kadmos** posted flankers one meter tighter without order — geometry as apology the Chapter did not know how to speak — because siege animal flirtation was violence deferred, not denied, and deferral always collected interest in tide seventy-two would spend in dishonest arithmetic.

*Vox* officer tried to read tally aloud. Alaric cut channel with gesture Kadmos translated into *silence* without needing orders: tallies were bait for ears that had not earned numbers yet.

Afterward, on promontory slick as confessing glass, Alaric watched Marines breathe like men who had expected applause and received **arithmetic**.

Kadmos swore softly — no parade oaths now — voice hollowed.

— We prepared for knight — he said. — They fed us **siege animal**.

Someone laughed once — wrong sound, quickly swallowed — because laughter was shape relief took before it learned discipline.

On the treeline, Threx’s lander coughed heat at treeline. Red lenses advanced with samples hungry as marriage. Alaric turned helm once — gesture entire warning.

— Swabs — Drusus spat toward Genetor without turning. — **Blood on Fan only.** Nothing labeled Batavi without my seal.

Threx’s *vox* crackled amusement drowned in politesse.

— Apothecary’s generosity is noted.

Iria climbed until stone granted her height, cloak clattering.

— You met **soldiers** — she said — not loud, but carrying. — **Hunters** will think them *noisy*.

Drusus cleaned blade without ceremony. When he spoke, it was not comfort. It was **naming** — first crude taxonomy that would later save them and shame them by turns.

— Event classification updating. Threat dichotomy: **Type A** — display predation. **Type B** — area denial pack tactics.

Varro inclined *Codex* death-skull toward dark where mangroves pretended innocent.

— Two gospels — he said. — One war.

Lex Ignis auspex choir tried to classify flanker muscle density mid-battle until Threx himself snapped hold order across their channel — *sample later; fight now* — because even acquisition priests could recognize when mathematics became **suicide**.

Red lenses swept treeline like hungry punctuation. Chapter learned to read that punctuation as weather, not ally.

Below the promontory, shore parties waded with lamps and poles, pulling auxilia from sucking mud way you pull teeth — slow, reluctant, admitting pain as part of extraction.

Officers stopped counting aloud when count climbed past what ink wanted to own. Kessler kept counting anyway, because stopping felt like leaving Certo’s mistake unfinished.

Sea’s ledger would later insist on own arithmetic. For now, living only knew Fan had taken **more** than it returned, and *more* was word Administratum forms tried to soften into *missing pending verification*.

Wind pulled oil-rain sideways like veil. Somewhere under Fan, tide fingers searched pockets living had vacated — **counting** what would wash up when mercy or mockery next chose to speak.

She did not promise safety. She marked interval for Alaric with lifted shell — small compass, small threat — and let him interpret compass as he interpreted everything: as obligation.

Chapter called it victory on *vox* because **victory** is sometimes lie that keeps throats un-slit until morning. *Vox*, at least, could be logged. Grief could not, not cleanly, not yet.

When shore parties stopped counting what ink could own, Alaric did not say *We hold.* He watched mud and learned **dog logic**: packs do not debate knightly things. They **finish**.

Finish, here, meant stripping Fan of useful angles until dawn. Meant forbidding victory parades. Meant ordering Drusus to keep Type A / Type B scratch visible on slate where officers could see it and enemies could not — because taxonomy was now weapon as real as bolter, and hiding it behind humility would have been another kind of **cowardice**.

Rain washed blood toward tide-line in threads too orderly to comfort anyone who remembered Sallow Morrow’s paired boots. Order, again. Always order — just wearing different mouth.

Varro filed day under *two liturgies, one crucible* — not for Strategium, for himself — because chaplains who lose track of what they witnessed become men who bless wrong things.

He did not pray for victory. He prayed for **discrimination**: soul’s ability to tell hunger from duty when both wore same rain.

On Fan, rain fell hard enough to baptize shame and call it weather — honest, at least, about what it was doing.

**Iria** passed the promontory wire once with tide chart folded into her cuff — interval risk, same vaccine cables would learn on gulf spines — and did not seek Alaric’s eyes. Seeking would have turned witness into **spectacle**. She adjusted a water-point tally instead — correction for schism, hunters withdrawing while soldiers remained — the same parallel filing Valerius would later teach as *stamp twice or choke together*.

**Kessler** posted auxilia in pairs with backs to backs and told them to shoot the ground first if laughter came bolt-deep without humor — lesson not yet named Versibar, already felt in firebase ash waiting south of this rain.

**Dog logic** had teeth. Chapter would not forget bite.

**Kadmos** posted countersigns that changed at random minute-offsets until even sergeants laughed once — one laugh, then silence — because laughter was another rhythm, and rhythm was currency the arrangement had taught them to spend carefully. **Varro** filed threshold binding as proto-rite — seal attempted, not victory — and listened to deck whispers that were almost Batavi until static swallowed them: eighty-one percent mimicry, flirtation without consent still violence.

**Threx**’s Skitarii swabbed chapel gallery under protest and logged *Compliance deferred* beside *Castellan denial* — appetite deferred, not denied — while **Valerius** copied forty-six void names into a column that would grow tall enough to shame admiralties who pretended mirrors washed hands clean.

**Iria** passed the Needles wire one last time with tide chart in her cuff and did not seek audience — active silence shielding the mesh against excuses that would have turned dog logic into **folk liturgy**. Two nods with **Kessler**, no speech worth the rain.

**Drusus** appended a closing margin — *treat mimicry above eighty percent as hostile flirtation; do not brief as prophecy* — and felt Pier Nine’s pupil trace rhyme in the sealed buffer like grit in a seal joint. Sequence, not oracle. Triumvirate first.
