## II. Grammar Breaks

Alaric had held line until holding became *grammar* — subject, verb, object: **enemy**, **clear**, **advance** — clarity that makes victory possible and brotherhood bearable.

Then grammar **broke**.

Not all at once. Never generously. More like ice faulting under load: hairline first, then roar you feel in teeth before mind permits adjective *catastrophe*.

It began as heat — not fever, not infection in any ledger Drusus would sign without argument — **sovereignty**: permission body grants itself when **will** turns from general into mere **counsel**.

Visor interior painted **red** — not warning-light red alone — **hunger-red**.

World simplified into **vectors of release** — open aisles, closed aisles, bodies that *blocked*, bodies that *yielded*, threats deserving silence and silence arriving in **caliber**.

Auxilia saw first. Mortals always do. Gods hide behind myth. Monsters arrive **visibly**.

Brothers who loved him learned lesson Varro had been selling for years without selling: binds fail at stress nodes. Stress nodes arrive when Castellan grammar stops being **language** and becomes **weather** — unstoppable by shouting; barely postponable by chemistry and bind; inevitable as tide if you admit atmosphere has turned.
