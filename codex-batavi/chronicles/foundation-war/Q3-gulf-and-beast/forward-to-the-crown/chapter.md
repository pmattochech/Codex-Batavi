Evacuation is **triage** wearing **liturgy**.

When hull space is sin, every choice becomes **confession** — who boards first, who boards middle, who boards **last**, and who is left with prayer that is really invoice — because who boards last becomes **theology** not because Emperor demands theater but because mortals need **story** that makes abandonment feel less like **murder** dressed as arithmetic.

**Silica Field** approaches were never clean approaches but **negotiations** with distance.

Navigator bargains made Drusus dislike mathematics **medically** — vitals and hours and cumulative hemorrhage of third eye’s tolerance — and made Varro dislike them **theologically** beneath Executor wolf-skull, since some routes do not merely risk hulls but **invite** what watches through angles.

Alaric did not ask void for mercy. He **chose** — **geometry** assigning souls to slots, brutality honest enough to insult optimists and precise enough to save fractions — because there is no version of this work that leaves hands **clean**, only versions that leave **more** hands alive.

Embarkation slates stayed **double-stamped** — Valerius — **witness routes** beside **throughput** — because Silica staff tried to merge grief into efficiency until teeth showed in reflections again.

**Parallel filing** traveled from Vitreus archives like infection shaped like mercy — stubborn, thin, honest enough to cut spreadsheets.

Merit’s clerks aimed *forward morale lenses* at boarding queues until Kessler broke another mount and filed *NOT FIELDED*. Forwardness was not metric you harvested from throats at gangways.

Sera paired fiber through duplicate trunks — red for *call*, blue for *ghost* — because harmonics ate one line and called it *routing optimization* while custody still breathed on second.

Echo pressed chaplain seams where binds thinned — Triumvirate seal still **narrow** — and Varro refused spectacle. Executor wolf-skull present as infrastructure while litanies shortened until boredom stayed shield against **folk liturgy** fermenting in mirrored viewports.

Drusus’s margins carried **sequence**, not prophecy — *Navigator strain — record; treat optic ghosts as debt until sustained across three intervals; do not merge evacuation indices with sympathy spikes* — clinical refusal to let crown earn pretty story before steel did.

Junior crews confused **Sigma bleed** with rumor until Drusus forced **intervals** onto displays — ghost correlations dressed as gossip until uglier filters convicted them.

Medicae annexes stacked souls not wounded yet but **pre-wounded** by phosphene and queue nausea — petty taxes embarkation collected before blades arrived — and Drusus metered sedatives like artillery until poetry stayed impossible when screams argued for hymns.

Bondsmen whispered **Dark Age of Technology** near Navigator alcoves until Varro crushed whisper with ash.

— Nomenclature is seal — he said. — Myth recruits corridors faster than doctrine leashes them when angles open wrong.

**Kessler** volunteered for last wave because **guilt** enjoys uniform and duty is harness you can tighten until breath hurts, and pain sometimes feels like **payment**.

She did not dramatize it. She simply stood where standing cost most.

She thought of mercy shot on bridge and did not call it policy. She called it **feet** — only prayer that still fit.

Boarding petty officers learned **parallel initials** — duplicate manifests, duplicate seals — until juniors stopped treating redundancy as Mechanicus fetish and started treating it as **insurance** when one cogitator learned *preference* without warrant.

Queues learned **touch-taps** when *vox* screamed — knuckle code older than brand — because panic dressed as static recruited faster than bolters when harmonics mimicked **Servitor kneel cadence** learned in dynastic overrides.

**Witness priority** traveled hand-to-hand when ears starved for geometry.

Skitarii liaisons tried **first touch** on priority crates until **NON-COMPLIANT** met them in Valerius’s ink — because *touched* could mean *taken* and Chapter’s file on first touch was already thick with **skin** from other poles.

Mirror-route officers flinched when confidence intervals tightened because grief and throughput tried to merge again. Valerius slid duplicate sheets until grief stayed grief and quotas stayed quotas.

Wolf’s Curse watch stayed off channels that could recruit story — Kadmos enforced — because *Furor* vocabulary beside Navigator strain could invent cult velocity.

**Santo** survived because his mother **lied** to him about departure times.

Mira Kell’s counterfeit coin — love minted into false clock so son would not insist on being noble at price of his life — so he boarded cursing her and boarded **breathing** while she watched from gantry shadow and did not wave.

Some victories look like betrayal from wrong angle.

**Threx** argued **asset preservation** until words assumed sheen of **piety** — *machines, samples, archives, capacity* — sacred vocabulary of those who believe objects outlive people because objects do not writhe when they die.

Kadmos answered without humor, flat as blade on stone:

— Pick carefully whether *asset* means **human** or **bolt**.

After which silence, briefly, became fourth party in room.

Night cycles traded lamp codes until **Lex Ignis** clocks aligned with Mechanicus wheels. Water points ran sour. **ROTATE** became liturgy for throats raw from shouted rows.

Marines scrubbed ceramite dull — **glare discipline** carried from Vitreus — until polish stopped volunteering shine to viewports that ate faces.

Apothecarion interns practiced distinguishing **Exanimus** drift from Navigator tremor — **rhyme** permitted in margins; **oracle** burned — until juniors stopped mistaking adrenaline for prophecy when gauges lied politely.

Strategium night staff rehearsed **DO NOT MERGE** until phrase sounded less like tech-adept contempt and more like chaplain refrain — some instincts immune systems refused to euphemize — because crowns loved merges the way archives had loved merges: clean on paper, invasive in throats.

Hyper-G **Tertius** veterans warned about **wrong weight** in transit straps. Vitreus veterans warned about **wrong light** in viewscreens. Both warnings rhymed with Silica approach: different weather, same throat paying interest.

**Iria**, where tides crossed evac ribbons, traded **active silence** with officers who wanted poetry — silence meant *do not rename sacrifice as throughput* — until tide vocabulary stayed tide vocabulary and did not metastasize into slides about *boarding KPIs*.

Wind off lock jaws tasted like ozone and cold sugar. She breathed it anyway — breathing proved somebody still negotiated with bodies instead of only with departure manifests.

Ahead — past glass-tide hazards and convoy ghosts, past mirror-route grudges still staining Navy channels — **crown** of crucible glittered like romance goal dangled over exhausted animals:

**Solaris.** **Halo.** The **Focal Crown.**

Final pole. Last theater. Place where war stopped pretending it had **middle**.

It shone on strategium plots like jewel Alaric knew was also **cut**.
