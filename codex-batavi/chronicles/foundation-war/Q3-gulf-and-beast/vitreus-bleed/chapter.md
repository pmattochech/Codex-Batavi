## I. Glass as Judgment

**Backward braid:** every blind-fire lesson pole had taxed — seas multiplying targets, optics lying politely until men died **correct** — now lie wore **pulse**, and pulses make auditors nervous differently than reflections do.

On Vitreus, seas had always been lenses: cruelty dressed as geography. Now they were also **verdicts**.

Echo pressed chaplain seams where binds thinned — Triumvirate seal still **narrow** — and Varro refused spectacle. Executor wolf-skull present as infrastructure while litanies shortened until boredom became shield against **folk liturgy** fermenting in mirrored bulkheads.

## IV. Triumvirate Held (Not Victory)

Triumvirate function ***held*** — word chosen carefully, because *victory* would have been lie and *control* would have been blasphemy.

**Alaric** remained **caged** inside **Vermilion** protocol — trial-cloth and **Alpha** watches not shame theater alone, but **harness** mortals could understand when they needed something to trust besides god’s mood.

He still issued sentences where sentences could pass without breaking seals. He did not pretend freedom and did not **need** freedom to be **present** — worse for some witnesses, because presence without release looks like **mercy** until you realize it is **discipline** wearing mercy’s mask.

Not liturgy. **Order**. Chapter’s iron answer stayed sheathed while Castellan still learned what sheath meant.

## VI. Wrong Light / Wrong Smile
