## I. Etiquette as Weapon

The **override** did not arrive as saber-rattle. It arrived as **etiquette** — ancient, flawless, obscene in its politeness: a **last command** spoken in frequencies that made servitors kneel and made mortals *listen* before they understood they were obeying something that did not recognize their species as **audience**, only as **obstruction**.

The **backward braid** was everything the bridge had already paid for — **skin debt**, **witness priority**, silence zones mapped in muscle memory — compressed into a single instruction the tomb delivered as if doing mortals a courtesy by letting them **hear** their own obsolescence clearly.

**Canoptek** swarms moved to the echo like iron hymns — blind, in the strategic sense, to living stratagem. They did not need to *understand* Batavi cleverness. They needed only to **execute** geometry older than the Imperium’s grief.

Valerius had stamped embarkation slates from **Maw** exercises **twice** — once for biocontainment, once for crews — because confusing pole debts had already bankrupted language once. Here he stamped evacuation queues **twice** — once for bodies permitted air, once for names permitted memory — until duplication stopped feeling like bureaucracy and started feeling like **mercy with receipts**.

Drusus’s margins traveled in kit paste-smears — *tremor rhyme — record; treat harmonics spikes as debt until disproven* — not prophecy, **sequence**.

**Echo** pressed Triumvirate seals without earning vocabulary. **Varro** answered with binds short enough to survive override static — Executor wolf-skull present as infrastructure, not applause.

## III. Inheritance Violence

What followed was not merely battle. It was **inheritance violence** — dynasty asserting ownership over corridors, over air, over the right to decide which flesh counted as traffic and which counted as **stain**.

The Chapter discovered, again, that tombs do not debate **rank**. They debate **clearance**, then enforce verdict with politeness of a blade wiped clean between cuts.

**Flayer-adjacent** logic flickered at edges: not same outbreak as bridge stacks, but a **rhyme** — timing too neat, cruelty too *curated*, as if something enjoyed **dressing** slaughter in ceremony.

Drusus felt pattern itch behind his eyes — variables uncomfortably like **mirror-route** debriefs: delays that *pretended* to be accident, losses that *pretended* to be entropy, harmonics that *pretended* to be incompetence.

He **refused** the thought the moment it tried to become word: too early, too poisonous. Some heresies arrive dressed as **insight**. Apothecaries learn to starve them until evidence is steel, not intuition.

Still, his hand wrote an annex title he did not read aloud — *dynastic coupling; suspected* — and the paper’s whisper felt like swallowing glass.

Skitarii skirmishers — **Threx**’s kin in all but patience — tried **first touch** on canoptek spines for yield until Alaric threatened to file *first touch* in blood if Mechanicus ambition split rear guard the Chapter had already paid for.

— You breach — he said, voice flat as deck plate. — We hold. Not for trophies.

The argument was same gulf argument. Only the water was now **etiquette** and the fish were servitors.

## V. The Shrinking Aperture

Witnesses — mortals stupid-brave enough to **film** with illegal **pict** — became problems the tomb solved in preferred dialect: **sealing**.

They were herded into shrinking aperture with promises whispered by officers who needed lie to swallow their own stomachs:

— Mercy — one said, voice breaking on the second syllable. — Containment. Preservation. Please.

— Please — another repeated, because repetition was all that fit through the door.

Nobody believed **mercy**. They obeyed anyway — because alternative was watching children learn what flensing sounded like in stereo.

Valerius walked the queue with clipboard like relic of saner war — names, ages, allergy notes absurd as poetry — because logistics is sometimes only mercy that does not pretend to be divine.

**Sergeant Kessler** stood where line threatened to become mob — feet planted, voice scraped to useful register that turns panic into **rows** — because rows were only shape that fit through shrinking door without tearing itself apart.

Children pressed against restraints like small animals. Kessler would not let them watch servitor ballet long enough to learn its steps. Some lessons, she had decided, were not *debt* you paid early for discount.

She thought of mercy shot on the bridge and did not nod at anyone who looked like they needed reassurance. Reassurance would have been receipt. Receipt would have made sealing **policy**.

**Drusus** moved among the herded with filtration packs and instructions spoken slow enough to obey — *infection control*, not *brood* — because shrinking aperture was debt Beast Records would spend when ceramic law stepped and vanished at Incus forge hum.

The aperture closed — not with drama — with **finality**.

Screams — brief, human, furious — **became geology**: not metaphor-only; harmonics carried into stone and cable until later crews swore certain spans *whimpered* when struck, as if bridge remembered **throat**.

## VI. Duty as Abacus

**Alaric** sealed the breach the way he sealed most catastrophes: with **lives** spent deliberately, priced in advance, weighed without flinching.

**Duty** as abacus — not cruelty performing as leadership — **arithmetic** performing as leadership, which is crueler to kind of person who needs stories where every death means something *visible*.

Some deaths meant only *distance*. Distance saved next rank. That had to be enough.

He placed bodies where tomb’s geometry demanded payment and did not explain. Explanation would have been insult to dead and comfort to living in wrong proportions.

He did not gift tombs spectacle. He did not speak Chapter’s iron answer as liturgy. He paid clearance in bodies and walked narrowed ford afterward — hinge-work — measurable in spans held, apertures closed, names Valerius would write by hand because stamped paper could not carry them all.

Bondsmen whispered **Dark Age of Technology** near stasis flicker until Varro crushed whisper with ash.

— Nomenclature is seal — he said. — Myth recruits servitors faster than doctrine leashes them when frequencies kneel.

**Kadmos** aged in weeks — not in ceramic cracks, but in small tells veterans recognize: fewer jokes, harder eyes, patience that felt less like virtue and more like **fatigue** wearing discipline’s uniform.

On one span he held retreat lane open until last auxilia boots cleared, then stepped backward like man closing ledger, because **rear** was where oaths lived when glory tried to sprint ahead of sense.

He had held Maw mud the way he now held this span: not different wars, same **interest** wearing new weather.

The helm did not judge him for it. The swamp had already taught them all that **liturgy** and **logistics** share a spine.
