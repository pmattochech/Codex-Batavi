## I. Arithmetic as Accusation

Tally arrived as **halving** mythologized into math.

Mortal dead came in **stacks** — names compressed into bundles because no mouth could pronounce them fast enough to keep pace with duty — while transhuman dead numbered **too many**, and yet fewer than fear had forecast in theatrical moods, which should have comforted and did not.

**Backward braid:** every filing refusing to be only riot — **Vitreus bleed (optic/warp coupling; contained)** still stinking of wrong light, **Castellan hazard watch** still echoing in *vox* drills, gulf **witness priority** crossed out in margins — until arithmetic stopped looking like neutral columns and started looking like **accusation**.

*Half*, some sergeants joked — bitter as burned oil — as if fractions could tame grief by reducing it to one syllable. Officers pretended not to hear because hearing would oblige them to answer.

**Sergeant Kessler** did not joke. She made squads count boots aloud until counting became only prayer that still fit in mouth — **feet**, not heroes, because heroes had become expensive word.

Embarkation slates from Vitreus still arrived **double-stamped** — Valerius — **witness routes** beside **throughput** — because poles kept merging skin into efficiency until teeth showed again in reflections. Archives continued discipline on paper — **parallel filing** — duplicate custody tags until juniors learned redundancy was insurance, not vanity.

Merit’s clerks aimed *recovery KPI lenses* at mourning stacks until Kessler mounted hammer invoice and filed *NOT FIELDED*. Grief throughput was not metric. Grief was **inventory** honest enough to hurt.

Sera paired fiber through separate trunks above archives’ false night — red for *call*, blue for *ghost* — because harmonics ate one seam and called it indexing while chains of custody still breathed on second line.

## III. DO NOT MERGE

In archives — cold air, sterile light, illusion that paper could disinfect memory — **Drusus** found **pattern-harmonics** in sorting stacks.

Neither errors nor ordinary noise of exhausted scribes mistagging flayed tissue as *unconfirmed*. Something else — hands, almost, or *idea* of hands — **adjusting** algorithms from wrong side of screen, teaching machine habits it should not own.

Fingerprint **without** signature. Maker **without** warrant.

**Reading** beat was watching confidence interval **tighten** without new data arriving — watching clean process learn **preference** — until screen’s politeness felt obscene.

He locked file under phrase older than orders, borrowed from tech-adept contempt and repurposed as sacrament:

**DO NOT MERGE.**

Some instincts are not superstition but immune systems.

Beneath lock he appended *possible hostile coupling; archive air-gap recommended* in flat ink of man who would rather be called paranoid than **late**.

Junior clerks confused **Sigma bleed** with gossip until Drusus forced **intervals** onto ledger screens — ghost correlations dressed as rumor until uglier filters convicted them.

Medicae annexes stacked clerks not wounded yet but **pre-wounded** by sleepless tagging — petty taxes paperwork collected before blades arrived — and Drusus metered caffeine like sedatives until poetry stayed impossible when spreadsheets sang.

Bondsmen whispered **Dark Age of Technology** near cogitator alcoves until Varro crushed whisper with ash.

— Nomenclature is seal — he said. — Myth recruits stacks faster than doctrine leashes them when confidence intervals tighten without warrant.

## V. Valerius Cracks (Once)

**Valerius** — ever steady face, ever corridor’s calendar — **cracked** where nobody was permitted to see.

One glass shattered in quarters. Recaf bleeding across page of numbers too innocent to deserve staining. Hands still until they shook once and steadied, because Chapter could not afford quartermaster who **advertised** humanity at wrong hour.

Mira Kell stacked relief chits until fingers went ink-stained — counting as refusal to let **stacks** become pure abstraction — then set jaw when tally matched bundle she recognized from name she would not say aloud on Strategium deck.

Santo found shards swept into bin and said nothing. He only replaced glass before morning inspection — insubordination or love; on Incus, two overlap.

Iria returned to **Needle-songs** carried secondhand — bells and tide-law through mess-deck whispers and refugees who had learned melody as survival — and looked **smaller** at first glance not diminished but **focused**, way blade looks smaller when someone finally stops ornamenting steel.

She breathed **active silence** on local channels when staff tried to rename **arithmetic** as *resilience* — refusal to let convenience rename sacrifice as virtue while tallies still stank of wrong light and **DO NOT MERGE** learned to live in paper, not only glass.

Children in holding pens drew chalk routes on deck until parents scrubbed them off. Kessler forbade Merit pict loops there because *comfort* could become **folk liturgy** faster than archivists could air-gap file when confidence intervals smiled.

**Drusus** logged Valerius’s crack as *data point* in column he wished stayed empty — not betrayal, not weakness — while half standing was interval not closure and closure dressed as triumph theater still required witness before forward earned speaking.

## VI. Half Standing Is Still Wall

Alaric told Strategium while hololith painted losses in colors too polite to be blood:

— Half standing — he said — is still **wall**.

He did not yet say ***We hold.***

Phrase waited **halo-shy** not from cowardice but **respect** — some words earn speaking only when mouth has paid enough to pronounce them without lying.

Strategium nodded in small ways institutions nod when afraid and must not show it.

Work continued, as it always continues — both mercy and sentence — and somewhere in locked stack **DO NOT MERGE** glowed like small rude star, insisting some merges were not errors but **invasion**, and Chapter’s next duty was to refuse them long enough to learn **which**.

Drusus and Varro walked same archive span in different grammars — clinical lock beside liturgical lock — **Triumvirate differentiation** held: two seals on same anxiety, neither stealing other’s syntax, while Alaric — caged, still — **counted** in only language that could not be phished: *minutes*.
