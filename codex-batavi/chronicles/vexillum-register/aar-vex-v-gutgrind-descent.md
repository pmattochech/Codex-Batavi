# AFTER-ACTION REPORT — Gutgrind Descent

**Operation:** GUTGRIND DESCENT  
**Theater:** Shepherd moon Selene-Black, Aethelgard system (gas-giant tide well)  
**Date:** 403.M42  
**Unit:** **V** *Quinta* (full Vexillatio)  
**Enemy:** Orks — Waaagh! splinter Gutgrind (scrap-spire fortress)

**Full chronicle:** [gutgrind-descent.md](../14-vexillum-chronicles/gutgrind-descent.md)

---

## 1. Situation

Orks converted a decommissioned refinery spine into a vertical scrap-cathedral: lift shafts packed with shoota galleries and rokkit debris traps. Warboss Gutgrind held a personal deck at the apex behind a field-generator cage that degraded low-orbit lance solutions.

Strategic requirement: clear the spire for Navy targeting beacons while minimizing structural collapse — adjacent tide habs would saturate if the moon shell fractured.

## 2. Mission

Assault clearance from the top and simultaneous hook landings on mid-spine platforms. **V** *Quinta* (jump-certified) primary.

## 3. Execution

**Phase A — Mid-spine hooks:** Inceptor-class elements seized landings 3–7; boltstorm suppression on open gantries.

**Phase B — Vertical descent:** Assault Intercessor waves dropped along the outer truss face under cover from Phase A; melta teams burned generator couplings deck by deck.

**Phase C — Apex:** Legatus led closure on Gutgrind’s throne deck; Warboss killed in melee — power axe through neck seal and secondary heart (xenos anatomy confirmed post-sample).

**Duration:** 11 hours from first touchdown to beacon green.

## 4. Losses

**V** *Quinta*: 31 battle-brothers permanent casualty or Dreadnought candidacy; eight void-suit breach fatalities.

## 5. *Strategium* note (field 4 register)

The Foundation Aethelgard campaign (*Predator War* era) predates modern four-field *Alabarda* certification logic for Legatus standards. **Gutgrind Descent** is the audit-grade Ork triumph filed for **V** *Quinta* field 4.

## 6. Certification

**Field 4** charge: vertical closure on an Ork scrap-spire — jump-assault reference for the Aethelgard Crucible theater.
