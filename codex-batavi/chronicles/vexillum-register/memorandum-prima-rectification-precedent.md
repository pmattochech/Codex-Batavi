# MEMORANDUM — *Prima* fourth field (rectification charge)

**Classification:** *Strategium* / *Armourium* annex — tithe-facing register  
**Date filed:** 051.M42  
**Subject:** Precedent for **obligation field** on **I** *Vexillatio* ***Alabarda*** (not a battle honour)

---

## I. Purpose

This document records why **field 4** of the First’s battle standard carries **remembrance geometry** — broken cog, void plate, ash band, chain tally — instead of a **certified triumph** charge.

## II. Referenced event

**050.M42 | Malakor V** — execution of the **First Legatus** after treason under *Lupercal* claim. Full narrative and witness geometry (this memorandum is **not** a substitute): [malakor-v.md](../06-silent-vigil/malakor-v.md).

## III. Register language (mandatory)

* **Public / Administratum:** *Rectification charge* or **obligation field**.  
* **Prohibited on cloth:** slogan text naming the traitor or *Lupercal*; the wound remains **symbolic**.  
* **Induction line** (neophyte / transfer): *Debt we carry — not a crime you committed. The lattice failed once; your file starts under that weight so it does not happen again.*

## IV. Certification

**Field 4** for *Prima* is **not** submitted as an after-action victory. It is **instrument**: bounded guilt, forward duty, **no denial** under smoke.

*Strategium* acknowledges **no** competing “greatest success” panel for **I** *Vexillatio* on the same axis as **II–IX**.
