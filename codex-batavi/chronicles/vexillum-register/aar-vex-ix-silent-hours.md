# AFTER-ACTION REPORT — Silent Hours

**Operation:** SILENT HOURS  
**Theater:** Marco de Vetra — Vacuum Shouter relay and laser farm complex  
**Date:** 498.M42  
**Unit:** **IX** *Nona* (full Vexillatio)

**Full chronicle:** [silent-hours-marco-de-vetra.md](../14-vexillum-chronicles/silent-hours-marco-de-vetra.md)

## 1. Situation

Mixed human pirate and xenos cutout raiders assaulted the perimeter during a Maleventum-class EM storm. Post-capture intel stated objective: seize Shouter arrays intact for an external buyer.

## 2. Mission

Hold relay integrity until traffic cleared hash thresholds; deny intact capture of antenna and reactor vault couplings (scorched denial authorized).

## 3. Execution

**IX** *Nona* layered delay geometry.

Suppression interlock on approach alleys — boltstorm and heavy bolter teams rotated every 90 minutes to manage barrel heat under EM damp. Ruin held melta charges on coupling bays — order given twice; one bay scorched preemptively to spoil buyer handoff value. Legatus authorized a single Shouter pulse on minimal power to punch through the storm — message contents classified; result verified by Noviomagus acknowledgement.

**Duration:** 31 hours of continuous contested perimeter.

## 4. Losses

**IX** *Nona*: 27 killed; array damage repairable; no intact handoff.

**Field 4** commemorates relay survival under EM storm — time and signal victory, not local kill ratio.
