## 1. Situation

Traitors held dual anchors: Korvus-A’s macro-vault (storm cannon over the gulf) and Korvus-B’s spine (reactor bleed threat to the tether). Simultaneous assault was required — breach A without triggering B’s scuttle-charge consensus.

## 2. Mission

Single-clock breach: outer vault penetration and inner spine entry within one Strategium tick (±90 seconds authorized).

## 3. Execution

**VI** *Sexta* split: Terminator and Aggressor cadres on Korvus-A landings; Gravis Eradicator teams on Korvus-B’s ventral sill.

Synchronization: vox-disciplined count from the Legatus; first melta touch on A’s vault seam matched to B’s bulkhead thermal cut.

Close: inner-spine traitors failed to arm full scuttle — Nullity-adjacent interference and Ruin speed cited in a classified annex.

## 4. Losses

**VI** *Sexta*: 44 battle-brothers killed or incapacitated beyond immediate Apothecary recovery.
