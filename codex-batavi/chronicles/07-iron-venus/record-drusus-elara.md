# PERSONAL RECORD — DRUSUS ON ELARA SOLIS

*Not for the formal *Apothecarion*. Working memory of the Chief Apothecary. **578.M42** — written after consolidation of the Gate; memories describe events from **550.M42** onward.*

*The objective outcome of the discovery on the Parapet and the first night in the kitchen remains canonical in [iron-venus.md](iron-venus.md). This text is complementary: voice, conscious omissions, and clinical reading over time.*

## I. Arrival (550.M42) — dead file, live room

I made the **retroactive** wall reading afterward — sterile environment, microphone without acid rain distorting gain. Environmental logs coupled to the Castellan’s patrol pattern showed the expected: corrosive precipitation impact, servo vibration, footstep cadence. Superimposed, for **4.7 seconds** before the cut of internal helm audio, a frequency spike **incompatible** with tactical alarm: band of neonatal human cry, damped by cloth and basket. The auspex classified *non-hostile acoustic anomaly*. I manually reclassified as *potential parental-bond stimulus* — label outside any folder the *Administratum* opens without Triumvirate authorization.

What the file does not capture is the **instant** the doors gave.

Varro stood beside me, **wolf-skull helm** sealed — same brutal cast as a Fenrisian **Wolf Priest’s**, filed for **Judex** law rather than pack rite. I still had field solvent on his liturgical *Crozius* — smell of metal and of battle not quite closed. Alaric entered as a pressure front: basket to breastplate, absurd proportion, voice with micro-tremor the helm *vox* did not fully filter. I asked for a report in the tone of one who still believed the world fit a form. His answer — cold, hypothermia, relative cardiac failure — was **operational truth** at the limit of what one human sentence can carry.

On first neonate sweep: mild hypothermia, incipient dehydration, saturation at the lower bound of normal for estimated age. Background noise — Castellan’s breathing, hololiths — masked part of her cardiac signal; corrected in software and verbalized **healthy**, in the strict sense where *healthy* means *not dying this instant under my scanner*.

When I advanced to collect the “specimen,” the **snarl** came.

Instant telemetry: contraction spike in his *masseter* and pectorals, her heart rate **stable** while mine rose 11 bpm — suppressed in 0.8 seconds; irrelevant to her chart, loggable on mine. Private channel to Varro — time economy. His answer spared wrong diagnosis: not *Jagd*. **Territorial anchoring** with overlay of parental protocol absent from the *Codex*.

I withdrew my hands, telegraphed motion: visible forearm, open palm, slow displacement on axes his combat reflex usually accepts as “medic, not target.” Each centimeter was **calibrated**. When he yielded, the scanner was priority again.

**Healthy** did not include: psychosocial prognosis; impact on command of five thousand Astartes; risk of protective hypervigilance incompatible with *Friction Geometry*. That does not fit a form. It fits here.

What I **did not** verbalize in the room, while Varro watched and Alaric still shook: the Castellan’s biometrics showed **anomalous synchrony** — his heart rate descending to a band I had not logged since before consolidation of the Vigil, while hers rose toward stabilization. Correlation is not causality. I kept the correlation. I kept silence around her so as not to reintroduce noise into the graph we were **flattening**.

## II. Medical wing and Triumvirate — two patients, one time

His phrase — *needs help; I don’t know what to do* — translated to clinical variables, designates **two simultaneous patients**: the neonate (thermal and nutritional failure correctable) and the Lord-Castellan (parental protocol failure absent from the manual that taught both). I did not say that aloud. To say it would have been **attention waste** in a moment when any fracture in his trust would cost more lives than a dose error.

Varro set gauntlet on his shoulder and spoke of purpose; Alaric answered with **fear**. I logged: minimal elevation in the Castellan’s voice tone, compatible with confession, not command. Fear does not invalidate function — it **validates** that a threshold still exists between force and crushing. I did not comment. Comment would have been second stimulus.

Varro spoke *necessary flame*. I logged: stable tone, posture without attack lean, **1.2 second** pause before phrase — consistent with doctrinal choice, not tactical hesitation. I did not cross-comment with Varro. In that time band, comment between spiritual and biological is noise.

**I did not prescribe:** Mechanicus creche intake; bureaucratic adoption by sector noble family; any solution that **optimized** the child and **discarded** collateral on the Castellan. Pure optimization is rule in the *Apothecarion*. This was the exception where the Castellan’s failure was the most valuable datum — proof the axis still responded to human stimulus off the battlefield.

I summoned Martha. The decision was the most efficient available.

## III. Kitchen, Martha, Tobias — variables the incubator does not replicate

In the kitchen I was not the focus. That eased observation.

Martha pushed my gauntlet aside without formal authorization. Micro-spike of adrenaline **mine** — suppressed in 0.3 seconds; irrelevant. Relevant: immediate thermal contact — damp wool, human heat, smell of tallow and soap. Neonatal skin temperature curve on contact: **faster rise** than with ward thermal blanket, with less HR oscillation. I noted internally: *human thermal anchor — superior efficacy to standard protocol for bonding.*

Tobias dragged the reinforced chair. I calculated load: seat held the *Castra-Lupus* without audible structural failure. **Non-pharmacological support factor** — proper name filed without epithet.

When they declared **Elara Solis**, I logged phonetics and suggested civil **registry**. No poetic comment. Poetry does not go in data columns; it enters **risk** — a loaded name can turn mass hysteria. Risk was Varro’s to doctrine; I took the cry curve: decibels falling after warm milk intake, pattern compatible with hunger and safety, not pathology.

When Alaric held the child in his lap with gauntlet still armored, remote pulse auspex — discreet, on **his** forearm — captured cortisol drop I had not seen in **centuries** of continuous archive. There was no romance in the data. There was **endocrine stabilization** equivalent to a well-run week-long siege victory.

I did not read the graph aloud. Martha did not need it. Alaric needed silence about the measurement — else awareness of being observed would reintroduce the noise we were removing.

## IV. Development — model under update

**Early years.** Routine checkups the file calls “improvised post-discharge”: weight, reflexes, hearing, environmental parasites typical of Noviomagus. She grew within expected curve for local genotype, with positive deviation in **visual acuity** — likely selection from early map exposure and low industrial light, not miracle.

In an episode Martha described as “she touched the edge of your white sleeve,” I measured autonomic response: curiosity without panic — HR +8%, return to baseline in under forty seconds. Internal classification: *safe exploratory contact with clinical authority figure.* I washed gauntlets afterward with the same rigor as before any procedure; not because of her — because of **protocol** barrier between battle blood and home.

I taught her what I teach whoever must survive here: crying over a wound is **hydraulic cost**; stanching blood is **priority**. She learned fast. Children learn fast when tone does not lie.

What I had to **recalibrate afterward** — with accumulated data — was the model: *hydration waste* in stress tears **not** infant-military does not compare to the same metric applied to an eight-year-old under **Primarch** psychic pressure. Recalibration is honest work. It is not error; it is **model update**.

Parapet visits with escort: I logged gait pattern — short step at first, then normalized extension; genuine interest in firing angle, not theater. Questions per minute above age mean. **No** psychic anomaly; **cognitive bias** toward geometry. Filed as advantage, not pathology.

**565.M42.** Guilliman at the *Castrum Ferrum*. I monitored clinical rear guard: cortisol in honor guard, muscle tension in veterans with reactive Furor history — nothing requiring sedation; everything requiring visible *Apothecarion* **presence**. The political stage did not interest me. The crowd’s thermal map did.

When Elara broke the cordon, **my** pulse rose three beats — shameful log, corrected same minute. She did not know. Alaric knew to look at me long enough to see I would not order sedation of a child for **high-command panic**. That too is datum.

**Tobias.** Industrial Sector 7. I arrived with teams when too late for tissue, not for data. He held the hatch to the end. Internal report: **exposure time**, **estimated dose**, **lives evacuated** by his manual decision. *Vermilion* classification by context, not drama.

In the antechamber, when I closed the field, Elara was alive — BP at high bound, dilated pupil, mild hypocapnia from hyperventilation. I did not speak of Tobias until the graph stabilized. She did not look at me. I did not demand she look.

**Naval Academy.** From then her file ceased to be growth alone — it became **load**. Periodic updates I call *preventive maintenance of the command asset* when someone asks; content is comparative — sleep, ambulatory blood pressure, chronic stress markers against academy age cohort. Nothing outside parameter for those who chose command. All worthy of **vigilance**.

## V. Close (578.M42) — what time added to the file

In 550.M42 I did not know Elara Solis would become fleet admiral. Not an unknown the biological model allowed predicting — it was **political** unknown, outside my domain. Today I know. The updated model confirms: she remains unmodified human within stable parameters; wear is expected for accepted load.

What **remains** unknowable: how far one human life can pull a Chapter of Astartes without becoming lever or target. That is not measured with auspex. It is measured in decades — and in errors we have not yet made.

I do not “always know.” **I catalog.** And when the catalog fails, I update.

If this file is ever read by someone other than me, let them read it for what it is: clinical footnote to a miracle we refuse to call miracle in the official report.
