## I. Throne receipt (Noviomagus Prime — Lower Gutter, Third Shift)

The rain had no virtue of cleansing; it only etch-primed every lung for the next shift. **Mira** stood under the awning of Stall Seventeen with her **civil plaque** warm in her palm — iron rectangle, district stamp, **registry number** she could recite asleep. The forge whistle had not blown yet. That meant God, in Noviomagus grammar, had not started counting.

Beside her, **Uncle Bas** rolled a torque wrench between fingers stubbier than bolts. He never went to chapel. He went to **the stamp booth** after every clean run.

— They’ll want **Throne receipt** today, — Bas said. Not High Gothic flourish — **shift orthodoxy**. The Munitorum clerk had posted a pink slip: **inventory divergence** in Bay Four. Someone’s hands had eaten metal without confessing.

— Wasn’t me, — Mira said.

— Nobody said *sin.* They said **unlogged scrap.** Same hymn, different choir.

Down the gutter, a **grey clerk** in translucent slicker tapped a portable slate. Mortals leaned out of doorways — not reverence for the man, reverence for **procedure** that kept blood off the ledger. He stopped at Seventeen.

— **Bay Four scrap weight** — fifteen kilograms shy. Names on the wrench log.

Bas lifted his chin. — **Third Shift** didn’t touch Four. Ask Second’s **Cable kin.**

The clerk didn’t bristle. **Ledger grace** lived in amended forms; anger cost heat. — Then Second eats the fine, — he said mildly. — Unless someone shows **Throne receipt** — chain-of-custody seal on the lift, **manifest hash** intact — proving Four never opened.

Mira remembered the sound last night — not a thief, **a stumble**: a drum of clips rolling into a grate. She could say it. That would dock **witness debt** onto a stranger’s throat.

Bas looked at her. His eyes weren’t parental; they were **inventory**.

She swallowed rain. — **I heard Four’s grate sing** at second hour. Nobody logged it. I’ll sign **if you pull the grate cam.**

The clerk nodded once — baptism by **cross-out**. — File by shift end. Emperor **feeds** the honest tally.

Nobody had burned incense. Third Shift still felt **holy**: the whistle screamed; iron answered; Noviomagus kept pretending heaven was **a seal that closed**.

## II. Salt-in-the-ration (Aethelgard Prime — Needles estuary)

**Iria** knelt in mud that wasn’t shame — **Aethelgard** taught early that **tide** owned knees. Her brother’s funeral wasn’t in a basilica. It was at the **salt line** where brackish met **Needles** blood-memory: estuary theology, **bridge saint** weather. The priest — young, imported from **Castra-Vetera** for the season — tried **High Gothic** wreath language.

The **river arbiters** let him finish. Then old **Maery** stepped forward with a crust of **hardbread** from Khan-Bator relief — same crop that fed half the sky lanes — and broke it into three dry pieces.

— **Salt-in-the-ration, —** she said, voice full of **bottle creole** cadence. — He pulled nets when the moon pulled water. **Witness debt** says we eat the same slime the Throne eats. **Throne receipt** is **not** paper today. It’s **mouth.**

The priest blinked — politics of bread.

Iria placed a flake on her tongue. Ash and starch. **Ground rite** without copper wire: **discharge** grief into work later.

Her nephew — too young for vows — copied. Three generations chewed in silence that wasn’t **Sidus silence**, only **estuary** courtesy: **don’t spill noise** into a tide that already argued with the sky.

When the priest tried to sprinkle **official** sacred oil, Maery lifted a palm. — Your seal’s fine for **your** registry. Ours is **salt.** God measures **brine**, not eloquence.

Nobody called it heresy. On Aethelgard, **faith of work** wore **mud boots**. The clerk who survived here learned **ledger grace** meant: **don’t file tears as dilution**.

## III. Strait house (Great Reef — approach to Maleventum)

**Kace** had fifteen minutes to choose **sin** or **weather**.

The family **strait house** sat on a nickel spine inside the **Reef** — conductor-rich hell where arcs wrote **temporary scripture** across hulls. Their charter said: **ground** before **greed**. **Listen vow** on open band: **no shout** during storm charge — **Men of Iron** hangover turned civic religion.

Today’s squall wasn’t ion yet; it was **Administratum** mail. A tithe cutter claimed **Kace’s** cousin routed **undocumented tonnage** — marriage of **route rights** forged without **Maleventum** stamp.

— **Throne receipt** — the cutter’s officer demanded from the dock grill. Not angry. **Grey clerk piety** could be gentle as a blade.

Kace’s mother **Venn** answered from the shadow — **cable kin** wrists, RF burn maps on her forearms. — **Route marriage** files at **Ultima Arx**. You want **ledger**, not **blood**.

— I want **hash match**, — the officer said. — Or I seize **first weld** claim on your salvage barge.

That was war dressed as **inventory**.

Kace felt **witness debt** pinch — not to the Throne alone, to **cousins** who died earning **Reef** breathing room. He lifted the family **ground strap**, cold copper tongue, and clipped it to the dock rail — **ground rite** literal.

— **Listen**, — he said, switching to **bottle creole** because fear made honesty plain. — **Strait house** logs are **receive-only** archive. Pull the **cam hash** from **Station-Maleventum** — **delay saint** protocol — we don't **transmit** proof on open **vox**; we **laser** to **Marco** frame.

The officer paused — **silence theology** winning **seconds**.

— Show me **your** receipt, — Venn added softly. — **Seal** from **Castra-Vetera** routing office. If your cutter **hash** doesn’t match **Arx** mirror, **you’re** the divergent tonnage.

It was **ledger grace** played as **knife**.

When the cutter finally cycled engines — retreat as **amended paperwork** — Kace’s hands shook not from arc fear but from **relief**: **faith of work** on the Reef meant **correct procedure** stayed alive longer than **heroics**.

Venn touched his shoulder. — **Salt-in-the-ration**, boy. We eat **storms** same as **Khan-Bator** eats slime. **Emperor** loves **continuity**, not **noise**.

Lightning wrote a line across the Reef. Nobody cheered. **First weld** rights stayed **unstolen** — **Throne receipt** enough for one day.
