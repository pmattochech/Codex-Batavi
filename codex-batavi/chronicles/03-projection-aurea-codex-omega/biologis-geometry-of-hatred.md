The tiered response scale (depth of corruption × proximity to Horus Lupercal’s line), the formal distinction between *Blutrecht* and the **Amplified Axiom of Reason** (response to generic Chaos without recognizable gene-seed), and containment protocols by axis were formalized in the months following in the **Codex of *Das Erbe***—see the biological encyclopedia.

## I. THE ANOMALY OF RESPONSE (END OF THE PENDULUM)

Scientific arrogance is the first step toward tactical blindness. Until the fratricidal confrontation against Valerius’s traitor line, the Onyx Wolf, every predictive model in the *Apothecarion* operated under the simplistic comfort of Pendulum Theory.

We devoutly believed the gene-seed Cawl forged oscillated horizontally along a straight containment line between two poles of extreme survival:

1. **The left (*Ruhe*):** The lethargic automaton. The cold, logical response to Tyranid cosmic horror.  
2. **The right (*Jagd*):** The atavistic predator. The physical, hypertrophied response to Ork *Waaagh!* brute force.

Yet when we landed on Malakor V to face the betrayal of our own flesh — **traitor Astartes steeped in the arch-traitor’s cadence** — the biometric sensors embedded in the 1st Company’s breastplates registered a catastrophic divergence in telemetry. The graphs did not lean left or right.

My battle-brothers did not enter muscular frenzy (*Jagd*), nor did they shut down their limbic systems into radio silence (*Ruhe*). They kept Castra Vetera’s brutal tactical discipline, but neurochemistry specifically tied to retribution and focused hatred hit absurd vertical scales.

## II. THE GEOMETRIC DISCOVERY (ASCENT OF THE PYRAMID)

I personally monitored Alaric von Helis’s biometrics through a skull-servo as he walked Malakor V’s ashes to confront Valerius. In that instant the truth showed itself: the Batavi biological response vector was not oscillating laterally on a two-dimensional line. It was ascending.

**\[Chief Apothecary’s correction note\]** *"My secular diagnosis was fundamentally wrong. The *Batavian Iron-Guard* organism is not a horizontal line tied at two containment ends. *Das Erbe* is, in fact, a Pyramid of Genetic Response—a triangle of adaptive lethality.*

*When we face Chaos—and more specifically, the arch-traitor’s line or the sacrilege of Astartes heresy—our gene-seed does not seek the automaton’s balance or the beast’s blind slaughter. The biological vector moves inexorably toward the pyramid’s upper vertex. The **chimera** — **Strand β** foremost — does not process Valerius’s corruption as an ‘external enemy’ to fight; it processes it as a deep biological insult to be surgically purged. The result in our force’s minds is not chaotic madness, but terrible clarity, focused solely on lethal, sadistic execution of the target."*

## III. THE THIRD EXPONENT: THE TORMENT

Through this record I rectify the Chapter’s compendia. I officially classify this third and most dangerous state of vector ascent as ***Blutrecht*** (replacing the ineffective provisional designation “*Heavy Rains*”).

* **Psychological/genetic trigger:** Fratricidal betrayal, Astartes heresy, direct exposure to Horus Lupercal’s corrupted line.  
* **Vertex physiology:** Induction of hyper-sharpened sensory focus specifically for detecting tactical lies and moral weakness. Overall physical strength rises exponentially but, unlike *Jagd*, is controlled and directed by cold, calculated malice and cruelty. Under *Blutrecht*, the Astartes ceases to be line infantry and becomes an inquisitorial executioner.  
* **Observed tactical effect:** Ultra-efficient cruelty. Parsing the duel’s telemetry, Alaric did not fight Valerius to take ground or establish dominance. He fought to humiliatingly dismantle the traitor’s very existence. In *Blutrecht*, the warrior does not use the sword; he is the Judge used as blade by hatred itself.

## IV. THE LOGISTIC REBOUND (THE VOID OF PURPOSE)

The discovery of *Blutrecht* answered Malakor V’s anomaly but revealed the highest terminal cost embedded in our chimeric biology.

*Das Erbe* always collects its debt with entropic interest:

* If *Jagd* breaks the body in multi-organ failure…  
* If *Ruhe* freezes the mind in automation’s cold *loop*…  
* … *Blutrecht* fractures the **will to live** permanently.

At the end of Valerius’s brutal execution, when the singular object of infinite hatred was physically removed from the universe, the retribution vector sustaining the Lord Castellan collapsed at once. He reached the pyramid’s top and found nothing there but vacuum.

Lord Castellan Alaric von Helis suffered a vertiginous chemical crash and entered deep clinical transhuman depression. Without the gravitational focus of his hatred anchoring his mind, basal existence lost immediate meaning. He did not collapse to the floor in physical coma as under *Jagd*, but became a gray monolith of impenetrable grief.

During the Silent Vigil, the man who killed Valerius led this Chapter through duty alone, by pure grim mechanical inertia. A giant running on eternal depression’s autopilot.

**Medical and doctrinal conclusion:** *Das Erbe* is not a pendulum; it is a perfect triangle of survival and extreme lethality. But *Blutrecht*’s upper vertex is the most formidable trap for a *Batavi*’s soul. From this cycle onward, *Cohors Batavorum* operational continuity depends on never letting a battle-brother remain in *Blutrecht* too long without an external anchor (a lethal gravitational force / Point Zero) strong enough to haul him by the shoulders back toward the center of our humanity.

The seed gives us the weapon. But the price of the shot is almost always the sanity of whoever pulls the trigger.

*Instrumentum Solum.* — Drusus. Sealed in Castra Vetera’s vaults.
