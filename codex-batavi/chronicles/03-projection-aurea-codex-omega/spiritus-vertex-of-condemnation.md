## Prologue: The Lie We Preached

For decades Reclusiam doctrine preached a two-dimensional comfort.

We believed the Chapter’s spirit oscillated on horizontal line: **Wild Hunt** (Beast) on one side, **March of the Machine** (Machine) on the other. My function was to be **Anchor** — hold pendulum at center, at **Axiom of Reason**, while Drusus measured swing and Alaric bore blade weight.

I trained novices to fear sideways travel. I wrote litanies against fire and litanies against ice.

Malakor V proved the spirit does not move only sideways.

It **rises**.

Our soul is not line. It is **triangle**. It is **pyramid**.

1. **Left base — March:** Machine answer to cosmic horror.  
2. **Right base — Wild Hunt:** Beast answer to brute force.  
3. **Upper vertex — The Cull:** Headsman answer to **treason**.

I did not want a third direction. Theology prefers axes you can brace with both hands. The galaxy handed us a **height** instead.

---

## I. Before the Corridor

Intelligence was planted three weeks in advance. Alaric built the hook alone on the *Strategium* table where he had once built campaigns beside Valerius — same geometry, inverted intent.

I was not consulted on the bait. I was consulted on **containment**: III and V **Vexillationes** four hundred meters out, ordered not to advance regardless of sensor readings. Single combat by design. Two men who had mapped the same corridors for thirty years.

Drusus remained rear guard with auspex choir. I stood at auxiliary hinge where field sensors were deliberately blind — Chaplain as **witness**, not audience.

The theater had already taken **~300** names. Rot-linked anchors. Loyal dead. **Versibars** judged under Tier III correlation. *Prima*’s treason was not an ambush; it was **weather** the Chapter had to survive before sentence could be pronounced on the First Legatus.

When Versibar entered the access corridor — four and a half meters of taught doctrine turned profane — Alaric waited at **~2.85** meters of controlled intermediate lock. Instructor and student. Predator and predator. **Brother and error**.

I felt no *Waaagh!* pull. No hive drought. What I felt was **vertigo** — as if the *Reclusiam* floor had become staircase.

## II. The Judge’s Vector (Forty-Three Minutes)

Unlike Wild Hunt, **Cull** is not hot fury. It is cold absolute hate sharpened until it hums.

When we face traitor of our own lineage, gene-seed reads corruption not as external enemy but as **offense against cosmic order** requiring rectification. Astartes ceases to be soldier. Becomes **Judge**.

Valerius spoke with human clarity from lupine throat. Alaric answered in angles — stance adjustments, pressure geometry, forty-one minutes of closing options until kneeling was the only remaining proof.

— You planted the report — Valerius said.

Alaric did not answer.

— I knew it was a hook. I came anyway.

The beast fell back half a step, reassessing.

— Because even with a hook, you needed four tries.

I watched and understood what Drusus would later graph: **no lateral swing**. Ascent. Retribution chemistry climbing while discipline remained **laminar** — terrifying combination.

Valerius wanted the Beast. Wanted roar. Wanted proof that betrayal made him **more** wolf than law.

Alaric gave him **silence** instead.

When the forty-third minute came, Versibar knelt on oxidized metal — not from weakness, from geometry exhausted. Yellow slit eyes fully aware. No fear.

— You will carry this — Valerius said. — Three hundred names. Everything the First was.

Alaric raised force sword.

— You were carrying enough before me — he said. — I know the weight of the cord.

A pause.

— I do not regret it. You need to know that. Of nothing.

He struck.

No words before on open channel. No words after.

For eleven seconds the sector was not silent.

Auspex showed what officers would not name until Reclusiam gave it word: Castellan’s discrimination lattice failed. Quadrupedal gait. Thermochrome flush — industrial grey bleeding crimson along joints as if armor were catching up to Chapter red code. IFF collapse.

I entered from auxiliary hinge without *vox*.

I did not preach. I raised **Iron Verdict** and met mass with geometry — Sentence field inverted not to ash the Wall but to bite through plate and black carapace until servos screamed. Counter-strike. Bind. Retreat step. Again.

Alaric’s helm turned toward black armor. Half-second witnesses swear was not tactical.

Then crimson drained from plates. Castellan stood.

Four seconds silence.

— Sector clear — he keyed. — Biometric data collection authorized. Drusus’s team may advance.

Sentence executed. **Cull** satisfied. Vertex still occupied — for a moment — by man who had become guillotine.

## III. Cost of Ascension — Void of Purpose

**The Cull** exacts highest price: **Purpose**.

* Wild Hunt burns flesh.  
* March freezes empathy.  
* **Cull empties meaning of existence.**

In instant traitor died, hate-vector holding Alaric at pyramid peak **evaporated**. He did not collapse as after Skarjaw rebound. He walked toward exit while confirmation reports arrived — posture perfect, interior **vacuum**.

I watched from hinge shadow. Drusus watched from landing field. Neither approached. Some reboots require distance. Some collapses require **witness without touch**.

The man did not die in rubble of Malakor V. Something in him did.

What remained was **Iron Wall** — tool operating on mechanical inertia. He would lead Chapter through **Silent Vigil** century with duty Type III had engraved in marrow long before Malakor: anchor without asking interior permission.

That is not March *loop*. That is **grief wearing armor** because armor is the only language he still trusted.

## IV. Varro’s Word (Three Days Later)

Noviomagus Prime. Acid rain in northern logs — coincidence, not omen. Batavi learn not to discuss cause order aloud.

I gathered eight Chaplains in chamber with no open *vox* and spoke oath that would travel corridors as **Varro’s word**:

— The vertex is not sin — I said. — **Stuck** at vertex is sin.  
— The Headsman serves the Imperium once — I said. — The Headsman who lives in the scaffold becomes **hangman of the living**.  
— We will not climb without rope — I said. — Rope is mortal bond. Rope is pack we protect. Rope is name spoken by weak hand on pauldron.

Alaric was present.

He stayed silent while I spoke. When oath ended, he said nothing. Did not deny. Did not qualify. Did not bargain.

Drusus logged that silence as only moment he saw Castellan yield slice of authority without demanding condition in return.

I logged differently:

*He yielded because he had nothing left to bargain with.*

## V. Reclusiam Theological Verdict

**The Cull** makes us perfect traitor-hunters and leaves us **hollow**.

When we eliminate object of focused hate, we risk eliminating only thing that kept us lit. Pendulum Anchor could brace left and right. **Pyramid** demands Anchor learn **height** — or fall when apex empties.

My mantra of Reason fails against those who have **too much** reason. Alaric was perfectly logical that day: traitor dead, mission complete, therefore tool’s function ended. Mathematics without **Zero Point** is spiritual suicide.

I pray Chapter never climbs vertex again without human hand to pull us back.

Not necessarily Chaplain hand. Not necessarily Apothecary syringe. **Mortal** hand — cook-fire, metal cup of water, child’s cry through rock, name spoken wrong and still **recognized**.

Inventory rectification is lie men tell when they cannot admit brotherhood hurt. Alaric would confess that lie in 160.M42 addenda. On this date I file only what Reclusiam knows without confession:

*Justified hatred of traitor must be balanced millimeter-fine by love for what traitor threatens to destroy.*

Without counterweight, ascending to Cull is signing pact with vacuum.

---

## VI. Anchor’s Corollary (050.M42)

Wild Hunt debases to beast — beast can be leashed.  
March elevates to machine — machine can be rewarmed.  
**Cull elevates to Judge — Judge without sentence becomes nihilist.**

I am Anchor. My work was never only horizontal. It is **triangular** now:

* Brace the bases when fire or ice votes.  
* **Catch the ascent** when mirror reflects mirror.  
* Tie rope to **Zero Point** before climb begins — or spend century watching Castellan stand in rooms one second longer than presence should require.

Malakor V ended First Legatus story. It began **Silent Vigil** — not dome time trick, not warp rewind, **man** absent while still commanding.

Three hundred plates on cord that night. Not three hundred and one.

Drusus did not log missing plate. I did not ask why.

Some silences are **theology** too heavy for *Vermilion* ink.

*Instrumentum Solum.*

— Varro.
