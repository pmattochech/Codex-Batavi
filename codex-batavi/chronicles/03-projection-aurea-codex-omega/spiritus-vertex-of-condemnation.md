# LIBER SPIRITUS: THE VERTEX OF CONDEMNATION (THE STORM)

*(Theological record: 050.M42 | The Malakor V incident)*

***Classification:** Vermilion / Triumvirate eyes.*  
***Reporter:** Master Chaplain Varro (the Judex / the Anchor).*  
***Troop spiritual state:** *Blutrecht* (the headsman’s vertex).*

## I. GEOMETRY OF THE SOUL: THE PYRAMID

*For decades Reclusiam doctrine preached a two-dimensional lie. We believed the Chapter’s spirit oscillated on a horizontal line: *Jagd* (Beast) on one side, *Ruhe* (Machine) on the other. My function was to be the Anchor, holding the pendulum at the center, at the Axiom of Reason.*

*Malakor V proved the spirit does not move only sideways; it rises. Our soul is not a line; it is a triangle. It is a **pyramid**.*

1. ***Left base (*Ruhe*):** The Machine. Response to cosmic horror.*  
2. ***Right base (*Jagd*):** The Beast. Response to brute force.*  
3. ***Upper vertex (*Blutrecht*):** The Headsman. Response to treason.*

## II. THE JUDGE’S VECTOR (THE STORM)

*Unlike *Jagd*, *Blutrecht* is not hot fury. It is cold, absolute hate. When we face a traitor of our own lineage, the gene-seed reads corruption not as an external enemy but as an offense against cosmic order that must be rectified.*

*The Astartes ceases to be a soldier and becomes a **Judge**. There are no screams, no rage — only the physical need to dismantle the offense.*

***[Duel observation: Alaric vs. Valerius]** I watched Alaric advance with a guillotine’s silence. Valerius, the Onyx Wolf, meant to provoke the beast, wanted to hear Alaric roar. But the Lord Castellan made no sound. He dismantled the traitor with surgical, cruel efficiency. There was no glory — only the systematic pruning of a weed. When Alaric crushed Valerius’s skull, he did it with the same impassivity as stamping a bureaucratic form. Sentence executed.*

## III. COST OF ASCENSION: THE VOID OF PURPOSE

**Blutrecht* is the most dangerous state of our triad, for it exacts the highest price: **Purpose**.*

* *Jagd* burns the flesh.*  
* *Ruhe* freezes empathy.*  
* *Blutrecht* empties the meaning of existence.*

*In the instant the traitor died, the hate-vector that had held Alaric’s soul at the pyramid’s peak evaporated. He fell spiritually. The man died in the rubble of Malakor V; only the “Iron Wall” remained. He became a tool operating in vacuum, acting on pure mechanical inertia for centuries — until fate drew him back toward humanity.*

## IV. RECLUSIAM VERDICT

**Blutrecht* makes us perfect traitor-hunters but leaves us hollow. When we eliminate the object of our focused hate, we risk eliminating the only thing that kept us “lit.”*

***Varro’s note:** “My mantra of Reason fails against those who have too much reason. Alaric was perfectly logical that day: the traitor was dead, mission complete — therefore the tool’s function ended. I pray the Chapter never climbs the pyramid’s vertex again without a human hand to pull us back.”*
