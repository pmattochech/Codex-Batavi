## I. THE CASCADE

Sabotage did not shout. It **whispered** in manifold pressure curves and photonic relay harmonics until the forge’s own lungs tried to **cough** light.

On plate Seven, the glass sea below the arcology threw back a second sky — distorted, too bright, a lens the hive lived inside without knowing it was **focused**. When the cascade began, yield estimates — *Armourium* dialect, not poetry — rated the event **forgeship-killer** and **primary dome breacher** if allowed to run its course.

The Mechanicus screamed in binaric until the air itself seemed to **taste** like ozone and offended machine-spirits.

The *Strategium* answered with **VII** *Septima*.

---

## II. SHARPER THAN THE LENS

**Lucan Phaetron** pressed thumb to chestplate out of habit — not prayer — and felt the **charred lens fragment** on its chain bite through ceramite lining. Memento from an old collapse; **mnemonic** for what happened when vents lied.

His *vox* to the line dropped into the ritual silence his dossier would later call standard for overcharge windows.

— **The glass forgives nothing.** A beat — long enough for las-fusil spotters to breathe once. — **Be sharper than the lens.**

Ruin teams moved node to node along the manifold spine with plasma incinerators **underburning**. Each time a brother’s finger itched for **full** overcharge — for the hero shot that would **end** this faster — Phaetron’s channel carried the same law:

— **No breath without dual seal.** Legatus **and** Techmarine. Per **node**. If Kadmos’s priest argues, you **still** wait.

The Master of the Forge was not on plate Seven in body; his **rite** was. Somewhere in the chain, a Techmarine’s auspex ticked **authorized** while Phaetron ticked **human** — two signatures, one vent window, **no** shortcuts.

*Septima* had trained on Vitreus until **vent** was religion and **discipline** the only miracle. On Seven, religion met **accounting**: bleed the manifold into **vacuum ports** engineered for panic, not pride.

---

## III. MEAT AND SERVITOR

Suppression held the perimeter while cultists and **rampant** servitors threw themselves at grey plate as if volume could cool plasma by **mass**.

Bolters spoke in the failure-mode backbone *Septima* still trusted when photonics went mad. Phaetron did not mock the *Codex* for it. He **used** it — short bursts, assigned arcs, no tracer vanity that could blind a spotter’s glass.

Inside the burn lanes, thermal bloom turned skin into **fiction**. Apothecaries knelt where flesh peeled and did not scream because screams cost **air** the wounded still needed. Drusus’s distant annex would later file burns at this scale under **failure-mode data**, not glory — coefficients of what happened when a forge tried to become a **sun**.

---

## IV. SKITARII ASH

Allied skitarii died — number **redacted** in Chapter files, sealed under treaty ink with the Mechanicus. Their magi did not ask for public martyrology; they asked for a **spine** that could be **reclaimed**.

*Septima* lost **nineteen**.

Phaetron counted them the way he counted vent nodes: without flinching, without **spending** the count on rage. Rage vented wrong; *Septima* had buried brothers for that lesson long before plate Seven.

When the sky over the primary dome **did not** go white — when the city did not learn what a forgeship-killer looked like from the **inside** — a Magos spoke a word the lingua-technis translated roughly as **debt**.

Phaetron answered with a nod. *Quinta*, he thought with the grim humor he saved for off-line mess halls, would have paid the same afternoon in **jump fuel**. *Septima* paid it in **restraint**.

## V. OTHO’S NOISE BUDGET *(annex note)*

Later, when *Librarium* reviewed the plate’s auspex ghosts, **Otho**’s office noted odd null pockets in relay harmonics — **compatible** with sanctioned **Nullity** slots Phaetron had insisted on scheduling around sensitive forges. The Chapter does not print a battle hymn about it. It prints **no catastrophic vent chain** — the same victory *Strategium* had once cited when Phaetron was only a lieutenant holding a **lens dock** against Drukhari dark.

---

## VI. FIELD FOUR

The fourth field on **VII**’s *Alabarda* is not a beast slain. It is **heat** **caged** — Vitreus law on cloth: the hive does not die because a Marine **wanted** to look heroic on full overcharge.

**Null-Vent Covenant** is the name the register uses. The line remembers it as **breath** shared with a Techmarine’s tick — **two** signatures, **one** miracle, **zero** excuses.
