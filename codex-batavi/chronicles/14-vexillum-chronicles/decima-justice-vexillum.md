## I. NOT A TROPHY SKIN

The *Strategium* did not ask for a battle honour in the **II–IX** grammar.

No kill tally. No named xenos spire. The tenth lattice’s standard was to carry **sentence** the way other Vexillationes carried **triumph** — a difference the Administratum scribes learned to phrase as **separate battle standard** so tithe auditors did not file *Decima* beside Legatus logistics by mistake.

**Kadmos** laid the stencil on the adamantium bench like a surgeon laying a rib-spreader — respect without tenderness.

— **Wolf first** — he said. — **Not the seal wolf. The *other* one.**

The painter’s hand shook once. Shame, not fear — the human reflex to make monsters **sympathetic**. Varro’s *vox*, flat as a verdict, corrected without raising volume.

— **Sympathy is heresy here.** Not the Chaplain’s theater voice — **register** voice. — The feral thing is *treason’s body*. The blade is *Triumvirate will*. **We do not make it pretty. We make it legible across a kill-zone.**

---

## II. BLADE LAW

White gladius. Black contour. Echo of the Chapter seal’s law — not copy, **citation**.

The feral wolf — transhuman lupine mass, wrong in the joints, **not** the clean stencil novices salute — met the blade in the heraldic moment **after** impact: transfixed or slain, *Armourium* allowed either read so long as **dead** read from distance.

Secondary field: reduced icon. Tertiary: **X**, small, stamped where it would not compete. Quaternary: **three-dot** triangle — Wall, Scalpel, Judge — or grey band when paint failed at scale.

Kadmos signed heat tolerances for battle smokes. Varro signed **oath alignment**. Drusus’s name appeared only on the *Medicinae* margin — *Blutrecht* telemetry cross-file, **not** cloth aesthetics.

---

## III. THE HAFT

The brother chosen to drill carry was not yet **Markus Graile** — decades would pass before that captain’s file sat thick enough for *Decima* line face. The first name on the bearer log is **redacted** in public annex; corridor myth calls him only **the First Ancient**, which is both honor and **burden**.

He lifted the pole in the annex and felt weight unrelated to mass: **five hundred** pairs on the cord elsewhere in the fleet memory, **not** five hundred and one.

He did not ask why the count mattered. *Decima* learned early not to ask certain questions aloud.

---

## IV. CASTELLAN ESCORT

Alaric did not attend the painting. He did not need to see pigment dry to **own** what it meant.

When the justice vexillum first flew beside *Castra-Lupus* on a practice march, witnesses described the Castellan’s helm turning **once** — not toward the cloth, toward the **distance** where Malakor’s coordinates lived on every silent map.

No speech. *We hold* was not the word for that hour.

---

## V. LATER — THE LESSON

Years into the Silent Vigil, **Markus Graile** — *Decima* captain, median plate, plain gladius — would stand before loaned sergeants from **I–IX** and tap the justice charge with one knuckle.

— **I am not the Throne** — he would say, voice even so host Legati did not have to flinch on the net. — **I am the lesson they rehearse before the Throne moves.**

He did not claim to have stitched the wolf. He claimed to **carry** it without letting the Chapter mistake **cloth** for **absolution**.

---

## VI. REGISTER

The justice vexillum is **doctrine made cloth** — *Varro*’s oath and *Blutrecht* geometry in heraldic form. It is **not** interchangeable with *Alabarda* logistics; relay rules differ; the tenth flies **sentence**.
