## 1. Thesis

The **Valerian Heresy** is **domestic strife** that forges **Batavi identity** — not a minor traitor incident.

| Layer | Lock |
|-------|------|
| **Historical** | Chatti **seditione domestica** → Batavi downstream ([Tacitus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Batavi_%28Germanic_tribe%29)) |
| **Mood** | Heidevolk *Batavi* — *Een Nieuw Begin* (brother split → unity lost → people reborn downstream) |
| **40k** | Brother-war at **~5,000** strength; **Malakor** ends it; **vexilla** **after**; **Vigil** = who they **are** |

**Foundation War** supplies the **name** (*Der Batav* / **Cohors Batavorum**). **This war** supplies the **character** — island debt, wall vs tide, receipt culture.

## 2. Command geometry

### 2.1 Two powers (no vexilla)

Post-crucible (**~045.M42**), the mesh is too large for one spearhead. Chapter **bisects**:

| Command | Holder | Cheek (staff) |
|---------|--------|----------------|
| **Receipt / council / forge orbit** | **Alaric von Helis** | Central Bastion narrative; Castellan face |
| **Threshold / Chaos bleed / forward** | **Valerius** (First Legatus) | Worst sector; pyrric defense |

**No *Alabarda* vexilla** for PRIMA–NONA during this era — only **two hosts**, two logistics souls.

**Growth:** **~500** at crucible close → **~5,000** by **~080–085** (illegal scale **before** Malakor; collar + formal **~5,000** **after** Primaris).

### 2.2 Why two powers nearly broke the mesh

**~2,500** under one rebel almost **unmade Castra Vetera**. Post-heresy reform: **nine specialty Vexilla** + DECIMA — **fragment the hammer**. Future **Legati** = officers who **already led** squads in each theater specialty during the long war.

## 3. Fall architecture

### 3.1 Movement A — broken heart (credit)

| Audience | Sees |
|----------|------|
| Planet elites / parade | **Alaric** — Castellan, Execratio return, “king” |
| Valerius operations | Bleed, pyrric cost, door held |
| Alaric’s **private / annex** files | Trust, credit, hardest sector = **honor** |

Valerius need **not** be wrong about pain; he need be **wrong about remedy**.

**Arc:** love/trust → **grief** (invisible bleed) → **resentment** (warrior became politician) → **hate** (stole **my** future / the succession the mesh already whispers).

### 3.2 Movement B — **why?** (not possession)

**Lock:** Warp taint does **not** rewrite perception or loyalty. It asks **one question** in many shapes:

- Why **him**? Why **my** men? Why **kings and senators** while we bleed?
- Why don’t they **see**? Why **hate** me? Why **abandon** us here?

**Artifact / Word Bearer link** (author scaffold): plants the question; decades of will **answer alone**.

**Chronology blindness:** early years = wrong relic, dreams, static — **not** “Word Bearers did it” in briefing.

### 3.3 Final seal (unnamed Horus)

**Once**, at ascension / open declaration — **not** battle chatter:

— I see it now. It all makes sense. That is why **he** did it. Now I see why. Now I know why.  
— That is also why **I** have to do it. And why I will **succeed** — unlike him.

### 3.4 Endgame theology (Valerius POV)

| Act | His read |
|-----|----------|
| Kill Alaric’s marines | **Harvest** progenoids — parallel stock, “pure” |
| Ritual / sacrifice / ascension | **Purify** Chapter; **free** Castra Vetera (mortals + Astartes) from Imperium that **abandoned** the island |
| **Versibar + Daemon Prince** | **Last years only** — lucid crown of his answers |

**Loyalist read:** gene-theft, warp cult, ultimate treason.

### 3.5 Followership

**Most** of Valerius’s host follows — shared theater, shared **answers**.  
**Corbec Ardens** + **small lawful core** = **internal resistance** (witness lattice for Reading B / Malakor).

## 4. Apex scene (Malakor — dialogue lock)

**Valerius:**

— Why?

**Alaric** (sparse; truthful — **pack**, not throne):

— Because we hold the line. We held it before. We hold it now. **We are the wall that stays.** You are the tide that breaks against it — and fails.

| | Alaric | Valerius |
|---|--------|----------|
| **Self** | Stone in the **pack** | **Above** pack / mesh — surgeon, liberator |
| **Metaphor** | Wall | Tide / river |

No conversion beat. Recognition that **language is exhausted** → execution / terminis.

**Optional micro-beat after:** Valerius one line (*You still think you are stone*) — **or** silence only.
