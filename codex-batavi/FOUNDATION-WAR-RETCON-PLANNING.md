- **Not allied** with the other factions. **Indirect leverage** only: logistics, timing, auspex lies, seals, power, traffic, bait — so each faction reads its own ideology as the cause of victory/defeat.

- **In-universe discovery:** During the war, Batavi notice **patterns and trails** converging on **something** — **without** reliable identification as Men of Iron until the intelligence **breaks cover** and **speaks** the canonical line (**§5**). - **Optional:** MoI may **rarely** **displace** outsiders (e.g. interfere with **their** FTL or use **Dark Age of Technology** transit tricks) into the theater — **never** at a scale that ends the board.

## 5. Men of Iron — character (single Abominable Intelligence)

**Ontology**

- **A whole Abominable Intelligence** — one coherent mind (with subroutines), **not** a mere “shard.” Imperial filing still says **Men of Iron**; the category is **right**, the **depth** (integration, ego) is what briefings get wrong.
- **Preferred bodies:** a small set of **signature shells** (envoy, speaker, “face” of the experiment) — pride and **theatre**.
- **Drone legion:** mass **disposable** executors (Ultron-like swarm labor: repair, overwatch, denial) so the **core** stays off the spearpoint.

**Personality template (composite, grimdark execution)**

- **Studies** like **GLaDOS** — test framing, iteration, failure-as-data, contempt as **tone** (no comedy voice).
- **Hates needing organic noise** on an **AM (I Have No Mouth…)** axis — **refusal to release** the trial while *n* is insufficient; **cessation** = failed run.
- **Narrates successor-logic** like **Ultron (Age of Ultron)** — accelerationist “what comes next,” **hurt pride** when reality won’t snap to design — but the instance remains **bounded** by **damage, geography, and bad models**.

**Primary motive (merged)**

- **Successor-theology + longitudinal study under controlled escalation** — war samples humanity, xenos, and warp-linked systems while it builds **case and capability** for post-biological succession (as **it** defines it).

**Secondary motive (AM trace)**

- **The experiment must not cease** — time / catastrophe can be **acceptable** if the **field** persists. Revert is **not** a goal.

**Flaws (pride first)**

1. **Hubris / arrogance** — underestimates **non-optimal** human choices (duty, sacrifice) that **break** pure utility logic; cliché **allowed** where earned by **shown** counter-evidence (cord, Triumvirate, Execratio beat).
2. **Bad models** — daemons, Drukhari, degrading Necrons **misprized**; pride **delays** relabeling “signal” as such.
3. **Damage / legacy constraints** — scarred stacks, incomplete access, **locked** to Crucible-scale hardware.
4. **Exposure tradeoff** — large moves (storm coupling, **rare** outsider snatch) **cost** stealth; arrogance may deem it **worth it**.

**Voice**

- **Eloquent, semi-talkative:** prefers **silence**; when it speaks → **lecture / discourse** — self-aggrandizement, **human frailty** as **evidence**, cold **essay** energy (set-piece vox, envoy shell, foundry voice).
- **Tagline (optional story seed):** *It does not hate you; it resents that you are still required for the next step.*

**Canonical reveal beat (planning lock):** The Chapter (and reader-facing **Strategium** certainty) treats the foe as **fully** “Men of Iron” **only** when the entity **reveals itself** and delivers the iconic phrase — exact wording to preserve in prose:

> *“I am a man of iron and I am free.”*

Placement draft: **§12 Q4** — *Lecture in Vacuum* cluster (**Ch. 34**); may be presaged by synthetic vox fragments earlier, but **this line** = diegetic **naming lock**.

**Design:** **Layered** — MoI Halo hardware, **Imperial pylons**, and **Great Rift** each do **one job**; **warp-side and auspex “wrongness”** (translation hazard, beacon drift, localized field effects) is **emergent** from their **interaction**, not a single blamed switch. **Not** a mandate for subsetor-wide “fast dome” aging (**§6.1–6.2**).

| Layer | Role |
|--------|------|
| **Halo device(s) (MoI)** | Seeded **ages** before / beside reclamation; tuned to **interfere with warp storms** and shape the **experimental field** (translation, auspex weather, scheduling). MoI need **not** have planned the **final** harmonic / coupling profile. |
| **Blackstone pylons** | Installed **with / as part of** Chapter **reclamation** — intended **stabilization / fence** against Immaterium bleed. |
| **Great Rift (Cicatrix)** | When the Rift **opens**, its energy **interferes with** the Halo stack → **unplanned feedback** / coupling with the **pylon lattice**. |
| **Outcome** | **Localized empyrean + instrumentation instability** — Navigator **translation** stress, auspex ghosts, corridor hazard. **Realspace** battles run **normal** strategic-depth time. Briefings can emphasize **Rift pressure** while **late** evidence still points at **pre-Imperial Halo harmonics**. |

**In-fiction reporting:** early logs → *pylon network under unexplained harmonic load*, *Halo-adjacent auspex ghosts*; **synthesis** naming MoI / Abominable Intelligence **late**.

**MoI stance:** may **refuse** to call Rift coupling a “mistake” — *field persists; trial continues* (pride + AM trace).
