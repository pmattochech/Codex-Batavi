## 1\. COHORTS AND VEXILLATIONES (GRID MODEL)

The **four specialty cohorts** are not units “apart” from the Chapter: in **each** Vexillatio there is an **organic contingent of fifty** warriors **per** cohort (4 × 50 = **200**). The remainder of the **500** ceiling is **pure line** (**284** in Vex. I–IX; **279** in X), **organic support** (**16**: 4 Apothecaries, 4 Techmarines, 3 adjutant Chaplains, 5 **Batavian Cipher** Librarians), and, **only in the Tenth**, **five** supreme posts — see [vexillationes-and-visual-summary.md](vexillationes-and-visual-summary.md) §1.1.

**Instruction:** veterans of the **10th Vexillatio** (*anchor guard*) rotate as **certified instructors** through the other nine Vexillationes, so each fifty-Marine detachment reproduces the same tactical *standard* (Silence, Shadows, Stasis, Nullity) without doctrinal fragmentation.

**Path to the Tenth:** time in line + sequential service in all four specialty detachments + extreme merit + **Batav Wolf trial** (see [military-hierarchy.md](../personae-command-index/doctrine-and-organs/military-hierarchy.md) §3). ## 3\. SILENCE COHORT — DOCTRINE OF PATTERN AND ANOMALY

The Silence Cohort is **not** reduced to *cogitator* analysts in bunkers. The *standard* demands **presence in the urban mesh**: patrols in service armor or discreet *aquila*, crossing worker districts, docks, ration markets, and service tunnels. Data feeds remain tools; the transhuman body is the **sensor package** against disguises the archive never predicted.

**Core specialty:** **patterns** — what a mortal in *this* shift, *this* wharf, *this* forge collectively “usually is” (gait, speech, smell, sound, gaze). The Cohort trains **anomaly detection on the pattern**, not a single trick. Scent is **one** channel; at the same tier come augmented hearing for **body sounds** (breathing, swallowing, micro-noise of joints and muscle under load), **gait biomechanics** (real weight versus played role, symmetry, recovery from imbalance), **prosody and lexicon** (rhythm, natural hesitations, phrases “too correct”), and even **where the target looks** — fixation order, gaze duration, saccades that lead or lag expected social gesture. All feed one model: local *baseline* versus deviation.

**I. Chemoreception (*A veil among many*)**  
Astartes biology, calibrated for decades to the same industrial atmosphere, accumulates a Noviomagus olfactory *baseline*: soot, burnt oil, human sweat mixed with forge detergents, lactates, discharge ozone. An infiltrator — even with flawless papers — carries **wrong volatile matrices**: soaps from another world, pheromones “too clean,” absence of cutaneous micro-ferment expected after an eighteen-hour shift, or **hybrid** chemical note (sweet-metallic, disguised ammonia) under civil cover. The ivory Batavi **crosses** chemical signal with the other channels — never isolates the nose as sole proof.

**II. *Nothing is too perfect* (Alpha Legion and hyper-correction)**  
The Alpha Legion prizes disguise **flawless** by manual: posture, speech cadence, work ritual, and stress reactions aligned with what enemy doctrine judges “ideal human.” A real mortal hesitates, stammers, looks away, tires asymmetrically; the double **plays the part without noise** — and fails there. Silence Cohort **validates perfection**: when the whole set is **too coherent** — step, voice, gaze, and silences all “rehearsed” — the deviation **is** the perfection. They hunt the “recorded video”: blinking at regular intervals, mirrored gestures from a model, absence of micro-asymmetries a native body accumulates over years of shifts.

**III. Larval dissonance (Genestealer / cult)**  
Another anomaly class: movement almost right but **out of phase on transition** (shoulder, neck, gaze anticipating social gesture by one too many milliseconds), or internal sounds and cadence that do not match the civil mask. The disguise may be socially acceptable; the trained Batavi reads **error in the integrated pattern** — not one tic, but the sum.

**IV. Combined employment**  
Pattern analysis **does not replace** *Lex Imperialis* or custody for interrogation: it **selects targets**, isolates cells, and avoids mass false positives. Where the threat is purely psychic or hive-scale fleet, Chapter biology and other cohorts take different vectors; where the enemy **mimics the Imperium**, ivory is the first **triage barrier**.
