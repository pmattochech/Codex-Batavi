# Frontier patterns — Primaris-grade *Armourium* designations

*Noviomagus and allied forges certify **frontier patterns**: standard Imperial weapon **classes** (bolt rifle, plasma incinerator, meltagun, etc.) passed through environmental trials named for **Nine Phalanx** anchors. The name marks **maintenance schedule**, **seal chemistry**, **sight calibration**, and **expected failure mode** — not a different STC core.*

---

## 1. Naming rule

**`[World]-pattern [Imperial class]`**

Examples: *Noviomagus-pattern bolt rifle*, *Vitreus-pattern plasma incinerator*, *Incus-pattern heavy bolt rifle*.

- **World** = Legatus seat or proving ground that signed the acceptance trial.  
- **Class** = common Adeptus Astartes / Primaris designation (American English in prose; game terms stay GW-canonical where used).

A Marine may carry a **Khan-Bator-pattern flamer** while serving in Vex. II — logistics attached that weapon to the cell; theater bias is **modal**, not tattooed.

## 2. Pattern families by anchor (cross-reference)

| Anchor | Typical pattern families | Notes |
|--------|--------------------------|--------|
| **Noviomagus Prime** | Bolt rifle, bolt pistol, storm bolter / boltstorm derivatives, chainsword, power gladius | **Reference standard**; other patterns measured against it. |
| **Castra-Vetera Prime** | Stalker bolt rifle, bolt rifle (marksman configuration), grav-gun | Emphasis on **controlled delivery** and optics. |
| **Khan-Bator Prime** | Flamer, hand flamer, pyreblaster-class, meltagun, heavy bolt pistol | **Thermal** and **short bore** packages. |
| **Incus-Gravis** | Heavy bolt rifle, missile launcher (tactical), meltagun, thunder hammer, power fist | **High-G** shock mounting, reinforced stocks. |
| **Aethelgard Prime** | Assault bolt rifle / boltstorm (jump-rated), heavy bolt pistol, chainsword, power axe | **Descent** and **lash-down** certification. |
| **Bifrons-Ferrum** | Plasma cannon (heavy), storm shield interfaces, power fist, cyclone missile mounts | **Siege** and **Indomitor** integration. |
| **Vitreus** | Plasma incinerator, plasma pistol, las fusil | **Heat bleed** and lens-safe venting. |
| **Maleventum / Reef** | Bolt carbine, combat shield pairing, power sword | **EM-hardened** auspex and tritium bias. |
| **Marco de Vetra** | Boltstorm / sustained bolt packages, heavy bolter (tactical mount), missile launcher (tactical), melta, bolt carbine | **Relay-holder** maintenance: heat cycling for long bursts, **demo-safe** fusing, **laser-window** EM hygiene. |
| **Singularity-Point** (Cerberus watch; not Legatus seat) | Instigator bolt carbine, melta rifle, neo-volkite caliver | **Rift-adjacent** trials; **low-volume** batches; strict accountability; issued to *Nona* rotations and specialist cells. |

*Which Vexillatio leans on which family:* [vexillatio-operational-armory.md](vexillatio-operational-armory.md) §3.

## 3. Melee taxonomy (Chapter convention)

- **Power gladius:** default **line** thrust weapon aligned with *Second Blade* doctrine ([friction-geometry.md](../personae-command-index/doctrine-and-organs/friction-geometry.md)).  
- **Chainsword:** **saw mass** — Ruin clearance, assault landings, Shadow cohort work.  
- **Power axe / power sword:** role- and theater-assigned; Vex. V and VIII modal.  
- **Thunder hammer / power fist:** veteran sergeants, Ruin, Siege, and Vex. VI heavy cells.
