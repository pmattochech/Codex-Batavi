# Vexilla operational armory (theater bias)

*Strategium register, ca. 570.M42 — how each Vexilla **leans** in weapons and chassis, without surrendering general-purpose capability.*

---

## 1. Doctrine (read this first)

**Any Vexilla can fight any war.** Castra Vetera’s *Friction Geometry* forbids single-axis chapters: a forge-world garrison may be ordered to the Threshold tomorrow. What follows is **forging bias**, **range certification**, and **modal loadout** — not a ban list.

**Theater-driven:** Local threat, logistics spine, and *Armourium* output at the Legatus seat shape **proportions**. A Vexilla famous for plasma still fields bolters; it simply **certifies** more plasma gunners and **stocks** more fusion cells on its barges.

**Cohort lock:** The four fifty-Marine cohorts (Silence, Shadows, Stasis, Nullity) are **the same jobs in every Vexilla**. **Shadows operate under Silence** for lattice orders and mesh pricing; black pauldrons execute what ivory (or the Legatus relay) has already named. Cohort **weapon patterns** stay Chapter-standard — **Shadows** melee is **knife → gladius**, not chainsword — see [cohort-standard-kit.md](cohort-standard-kit.md). Only **pure-line** melee bias shifts by theater (notably **chainsword-primary** on **QVINTA**).

**Legatus *Alabarda* head:** Each Vexilla’s **pole head** (and NONA **grav-lock** haft modes) is **theater-certified** — *Types* **Primus**–**Fulmen**: [alabarda-head-groups.md](alabarda-head-groups.md).

**Pure line** (Suppression / Ruin / Siege) is where **Vexilla character** shows in volume: extra jump seats, extra heavy chassis bays, or extra thermal weapons on the manifest.

**Named patterns:** Frontier world names denote *Armourium* acceptance trials and maintenance packages on **Primaris-scale** weapons — see [frontier-patterns-primaris.md](frontier-patterns-primaris.md).

## 3. Operational proficiency and modal armory

**Modal share** is illustrative: roughly **what extra tickets** appear on a typical embarkation slate, not a formula etched in adamantium.

### PRIMA — Noviomagus Prime)

**Proficiency:** Baseline **industrial war** — the Chapter’s reference tempo for ammunition economy and line replacement.

**Modal lean:** **Bolt weapons** (rifle, pistol, storm/boltstorm-family where issued), **chainswords**, **power gladii**. Highest **sustained automatic fire** culture; *Armourium* treats other Vexilla’ oddities as deviations from **Noviomagus-pattern** acceptance.

**Pure line:** Suppression-heavy roster; Ruin and Siege maintained at table strength without exaggeration.

**PRIMA Legatus seat — seat vs deployment (canonical A + B):**

- **045–050.M42 (First Legatus era):** The **500-cell block** was always **PRIMA**; the **Legatus** exercised command from **forward campaign anchors** in the **Threshold / Cicatrix Maledictum–adjacent** theater — stations, reef-adjacent bodies, and **Malakor V**–class kill-boxes — not from Noviomagus Prime. The Chapter’s **industrial spine and tithe heart** remained the **Central Bastion** (Noviomagus mesh). *Strategium* treated that as **deployment geometry**: worst sector, best operator — without confusing **forge law** with **where the First stood to shoot**.
- **Post–050.M42 (*Strategium* re-seat):** After the **Malakor V** execution and **PRIMA** losses, the Triumvirate **declared** PRIMA’s **registered *Legatus* primary anchor** **Noviomagus Prime** — **logistics** (reference *Armourium*, line-replacement tempo), **tithe neatness**, and **symbolic severance** from the traitor’s forward map. **Rift-lip habit** and relay sacrifice geometry devolve chiefly to **VIII (OCTAVA)** and **IX (NONA)** in the modern register; PRIMA **still deploys** anywhere the mesh orders. *See* [dossier-valerius.md](../personae-command-index/character-dossiers/expunged/dossier-valerius.md) §IV; [master-chronology.md](../personae-command-index/events-and-chronologies/master-chronology.md) §III.
- **PRIMA *Alabarda* vexillum:** fourth panel field = **obligation / remembrance** (lattice **debt**, not successor’s personal guilt — §2.2), **broken-cog** border; full nine-grid register [vexilla-by-vexilla-design.md](../personae-command-index/intro-and-heraldry/vexilla-by-vexilla-design.md).

### SECVNDA — SECVNDA (Castra-Vetera Prime)

**Proficiency:** **Measured fire** — corridors, crowds, and legally sensitive kills (minimize stray mass reactive).

**Modal lean:** **Bolt rifles** with magnified optics, **stalker-pattern** long barrels, **grav-guns** as designated support. Fewer indiscriminate area tools; more **assigned target** packets.

**Pure line:** Suppression cells skew toward **disciplined semi-auto** and **covering angles** over volume saturation.

**Castellan sustainment (Chapter law):** SECVNDA holds ledger authority for **Castra-Vetera armory vaults** — repair, refit, pattern fidelity, and sealed TDA libraries including ***Depositorium Cataphractae*** (Cataphractii-pattern reserve; first draw for ***Castra-Lupus — Helis*** post–Execratio). Noviomagus forges **birth** new plate; **Castra-Vetera Prime** **keeps** what must not be lost — predecessor *Castra-Lupus* configurations archived here, not reissued. [`castra-lupus-doctrine.md`](castra-lupus-doctrine.md) §4.

---

### TERTIA — TERTIA (Khan-Bator Prime)

**Proficiency:** **Agri-industrial hazard** — silos, chemical moons, monoculture bunkers, oxygen-rich fires.

**Modal lean:** **Flamers**, **meltaguns**, **pyreblaster**-class Primaris burners, **heavy bolt pistols** for tight work. Sealing and **chem-resistant** O2 protocols tie to armor more than to a single gun, but **thermal breach** weapons dominate the armory queue.

**Pure line:** Ruin division enjoys **short-range fusion** and **tank-melta** drills; Suppression carries more **hand flamers** than the Chapter median.

---

### QVARTA — QVARTA (Incus-Gravis)

**Proficiency:** **High-gravity static war** — shingle seas, inverted hives, trench lines that do not forgive movement.

**Modal lean:** **Heavy bolt rifles**, **meltaguns**, **missile launchers** (tactical), **power fists** and **thunder hammers** on veteran sergeants. Anchoring drills pair with **gravitic boot doctrine** (see [noviomagus-standard-engineering.md](noviomagus-standard-engineering.md)).

**Pure line:** **Siege** and **Ruin** skew upward: more portable heavy weapons, more **storm shield** pairs in locked formations.

---

### QVINTA — QVINTA (Aethelgard Prime)

**Proficiency:** **Storm assault** — tide moons, gas-giant theaters, vertical infrastructure, **jump** certification. Foundation crucible memory and **Wild Hunt**–class closure when enemy mass or Waaagh! resonance spikes endocrine assault tempo ([projection-aurea-wolfs-curse.md](../biological-encyclopedia-bestiary/projection-aurea-wolfs-curse.md)).

**Modal lean:** **Highest Chapter allocation** of **jump packs** and **assault Primaris** formations (Inceptor-class deployment where *Armourium* certifies). **Boltstorm** and **heavy bolt pistols** on descent; **power axes** where the strike needs mass behind the edge.

**Melee (pure line — Chapter exception):** On QVINTA only, **chainsword** is the **primary melee weapon** for line and assault cells — saw-mass brutality after landing, not the Noviomagus-default **power gladius** rhythm. The mesh reads it as **Aethelgard’s nature** made armory policy: vertical hunt, tide pressure, the same visceral closure *Der Batav* myth trained into the cells. **Shadows** in QVINTA still field **knife → gladius** under **Silence**; the chainsword culture is **grey pauldron / jump-line**, not black.

**Pure line:** Suppression trains **rapid closure**; Ruin carries **melta** for hull touches after landing.

---

### SEXTA — SEXTA (Bifrons-Ferrum)

**Proficiency:** **Siege and vault war** — dual-world gulf fortifications, stasis arsenals, Dreadnought-adjacent logistics.

**Modal lean:** **Largest standing authorization** for **Tactical Dreadnought Armour** squads and **Gravis** siege cadres (Aggressor / Eradicator-class patterns). **Storm shields**, **plasma cannons** on heavy chassis, **power fists**, **cyclone** and missile support on approved teams. Not every Marine is in plate; the Vexilla simply **owns** the Chapter’s densest **Indomitor** pool.

**Pure line:** Siege division is the **cultural core** of the Vexilla; Suppression exists but trains **breach escort** more than open skirmish.

---

### SEPTIMA — SEPTIMA (Vitreus)

**Proficiency:** **Thermal and photonic stress** — glass seas, lens forges, environments that punish careless plasma venting (so Batavi train **harder** there).

**Modal lean:** **Plasma incinerators**, **plasma pistols**, **las fusils** (marksman cells), **neo-volkite** where Mechanicus contract allows. Energy weapons are **modal**, not exclusive; bolters remain the failure-mode backbone.

**Pure line:** Ruin division overlaps with **plasma AT**; Suppression uses **overcharged** shots under strict *Armourium* ritual.

---

### OCTAVA — OCTAVA (Great Reef / Station-Maleventum)

**Proficiency:** **Void fight** — boarding, station assaults, reef transit under ion scourge.

**Modal lean:** **Combat shields**, **power swords**, **bolt carbines** / **compact bolters**, **grav-chutes**, **melta** for bulkhead work. Sights and auspex filtered for **Maleventum**-class EM noise; spare **void** seals on magazines.

**Pure line:** Suppression specializes **corridor interlock**; Ruin carries **breaching charges** and **hull melta** as a norm, not an exception.

---

### NONA — NONA (Marco de Vetra)

**Proficiency:** **Relay survival and sector cry** — the Legatus seat is the **bait-moon** whose strategic purpose is **hyper-focused laser** traffic to the Wall and, if the Threshold must be bought with blood, the **mobilization shout** that wakes the entire Chapter. Tactics skew to **layered delay**, **perimeter fixation**, and **denial** of capture-intact antenna and reactor spines — Friction Geometry where **time** and **signal** matter more than local kill ratios.

**Modal lean:** **Sustained kinetic** to **pin** assault waves (**boltstorm**-family, **heavy bolter** teams where the *Armourium* assigns them), **tactical missile** for clustered approaches, **melta** and **demo charges** for bulkheads and **scorched** couplings. **Bolt carbines** and **combat shields** for tight industrial alleys between array farms. **EM budget** discipline so friendly noise does not drown the **Shouter** when it must punch through Maleventum-class hash.

**Pure line:** Suppression trains **interlocking sustained fire** around critical sites; Ruin holds **melta** and **breach denial** as default; Siege owns **storm shield** locks on reactor and laser vault approaches.

**Subordinate watch (not the Legatus seat):** **Singularity-Point** and Block Station **Cerberus** remain Threshold strategic assets; NONA rotates **Cerberus detachments** and certifies **rift-adjacent** small arms under **Singularity-Point** trial protocols (*instigator* carbines, **neo-volkite** where stocked) — see [frontier-patterns-primaris.md](frontier-patterns-primaris.md).

---

### DECIMA (anchor guard)

**Proficiency:** **No single theater** — Triumvirate escort, *Strategium* reserve, **instructor** certification for cohort modules across PRIMA–NONA.

**Modal lean:** **Balanced manifest** — every pattern appears at **median** Chapter proportions so veterans model **standard** behavior. Access to **elite guard** chassis pools (*Castra-Lupus* cohort and immediate escort — see [vexilla-and-visual-summary.md](vexilla-and-visual-summary.md) §4) sits here, not in PRIMA–NONA.

**Pure line:** Deliberately rotated through **all** division billets to preserve teaching credibility.
