# Cohort standard kit (Silence, Shadows, Stasis, Nullity)

*The four specialty cohorts are **job positions** replicated inside **every** Vexillatio (50 Marines each). Their **weapon doctrine** is **Chapter-wide**: the same role gets the same tool mix on Noviomagus as on **Marco de Vetra**. **Vexillatio** only changes **which frontier pattern** is stamped on a given bolter — see [frontier-patterns-primaris.md](frontier-patterns-primaris.md).*

**Minor alterations** below mean: **support weapon**, **sidearm**, **accessory load**, or **maintenance package** — not a different tactical identity per Vexillatio.

---

## 1. Silence Cohort (counter-intelligence)

**Mission set:** Urban mesh presence, pattern-and-anomaly detection, custody handoff — not replacing Arbites or *Lex Imperialis*.

**Standard long arm:** **Bolt carbine** or **collapsible-stock bolt rifle** (same ballistic family as parent line; compact for vehicles and crowds).

**Sidearm:** **Bolt pistol**; some cells **heavy bolt pistol** for deterrent shots without rifle presentation.

**Melee:** **Combat knife** standard; **power gladius** on file leaders.

**Support / accessories:** **Concussion grenades**, **restraint webbing**, **portable auspex**, **vox-scrambler** pouches. Non-lethal loads are **modal** by *Strategium* rules of engagement, not by Vexillatio.

**Doctrinal note:** Patrol dress may be **service armor** or discreet **aquila** — see [specialty-cohorts.md](specialty-cohorts.md) §3. Weaponry still maps to the table above when armor is full Astartes plate.

---

## 2. Shadows Cohort (infiltration)

**Mission set:** Labyrinth war, ducts, kill-box preparation, blind or low-light entry.

**Standard long arm:** **Bolt rifle** with **suppressor** configuration where mission allows; **stalker-pattern** barrels for overwatch pairs.

**Sidearm:** **Bolt pistol**; **heavy bolt pistol** for breaching teams.

**Melee:** **Chainsword** primary; **combat knife** silent work.

**Support / accessories:** **Smoke**, **photo-absorbent** cloak mounts, **grav-chute** or **rappel** kit by operation. **Melta** charges for sealed hatches — small issue count, **same doctrine** everywhere.

---

## 3. Stasis Cohort (blocking and defense)

**Mission set:** Hold geometry, corridor seal, **testudo** discipline, counter-assault.

**Standard long arm:** **Bolt rifle** (full length for bayonet / lock-step).

**Shield:** **Storm shield** or **combat shield** by assignment; **scuta** formation sets drawn from the same pool as pure-line Siege ([friction-geometry.md](../personae-command-index/doctrine-and-organs/friction-geometry.md) §2).

**Melee:** **Power gladius** or **power maul** — Chapter table; maul for shock, gladius for thrust finish.

**Sidearm:** **Bolt pistol** mag-locked for **shield wall** reload discipline.

**Support / accessories:** Extra **magazine** bandoliers, **portable barrier** pins compatible with gravitic boots.

---

## 4. Nullity Cohort (anti-psychic operations)

**Mission set:** Blank-adjacent tactics, **Librarium** liaison, witch-mark interdiction **without** replacing Codex Librarius battlefield role.

**Standard long arm:** **Bolt rifle**; marksman pairs may field **las fusil** or **stalker bolt rifle** under *Armourium* seal (same as Chapter marksman doctrine elsewhere).

**Melee:** **Power gladius**; **null rod** (or sanctioned equivalent) carried by designated **Oblivion** cells — count **low**, training **high**.

**Sidearm:** **Bolt pistol**.

**Attached *Librarium*:** Each Vexillatio embeds **Batavian Cipher** Librarians (five per 500); they are **not** cohort headcount but **liaison** to Nullity. Their weapons follow [council-and-librarium.md](../personae-command-index/doctrine-and-organs/council-and-librarium.md) and order policy — **force stave**, **bolt pistol**, etc.

**Support / accessories:** **Psy-occlusion** warding kits, **cognitive hazard** beacons, vox codes tied to Nullity–Cipher protocols.

---

## 5. Uniformity summary

| Cohort | Long arm (modal) | Melee (modal) | Typical extras |
|--------|------------------|---------------|----------------|
| **Silence** | Compact bolt / carbine | Knife → gladius | Auspex, concussion, restraints |
| **Shadows** | Suppressed / stalker bolt | Chainsword | Smoke, melta charge, grav-chute |
| **Stasis** | Bolt rifle + shield | Gladius / maul | Scuta pins, extra mags |
| **Nullity** | Bolt rifle (marksman variants possible) | Gladius + null rod cells | Cipher liaison, warding |

**Vexillatio-level bias** (plasma-heavy VII, jump-heavy V, etc.) applies chiefly to **pure line** and **embarked heavy** pools — [vexillatio-operational-armory.md](vexillatio-operational-armory.md).
