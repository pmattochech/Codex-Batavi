## Core creed (region-wide)

### Faith of work

Working term for **orthodox Castra Vetera piety**: the Emperor is **honored first** through **completed labor**, **logged output**, and **maintained infrastructure** — not through eloquence alone. Competes with **Ministorum** ecstatic modes without abolishing them: *“The sermon ends; the shift does not.”*

### Shift orthodoxy

Correct **completion** of a duty cycle — timing, torque, tally — treated as **moral** correctness. Heresy can be filed as **gear negligence** or **unlogged scrap** as easily as **doctrine deviation**.

### Throne receipt

Any **Administratum-admissible proof** of useful output: stamped manifests, birth registrations to quota, repair tickets, **seal hashes**. In vulgar speech: *“Show me your Throne receipt”* means **prove you paid the species forward**.

## Administrative face (grey-clerk piety)

### Grey clerk

Low Gothic **tag** for adepts whose **virtue looks like paperwork** — translucent slickers, portable slates, pink slips — **not** a formal rank. Overlaps **faith of work** when **correct seals** save lives; **collides** with tide, **spark law**, or slime bloom when **schedules lie**. Political texture: [grey-clerk-piety.md](../political-relations/grey-clerk-piety.md).

## Silence, signal, and Men-of-Iron hangover

### Listen vow

Oath **not** to transmit on open channels during watch — originally **military–Mechanicus** discipline after **automation** and **cognito-hazard** scares; generalized into **civilian** virtue on worlds where **broadcast sin** is literally dangerous (Reef, relay houses, Station-IX culture bleed).

### Sidus silence (also **mute feast**, **zero outbound**)

Annual or shift-bound ritual: **no vox**, **no billboards**, **no public ping** — holiness as **receive-only**. Named after **Sidus Silentii** texture but practiced **system-wide** in attenuated form.

### Ground rite

**Earthing** ceremony before hazardous work — **Reef** and **electrical** cultures literalize it; forge cultures treat it as **metaphor** for **discharge grudge** before shift.

## Language stratum

### Bottle creole

Basal **trade register** shared across the Castra Vetera mesh — **Age of Strife** bottle inheritance: numbers, debt, storm names, feast rails. **Not** High Gothic; **not** pure local dialect; the **middle tongue** of **haul contracts** and **marriage brokers**.

### Salt-in-the-ration

**Shared metaphor** for **common fate** — from **Khan-Bator** protein theology; understood **region-wide** as *we eat the same slime*. Invoked in oaths and insults.

## Law, debt, and archives

### Witness debt

Moral **IOU** to the dead and living who **held the line** beside you in production — **not** the Batavi **Frontier Wolf** chain, but the **civilian** rhyme: *your name stays on my ledger*. Feeds **proximity inheritance** without requiring Astartes presence.

### Ledger grace

**Forgiveness** expressed as **amended paperwork** — peak form on **Castra-Vetera Prime**. *“Grace is a cross-out and a re-stamp.”*

### Grey clerk piety

Semi-ironic honor for **Administratum** functionaries who treat **correct filing** as **divine service**. Insult variant: *grey clerk soul* — someone who loves **procedure** more than **people**.

## Kinship tied to infrastructure

### Cable kin

Families and guilds tied to **elevator**, **tether**, or **bridge** maintenance — **oaths** reference **tension**, **sway**, **cut-day drills**. Not blood-only; **initiation climb** can **adopt**.

### Route marriage

Union **witnessed** by a **navigator caste** or **strait pilot** — legally binding when **route rights** merge. Common near **Atlas Elevator**, **Great Reef**, **Maleventum** straits.

---

## Salvage, forge, and claim

### First weld

**Salvage law** spiritualized: **first sustainable weld** to wreckage establishes **claim** — **Logus Ring** and **Steel Belt** vocabulary; thieves are **jumpers**.

### Calibration saint

Folk **engineer-saint** — blesses **torque**, **dosage**, **mirror angle**. Humor and devotion overlap; **Mechanicus** sometimes **tolerates**, sometimes **purges** local icons.

---

## Threshold / relay register

### Delay saint

Figure **canonized** for **holding** a signal, **beam**, or **window** until **burn** — **Marco de Vetra** mythology; verb: *to martyr the shout* (hold past safe duty).

### Second-cult (informal)

Initiates of **Singularity-Point** timing mysticism — **not** official cult; **watch phrase**: *forty-two breaths*.

---

## Usage note

Use **sparingly** in dialogue — one or two **marked** terms per scene sell **place** without exoticizing mortals into cartoon tribes. **High Gothic** remains **administrative**; **bottle creole** colors **kitchen and dock** speech.
