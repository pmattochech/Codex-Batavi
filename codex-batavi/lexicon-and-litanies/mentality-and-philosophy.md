# [WIKI] MENTALITY AND PHILOSOPHY OF THE *COHORS BATAVORUM*

* **Axiom of Reason (*Axioma Rationis*):** Cornerstone of culture. Emotion is not denied but strictly regulated by logic.  
  *“Service demands reason, not devotion. Reason steers duty without blind submission. Duty is held through regulation of the self. I serve the Emperor; a loyal wolf.”*  
* **Instrumentum Solum:** Belief that the Astartes is not demigod or hero but only a high-precision tool. “Only a tool” is the mandatory standard answer to any praise or glory.  
* **“Unbent” philosophy (native):** Chapter resilience does not come from external hypnotic indoctrination but from Alaric’s unbreakable psyche. Capacity to stay upright and functional under pressures that would shatter others. Logical refusal to yield to failure.  
* **Shared sweat doctrine:** Labor integration. Astartes off the battlefield work civil construction and industrial maintenance beside the population to build loyalty bonds and “anchor” their humanity in the common citizen’s reality.  
* **Voluntary prison dogma (the biological ratchet):** The **Nine Phalanx** — the nine worlds of the Crown set — is not only the *Cohors Batavorum*’s fortress; it is its non-negotiable medical quarantine. The Triumvirate understands that its chimeric biology, violently activated by frontier pressure, is a one-way ratchet. If a Batavi is dispatched on a distant galactic crusade, stripped of Noviomagus’s frozen infrastructure, deprived of Apothecary Drusus’s chemical protocols, Varro’s analytical terror, and above all the heat of civil forges (Shared sweat doctrine), his mind will collapse. Without his population’s anchor, the warrior will invariably succumb to *Execratio*, becoming an irrational monster. They were born for the Wall, forge the Wall, and by irreversible organic restriction will rot on the Wall.
