## I. What this corpus is

**Tenets of the Grey Wolves** is the Chapter’s working name for the *Reclusiam* catechism: spiritual **maintenance manuals**, not Ministorum sermon theater and not Fenris campfire mysticism.

**Grey** here means **industrial-grey Batavi** plate and Wall temper — not a claim of Space Wolves progenitor stamp on the open record. **Wolves** means Batavi soul-geometry and *Canis Batavorum* inheritance of patience-then-break, not Russ-line paperwork.

Faith register: **oath**, **Throne-weight**, **Ancestor-Sovereign** — the divinity the Emperor *carries*, not God-Emperor worship in the Black Templar / Ministorum sense. Stock protocols remain in force beside this corpus:

| Stock | Line |
|-------|------|
| **Faith Protocol** | *The Emperor dictates, we comply.* |
| **Iron Protocol** | *We hold.* |
| **QVINTA cadence** (assault / Aethelgard) | *We hold, as the Needles hold the cold tide.* |

## II. Delivery — two channels

Every licensed entry below may run on **either** channel. Wolfkeepers are expected to know both.

| Channel | Form | Forbidden drift |
|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Low measured lines** | Short litany on squad / command net; flat diction; Judex-cold when Varro speaks | Roared hate-cant; God-Emperor glory theater |
| **Silent liturgy** | Thurible cadence, incense will-throttle (Judex), candle geometry, crozius mark, counted stillness | Empty mute mistaken for laziness — the silence *is* the rite |

Outsiders (including Word Bearers on first contact) often misfile the quiet as anti-faith. Batavi file it as **door discipline**: do not feed the rift free names.

## III. Licensed litanies (Chapter coinage)

### 1. Litany of Iron — *hold*

**Job:** Refuse kneel; seal wall geometry; pair with **Iron Protocol** as the short answer.

**Spoken:**

— Iron remembers. Flesh obeys. The wall does not kneel.

**Answer (Iron Protocol):** — We hold.

**Silent twin:** Crozius once vertical; thurible unmoving; incense held at working density.

---

### 2. Litany of the Batav — *kill commit*

**Job:** Aggressive combat commit — the Batav’s leap, not a rage sermon. Named for *Der Batav* / *Canis Batavorum* kill-shape (Needles → tide → armored skull), not Fenris chase-howl.

**Spoken:**

— From the Needles. Into the tide. Break the armored skull.

**Optional sting:** — No second breath.

**Silent twin:** Crozius forward (not vertical); incense spiked dense for a short count; thurible one hard arc; lenses / fire-orbs at kill-bright.

**Stack note:** Litany of Iron = hold. Litany of the Batav = finish. Do not swap them under pressure for theatrical effect.

---

### 3. Ash-Oath — *Ancestor-Sovereign*

**Job:** Bind the file to Throne-weight without Ministorum god-language.

**Spoken:**

— He carries the weight. We carry the wall.

**Silent twin:** Crozius vertical; pack-candles held; one helm-nod down the file.

---

### 4. Litany of the Closed Door — *anti-choir / rift hygiene*

**Job:** Deny the Immaterium free purchase through noise, names, and bargain-speech.

**Spoken:**

— Do not feed the rift your names. Hold the door shut.

**Silent twin:** Incense dense; thurible still; no open-channel speech for a counted breath.

---

### 5. Sentence Cadence — *execution geometry*

**Job:** Mark a kill or an internal verdict without flourish.

**Spoken:**

— Named. Weighed. Ended.

**Silent twin:** Iron Verdict (or line crozius) rises once; no second flourish.

---

### 6. Death Wolf Measure — *Judex only*

**Job:** When the Judex projects the **Death Wolf** soul-face (*anima* show, not Librarium scry). Line Wolfkeepers do **not** recite this as personal brand.

**Spoken (rare):**

— What remains after the hunt. What closes what cannot be kept.

**Silent twin (common):** Fire-orbs brighten; jaw-plumes thicken; often **no words** if the field is already loud with hostile song.

**Read for enemies with warp-habit:** Not god, not holy man — **penitent / ending wolf**. Dread or despair over temple-terror. See [wolfkeeper-and-reclusiam.md](../personae-command-index/doctrine-and-organs/wolfkeeper-and-reclusiam.md) § *Anima* and Death Wolf.

## IV. What this corpus is not

* Not a claim that Batavi are Space Wolves successors.  
* Not permission to invent barracks hate-roars and stamp them *Tenets*. Elevation still passes **Judex filtration** ([proximity-substrata-and-witness-inheritance.md](../personae-command-index/doctrine-and-organs/proximity-substrata-and-witness-inheritance.md)).  
* Not a substitute for **Mortivigil** funeral / Wall-fusion craft.  
* Not **Haruspicium** / **Blindgate** — those remain *Librarium* (Otho).
