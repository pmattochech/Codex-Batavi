# SYSTEM I: THE CENTRAL BASTION (Detailed atlas)

> Macro context and summary atlas: [`general-atlas.md`](../general-atlas.md)

## SYSTEM I: THE CENTRAL BASTION (Trinity of Order)

The Central Bastion is not a natural star system; it is an aberration of orbital engineering and the definitive answer to *Imperium Nihilus* isolation. By merging the Chapter’s essential worlds into one suffocating gravity well, the Wall eliminated dependence on the **Warp** for day-to-day logistics. Space here is not empty but a claustrophobic ecosystem of matter, light, and unbroken pollution.

### 1\. THE STELLAR FOUNDATION (The Stable Triad)

The gravitational core is anchored by three stars in a tight orbital dance, bathing the planets in perpetual light and radiation:

* **Noviomagus-Alpha:** A yellow dwarf, akin to Holy Terra’s sun, whose shine is eternally choked and filtered by macro-forge dust, reaching the planets as pale, sickly light.
* **Sol-Vetera:** The second yellow dwarf, providing the stable, clean light revered by *Administratum* scribes.
* **Bator-Sol:** A class F (white-yellow) star whose radiation hyper-accelerates vegetal cell growth to sustain the super-tithe biological machine.

### 2\. THE IRON HIGHWAY (Congested Void)

Transit between worlds in this system is entirely in realspace (sublight). The central route, nicknamed “the Green Vein,” is the sector’s most watched logistics corridor. The vacuum is fouled with metallic micro-dust that forces ships to fly on instruments (IFR), threading colossal freighters, interdiction patrols, and customs stations in an unbroken, oppressive flow.

### 3\. THE MACRO-HABITABLE BODIES (The Anvil)

Orbiting close enough that city lights are naked-eye visible in one another’s night skies stand the three pillars of the foundation:

* **Noviomagus Prime (Capital of the Geartrain):** Civilized forge world under direct rule of Alaric von Helis. At 1.12G, its crust has been hollowed and packed with adamantium and ferrocrete. Wrapped in permanent soot and scourged by acidic *Black Rain*, it holds *Castrum Ferrum* and 120 billion souls dedicated to producing arms and armor.
* **Castra-Vetera Prime (The Administrative Throne):** The sector’s bureaucratic brain. An ecumenopolis roofed with hives devoted purely to judgment, law, and record storage. It is the center of nobility and tithe governance, climate held in perpetual autumn, where the presence of a single Batavi is honored as the Emperor’s own manifestation.
* **Khan-Bator Prime (The Iron Granary):** The dense-atmosphere, super-oxygenated agri-world. Dominated by monoculture plains, nutrient-slime oceans, and 10 km silo-cities. The planet is the caloric base for 5,000 Batavi and trillions of civilians, watched without mercy by the 5th Company to prevent diversion.
* **Elysium-Minor (The Flesh Greenhouse) [NEW]:** Placed at surgical distance from *Sol-Vetera*’s clean light. Officially a “paradise world” with flawless artificial skies and calm beaches, offered as top rest reward for the sector’s most productive workers and soldiers. Under Apothecary Drusus’s silent audit, its atmosphere and local food are saturated with pheromones and reproductive stimulants. A disguised incubator built to maximize birth rates and feed the endless human biomass the grinders of Noviomagus and Incus-Gravis demand.

### 4\. SATELLITES AND PERIPHERAL CONGESTION (Natural Frontiers)

The gravity well traps dozens of minor bodies, shattered or forged to strict utility:

* **The Steel Belt (The Debris Rim):** A dense ring separating Noviomagus from Horreum-Secundus, built from native minerals, industrial waste, and destroyed hulls. Its inner ring, “the Grinder,” sees constant collisions that sand ship shields, forcing invaders onto predetermined channels covered by silent batteries.
* **Castra-Minor (The Scribes’ Moon):** Orbiting Castra-Vetera. An airless moon sheathed in inert-gas domes and kilometers of iron shelving. A dead archive guarded by the “Guard of Silence,” where the sector’s information goes to rest in darkness under mute monks’ scrutiny.
* **Golden Grain Station and the Atlas Elevator:** A 35,000 km nanotube cable anchoring Khan-Bator to space, the system’s main export bottleneck. Run under extreme Batavi defensive rigor, ready to be severed if the enemy threatens the surface.
* **Vacuum Harvest Fields:** A ring of glass and adamant mega-stations orbiting Khan-Bator. These hydroponic domes use mirrored radiation to grow rare vegetables and neurochemical components strictly controlled by the Apothecarion for **Wolf’s Curse** biological stabilization.
* **Bator-Secundus (The Chemical Moon):** Orbiting Khan-Bator. A toxic sphere of ammonia vapor and bright reagent lakes, studded with synthesis spires distilling fertilizers to keep the monoculture alive.
* **Bator-Tertius (The Voracious Greenhouse) [NEW]:** A greenhouse moon whose ecological control was abandoned millennia ago. Bathed in *Bator-Sol*’s relentless radiation, its vegetation mutated into kinetic, predatory forms (guillotine trees). Home to hypertrophied, super-irradiated *Grox-Prime* variants, the moon serves two non-negotiable ends: lethal jungle-infiltration training for the Shadow Cohort, and elite “flesh tithe” ground, supplying ultra-dense protein for stabilized Batavi metabolism.
