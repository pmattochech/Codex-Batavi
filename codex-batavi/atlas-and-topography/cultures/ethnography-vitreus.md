## 0. Preamble

### 0.1 Physical frame — clarity as budget

**Vitreus** hangs in the **Ferrum-X** glare of System II’s Crucible: a white-dwarf verdict that does not warm so much as **declare**. Surface geology is ~**90% vitrified silica** — continents of cooled and re-melted glass, crusted pans, ridge-lenses, and **oceans of molten glass** that behave as optics before they behave as water. Horizon is a joke told to beginners. Sunlight strips color from metal and patience from lungs. Citizens do not ask *how hot* first; they ask *what angle is honest today*.

**Slow death:** silica ash in the throat; retinal burn sold as “industrial weather”; static crackle climbing calves when charge leaks from poorly grounded pans; blindness as moral risk and occupational tax. Filter-grade elsewhere is **rain**; here it is **glare kit** — smoked lenses, dark veils, instrument trust before eyes. Children learn early that beauty **invoices** you.

**Scale:** Mechanicus **lens sovereignty** and photonic processing hub for the sector — blind laboratories, refraction courts, orbital plates above glass seas, skitarii-adjacent factory chapels. Population thinks in **facets**, **vent windows**, **melt schedules**, **receipt angles**, and **which mirror is lying**. Provincial feel is not Noviomagus soot-capital and not Aethelgard mud-estuary: it is **Byzantine optic bureaucracy** under a dwarf star — High Gothic law filed as **geometry**, faith of work filed as **clarity with a seal**.

**Budget metaphor:** **clarity**. Every lux spent without yield is debt. Every reflection without logged purpose is suspect. Mortals budget attention the way forge-worlds budget rust: *don’t stare; measure*.

### 0.2 Fork

**Not Noviomagus:** no Black Rain elevator religion; seals still holy, but the holy receipt is **optical yield** and **angle compliance**, not torque and rain schedule. Gear-prayer knuckles exist among visiting forge hands; locals answer with **facet taps** on smoked glass.

**Not neighbor Incus-Gravis Secundus:** Secundus worships **clean light trails underground** and fears **bright waste** in tunnels. Vitreus lives *inside* brightness and fears **stray scatter** — light that leaves the approved facet, blinds a cohort, or invents a false enemy in a reflection. Secundus hides from a lethal sky; Vitreus **negotiates** with a sky that files every glance as evidence.

**Not Aethelgard:** tide calendars do not rule. Melt psalms and vent bells do. Mud saints become **lens clergy**; bridge guilds become **pan-grounders** and **glass divers**.

**Porta Nihili accent:** God is **clarity**. Sin is **stray scatter** — moral and optical. Blessing is **angled prayer** — face the correct facet. Speech is cheap; **instrument logs** are holy. Men of Iron hangover reads as **don’t trust pretty telemetry** and **mute during overcharge windows**.

**What outsiders misread:** beauty as ornament; dark veils as mourning fashion; glass divers as “primitive blind cult”; refraction lawyers as theater. Locals treat beauty as **instrument**, impairment as **honest status**, and jurisprudence as **optics with teeth**.

### 0.5 Local mortal sin & Legatus read

- **Local mortal sin — stray scatter:** Light, reflection, or photonic yield spent **outside** logged facet, approved angle, or sealed purpose — including vanity polish that doubles glare, unlogged scrap-lens diversion, false-route mirrors, and “pretty” telemetry that blinds workers. Enforced by refraction courts, Mechanicus yield auditors, PDF glare wardens, and district stamp booths. **Class-colored:** Rabble = stolen veil-minutes and illegal shade that dumps glare onto neighbors; Common = misfiled melt waste and open stare during vent windows; Upper = auctioned spectacle processions and off-books lens gifts that break treaty angles.
- **Legatus public read — the Glass Burn:** Mortals name Lucan Phaetron as the seat’s **vent-window signer** — brightness without forgiveness for lazy eyes. Surface only: fear of careless plasma bloom, pride that *Septima* holds the glass, rumor of knuckle-click before hard calls. Not Chapter doctrine; not Null-Vent clinical file in populi mouths as sealed taxonomy — only “the Burn held the plate” folk memory after **112.M42** orbital gossip.

## 1. Military (mortal)

Mortal force on Vitreus is organized around **glare geometry**, **pan corridors**, and **lens-dock security** — not trench mud or hive elevator wars. The Planetary Defense Force trains first as **instrument trust**: count angles, not shapes; treat reflections as possible liars; hold vent silence when overcharge bells ring. Astra Militarum draft pulls from melt guilds and ash berms; career militia pride sits in **Shared Sweat** pickets along glass seas where auxilia learn that beauty invoices the eye.

**Arbites posture:** thin but sharp at refraction courts and tithe optic registries — more interested in **diversion of lenses** and **false angle stamps** than street brawls. Riot geometry favors **corridor choke and shade denial**: cut glare kit, force open-pan exposure, break will before breaking bones.

**Who dies first when the Wall is late:** ash berm rabble and unshielded pan scrubbers; then PDF glare pickets holding docks; Upper buy distance until cascade politics force them into sealed galleries that still crack.

**Shared Sweat surfaces (civilian eyeline only):** melt-shift water shared with billeted Guard; glare-kit swaps during bloom locks; silent respect when *Septima* shadows a lens dock — mortals adapt routes, do not pray to Astartes. Parallel facet keys exist; doctrine does not.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Levy fodder, ash-berm pickets, “accidental” forward observers on charged pans. Riot meat when shade is denied. Die first; joke second.
- **Common:** Career PDF, dock wardens, glare-discipline NCOs who teach recruits *don’t stare*. Shared Sweat pride without speeches. Draft windows hit melt guilds hard after bad yield years.
- **Upper:** Officer houses, Magos-liaison security cadres, Facet House staff who negotiate billet shade without claiming Chapter floors. Want influence over picket schedules; fear being owned by either Mechanicus treaty or Batavi shadow.

**Worked examples**

- **Draft:** After a photonic tithe shortfall, Common melt hands drawn into Guard companies that still speak optic cant in foxholes — “left is not left; left is cheaper.”  
- **Riot:** Shade-squat clearance — Arbites cut illegal canopies; Rabble scatter into open bloom; PDF holds corridor with smoked lenses while priests argue whether denial of shade is mercy or sin.  
- **Arbites contact:** Refraction court case — merchant accused of **stray scatter** via vanity mirror row that blinded a dock shift; sentence is fines, veil confiscation, and public **shard confession**.  
- **Shared Sweat:** Auxilia and dock hands share iodine tabs and veil-minutes during Ferrum-X bloom; Chapter presence rearranges market stalls without sermon.

## 2. Culture

Amenities are scarce, bought, or instrument-shaped. Leisure is **licensed shade**, **slow-eye contests** (patience before burst — who looks last), melt-psalm choirs humming while glass runs, and illegal beauty-staring punished as soft heresy. Aesthetic: smoked glass, matte anti-glare lacquer, crystal data-jewelry, dark veils as **honest impairment status**. Public shame is open stare and pretty vanity; public success is clean angle audit and surviving a glass-dive season.

Non-Mass rites: **melt psalm** (hum on the run), **shard confession** (break glass — truth proportional to fragments), **angled prayer** (face the correct facet), **facet taps** (three knuckles on smoked plate — local gear-prayer cousin).

**By class — include Sound**

- **Rabble:** Amenities = stolen shade, scrap smoked film, static-kiss dare games. Leisure = blind-guide storytelling and illegal melt-watching. **Sound:** clipped, dirty humor — “beauty invoices,” “little lightning,” “empty sockets eat better.” Success display = surviving open pan with both eyes still useful. Shame = dying eyes-open into bloom.
- **Common:** Guild halls with matte walls; leisure = slow-eye contests after shift, melt-psalm leagues, rationed veil theaters (audio first, light second). **Sound:** exhausted loyalty, long patience clauses, contempt for loud glare. *Rain-closed face* analog = *slow eye*. Soft respect for Shared Sweat; soft hate for missionaries who demand procession dazzle.
- **Upper:** Anti-glare galleries, private facet salons, crystal memorial lenses. Leisure = refraction concerts (instrument music scored to safe angles), dark-veil receptions, charity veil-hours auctioned as piety. **Sound:** cooler High Gothic with optic cant underneath. Facet Houses: quieter, understatement as status. Peak Magos-liaison: measured brightness — never accidental scatter.

## 3. Society

Rank is read as **kit and key** before blood. Humiliation is forced open-stare, veil confiscation, or public shard confession. Street law favors **instrument testimony** over eyewitness — eyes lie; logs less often. Local governance: district melt councils, refraction courts (optical metaphor for jurisprudence), Planetary Governor shadow under Mechanicus treaty weight. Representation is guild plaques and house facet keys, not demagoguery. Rationing hits **veil-hours**, **glare cartridges**, **iodine**, and **shade slots** before bread — though bread fails too when ash seasons choke logistics.

**Tax-as-wound:** optic tithe and lens quotas humiliate; exemptions for licensed dark-veil impairment and Magos-critical lab castes breed resentment. Marriage/contract: **echo marriage** cousins from Secundus appear in tunnel-adjacent districts; dominant Vitreus form is **facet contract** — houses merge angle liabilities and veil patents; divorce is partition of glare rights.

Death/name civic geography: memorial **crystal lenses** and ash-pan release; Wall-name rumor exists as weather, not *Reclusiam* crypt law. Glass-diver memorials favor **empty-socket honesty** plaques — status in death as in life.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Pre-forgotten; street law is scrub-knife and shade theft. Governance arrives as clearance sweeps. Tax is ash quota and bodies. Forgotten means never crystal-named.
- **Common:** District melt councils; plaque marriages; tax wound = yield shortfall shame. Holy object at home is angle stamp beside chapel icon.
- **Upper:** Court patronage; Facet Houses broker between lab and chapel; tax exemptions disputed in public audits. Memorial lenses with house seals; refuse to be forgotten locally.

## 4. Religion

**Official creed:** Ecclesiarchy-baptized on record — God-Emperor language in booths and chapels where soot-analogue ash allows. **Local accent (Porta Nihili):** God is **clarity**; virtue is **clean facet**; prayer is **angled**; silence during overcharge windows is offering. Stamp booth / instrument log often outranks cathedral thunder when holiness needs a receipt — *“God saw the angle.”*

**Priests vs laity:** Lens clergy and refraction-adjacent confessors compete with Ministorum imports. Compromise priests wear smoked vestments and bless **veils** before souls when bloom is high. Holidays that stop work: **Load of Clarity** (audit festival — carry correct facets, don’t sing loud), **Melt Psalm Vigil**, **Null-Window Silence** (folk holiday after orbital cascade rumor — mute during vent bells). Texts in practice: High Gothic collects + local **angle primers** + crystal-scribed yield psalms.

**Religious punishment:** shard confession; veil confiscation; forced open-pan penance (sometimes lethal — Arbites watch for “accident theology”); glass-eater metabolic cult remains **suppressed heresy** (literal silica ingestion as sacrament — prosecuted hard).

**Booth vs chapel:** Instrument booth for yield holiness; chapel for baptism and imported feast days. Conflict when procession glare creates **stray scatter**.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Faith = scrub and shared veil; priests who cannot audit a glare kit = entertainment or threat. Glass-eater whispers die in ash unless hunger is worse.
- **Common:** Throughput theology in optic cant; mute Mass preferred; melt psalm as daily office. Soft heresy = vanity polish.
- **Upper:** Patron chapels with matte glass; Facet Houses fund mute vigils; peak houses auction “correct devotion” seals. Fear being called fringe mystics by Mechanicus or soft by Ministorum.

**Contrast only:** Batavi Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight ≠ God-Emperor worship. Shared words (*Emperor*, *Throne*, *seal*) ≠ shared creed. Mortals still use chapel language; seat shadow does not convert them into Chapter theology.

## 5. Economy

**World function/export:** Photonic processing, precision lenses, crystal circuit–adjacent optics, Mechanicus lab throughput for the Crucible and wider Castra Vetera mesh. Glass seas are both hazard and feedstock. Orbital plates feed sector photonic demand; treaty redacts hide skitarii cost.

**How work runs:** Melt schedules, pan scrub rotations, lens-dock shifts, lab guest labor, glass-diver route contracts. Wealth means **controlled sight**, **facet keys**, **veil patents**, and **clean yield hashes** — not gold display that scatters light.

**Tithe payment:** Optic and lens quotas sealed by Administratum + Mechanicus dual stamps where treaty requires. **Failure smell:** ozone and burnt silica after cascade near-miss; ash in the throat after bloom mismanagement; the social smell is **shame-glare** — districts that “went pretty” and blinded their own docks.

**Exemptions:** Magos-critical castes; licensed dark-veil impairment (status and medical); some Facet House notary immunities disputed every audit cycle.

**Markets vs ledger fiction:** Real markets trade veil-minutes, iodine, scrap lenses, shade slots. Ledger fiction claims perfect angle compliance; everyone knows scrap diversion exists — naming it **stray scatter** makes it prosecutable.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Ash reclaim, illegal shade rents, diver-apprentice hazard stamps. Wealth = another day of useful eyes or successful empty-socket transition.
- **Common:** Guild wages in plaque credit; melt bonuses; PDF stipends. Wealth = guild veil and on-time yield.
- **Upper:** Treaty rents, court fees, lab patronage, orbital charter cuts. Wealth = keys and the right to *not* look.

## 6. Education

Literacy on Vitreus means **angle literacy** first — reading instruments, facet maps, melt schedules — then High Gothic law. Who learns: guild tracks, Facet House tutors, Mechanicus lay-schola for compliant hands, chapel primer schools that lose students to glare season. Who is kept dark: ash berm children until a diver guide or guild stamp lifts them; deliberate dark-veil initiates trade sight for route status.

**Tracks:** guild melt · house facet · chapel · Administratum optic registry · Mechanicus lay-tech · glass-diver oral route schools (instrument + scar literacy).

**Curriculum-as-virtue:** patience (*slow eye*), mute discipline, distrust of pretty telemetry, angled prayer geometry, anti-scatter ethics. Cost in bodies (retinal burn, static injury), years (apprentice dive seasons), patronage (guest ink for lab schola).

**What literacy does:** Lets you argue in refraction court; keeps you alive on pans; buys Facet House porch time; makes Administratum stamps mean something other than death.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Oral ash lore; stolen instrument scraps; diver stories as survival school. Formal literacy rare; scar literacy common.
- **Common:** Guild schola; PDF glare curriculum; chapel primers. Literacy = plaque and court survival.
- **Upper:** House tutors; Magos-liaison academies; dual High Gothic / binary etiquette. Facet Houses teach bridge speech — lab and chapel without owning either throne.
