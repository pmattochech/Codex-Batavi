## 0. Preamble

### 0.1 Physical frame — beam as budget

**Marco de Vetra** is a **bait-moon** in the **Vigil-Lucis** listening void of **System III: the Threshold** — airless crust, reactor spines, antenna farms, and **hyper-focused laser** trunks that feed the Wall when the mesh permits. Gravity is **light** enough that a dropped tool hangs in memory before it lands; the slow death is not drowning rain or tide mud but **alignment drift**, **EM hash**, and **heat that becomes noise**. Citizens think in **beam hours**, **silence windows**, **coupling seals**, and **release schedules**. The Throne is honored when **the line holds quiet** until the budgeted shout — and when the **tithe of latency** (data that arrived *on time*, not early) reads clean.

Scale is **sparse** by Noviomagus measure: service castes, hereditary laser-house crews, signal clergy, reactor tenders, mute runners, and dock magistrates who treat **vacuum** as weather. Hab-rings and buried galleries hug reactor heat; surface walks mean **suit liturgy** and **grounding straps**. Maleventum-class storms arrive as **hash weather** — sensors fry, elegant auspex lies, and the moon’s pride becomes **manual click-code** and **preemptive scorch** of couplings the enemy must not take intact. Provincial feel: this is not a forge capital and not a granary — it is a **mission-control civitas** pretending to be a town, forever one unbudgeted ping from becoming a target.

**Budget metaphor:** **Beam.** Alignment is crop; downtime is famine; silence is offering; heat is sin’s smell. Money is spoken as **window**, **joule**, **clearance**.

### 0.2 Fork

**Not Noviomagus:** No Black Rain elevator theology, no forge throughput as primary holiness, no stack-plaque spine. Seals still matter, but the holy receipt is **scheduled silence kept** and **scheduled shout released** — not torque logged on a die.

**Not neighbor Threshold bodies (this pass):** **Station-IX (*Sidus Silentii*)** is the **monastic receiver** — cold vow, listen-only, laser to Vitreus. Marco is the **bait and shout** — delay-and-deny until the cry flies or the moon burns. Do **not** collapse the two into one silence cult. Station-IX and other Vigil-Lucis peripherals are **Wave 3 cross-links only** here — fork stubs remain on [worlds-threshold.md](worlds-threshold.md); no Phase C wing essay in this file.

**Porta Nihili accent:** Throne is **heard when permitted**. Virtue is **hold** — *“shout only when budgeted.”* Sin is **premature cry**. Outsiders misread Marco as “quiet monks” (Station-IX error) or as “broadcast zealots” (Navy rumor). Locals are **neither**: they are **schedulers of permission**.

### 0.3 Sorting border (class police)

- **Primary sorter:** **Transmit clearance** — who may open an outbound path (laser trunk, antenna farm, suit vox beyond suit-to-suit) without a house seal and a delay-clerk stamp.
- **Secondary sorter:** **Laser-house string / vitra lineage** — which farm spine, coupling bay, and alignment guild owns your name; guest ink is rare and watched.
- **Felt before a clerk speaks:** Who hushes first when the hash rises; who still wears a **warmth badge** (reactor-side) vs a **cold collar** (array-side); who walks the mast foot without looking for a runner’s nod.

#### Exposure / rights / meaning

| Stratum | Exposure | Rights | Meaning |
|---------|----------|--------|---------|
| **Rabble** | High — suit labor, cable runs, reclaim of scorched couplings, outer gallery cold | Suit-to-suit only; no farm-spine keys; draft fodder for mast-foot riot | Bodies that keep the beam possible without permission to speak it |
| **Common** | Managed — hab rings, shift tunnels, farm galleries under hash shutters | Shift transmit windows; plaque + delay stamp; wrong window = **premature cry** charge | Spine of delay-and-deny as civilian habit |
| **Upper** | Bought distance — sealed galleries, house alignment vaults, civil rooms parallel to Chapter decks | House keys to laser trunks; vitra-string inheritance; patronage guest ink | Own the schedule; fear being the house that *leaked* |

#### Worked intersections (fiction border)

1. **Hash lock** — all strata wait; order of unlocking teaches class (rabble last out; upper already inside sealed galleries).  
2. **Mast-foot corridor** — rabble/common meet; upper almost never without escort. Shared Sweat and *Nona* perimeter watches flatten pride here — briefly.  
3. **Coupling bay after scorch drill** — common up with repair plaques; rabble reclaim; upper signs the denial ledger without smelling the melt if they can help it.  
4. **Vitra-string pattern** — hereditary laser houses move between common peak and lower-upper bridge without claiming Chapter decks.

**Border tell:** silence that is **chosen** vs silence that is **enforced**; a hand that reaches for a transmit stud and stops.

### 0.5 Local mortal sin & Legatus read

- **Local mortal sin — premature cry:** Any **unbudgeted outbound** — open vox beyond suit mesh, laser paint without clearance, antenna test outside the window, panic pulse during hash, “helpful” early warning that paints the moon. Enforced by delay clerks, farm magistrates, Arbites when blood follows, and social death when it does not. **Class-colored:** Rabble = panic ping / stolen suit beacon; Common = mis-stamped window / “just a calibration”; Upper = patronage leak / house rivalry shout disguised as piety. Wolf metaphor discouraged in chapel speech; **signal wolf** (someone who howls early) is allowed cant.
- **Legatus public read:** Mortals call Cassian Vetra **the Warden** — the bait-moon’s governor. Surface rumor: he lets enemies spend shells on the moon so the shout behind him stays clean. Attribution (mortal retellings of a formal line): *“Let them spend shells on me. The shout is not mine — it is yours, behind me.”* Full *Nona* bleed mechanics: [`nona-marco-de-vetra-culture-bleed.md`](nona-marco-de-vetra-culture-bleed.md).

## 1. Military (mortal)

Marco’s mortal force exists to **keep the beam legal and the moon expensive**. Geometry is corridors, mast feet, reactor rings, and farm spines — not hive plazas. When the Wall is late, **rabble die first** on outer cable runs and reclaim details; common PDF hold mast-foot and trunk gates; upper officer houses sign denial ledgers and argue reserve release timing they do not control.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Levy fodder, suit pickets, cable-cut details under hash, “mute runner” drafts who carry chalk and never echo on open net. Riot experience = panic in a gallery when a scorch order lands twice on the net and bodies still try to save a coupling that must burn. Friend–foe discrimination at the mast foot is taught as **who stops transmitting**, not as elegant auspex faith.
- **Common:** Career PDF and farm militia — rotation clocks, sustained pin drills borrowed from watching *Nona* without copying Chapter kit. Shared Sweat surfaces: parallel watches on array decks, civilian delay clerks feeding military windows, pride without speeches. Arbites posture is **corridor law** — premature cry that becomes riot is their meat; theology alone is not.
- **Upper:** Officer houses, dock magistrates with gun rights, vitra-string security patronage. They fund mast-foot canteens and memorial galleries for shout martyrs; they fear being the house whose leak forced a preemptive burn.

**Worked examples**

- **Draft:** Hash-weather levy — rabble sealed into outer runs; common keep trunk gates; refusal to mute suit-vox = premature cry charge before cowardice charge.
- **Riot:** Post-scorch crowd at Coupling Bay Seven — families of reclaim dead vs delay clerks; Arbites break bones; *Nona* perimeter presence ends argument without sermon (surface only).
- **Shared Sweat:** Civilian alignment crews and Chapter array watches sharing a dry gallery after EM storm — silence as courtesy, not doctrine class.

## 2. Culture

Amenities are **scarce, bought, or forbidden**. Leisure that makes heat or noise is taxed as spiritual risk. Aesthetic: matte greys, cold-collar cloth, warmth badges as class flex, memorial walls of **delay saints** and **shout martyrs** (historical crews who held beam until burned). Public shame = being named **signal wolf**. Public success = a clean release slate and a house whose trunk stayed dark until ordered.

Non-Mass rites: **shouter’s fast** (yearly no-transmit day — even legal windows sealed); **beam wedding** (illegal silhouette paint — still done in rumors and prosecutions); **vitra string** naming (child bound to a farm spine); runner chalk-oaths; counting kisses borrowed thinly from Tempest-V gossip but localized as **seconds between allowed pings**.

**By class — include Sound**

- **Rabble:** Leisure = shared warmth gel, illegal suit races in low-G galleries, ghost stories about Silent Belt hermits. Sound: clipped; humor about “who pinged first”; insult *signal wolf* aimed sideways at anyone with a badge. Rite: *pipe watch* becomes **strap watch** — hand on grounding strap, hand on brother.
- **Common:** Guild canteens, stamp-booth gossip, dry satire of missionaries who shout. Sound: exhausted loyalty; *rain-closed face* analogue is **hash-closed face**. Soft contempt for broadcast piety; quiet respect for the Warden’s once-per-cycle rotation line (mortals notice he does not repeat slogans that echo wrong).
- **Upper / Vitra bridge:** Patron galleries, sealed concerts that use **light**, not sound; chapel endowments that fund silence discipline. Sound: cooler sentences; liturgical cadence in public copy of Varro-adjacent form without Chaplain claim. Vitra bridge: quieter than peak; less auctioned piety; *face-hold* analogue is **window-hold** — keep the house face calm when the farm wants to scream.

## 3. Society

Rank is **clearance**, not rain filter. Humiliation is being walked past a trunk gate without a nod. Street law: mute runners’ chalk marks are soft jurisdiction; dock magistrates mediate; Administratum appeal is slow and smells of paper that cannot stop a beam leak. Representation: farm councils and delay guilds — not noble gene alone; **witness debt** (you held my window, I owe your child a shift). Rationing: warmth, gel, alignment time, guest ink. Tax-as-wound: tithe of **latency and silence** — failure to deliver scheduled traffic, or delivering it early, both wound.

Marriage/contract: **vitra string** bonds (house + spine); **listen marriage** rumor imported from Station-IX gossip but practiced here as biometric read between partners **without** outbound — intimacy as mutual silence; **beam wedding** illegal. Death geography: memorial galleries for delay saints; Wall-name rumors for those who died under Chapter shadow — civilians do not own *Reclusiam* crypt law.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Unstamped; survive by runner networks; marriage = chalk and shared gel; forgotten easily if dead in open hash.
- **Common:** Plaque society; contracts logged to release calendars; divorce as **window misalignment**; children named after first clean silence kept.
- **Upper / Vitra:** House seals; patronage guest ink; memorial marble; status anxiety = over-invest in correct silence so outsiders cannot call them fringe broadcasters.

**Friction matrix**

| Pressure | Rabble | Common | Upper |
|----------|--------|--------|-------|
| Who is “forgotten” | Always | By the Imperium, not by the farm | By Terra; refuse to be by Marco |
| Holy object | Strap, knife, gel | Delay stamp, plaque, release slate | Chapel + audit + trunk key |
| Ministorum | Useless / dangerous noise | Wrong accent; sometimes useful stamp ally | Patronage tool + status mask |
| Batavi presence | Watch / fear / dark pride | Shared Sweat respect; Warden rumor | Necessary shadow; want influence without owning the shout |

## 4. Religion

Official creed remains **Ecclesiarchy-baptized** on record. Local accent is **release-schedule mysticism**: God loves the useful hand that **does not shout early**. Priests vs laity: signal clergy and delay saints often outrank imported thunder-preachers in practice. Holidays that stop work: **shouter’s fast**; hash-lock vigils; memorial of historical shout martyrs; Imperial feast days compressed into silence windows so liturgy does not become leak.

Texts in practice: release slates, delay stamped collects, High Gothic Mass when sealed galleries allow. Religious punishment: public muting (ritual gag / collar), reclaim labor, and — when premature cry costs lives — Arbites plus farm exile. **Stamp booth vs chapel:** delay stamp booth wins when holiness needs a receipt; chapel wins for baptisms and upper *face-hold*. Compromise: priests who can audit a release slate are trusted; ecstatic open-vox preachers are entertainment or suspect.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Faith = strap sealed, brother not left in hash; chapel is distant heat.
- **Common:** Throughput theology as **latency piety** — God paid in windows kept; sin named **premature cry**.
- **Upper:** Correct devotion as status mask; endow silence disciplines; fear useless piety as much as heresy.

**Contrast only:** Batavi honor **Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight** — not Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Shared words (Throne, seal, silence) ≠ shared creed. Mortals still speak chapel language; the **feel** is schedule-first. Do not dump Chapter sealed biology into populi mouths.

## 5. Economy

**World function:** Relay moon — laser trunks, antenna farms, reactor power for bait and shout. Export is **scheduled traffic** and **denied intact capture** (scorched couplings as strategic product when required). Work runs on alignment shifts, coupling maintenance, suit reclaim, delay clerking, and farm security.

Tithe mechanics: latency quotas, silence audits, beam-hour accounts to Administratum and Wall logistics. **Failure smell:** early ping residue, hash burns that should have been quiet, scorch smoke when someone forced denial, empty release slate when traffic was due. Exemptions: vitra-string houses with certified trunks; Chapter-adjacent civil rooms (parallel, not “upper class”); temporary Shared Sweat windows during storms. Real markets: warmth gel, guest ink, illegal beam-wedding kits, runner chalk routes. Ledger fiction: “continuous readiness” when half the farm is dark by doctrine. Wealth under this sky = **clearance + house string + warmth**, not coin piles.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Sell labor and gel-minutes; steal reclaim; die poor in hash.
- **Common:** Shift pay in warmth and plaque rights; guild tithe fear; downtime = famine metaphor made literal in canteen rations.
- **Upper / Vitra:** Own spines; patronage markets; audit chiefs; wealth is **not being the leak**.

## 6. Education

Who learns: farm children with string claim; delay-clerk apprentices; Administratum relay track; chapel schools for upper; rabble learn suit seal and chalk by bruising. Who is kept dark: anyone without clearance — literacy without transmit right is common and intentional. Tracks: **guild** (alignment, coupling), **house** (vitra string), **chapel** (High Gothic + silence discipline), **Administratum** (release ledgers), **PDF** (mast-foot drills). Curriculum-as-virtue: counting, muting, friend–foe discrimination by behavior (who transmits), hash weather etiquette, scorch drill obedience. Cost: years of apprenticeship, patronage debts, bodies lost in training walks. Literacy *does* this: lets you read a release slate and know whether speaking is sin.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Oral chalk schools; suit catechism; reading optional, muting mandatory.
- **Common:** Guild schools; delay apprenticeship; literacy = employability at trunk gates.
- **Upper / Vitra:** House tutors; Administratum polish; chapel High Gothic; taught to sponsor silence without performing leaky piety.
