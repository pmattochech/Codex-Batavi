## B2. Hard edges (do not merge)

1. **Faith:**  
   Populi = Ecclesiarchy-baptized **inventory piety** — God loves the hand that keeps **both faces** on the Throne receipt; dual stamp booth beats idle cathedral thunder.  
   SEXTA = **Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight** — oath, Wall geometry, silence as discipline — **not** Ministorum God-Emperor worship.  
   Shared words (*Emperor*, *Throne*, *seal*, *hold*, *witness*) ≠ shared creed. A brother who taps both Gate posts is borrowing **friction habit**, not converting to booth theology. A priest who blesses a reconciled slate is not teaching *Axiom*.

2. **Verticals / keys:**  
   Mortals sorted by **span pass / cable right** → **half-stamp + tomb/arsenal proximity** → dual-face house keys / Span House rooms.  
   Chapter runs **parallel keys**: gulf-fort precincts, tomb approaches under Chapter seal, assault-boat schedules, homer-window blackouts.  
   Brothers are **not** Upper by tonnage; dual-senate magnates are **not** Legatus by inventory. A Span House that billets Guard liaisons still does **not** own SEXTA billet law.

3. **Shame ≠ asymmetric seal:**  
   Mortal sin **asymmetric seal** = logging, blessing, marrying, drafting, or tithe-filing **one half** while the gulf load / Gate pair / funeral tonnage stays **unreconciled**. Enforced by dual-dock clerks, bridge guilds, Arbites, stamp booths.  
   SEXTA / Gervas shame-load is **procedure born of wound**: Valerian-era **one-gate trust failure** (Gate A held / Gate B opened under loyal-seeming orders — **sealed detail**, thin rumor only on street); penance as **never again without independent witness**; *Alabarda* **field 4** (Korvus) as lattice debt the Vexilla carries — **not** the same crime as a clerk filing Gate A clean and Gate B “tomorrow.”  
   Do **not** write mortal asymmetric seal as “the brothers’ shame panel,” or Chapter witness-law as “they got fined for one-face.”

4. **Bridge ≠ billet:**  
   **Span Houses** (Phase A bridge band) = lower-upper soft power: rooms on the neutral span, both half-cants, sell reconciliation, host quiet Shared Sweat tables.  
   Chapter **billet / precinct keys** = war geometry, not face-hold patronage. Martha’s-kitchen-style civil soft doors elsewhere in the mesh are **not** Bifrons’ default; here the soft door is **Shared Sweat on the dock** and **Span House silence**, never a claim that Span House ink equals wolf rank.

5. **Xenos / civic hate ≠ Chapter geometry:**  
   Civic Cage anniversary hate, wake-charm heresy fear, and “mirror siege engineer” gallows talk are **mortal weather**.  
   Chapter **Friction Geometry**, redundancy mesh, and sealed **Iron Warriors**–aligned naming stay *Strategium*. Public tithe scrolls keep **undifferentiated heretic Astartes**. Mortals may cheer when both gates mark together; they do **not** recite twin-phase commit as doctrine, and brothers do **not** treat senate rivalry as the enemy’s breach face.

**Sleeping iron edge (mandatory texture, not a sixth creed):** Mortals meet Dreadnought stasis as **civic shadow** — sealed approaches, changed cable hum, prestige of proximity, fear of waking rumors, tomb-warden etiquette. SEXTA meets the same precincts as **stasis-adjacent vault war** and *Armourium* sustainment coordination. **Never** dump Apothecarion / *Reclusiam* clinical filing, sarcophagus taxonomy, or Furor law into populi mouths as fact. Wake-charms are heretek theater to Arbites; to brothers they are noise outside the seal.

**Deep contrast notes**

- **Time feel:** Mortals live in **hum schedules** and dual clocks (I-time / II-time). SEXTA lives in **pre-breach vigil** that looks like sleep to enemies, then **zero-latency** on *Three. Two. One. Mark.* Same cable tone; different ownership of the wait.

- **Speech:** Populi *hum-closed face* and *both ends or none* rhyme with Iron Protocol *We hold* — **rhyme only**. Do not collapse slogans into one creed.

### Populi → SEXTA (short + deep)

**Short:** Tonnage cant, dual receipts, and *both ends or none* enter brother duty-belts as **friction habits** — survey both horizons, refuse one-face haste, treat waiting as work.

**Deep examples:**

1. **Dock cant into construction litany** — Common stamp clerks’ *seal-both* and Gate-post taps migrate into pre-breach checks as **civilian rhyme**, not Ministorum conversion. A neophyte who grew on bridge-guild shifts already flinches at asymmetric draft smell; SEXTA names that flinch as **good geometry**, not booth holiness.  
2. **Span House quiet** — Lower-upper *face-hold* teaches brothers that mortal soft power can clear a liaison table without owning command. Useful; not affection rank.  
3. **Funeral weigh as reminder** — Brothers do not run weigh-bays, but the mortal insistence that death be **booked** reinforces SEXTA contempt for uncounted loss theater — every meter taxed, every brother filed.  
4. **Tomb-warden etiquette** — Civic “do not claim the sleeping iron” becomes the outer skin of precinct discipline: mortals already practice silence the Chapter needs at the dust line.

### SEXTA → populi (short + deep)

**Short:** Shared Sweat pride; quiet priests rise over idle thunder; Gate pair treated as one problem in PDF folk safety; *Pater Murorum* coinage; tomb dusk as weather, not hospital.

**Deep examples:**

1. **Twin-gate folk safety** — After years of Chapter shadow, PDF and bridge militia teach *don’t split the gates* as civilian doctrine. This is bleed **product**, not sealed Friction Geometry lecture.  
2. **Patience before breach** — Mortals misread vigil-first stillness as coldness or sleep; after Cage survival folklore, many re-file it as ***Pater Murorum* weather** — dangerous to shout into.  
3. **Parallel keys lesson** — Upper houses learn they cannot buy tomb vault doors; Span Houses sell approach patronage **around** the seal, never through it. Soft power maps the wall it cannot own.  
4. **Sleeping iron civic shadow deepens** — Chapter precinct hum changes train civilians to treat stasis districts as **inventory prestige + dread**, not Apothecarion tourism. Wake-charms become civil crime because the Wall’s silence made “waking” talk smell like sabotage.

### Locked seam scenes (fiction fuel — still Phase B)

**B4.1 Neutral-span munitions lift (Shared Sweat)**  
Guard, bridge-guild, and a SEXTA shadow raise a siege crate under green hum. No speeches. Class flattens until the magnet locks; then half-stamps and billet keys reassert. **Right tell:** work continues when the brother passes. **Wrong tell:** mortal claims kinship; brother sermons inventory piety.

**B4.2 Tomb-approach dusk**  
Hum drops a half-tone; wardens turn rabble at the dust line; upper patrons stand back with civic tokens. Chapter seal engages without explanation. Mortals name **sleeping iron**. Brothers file sustainment and precinct geometry. **No** clinical sarcophagus lecture on either side of the line.

**B4.3 Asymmetric draft scandal meets twin-gate witness**  
One half fills levy; the other buys exemptions. Dual-senate collapses toward neutral span. Arbites smell **asymmetric seal**. A SEXTA liaison refuses to accept a one-face embarkation slate — civilians cheer *Pater Murorum*; the liaison is enforcing **witness law**, not fining a booth clerk. Keep the rhyme; keep the edge.
