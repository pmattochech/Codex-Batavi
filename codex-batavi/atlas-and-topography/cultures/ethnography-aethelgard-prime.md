## 0. Preamble

### 0.1 Physical frame — mud as budget

**Aethelgard Prime** is the principal moon of **Tempestas-Major**, caught between **triple stellar pull** and the gas giant’s magnetosphere. **Two-kilometer tides** erase coastlines on schedules mortals treat as **sacred arithmetic** — not because priests invented the math first, but because **mistiming** deletes districts. Violet **storm light** under the giant’s canopy fades High Gothic signage; locals navigate by **ridge**, **bell**, **reek**, and **mud color**.

**Four major biomes** ring habitability: **snowy mountains**, **taiga**, **swamps**, and the **shoreline** belt where **Needles** estuaries meet **salt mud**, **falesia-style sea cliffs**, and **precipices**. Detail: [geography-aethelgard-prime.md](geography-aethelgard-prime.md). Civilization densifies on the **shore** and **slow-water** courts; mountains and taiga feed timber, melt timing, and relay sightlines when optics die in the murk.

**Budget metaphor:** citizens think in **mud**. Mud is time, debt, burial, and salvage. A household that “has mud left” still owns a berth behind an embankment, a net that can be mended before the next surge, and a name on a **water right**. A household that is “out of mud” has been pushed to **Needles** poorer flats, service stairs on cliff rope, or the swamp margins where **river arbiters** hear testimony in peat reek. Salt is the **receipt** of that budget — pans, brine cups, mourning bread — but the **spend** is always mud: where you stand when the water climbs.

**Provincial feel:** Imperial forms arrive late and wet. Administratum seals matter when they prevent drowning; Ministorum oils matter when priests wear **mud boots**. The Throne is honored when **gates shut on time**, **children are counted** before surge, and **nets** return with logged haul. This is a frontier barbaricum heart with estuary law — not Noviomagus gear stacks, not Khan-Bator grain plains.

**Slow death:** drowning is fast; **brackish creep**, **mud lung**, **cliff fall on ebb misread**, and **tithe delay** that starves a winter are the ordinary killers. Foundation War memory (mortal register) still smells like **Needles hunting** — unclassified mercenary pressure that taught grandparents to treat **wrong reek** as evacuation, not sermon (see §0.2 chronological blindness).

### 0.2 Fork

**Not Noviomagus:** holiness is not elevator key or forge stamp first — it is **witness** on water. Throughput theology here measures **gates**, **nets**, and **salt cups**, not torque plaques. Gear envy toward Noviomagus is real; **gear worship** is mocked as dry-net fantasy when a surge is climbing.

**Not Khan-Bator:** starch arrives as **relief and symbol** (*salt-in-the-ration*); Aethelgard does not own the granary calendar. Grain-brother rivalry is sporting until famine; then it is deadly serious. Local law still ranks **water right** above **warehouse seal**.

**Not Logus Ring:** salvage after tide withdrawal is **court-argued**, not vacuum first-weld aristocracy. Logus traders who land claiming **first weld** without estuary stamp become **ghost haul** suspects in Needles speech.

**Porta Nihili accent:** God is **tide-paid**. Virtue is **witness** — estuary theology: *“The Throne measures the delta; we keep the mud honest.”* Speech is cheap; **salt-cup testimony** and **gate tallies** are holy.

**What outsiders misread:** mud boots in chapel as irreverence (it is survival liturgy); **tide silence** as superstition (it is social contract); **Der Batav** morality tales as beast-worship (usually allegory — clergy tolerate until it slides); QVINTA patrol schedules as “occupation theater” (civilians experience them as **weather** that moves markets).

**Foundation War texture (mortal, chronologically blind):** grandparents speak of **Moon Hunt** seasons — open-record pressure from **unclassified xenos mercenaries** in Needles and swamp channels — as **hunting weather** that ate villages and taught **gate discipline** before Imperial forms returned thick. Mortals do **not** own Chapter doctrine, gene-line laundry, or sealed *Strategium* labels for that crucible. Rumor of “iron helms that fell like weather” is **habit memory**, not a street lecture on Vexilla law. Later QVINTA presence rhymes with that fear-geometry without explaining it.

### 0.3 Sorting border (class police)

- **Primary sorter:** **Tide exposure / embankment rights** — who may stand behind a shut gate when surge climbs; who works the open mud.
- **Secondary sorter:** **Water-right standing + salt-cup witness** — who may testify in estuary court; whose haul is presumed honest.
- **Felt before a clerk speaks:** brine reek grade on clothes, mud line on boots, which **tide bell** district answers, whether hands show **net scars** or **ledger ink**.

| Stratum | Exposure | Rights | Meaning |

| **Common** | Managed | District embankments, bridge approaches, registered berths | Gate schedule is moral calendar; late = shame before heresy |
| **Upper** | Bought distance | Cliff galleries, ridge houses, private spans, sealed salt stores | Still smell the surge; pretend distance is piety |

#### Water right / salt-cup witness

| Stratum | Stance | Meaning |
|---------|--------|---------|
| **Rabble** | Rare / bought | Testify only if a Common pulls them into **witness debt**; ghost haul temptation is daily |

#### Worked intersections (fiction borders)

1. **Surge lock** — bodies queue at embankment; class learned in who is waved through when the bell turns mean.  
2. **Ebb salvage plain** — rabble and common meet over scrap; upper almost never — ghost haul prosecutions bloom here.  
3. **Salt pan at dawn** — common mourning and Upper house seals; rabble paid in filter-bread equivalents (*salt-in-the-ration*).  
4. **Bridge span trial** — guild initiation; deaths sanctify; Upper patrons watch without walking.  
5. **Descent weather** — QVINTA drop windows empty a market street; rabble scramble; common re-route; Upper close galleries.

**Border tells:** mud line → salt-cup call → which gate you are named at. Insult: **dry net** / **open ledger**. Mercy: second shift for a stranger’s debt / guest berth behind a gate.

|--------------|------------------|

| Weathered service | Feed the delta; charity as rationed berths; fear useless piety as much as heresy |
| Loss as weather | Losses named on marble and mourning ledgers — still cost |

**Sound:** Cooler sentences; High Gothic stiff when clerks listen; Needles speech under the varnish when negotiating. Less auctioned piety than Noviomagus magnates — mud shame is harder to perfume.

**Holy object:** Clean audit + private span key + chapel lintel stamp.  
**How “forgotten” works:** Refuse to be forgotten by Aethelgard; accept Terra’s distance as weather.

#### Span-ledger houses (bridge band)

**Who:** Bridge-guild masters, salt elders with claw contracts, river-arbiter alumni who keep both mud court and seal booth fluent. Soft power = **witness debt** brokerage and quiet face-hold when QVINTA weather moves markets. Still Upper, not mid-Common — the mortals most used to parallel Chapter routes without claiming billet.

**Sound:** Quieter than cliff dynasts; more salt than oil.

### 0.5 Local mortal sin & Legatus read

- **Local mortal sin — ghost haul:** Taking surge scrap, first-weld metal, Needles fiber, or sonar catch **without** salt-cup witness and/or estuary-court stamp. Class-colored versions: Rabble *ghost haul* as hunger crime; Common as **open ledger** betrayal of district trust; Upper as diversion through private spans and misfiled tonnage (still called ghost haul when mud courts catch the smell). Enforced first by **river arbiters** and **bridge guilds**; Administratum and Arbites eat the case when tonnage or beacon fraud attaches. Used consistently in Religion (false witness under tide), Economy (failure smell), and later Phase B shame≠sin edge.
- **Legatus public read:** **Storm-Master** (surface) — Armin Sturmwahl’s QVINTA as **descent weather**. Mortals say the Storm-Master’s clock empties streets before drops; they **adapt routes**, fear helm silence geometry, and take dark pride when assault weather passes inland. No doctrine file; no gene-line gossip as street fact.

## 1. Military (mortal)

Mortal force on Aethelgard is **shore geometry** first: who dies holding a gate, who pilots mud flats under fire, who answers Arbites when a district riots over water rights. PDF and auxilia absorb **Needles** guides, cliff rope crews, and swamp scouts. Imperial Guard draft pulls **protein and salt** districts hard when mesh wars spike. Shared Sweat with Batavi is **parallel mud** — same embankment, different plate — not a lecture on *Axiom*.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Levy fodder and surge pickets; first into open mud when PDF is late; die on cliff rope under “volunteer” language; riot fuel when ghost-haul prosecutions starve a Needles block. Friend–foe discrimination on the street is **bell and reek** — wrong silhouette in surge fog equals panic, not auspex theory.
- **Common:** Career PDF shoreline cadres, bridge-guild militia, river-arbiter marshals; Shared Sweat pride without speeches; draft as honor-wound when grain brothers on Khan-Bator eat better rations. Train gate closure as combat drill.
- **Upper:** Officer houses, PDF patronage, void-charter escorts; buy sons onto dryer postings; fear QVINTA billet expansion onto private spans more than xenos rumor until hunting weather returns.

**Worked examples**

- **Draft call after failed tithe seal:** Common districts offer second-shift volunteers to keep Rabble children off the list; Upper houses “gift” kit and take receipt piety.
- **Riot on ebb plain:** Ghost-haul arrest sparks Needles satire poetry into stones; Arbites hold the bridge approach; PDF chooses whether to shoot into mud that must still fish tomorrow.
- **Shared Sweat surface:** Batavi and PDF close the same gate during surge under violet storm light — mortals remember **who stood**, not Chapter creed. QVINTA descent windows empty markets; civilians treat it as weather.
- **Foundation War echo (blind):** Old Needles stories of **mercenary hunting seasons** teach children to run for embankments when reek goes wrong — still taught as **grandmother law**, not as classified campaign history.

## 2. Culture

Amenities are scarce and wet. Leisure is **feast rails** keyed to tide tables, **bridge maintenance festivals** (torque wreaths, not flowers), Needles protest poetry (*crown-and-estuary* seasons), and bottle-creole market song. Aesthetic: salt crust, rope, mud boots in holy places, High Gothic that fades under storm light. Public shame is **dry net** and **open ledger**; public success is **punctual gate** and a span that outlives its engineer.

**Non-Mass rites:** salt marriage (hands in brine pan until crystals bloom); tide marriage (union logged to surge calendar); span trial (walk a fresh arch before first cargo); tide silence (no lies while water rises — custom, not sorcery); three tide bells mourning silence; ash-to-salt-pan funerals and pyre barges.

**By class — include Sound**

- **Rabble:** Leisure = illegal ebb fires, satire verses, shared sonar-meat scraps. Sound: fast, cruel-funny, tide metaphors. Success display = surviving a surge with a cousin’s ledger still open. Shame = body left in open mud without witness debt.
- **Common:** Leisure = guild halls on dry hours, bridge saint days, salt-cup weddings. Sound: exhausted loyalty, bottle creole dignity jokes aimed at Noviomagus dockers. Success = seal-clean water right and a gate that did not kill anyone this season. Shame = missed gate.
- **Upper:** Leisure = cliff galleries, imported oils, route-marriage banquets when Navigator kin visit. Sound: cooler High Gothic over Needles undertone. Success = face-hold during descent weather and clean tithe audit. Shame = loud devotion that fails when surge climbs — *foreign bell*.

## 3. Society

Rank is **water before blood**. River arbiters — elected **triads** rotated by district — mediate household debt and Administratum claw; decision style is **slow testimony** and **salt-cup witness** (rhymes with Khan-Bator grain oath, different medium). Salt families track lineage through nets, boats, and contracts with tide; adoption via **witness debt** (pull a dead cousin’s shift, inherit ledger). Bridge guilds (masons + rope engineers) hold initiation mortality as sanctifying. Needles districts: poorer mud, sharper satire, cradle of crown-and-estuary protest.

**Marriage / contract:** Tide marriage (divorce as “seasonal misalignment” — legal fiction arbiters watch for abuse); salt marriage; route marriage (off-world charter / Navigator rights — ambitious, sometimes suspect). Children named after first storm survived or first fish; **wolf** syllables avoided near chapels as bad-luck gossip, not legal ban. *Der Batav* morality tales color naming without gene-seed receipts.

**Governance / representation:** Planetary Governor + estuary courts; Arbites when tonnage/beacon crimes attach; Ministorum when flagellant fashion imports. Tax-as-wound: water-right fees and bridge tolls feel like **humiliation** when seals ignore surge; exemptions cluster on Upper spans and “strategic” PDF berths.

**Death / civic geography:** Burial impractical on sinking mud — pyre barges, river release, ash to salt pan. Memorial is **mourning ledger** and salt-in-the-ration funeral bread (Khan-Bator hardbread). Wall-name rumor exists as weather; *Reclusiam* crypt law is **not** mortal civic property.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Street law = Needles custom + whoever holds the rope; rationing is hunger arithmetic; tax is eviction to worse mud.
- **Common:** Arbiter voters and guild electors; rationing by berth and shift; tax wound accepted if gate stays funded.
- **Upper:** Patronage representation; rationing as charity theater; tax exemptions bought with tonnage promises.

**Friction matrix**

| Pressure | Rabble | Common | Upper |
|----------|--------|--------|-------|
| Who is “forgotten” | Always | By Imperium, not by estuary | By Terra; refuse locally |
| Holy object | Net knife / borrowed brine | Salt-cup / gate key | Audit + span key + lintel stamp |
| Ministorum | Useless / dangerous in surge | Wrong accent, mud-boot priests useful | Patronage tool + status mask |
| Batavi presence | Watch / fear / dark pride | Shared Sweat respect | Necessary shadow; want influence without being owned |

## 4. Religion

**Three layers in practice:**

**A. Porta Nihili basal (faith of work):** Shift orthodoxy as **net hours**, **gate closure**, **tally handed to clerk**. Throne receipt is **manifest** — proof the sea did not steal you.

**B. Imperial Ecclesiarchy:** Missionaries from Castra-Vetera import autumn liturgy, sealed oils, High Gothic collects. Friction: idle cathedral time reads as **theft from tide**. Local compromise: **priests in mud boots**, communion at embankment when possible. Aethelgard is an **exemplary mud case** for frontier Ecclesiarchy (see linked frontier file).

**C. Local syncretic complex (not illegal):** Bridge saints (dead engineers; maintenance festivals; torque wreaths); tide silence; *Der Batav* allegory (Chapter-adjacent symbolic predator in morality tales — clergy tolerate unless beast-worship). Headwater shrines in mountain passes attach to melt timing, not shoal Mass.

**Holidays that stop work:** Surge-eve gate vigils; bridge saint torque days; three tide bells mourning; salt-pan dawn after major funerals. Texts in practice: water-right tablets, mourning ledgers, High Gothic collects spoken stiff. Punishment: public dry-net shaming, arbiter fines, heresy charges that usually begin as **misfiled tithe / false beacon / delay that costs lives** — theology secondary until Ministorum imports flagellant fashion.

**Stamp booth vs chapel:** Salt-cup and gate tally beat empty thunder. Chapel wins when oils fund embankments.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Chapel = rain of words; holy = whoever shares brine. Ghost haul confession is hunger calculus.
- **Common:** Mud-boot priests respected; foreign-bell preachers mocked; ghost haul is **open ledger** sin against witness.
- **Upper:** Patronage Mass + private oils; ghost haul reframed as “ledger error” until mud court smells it.

**Contrast only:** Batavi Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight ≠ God-Emperor worship. Shared words (Emperor, Throne, seal) ≠ shared creed. Mortals remain Ecclesiarchy-baptized on record; Batavi honor is not Ministorum god-worship.

## 5. Economy

**World function / export:** Protein fisheries (deep sonar hunts — leviathan folklore), salt pans, Needles reed armor-weave for auxilia cloaks; secondary bridge tolls, mud-flat pilots, ebb salvage. Dependence: Khan-Bator starch as relief and symbol (*salt-in-the-ration* literary and gastric). Timber and charcoal from taiga feed bridge guilds; mountain melt timing is a traded warning.

**Work runs on tide clocks.** Tithe payment is tonnage seals translated into water rights and mourning ledgers. **Failure smell:** rotting haul in open mud, gates that open late, ghost haul markets on the ebb plain, Logus traders shouting first weld without estuary stamp, grain ships late until Needles satire turns to riot.

**Exemptions:** Upper private spans, “strategic” PDF berths, some Mechanicus shoreline shrines. Real markets: bottle-creole haul contracts, salt cups as micro-credit, witness-debt labor. Ledger fiction: Administratum tons that ignore surge calendars — mocked until drowning proves the form wrong.

**Wealth under this sky:** Berth behind a good gate; salt stores that outlast a storm; a span that collects tolls; claw contracts that translate mud into seals; for Span-ledger houses, the soft power to place a name on both salt-cup and stamp booth.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Paid in scraps and witness-debt meals; ghost haul temptation; wealth = a night behind someone else’s gate.
- **Common:** Registered haul, guild pay, toll shares; sin = unlogged salvage and dishonest scrap count.
- **Upper:** Tonnage patronage, route marriage capital, private salvage fleets that must still smell honest or face mud court.

## 6. Education

Literacy on Aethelgard is **survival grammar**: tide tables, gate schedules, water-right script, bottle-creole contract cant, and enough High Gothic to insult a clerk on purpose. Who learns and who stays dark is sorted by embankment rights long before a school bell.

**Tracks:** guild apprenticeship (bridge, net, pilot); salt-family ledger schooling; chapel catechism (often secondary to mud liturgy); Administratum claw cadet rooms for Upper and Span-ledger children; PDF shoreline drill as civic curriculum. Curriculum-as-virtue: punctuality, honest count, witness speech, silence during rise. Cost: years on rope and net; patronage for dry classrooms; bodies lost on span trials counted as tuition.

**What literacy does:** Lets you keep a water right; argue ghost haul; translate surge into seal; survive a claw audit; read QVINTA descent notices as market weather. Illiteracy is not innocence — it is exposure.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Oral Needles law, tide songs, grandmother Moon Hunt warnings; formal letters rare; bought reading for contract traps.
- **Common:** Guild schools + arbiter literacy; children learn salt-cup testimony before rhetoric; bottle creole fluency is employability.
- **Upper:** High Gothic rooms, Castra-Vetera tutor imports, route-marriage polish; still fail socially if they cannot smell a surge.
