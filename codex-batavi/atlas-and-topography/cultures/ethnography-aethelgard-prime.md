# Ethnography — Aethelgard Prime (moon-world of Tempestas-Major)

**Scope:** **Mortal** civilization — theology, kinship, law, language — not Vexillatio operations. Batavi presence noted only where it shapes **civilian** expectation.

## 1. Physical frame — tide as calendar

**Aethelgard Prime** orbits **Tempestas-Major**, caught between **triple stellar pull** and **gas-giant** grab. **Two-kilometer tides** erase coastlines on schedules mortals treat as **sacred arithmetic** — not because priests invented the math first, but because **mistiming** deletes districts.

**Four major biomes** ring the globe: **snowy mountains**, **taiga**, **swamps**, and the **shoreline** belt where **Needles** estuaries meet **salt mud**, **falesia-style sea cliffs**, and **precipices**. Detail: [geography-aethelgard-prime.md](geography-aethelgard-prime.md).

**Surface grammar (shore):** salt mud, **Needles** estuaries (thorn-mangrove analogues), **bridge spans** where stone is cheaper than eternal repair. **Storm light** — violet canopy — makes **high Gothic signage** fade; locals navigate by **ridge**, **bell**, **reek**.

---

## 2. Historical stratum

**Age of Strife bottle:** Aethelgard traded **salt**, **fiber**, **sonar meat** inside the Castra Vetera mesh; **bottle creole** soaked **Needles speech** — debt words, storm names, **feast rails** keyed to tide tables.

**Fracture:** **Men of Iron** route choke and **flare schedules** isolated neighbors; Aethelgard leaned harder into **water-law** because **land routes** died first. **River arbiters** gained power when **Administratum** wax seals arrived late.

**Imperial reunification:** Tithe ships returned vocabulary of **tons** and **seals**; locals translated them into **water rights** and **mourning ledgers**.

---

## 3. Economy — what tide allows

* **Primary extraction:** protein fisheries (deep sonar hunts — **leviathan** folklore), salt pans, **reed** armor-weave for auxilia cloaks.

* **Secondary:** **bridge tolls**, **pilots** through mud flats, **salvage** after tide withdrawal (competes with Logus ideology — **first weld** claims argued in estuary courts).

* **Dependence:** **Khan-Bator** starch arrives as **relief** and **symbol** — **salt-in-the-ration** metaphor is **literary and gastric** region-wide.

**Porta Nihili creed here:** God is **paid in throughput**, but throughput is measured in **nets repaired**, **gates shut on time**, **children counted** before surge.

---

## 4. Social structure — water before blood

**River arbiters:** elected **triads** rotated by district — not noble by gene alone; **debt negotiators** between households and **Administratum** claw. Decision style: **slow testimony**, **salt cup** witness (shared brine sip — rhymes with Khan-Bator **grain oath**).

**Salt families:** lineage tracked through **nets**, **boats**, **contracts with tide** — adoption common via **witness debt**: if you pull a dead cousin’s shift, **his ledger** flows to you.

**Bridge guilds:** masons + **rope engineers**; initiation is **span trial** — walk a fresh arch before **first cargo** — deaths **sanctify** rather than shame the guild.

**Needles districts:** poorer mud; sharper **satire**; cradle of **crown-and-estuary** protest poetry referenced in chronicles.

---

## 5. Kinship & marriage

**Forms:**

* **Tide marriage** — union logged to **surge calendar**; divorce filed as **seasonal misalignment** (legal fiction hiding abuse — arbiters watch).

* **Salt marriage** — hands dipped in **brine pan**; vow lasts until **crystals bloom** on the rim (superstition + chemistry).

* **Route marriage** (less common inland) — when **off-world** charter merges **Navigator** rights; seen as **ambitious**, sometimes **suspect**.

**Children:** named after **first storm survived** or **first fish** — **the Batav** myth colors naming (**wolf** syllables avoided near chapels — **not** legal ban, **bad luck** gossip).

---

## 6. Religion — three layers

**A. Porta Nihili basal (faith of work):** **Shift orthodoxy** expressed as **net hours**, **gate closure**, **tally handed to clerk**. **Throne receipt** is **manifest** — proof the sea did not steal you.

**B. Imperial Ecclesiarchy:** Missionaries from **Castra-Vetera** import **autumn** liturgy, **sealed oils**, **High Gothic** collects. Friction: **idle cathedral time** reads as **theft** from tide. Local compromise: **priests in mud boots**, **communion** at **embankment** when possible. See **ecclesiarchy-on-the-frontier** — Aethelgard is **exemplary** mud case.

**C. Local cult complex (not illegal, syncretic):**

* **Bridge saints** — dead engineers whose spans **outlived** them; **maintenance festivals** replace flowers with **torque wreaths**.

* **Tide silence** — **no lies** spoken while water rises — **custom**, not sorcery; breaks social contract if violated.

* **Der Batav** myth — **Chapter-adjacent** symbolic predator; mortals tell **morality tales**, not gene-seed receipts. Clergy **tolerate** allegory unless it **slides into beast-worship** (rare prosecutions).

---

## 7. Death & mourning

**Burial impractical** on sinking mud — **pyre barges**, **river release**, **ash to salt pan**. **Salt-in-the-ration** funeral (hardbread from **Khan-Bator**) appears in chronicles — **witness debt** chain: eat **same ration** as Throne.

**Mourning tempo:** **three tide bells** silence — rhymes with **Sidus silence** attenuated — **no outbound** festival noise.

---

## 8. Law — estuary first

**Priority:** **water right** → **contract** → **Administratum** appeal. **Grey clerk piety** respected when **seals** prevent **drowning**; mocked when **forms** ignore **surge**.

**Heresy charges:** usually **misfiled tithe**, **false beacon**, **delay** that costs lives — **theology** secondary until **Ministorum** imports **flagellant** fashion; then **Varro-grade** audits rare on mortals — **Planetary Governor** + **Arbites** eat the case first.

---

## 9. Language — Needles speech & bottle creole

**Needles speech:** fast consonants, **tide metaphors** for mood — **“sky borrowed money”** = storm coming.

**Bottle creole:** dominant in **markets** and **haul contracts** — mutually inteligible with **Noviomagus dockers** at **cost of dignity jokes**.

**Administrative High Gothic:** spoken **stiff** by clerks — locals mimic badly on purpose when **negotiating**.

---

## 10. Virtues & insults

**Virtues:** **punctual gate**, **honest scrap count**, **second shift for stranger’s debt**.

**Insults:** **dry net** (lazy), **open ledger** (traitor), **foreign bell** (someone who worships **noise** over tide).

---

## 11. External relations (mortal scale)

* **Noviomagus:** **supplier** fantasy vs **extractor** reality — **gear worship** envy.

* **Khan-Bator:** **grain brother** — rivalry **sporting**, famine **deadly serious**.

* **Castra-Vetera:** **law mother** — respected, **resented** for **forms**.

* **Incus spheres:** **sky bruise** gossip — **hard men** myths.

---

## 12. Batavi footprint (minimal — civilian eyeline)

**Quinta** garrison reads as **weather** — patrol schedules alter **market layout**. Mortals **do not** pray to Astartes; they **adapt routes**. **Proximity substrata** flows **both ways**: Marines absorb **salt oath** cadence; civilians absorb **helm silence** fear-geometry **without** doctrine file.
