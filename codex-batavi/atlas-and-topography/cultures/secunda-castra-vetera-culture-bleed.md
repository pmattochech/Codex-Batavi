## B2. Hard edges (do not merge)

### 1. Faith

**Populi:** Ecclesiarchy-baptized God-Emperor language on chapel headers and registry stamps; local accent = **procedure as holiness** — concordance between hostile departments, amended forms as penance, appeal windows as pilgrimage. Stamp / concordance booth often outranks chapel when a receipt is required; chapels still own baptism, marriage blessing, and burial books.

***Secunda* / Batavi:** Honor **Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight** — oath and Throne geometry, **not** Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Shared aquilas, seals, and Lex quotes are **shared Imperial furniture**, not shared creed. A mortal who hears Kessler cite Lex and whispers “the Emperor’s hand” is doing **civilian awe optics**, not Chapter theology.

**Do not merge:** Quiet Batavi helm permanence ≠ grey-clerk silence-as-piety. Quiet *Secunda* fire ≠ chapel denial of burial stamp. Concordance miracle ≠ Analytical Silence.

### 2. Verticals / keys — registry verticals ≠ parallel keys

**Populi sorter:** **Appeal windows** (which booth / hour-band / wing) + **vault-floor / registry access** (warm mid-tiers vs cold galleries). Ribbon color, wait length, and cold-air smell on a coat are class before a clerk speaks.

***Secunda* sorter:** **Parallel keys** — Chapter billets, Schedule K listed walls, *Depositorium* perimeter taboo, assigned-target packets, Arbites-liaison channels, Watch-proof honor blocks on Kessler’s plate. Brothers do **not** stand in noble private windows by bloodline; magnates do **not** become Legatus by vault depth-credit.

**Do not merge:** A Ribbon House **floor-pass** is not a Chapter key. A Chapter **billet corridor** is not an appeal hour-band. Guest ink on a civil stack that mentions *Secunda* adjacency is **cold paperwork**, not promotion into the Wall.

### 3. Shame ≠ false concordance (*misfile*)

| Side | Failure / shame object | What it is **not** |
|------|------------------------|--------------------|
| **Populi** | **False concordance** / *misfile* — forged stamps, swapped death dates, phantom hour-bands, advocate lies that fake peace between offices | Collateral math; range revocation; *Strategium* optics dissent |
| ***Secunda*** | **Shame of the stray bolt** — collateral as lawsuit against the Emperor’s optics; firing without assigned target; erasing a facade the Lex and Schedule K still list as witness-bearing; Kessler’s filed **written dissent** when *Strategium* forces Tier III plain language the Administratum cannot absorb | Forging a triple stamp; padding a cousin’s death date; selling petition ribbons |

**Kessler surface:** Anger arrives as **paperwork and range revocation**, rarely volume. Field proverb mortals overhear: *fire as if the Arbites were behind you — they often are.* That is **legal geometry shame**, not filing shame.

**Do not merge:** A house that wins a tithe hearing by false concordance has committed **mortal Throne-theft**. A brother who sprays a ministry atrium has committed **Wall failure under Lex**. Same hallway may hold both crimes; they are **not the same sin**.

### 4. Bridge ≠ billet — Ribbon Houses ≠ Chapter assignment

**Ribbon Houses (Phase A bridge):** Lower-upper advocate / mid-scribe dynasties with floor-passes and appeal vouchers — soft power between common dockets and Upper concordance theater. Still Upper, not mid-Common. They mediate **paper**; they do not issue kill packets.

***Secunda* billet / Shared Sweat assignment:** Stack spines, PDF canopy barracks, Guard rotation floors, corridor clearance musters where grey plate and mortal force briefly share a kill-lane. Hierarchy returns after the ditch is one. Martha’s kitchen / Solis face-hold is **I *Prima* / Noviomagus civil mirror** — **do not steal** as Castra-Vetera’s bridge metaphor.

**Do not merge:** A Ribbon House host who seats a common advocate is not billeting Astartes. A *Secunda* sergeant who hands a ribbon meaning *evacuate this floor* is not issuing an appeal voucher. Guest passes into Chapter-civil outer galleries (if any) are **not** autumn vow patronage.

### 5. Xenos / civic hate ≠ Chapter geometry

**Populi hate:** Forged-ribbon rings, false-concordance workshops, cult confraternities that wire demo to Schedule K voids, guest-Chapter hab-erasure myths, xenos as dock-rumor terror. Civic virtue = keep the atrium leaf-cycle calm and the triple stamp honest.

***Secunda* geometry:** Stalker barrels, assigned-target packets, minimal mass-reactive sprawl, friend–foe discrimination as **clearance language** (who is listed, who is schedule-protected, who is not). Hive Dominus compliance field-4 honor = ministry spires held **without** collateral breach — audit-grade hold, not wrath theater.

**Do not merge:** Screaming “purge the misfilers” is civic / Arbites emotion. *Secunda* choosing angles so a chapel wall survives is **measured fire**. Hate of Eldar / Ork / cult rumor does **not** equal Chapter threat geometry, and does **not** authorize civilians to “welcome” xenos retainers as bridge fashion.

## B4. Bleed both ways

| Surface | → *Secunda* | → populi |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Lex quotes in the lane** | Mortal expectation that fire will be *explained* | Brothers learn civilians treat clause-speech as weather that keeps facades standing |
| **Appeal-window discipline** | Queue geometry as crowd model — who panics when a shutter drops | Civilians learn stalker silence ≠ abandoned floor; it may mean angles being chosen |
| **Schedule K listed walls** | Populi rumor and Arbites lists become **no-spray** geometry | Guard/PDF learn grey plate may refuse mass-reactive through listed voids |
| **False concordance workshops** | Cult / criminal filing rings become Silence joint targets when they touch stacks | Civilians learn *Secunda* hunts **wired voids and forged peace that kills corridors**, not every misfiled cousin |
| **Triple stamp / seal etiquette** | Iron humor and seal-kiss as ambient consistory friction (borrowed manners, not creed) | Quiet priests / honest burial clerks rise in status when Caliper optics shame empty thunder |
| **Ribbon / evacuate tags** | Mortal ribbon grammar enters clearance shorthand at Shared Sweat musters | Rabble/common learn a ribbon from plate may mean **live**, not **file** |
| **Advocate galleries** | Liability theater Kessler refuses to feed with stray bolts | Upper houses learn collateral math can end careers even when stamps are clean |
| ***Depositorium* perimeter** | Sacred absence hardens into Castellan keep-law | Mortals keep “do not queue that shaft” as civic proverb — no tourist theology |
| **Autumn leaf-cycle optics** | Crowd-control calm as operational backdrop (not softness) | Civilians read muted orange atria as proof the Throne still prefers **judgment over firestorm** when *Secunda* holds |

### Populi → *Secunda* (short + deep)

**Short:** Filing culture teaches the Wall that **witnesses are infrastructure**. A ministry atrium is not empty volume; it is stacked oaths, burial dates, and tithe treaties. Kessler’s selection story — corridor wars where one mistranslated arrival or chronology scandal could collapse a tithe treaty — is the mortal world’s pressure written into Legatus habit.

**Deep examples:**

1. **Three-desk patience as angle discipline.** Common *three-desk pause* (touch each department edge before requesting stamp) rhymes with Suppression cells clearing sightlines before the shot — rhyme only. Brothers do not become clerks; they inherit the civilian lesson that **premature force voids the peace you meant to keep**.
2. **Ribbon saint ideal as anti-falsification.** Death-date honesty as near-religious craft migrates into *Secunda* contempt for forged clearance and phantom “friendly” packets. A false friend–foe discrimination entry is the Chapter’s cousin to *misfile* — **parallel, not identical**.
3. **Advocate liability fear.** Upper houses treat war as optics; *Secunda* answers by making collateral **lawsuit-shaped**. Measured fire is not kindness — it is refusal to gift confraternities a martyr atrium and Arbites a Chapter-shaped indictment.
4. **Overflow riot absence.** Pure queue riots rarely summon plate. That absence trains brothers that **not every paper wound is a bolt wound** — and trains mortals that the Caliper’s mercy is **selective geometry**, not love.

### *Secunda* → populi (short + deep)

**Short:** Measured fire becomes civic pride and civic terror in the same breath. Civilians boast that their Wall quotes Lex; they also learn that silence in a stairwell can mean someone is choosing who dies on purpose.

**Deep examples:**

1. **Caliper reputation as class preference.** Upper prefer Kessler’s optics to Chapters that erase habs for one sigil (Hive Dominus confraternity expectation). Common treat Shared Sweat hold-orders as weather. Rabble experience evacuate-ribbons as luck or doom — rarely as theology.
2. **Schedule K literacy.** After corridor wars, even mid-floor clerks can point at a listed wall and say *grey will not spend through that*. That is **populi absorption of Chapter geometry**, not Founding lore.
3. **Quiet vs thunder.** Empty missionary theater loses face when atria survive because someone refused spray. Grey-clerk piety gains; ecstatic theft of window hours loses — without converting Batavi into Ministorum.
4. **Watch-proof read (surface).** Kessler’s silver/black Watch honor block and bronze faceplate read to mortals as **judge-density** — more seals, more witness. They do not receive Deathwatch doctrine dumps; they receive the feeling that this Legatus was **certified to kill carefully among civilians**.
5. **Hive Dominus compliance echo.** Field-4 *Alabarda* honor (ministry spires held without collateral breach) leaks as street proverb: *the Caliper kept the stamps alive*. Sealed AAR math stays sealed; the proverb is allowed thin.

## B7. Fiction tells

- **Mortal → *Secunda* signal:** Ribbon color and wait length first; then whether the speaker fears a **closed window** or a **listed wall**; then whether they use *misfile* for paper crime or wrongly for bolt crime; Caliper nickname used as hope that the facade will still be there tomorrow.
- **Brother → populi signal:** Clearance / Schedule K / assigned target before sermons; Lex clause spoken on open channel when civilians are in arc; evacuate-ribbon grammar; refusal to spend mass-reactive through a ministry spine; silence that is **angle**, not abandonment.
- **Wrong:**
  - Collapsing shame of the stray bolt into false concordance / *misfile*.
  - Treating Ribbon Houses as Chapter billet, Solis kitchen, or wolf rank.
  - Sorting brothers by appeal windows / vault depth-credit as if they were tithe nobles.
  - Dumping Noviomagus Black Rain, scrap-sin, elevator plaques, stamp-booth-as-primary, or Corbec ammo clocks into Castra-Vetera mouths.
  - Collapsing Ancestor-Sovereign into God-Emperor worship.
  - Inventing Founding / progenitor; using opaque friend–foe abbreviations.
  - Letting early mortal POV “know” *Depositorium* clinical contents, sealed dissent minutes, or gene-seed laundry.
  - Reading measured fire as soft Chapter “love of clerks.”
  - Opening Phase C wing ethnography inside this file.
- **Right:**
  - Class felt at **appeal window** and **warm/cold vault door**; Chapter felt at **listed facade** and **assigned target**.
  - Local sin stays **false concordance**; Chapter shame stays **collateral / Lex optics**.
  - Bridge = **Ribbon Houses**; billet = **Shared Sweat / stack assignment**.
  - Kessler as **the Caliper** — measured fire, Lex-before-bolt, legal geometry civilians can see.
  - ***Depositorium*** as sealed *Secunda* facility absence, not culture-world.
  - Hive Dominus compliance as thin public proverb + sealed register, not street AAR.
  - Xenos / cult hate as civic emotion; Chapter geometry as clearance math.

### Worked seam — ministry spine / Schedule K (Hive Dominus texture)

**Civilian beat:** Confraternity demo threat on a listed void. Overflow rabble pressed toward a shuttered window. Common PDF holding a stack. Upper advocates already composing liability.

***Secunda* beat:** Assigned-target packets; no spray through Schedule K; Lex on channel; evacuate-ribbons to live floors; Arbites optics treated as real witnesses.

**Bleed tell:** Afterward, commons say *the Caliper kept the stamps alive*. Upper check whether their private window still has a wall. Rabble count who got a ribbon and who got a baton. Nobody receives Founding genealogy. Somebody still tries to sell a forged “Caliper clearance” slip by dawn — **false concordance** wearing Wall perfume — and that crime remains **mortal**.

### Worked seam — concordance booth vs stalker silence

A common family hears stairwell quiet and panics: *abandoned*. A Ribbon House advocate who has stood Shared Sweat once translates: *they are measuring*. If the advocate lies and stamps a fake “all-clear concordance” to calm a rival house, that is **misfile**. If a brother fires without packet into the atrium to “end the panic,” that is **Wall shame**. Same afternoon can hold both; the ethnography and the bleed exist so authors do not merge them.
