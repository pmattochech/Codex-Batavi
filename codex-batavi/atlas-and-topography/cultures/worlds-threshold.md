### Tempest-V

**Scale & role:** Airless **cannon logistics** — **macro-batteries** in crust; Maleventum hold culture under continuous recoil weather.

**Economy-ecology:** **Vibration** as climate; **shell shock** epidemic among crews.

**Porta Nihili flavor:** Same seal/hold piety — God is **patience**. Prayer is **count between impacts**. Sin is **hurry** — *“ballistic pride”* — **false pressure** cousin.

**Social structure:** **Shell monks** — chaplains doubled as **range officers**. Families **measure** love in **quiet seconds**. Strait-house manners without the dock magistrate cushion.

**Fork:** Fortress hold culture as **minimal myth / ballistic patience**.

**Shards:** *quiet saint* (unknown soldier — mortar saint), *counting kiss* (lovers tap seconds), *dust vow* (oath written in propellant residue).

### Nox (The Black Glass World)

**Scale & role:** **~4.2G**, **obsidian** sphere — **visual echo** aberration (light delayed); scout **training** under Maleventum latency ethic.

**Economy-ecology:** **Gravity** theology unavoidable — falling is **philosophy**.

**Porta Nihili flavor:** Same hold piety delayed — God sees **late** — *“truth arrives.”* Virtue is **slow trust**. Sin is **snap judgment** — optical and moral — **false pressure** cousin.

**Social structure:** **Gravity anchorites**, **echo guides** — teach **latency piety**. **Shadow marriages** — legally binding after **reflection delay** witnessed.

**Fork:** Hold piety as **latency / slow trust** under gravity-echo.

**Shards:** *lag prayer* (speak, wait for mountain’s answer), *glass pupil* (trained delay vision), *black feast* (eat only after stars shift — ritual patience).

### Abyssal-Anchor

**Scale & role:** **Star-hold** — **Rogue Trader** dock, **black-site** cells, **last entrepôt** on the Maleventum choke periphery.

**Economy-ecology:** **Dock noir** — contraband **confession** economy; tariff as weather.

**Porta Nihili flavor:** Same hold piety in salt — Throne loves **honest tariff**. Sin is **hidden hold** — spiritual **smuggling** — **false pressure** cousin. Chapels double as **customs**.

**Social structure:** **Harbor confessors**, **rope nobility**, **sign-language** unions — **silent trade** theology. Mute-runner ethic without Ultima Arx gun families.

**Fork:** Hold piety as **dock noir / honest tariff** entrepôt.

**Shards:** *wet seal* (contract dunked — ink lives), *anchor marriage* (ties dock lines together), *black-water saint* (smuggler who fed blockade — contested martyr).

### Singularity-Point & Block Station “Cerberus”

**Scale & role:** Unstable **navigational rift** / calm-window hazard — **42 stellar seconds** of usable quiet on the choke approach; Witness Ford hazard stack, **not** calendar magic or dome dilation.

**Economy-ecology:** **Timing** as primary resource — **clocks** holy.

**Porta Nihili flavor:** Same hold piety as **punctual** seal — God is **punctual**. Sin is **miscount** — **false pressure** cousin. Mass is **stopwatch liturgy** — **joint precision**.

**Social structure:** **Second-cult** initiates — **hereditary chronometrists**, **window monks**, **widows of mistiming** — memorial guild.

**Fork:** Hold piety as **punctual window cult**.

**Shards:** *forty-two breaths* (meditation), *calm wedding* (union only in window — rare), *cerber ring* (three-click oath).

## NONA wing (Vigil-Lucis periphery)

**Rule:** Same bait/shout civilization as Marco de Vetra — **premature cry** sin, Warden surface, delay-and-deny schedule piety, Rabble/Common/Upper (+ vitra-string bridge). Inherit [nona-marco-de-vetra-culture-bleed.md](nona-marco-de-vetra-culture-bleed.md). ### Station-IX (“Sidus Silentii”)

**Scale & role:** **Moon sensory organ** — **silver crust** array; **near absolute zero** cogitation; **laser** to Vitreus only — Marco shout inverted, not merged.

**Economy-ecology:** **Cold** as sacred — **heat is noise**.

**Porta Nihili flavor:** Same schedule piety flipped — Throne **listens**. Virtue is **receive**, not **broadcast**. Sin is **leak** — **thermal or data** — **premature cry** cousin when outbound.

**Social structure:** **Cold vow orders**, **listener priests**, **warmth apostates** (ones who left — rare — treated as **dead** socially). Signal clergy without shout martyrs.

**Fork:** Marco shout culture inverted as **monastic receiver** / cold vow / laser-to-Vitreus only.

**Shards:** *silver tongue* (lie = heat — taboo), *listen marriage* (partners read each other’s biometrics — intimacy), *sidus silence* (annual day of **zero outbound**).

### The Silent Belt & Ghost Buoys

**Scale & role:** **Stone spheres** — **listen-only** stations; **ghost ambush** hides on the Vigil-Lucis rim.

**Economy-ecology:** **Signal void** — **psychology** of **no echo**.

**Porta Nihili flavor:** Same delay piety literal — *“God is fed by **earned** silence.”* Heretics **ping** — mythic and actual — **premature cry** cousin.

**Social structure:** **Ghost hermits**, **buoy tenders** (loneliest posting), **no-call families** — **three generations** no **vox** christening.

**Fork:** Delay piety as **hermit archipelago** / earned silence.

**Shards:** *stone ear* (initiation — alone one orbit), *ghost kiss* (touch without speaking — marriage), *buoy birth* (must be silent — difficult).

### Glass Eye Observatory

**Scale & role:** **Master optics** — **invader heraldry** read before light **elsewhere**; Marco schedule piety as lens law.

**Economy-ecology:** **Resolution** — **sleep shifts** ruin eyes; **retirement** is **honor** and **tragedy**.

**Porta Nihili flavor:** Same schedule piety focused — God **focuses**. Sin is **blur** — moral **astigmatism** doctrine; **glare** = **premature cry** cousin. Blessing: **lens wash** — **weekly clarity rite**.

**Social structure:** **Lens cardinals**, **orbit readers**, **blink novitiates** — humor **dry**, **sight** precious.

**Fork:** Schedule piety as **observatory clergy** / focus vs blur.

**Shards:** *clear vow* (sharp words only), *retina relic* (dead seer’s glass — relic), *first glint* (named enemy seen — baptism).

### Vigil-Secundus (The Blind Garden)

**Scale & role:** **Black moon**, **radiotrophic lichen**, ***Sonar Leviathans*** — **strain test** arena; bait-moon piety without Marco’s beam crop.

**Economy-ecology:** **Dark** absolute — **sound** ecology.

**Porta Nihili flavor:** Same bait piety heard — God **hears**. Virtue is **quiet hands**. Sin is **panic pulse** — invites **predator theology** — **premature cry** cousin.

**Social structure:** **Hear-walkers**, **click monks** — **echo prayer** — **silent bell** order (iron bell **never struck** — **conceptual** worship).

**Fork:** Bait-moon piety as **blind sound ecology**.

**Shards:** *sound marriage* (bond by heartbeat sync), *lichen vow* (slow growth oath), *deep kiss* (dangerous initiation — acoustic lure taboo).

### Ice Sentinels (Comet Mine Belt)

**Scale & role:** **Ammonia ice comets**, **dormant mines** — **detonate** comet on **intrusion**; delay piety as extraction lottery.

**Economy-ecology:** **Cold** lottery — extraction as **high-mortality collective gamble** (one wrong pick kills the wedge).

**Porta Nihili flavor:** Same delay piety chilled — God loves **careful pick**. Sin is **greedy chip** — **heresy triggers mine** — literally and **morally** — **premature cry** cousin.

**Social structure:** **Ice harvest kin**, **suicide miner** saints — **honor cult** **extreme** — **small** populations.

**Fork:** Delay piety as **ice lottery / careful pick**.

**Shards:** *cold ring* (wedding band from core sample), *sentinel tooth* (ice shard relic), *last warmth* (share heat — sacred hospitality **law**).
