## B2. Hard edges (do not merge)

1. **Faith:** Populi = Ecclesiarchy on record + **gun-and-seal** accent (Throne is *hold*; baptism is pressure test; reef God is *well grounded*). OCTAVA = **Ancestor-Sovereign** / Throne-weight — oath and Wall geometry, **not** Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Shared words (*seal*, *hold*, *watch*, *silence*) ≠ shared creed. Mortals hear helm permanence and soft *vox*; they do **not** receive theology essays.

2. **Verticals / keys:** Mortals sorted by **pressure ticket / sealed-deck clearance** (primary) and **gun-family plaque / strait-house charter** + **mute-runner ink** (secondary). Chapter = **parallel keys** — boarding locks, precinct tickets, Watch geometry. Brothers are **not** Upper class by money; Strait houses are **not** Legatus by charter slot.

3. **Shame ≠ false pressure:** OCTAVA failure modes (auspex-trust when hull contradicts; loud open-band under hash; treating boarding as glory-lane instead of net feed; blocking inland terminal law as “not OCTAVA theater”) ≠ mortal **false pressure** (declaring sealed/grounded/safe against gauge, cam hash, strap, or Navy/dock slate). Reef **ungrounded boast** is the same mortal sin in current-grammar — still **not** Chapter shame lattice.

4. **Bridge ≠ billet:** **Strait houses** = lower-upper soft power (Iron Strait slots, Shared Sweat liaison desks, quiet *face-hold*). They are **not** Vexilla rank, **not** Navy commission, **not** Insular coffin-ledger ownership. A *route marriage* stamp at Ultima Arx buys civil ambition — not a seat at the *Alabarda*.

5. **Xenos / civic hate ≠ Chapter geometry:** Reef-pirate hate, Drukhari fear, Wasp-adjacent silence, and Guard/PDF “who shoots the hatch” drills are **civic**. OCTAVA runs **friend–foe discrimination** as boarding net and corridor interlock — threat geometry, nested fire handoff, hull truth over auspex. Civic welcome does **not** exist for xenos retainers; Chapter does **not** outsource targeting law to tavern friend–foe lore.

6. **Witness Ford ≠ time-magic (absolute):** Outward choke — convoy triage, chart escort, lost ships, bad emergence, mis-filed transit logs. Horror is **route failure** and **hull truth**. Mortals and brothers alike speak **coffin ledgers**, **Insular chart-houses**, statistical death of blind warp guesswork. **Forbidden tell:** island “bending time,” dome dilation, ricochet rewind. *Ordo Chronos* rumor (if any) = calendar correlation / beacon talk — **not** “the station runs fast.” See galactic lock.

**Populi → OCTAVA (short + deep):**

- **Short:** Soft speech, ground straps, hash-closed faces, and “hull doesn’t lie” enter brother habit as theater fitness.  
- **Deep:** A boarding wedge that spent a season on Ultima Arx outer rings learns to **count seals** the way mute-runners count silence — not because mortals taught *Axiom*, but because Maleventum weather punishes loud certainty. Reef *listen vow* and station open-*vox* discipline become the same muscle: transmit only when the net is hardline or the storm has been paid. Gun-family plaque culture teaches mortals to love a gallery; OCTAVA Suppression corridor interlock **rhymes** with that love and then exceeds it — brothers plant Contus / shield for lane closure, not for hereditary plaque pride. When civilians commit **false pressure**, brothers do not moralize in Ecclesiarchy terms; they treat the hatch as a kill-box that lied.

**Vexilla → populi (short + deep):**

- **Short:** Soft Voice / Static-Lord nickname; Shared Sweat pride; hull-check proverb; fear of parallel keys; Navy meetings that end before sermons finish.  
- **Deep:** Mortals file the Chapter as **weather that boards** — industrial grey, orange lenses in rumor, gasket gorget flash, mag-boot scoring on civilian decks. Common PDF try to copy boarding tempo and invent gallery **false pressure** by reporting “seal-clean” from hope. Upper Strait houses practice *face-hold* toward Chapter shadow the way Noviomagus Solis band practices kitchen soft power — **local analogue only**; do not import Martha’s kitchen law. Reef clans treat OCTAVA presence as storm with receipts: pilot fees rise, wasp-adjacent silence tightens, and ungrounded boasts die faster when brothers are in the hold. Guest Chapter stress (Templar legend) teaches boarding etiquette anxiety without granting gene-receipt tavern lectures.

## B7. Fiction tells

- **Mortal → Vexilla signal:** Body clips a **ground strap**, softens **open-*vox***, checks a **pressure ticket** (or reef **pilot word**) before offering a name — then notices whether the grey giant answers on open band or waits for **hardline**. Class readable in gasket smell, mag-tread habit, and whether they board the sealed lane without looking.  
- **Brother → populi signal:** Mag-boot scoring, void-seal count, filtered optic stack, soft channel until hardline; hull knock / Contus plant before auspex sermon; Navy window ended in minutes; chart escort treated as munition receipt, not pilgrimage.  
- **Wrong:** Gene-seed / Founding / *Axiom* / Furor clinical law as dock fact; Witness Ford as time dilation or “station runs fast”; Soft Voice as gentle personality brand rather than EM/open-band law; Strait house as OCTAVA officer; **false pressure** = Chapter shame lattice; *wasp vow* = Analytical Silence; Martha’s kitchen transplanted to Ultima Arx; Nox gravity mysticism or Abyssal dock-noir ethnography leaked into this bleed; opaque friend–foe abbreviations.  
- **Right:** Pressure and current as moral weather; **false pressure** / **ungrounded boast** as mortal sin; Soft Voice / Static-Lord as surface Legatus read; hull truth over auspex; Navy geometry-vs-burn and Insular coffin piety as Core seams; station galleries **and** reef holds as twin contact surfaces; Witness Ford as lost ships / bad emergence / chart escort hazard; chronological blindness — Templar intercept as legend graded by speaker date, not early-war omniscience.
