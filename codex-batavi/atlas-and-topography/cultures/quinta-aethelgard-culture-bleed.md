## B2. Hard edges (do not merge)

1. **Faith:** Populi = Ecclesiarchy-baptized + Porta Nihili estuary accent (tide-paid Throne; salt-cup witness; mud-boot priests). *Quinta* / Batavi = **Ancestor-Sovereign** / Throne-weight — oath and geometry, **not** Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Shared words (*Emperor*, *Throne*, *hold*, *seal*) ≠ shared creed. *Der Batav* morality tales on the shore are allegory tolerated by clergy until they slide into beast-worship; they are **not** Chapter gene-line receipts.

2. **Verticals / keys:** Mortals sorted by **tide exposure** and **embankment rights** (who stands behind a shut gate; who works open mud). Secondary police: **water-right standing + salt-cup witness**. Chapter = **parallel descent routes** — jump corridors, assault yards, certification gantries, drop-notice priority that empties markets without owning water tablets. Brothers ≠ Upper by money; cliff dynasts ≠ Legatus by patronage. Span-ledger soft power opens **civil** rooms beside *Quinta* weather — not billet rank.

3. **Shame ≠ ghost haul:** Local mortal sin = **ghost haul** (unwitnessed salvage — class-colored: hunger crime / open-ledger betrayal / private-span diversion). *Quinta* shame / failure grammar = **late drop**, **wrong grav-comp hum**, **missed puncture**, **static hold when motion was the scoreboard**, overspend of jump fuel without Alaric permission geometry. Do **not** file a brother’s late landing as ghost haul, or a Needles unwitnessed scrap ring as Legatus shame.

4. **Bridge ≠ billet:** **Span-ledger houses** (bridge-guild masters, salt elders with claw contracts, arbiter alumni) = named lower-upper **civil bridge** — witness-debt brokerage, face-hold when descent weather moves markets, quiet translation between mud court and seal booth. They are **not** *Quinta* billet, not jump-certified, not *Alabarda* escorts. Kitchen / Shared Sweat embankment sweat is **parallel mud**, not a promotion ladder into plate.

5. **Xenos / civic hate ≠ Chapter geometry:** Civic **Moon Hunt** grandmother law (unclassified mercenary hunting weather; wrong reek = evacuate) and Needles hate of hunting seasons ≠ Chapter Foundation crucible doctrine, Wild Hunt endocrine authorization, or friend–foe discrimination law inside the helm. Mortals run for embankments; *Quinta* files threat geometry and drop slots. Ork scrap-spire triumph gossip (thin, allowed) ≠ street lectures on *Alabarda* field 4 register logic. No civic “welcome” for xenos retainers; no mortal ownership of sealed campaign labels.

### Populi → *Quinta* (short + deep)

**Short:** Mud honesty, witness speech, and gate punctuality are the weather *Quinta* expects from Aethelgard auxilia — not cathedral ecstasy.

**Deep examples**

1. **Salt-cup testimony → honest count after clearance.** River arbiters train Common mouths to sip brine and speak slow truth. When PDF and *Quinta* clear an ebb plain after hunting weather or Ork scrap fallout, the Chapter wants **logged salvage**, not Needles rings. The bleed is procedural rhyme: mortal **witness** culture makes ghost-haul prosecutions faster beside assault yards — it does **not** make brothers sip salt-cups before orders.

2. **Tide silence → drop silence misread.** Civilians practice no-lie custom while water rises. Brothers practice Analytical Silence and sealed helm law. Shared surface: quiet under pressure. Misread risk: missionaries call both “superstition”; line brothers who borrow Needles metaphors must not teach tide silence as psychic rite.

3. **Needles cadence proverb → Iron Protocol answer (rhyme only).** Shore speech already owns cold-tide metaphors. Chapter *Quinta* cadence (*We hold, as the Needles hold the cold tide.*) is **assault / Iron Protocol** property first documented in sealed recovery chronology — **not** a street catechism about named brothers. Fiction may let a Common PDF sergeant hear the rhyme and feel dark pride without owning Moraine-Nine files.

4. **Bridge-guild mortality → vertical comfort.** Span trials kill apprentices and sanctify torque wreaths. *Quinta* recruits and trains on Aethelgard pressure zones partly because cliff rope and vertical mud are already civic tuition. Bleed: Needles guides and cliff porters become Shared Sweat partners; they do not become jump-certified by surviving a span trial.

### *Quinta* → populi (short + deep)

**Short:** Descent weather rewrites markets; Shared Sweat writes gate memory; Storm-Master is a clock, not a feast king.

**Deep examples**

1. **Drop notices as market weather.** Administratum claw and Span-ledger houses re-stamp routes when *Quinta* windows open. Rabble lose berths to scramble; Common treat it as moral calendar peer to tide bells; Upper buy distance and still smell thruster reek. No one on the street files Type Descensus doctrine — they file **empty stalls**.

2. **Chainsword / saw-mass as aesthetic rumor (thin).** Mortals see grey pauldrons and brutal close work after landings. They invent Needles satire about “iron that eats rope.” Allowed thin gossip: the Wall cuts ugly. Forbidden as street fact: pure-line armory exception essays, Wild Hunt endocrine law, Shadows knife→gladius under Silence.

3. **Quiet priests rise; foreign bells fall.** Helm permanence and countdown cool undercut idle cathedral theater during surge **and** during descent weather. Mud-boot priests who clear embankments beside Shared Sweat gain face; loud missionaries who demand Mass during Storm-Master windows lose it — same accent conflict as Phase A, now with a second clock.

4. **Gutgrind / Ork scrap-spire (thin triumph gossip).** After certified *Quinta* triumphs in-system (Selene-Black scrap-spire clearance), mortals may repeat **Ork weather broke** and take dark pride. They do **not** own *Alabarda* fourth-field register logic, Foundation War vs field-4 chronology, or sealed AAR taxonomy. Chronological blindness: grandparents’ Moon Hunt and later Ork clearances blur into “hunting weather” unless a clerk forces dates.

## B7. Fiction tells

- **Mortal → *Quinta* signal:** Mud line on boots → which tide bell they answer → whether they offer **salt-cup** or High Gothic first → how they flinch when **descent weather** empties the street without a surge → whether they ask **who stood the gate** or which saint to thank.

- **Brother → populi signal:** Grav-comp hum / countdown cadence → drop corridor priority over water tablet → chainsword saw-mass after landing (grey line) → silence that is **helm law**, not tide custom → refusal to sip brine as order-seal → Shared Sweat that ends when the embankment is one again.

- **Wrong:**
  - Dumping **Einar / *Amissi* / Steppenwolf** sealed doctrine, cull-withheld recovery, or Chaplain-escort law into Needles street talk as fact.
  - Collapsing Batavi Ancestor-Sovereign into Ministorum God-Emperor worship.
  - Treating **ghost haul** as Legatus shame or *Alabarda* debt.
  - Treating Span-ledger *face-hold* as *Quinta* billet or jump certification.
  - Writing tide silence as psychic sorcery; Storm-Master as feast king; Founding number / progenitor Chapter on the open record.
  - Phase C wing stubs inside this file; Noviomagus elevator piety pasted onto embankments.

- **Right:**
  - Water before blood; ghost haul as class-colored mortal sin; Storm-Master as **route weather**.
  - Parallel mud Shared Sweat — same gate, different plate; hierarchy returns after.
  - Two clocks: tide arithmetic and descent windows; Span-ledger brokers, does not own.
  - Moon Hunt = grandmother hunting weather (chronologically blind); Chapter Foundation myth stays inside briefings.
  - Shame ≠ sin; bridge ≠ billet; civic xenos hate ≠ Chapter threat geometry.
  - Thin Ork scrap-spire triumph gossip allowed; sealed field-4 / *Amissi* taxonomy not for parish mouths.
