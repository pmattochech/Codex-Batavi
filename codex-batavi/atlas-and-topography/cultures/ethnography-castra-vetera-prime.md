## 0. Preamble

### 0.1 Physical frame — paper as budget

**Castra-Vetera Prime** is the Central Bastion’s **bureaucracy brain**: an ecumenopolis of judgment halls, tithe ministries, noble registries, and **kilometers of vault** under **Sol-Vetera**’s clean yellow light — the star Administratum scribes still call *honest*. Climate control holds the surface gardens and atrium courts in **perpetual autumn**: muted orange canopies, holograph leaf-fall on timed cycles, optical calm engineered so crowds do not riot in summer heat or winter despair. The air tastes of **cogitation incense** — paper-light smog from endless slate-burners, binder resins, and scrubbed archive vents. Citizens think in **forms**, **dockets**, **appeal windows**, **concordance stamps**, and **vault floors**. Slow death here is not acid rain eating a filter; it is **being wrong on paper** until food, lodging, marriage, draft, and burial all fail to find you.

**Provincial feel:** Imperial High Gothic arrives **intact** and often **louder** than on Noviomagus — this world *is* the consistory that other planets resent. Local identity remains **filing-first**: the Throne is honored when **three departments that hate each other** still produce a matching seal. Gravity sits near Imperial median; the body’s complaint is **corridor kilometers**, **queue hours**, and **low-level lumen** under registry stacks, not forge soot. Night skies show neighbor city-lights across the Iron Highway — Noviomagus as forge rumor, Khan-Bator as calorie rumor — while Castra-Vetera’s own silhouette is **spires of ministries** and the sealed mouths of vault lifts.

**Sensory world:** dry leaf holographs that never rot; pink slips and ribbon tags; the hydraulic hush of vault doors; Arbites boots on polished ferrocrete; autumn gardens where nobles stage *autumn vows* under falling light that is not weather. Beneath the gardens: **registry vaults** cold enough to keep ink and gene-print stable — including Chapter-adjacent sealed facilities the populi name only as **“the Depositorium levels”** and do not enter.

### 0.2 Fork

**Not Noviomagus.** No Black Rain as class police. No throughput theology of torque and scrap. No plaque-and-elevator forge cant as the primary moral calendar. A Noviomagus visitor who treats holiness as a **stamp booth receipt after shift** is mocked as a **soot provincial** who thinks God lives in a thermal timer. Castra-Vetera holiness is **concordance** — three hostile offices agreeing in writing — and **amended forms** as penance.

**Not Khan-Bator.** No slime-tide calendar, no diversion-of-grain as the master sin, no oxygen-monitor orders as street priesthood. Calorie politics arrive as **tithe tables** and **ration seals** issued from ministry floors, not silo elevators.

**Porta Nihili accent:** The Emperor is paid in **correct filing**. Speech remains cheap; seals remain holy — but the seal that matters is the **triple stamp** proving departments that hate each other briefly told the same truth. Outsiders call the culture **cold**. Locals call it **honest**: theater piety without matching dockets is the real fraud.

**What outsiders misread:** Quiet corridors as cowardice (they are **queue discipline**). Autumn beauty as softness (it is **crowd-control optics**). Advocates as lawyers-for-hire in a Terra sense (they are **semi-licensed duelists of paperwork** whose careers end in vault exile or noble patronage). A single Batavi in plate as “the Emperor walking” — mortal awe language the Chapter does not claim as creed.

### 0.3 Sorting border (class police)

- **Primary sorter:** **Appeal windows** — which booth, which hour-band, which ministry wing a body may stand before without being turned as *wrong floor*.
- **Secondary sorter:** **Vault-floor / registry access** — which stack levels, cold galleries, and shelving tiers a badge, bloodline mark, or guest ribbon opens.
- **Felt before a clerk speaks:** Ribbon color on the sleeve; how long one waits without being named; whether the atrium leaf-cycle is still “public” or already “house reserved”; the smell of cold vault air on clothes (rabble almost never; upper often).

#### Appeal windows — exposure / rights / meaning

| Stratum | Exposure | Rights | Meaning |
|---------|----------|--------|---------|
| **Rabble** | Street-side overflow windows; dawn scrap-queues; often closed before their number is called | May file **petition slips** only; no advocate standing; wrong wing = baton or unpaid labor draft | The window is pilgrimage and punishment. Being seen is luck; being heard is almost theology |
| **Common** | District and guild windows on published hour-bands; scribe-bloodline coupons; limited advocate vouchers | May request **amendment** and **concordance check**; can buy one re-queue per tithe cycle if seals are clean | The window is calendar and honor. Missing your band is shame; forging a band is **false concordance** seed |
| **Upper** | Private house windows inside ministry atria; noble tithe-house galleries; overnight docket priority | May compel **inter-departmental concordance** hearings; retain advocates as household officers | The window is theater of power. “Waiting with the commons” is either penance-performance or political suicide |

#### Vault-floor / registry access — exposure / rights / meaning

| Stratum | Exposure | Rights | Meaning |
|---------|----------|--------|---------|
| **Rabble** | Service stairs, waste chutes, outer shelving scrub — never cold-core | No registry key; touching a sealed shelf without escort = arrest narrative ready-made | Vault air on a rabble coat = assumed theft |
| **Common** | Mid-tier warm registries; copy rooms; ribbon-saint memorial galleries | Shift badges for assigned floors; guest ink for one-day copy pulls | Floor number is caste tattoo you carry in the lungs |
| **Upper** | Deep cold galleries, house vaults, sealed treaty rooms; proximity (not entry) to Chapter-adjacent sealed caches | Bloodline keys, house Magos-liaison escorts, advocate floor-passes | Depth equals continuity. Shallow vaults are for people who can still be forgotten |

#### Worked intersections (fiction tells for the border)

1. **Dawn overflow queue** — rabble and desperate common meet; upper never. Leaf holographs still “public cycle.” Insult: cutting the ribbon line. Mercy: a clerk who “finds” one more slip before close.
2. **Midday concordance booth** — common default; three department stamps required. Upper sends advocates; rabble watches through grillwork and learns the sound of a denied third stamp.
3. **Night vault lift** — upper and sealed escorts; common copy-clerks on overtime; rabble scrub crews turned at the warm/cold door. Smell of cold air is the tell.
4. **Appeal duel gallery** — advocates for hire; common families mortgage ration seals for a single standing; upper houses treat it as sport with lethal career stakes.
5. **Schedule K / ministry spine corridors** (Hive Dominus texture) — civilians learn that some walls are **listed** and that grey ceramite may refuse to spend mass-reactive through them. Class still sorts who gets evacuated first when a confraternity wires demo to those voids.

### 0.4 Fiction strata lock

**Labels:** Rabble · Common · Upper *(Administratum legal castes exist in parallel; these labels are fiction strata)*  
**Bridge band:** **Ribbon Houses** — lower-upper advocate / mid-scribe dynasties who hold **floor-passes** and **appeal vouchers** without owning noble tithe-house vault keys. Soft power = mediation between common dockets and Upper concordance theater. Still Upper, not mid-Common.

#### Rabble

**Who:** Overflow petitioners, vault scrub and binder-reclaim crews, unlicensed form-runners, atrium beggars between window bands, “pink-slip ghosts” whose last registry entry was voided.

| Mood premise | How it functions |
|--------------|------------------|
| Duty without audience | Hold the outer queue and the waste chute so the ministries above do not drown in their own paper — geometry, not patriotism |
| Forgotten, not faithless | **Pre-forgotten** — names thin on the ledger. Oath: don’t steal a brother’s petition ribbon; don’t leave a body in a closed-window alcove without a scrap tag |
| Weathered service | Faith = hands that still hold a slip upright in the wind of atrium fans |
| Loss as weather | Missing cousins are “voided entries”; grief is short, then back to the overflow |

**Sound:** Short sentences. Bitter clerk jokes. Proto–*We hold* as “we don’t drop the slip.” Humor aimed at stamps that never come.

**Holy object:** Ribbon tag, stub of pink slip, shared ink-stick.  
**How “forgotten” works:** Never queued properly; death without a death-date stamp (*ribbon saint* horror in reverse).

#### Common

**Who:** Scribe bloodlines on mid-floors, guild copyists, district advocates-on-voucher, tithe school graduates, ministry runners with clean shift badges — spine of procedural holiness.

| Mood premise | How it functions |
|--------------|------------------|
| Duty without audience | Concordance *is* the prayer; Terra’s applause is not in the contract |
| Forgotten, not faithless | Know they are Nihilus-edge consistory. Baptize and file in **grey-clerk cant**, not cathedral ecstasy |
| Weathered service | Virtue = clean seals, on-time window, amended penance without theater. Sin = **false concordance** / *misfile* |
| Loss as weather | Levy cousins and Wall-name rumors filed beside docket silence |

**Sound:** Exhausted loyalty. Precise nouns (*manifest*, *hash*, *variance*, *window*). Soft contempt for loud missionaries; quiet respect for Shared Sweat without speeches.

**Holy object:** Triple-stamp block, civil docket plaque, hour-band coupon.  
**How “forgotten” works:** By Terra; not by the district registry if seals stay clean.

#### Upper (incl. Ribbon Houses)

**Who:** Noble tithe houses, ministry patrons, peak vault-key dynasties, Ministorum chapter-house sponsors — and **Ribbon Houses** as bridge.

| Mood premise | How it functions |
|--------------|------------------|
| Duty without audience | Audience is **local** (Arbites optics, Chapter shadow, rival houses), not Terra. Form keeps the Throne **legible** |
| Forgotten, not faithless | Status anxiety — over-invest in autumn liturgy and “correct” devotion so forge-worlds cannot call them paper parasites |
| Weathered service | Feed the machine with sealed labor and tithe math; charity as rationed advocate vouchers, not ecstasy |
| Loss as weather | Losses named in marble and ledgers — still treated as **cost** |

**Sound:** Longer High Gothic sentences; iron humor (*triple stamp kiss*). Ribbon Houses quieter than peak nobles; speak common docket when they must not look like predators.

**Holy object:** House seal, private window key, autumn vow holograph token.  
**How “forgotten” works:** Refuse to be forgotten by Castra-Vetera; fear being forgotten by the mesh.

### 0.5 Local mortal sin & Legatus read

- **Local mortal sin — false concordance:** Presenting two or three departmental “agreements” that do not actually match — forged stamps, swapped death dates, phantom hour-bands, advocate lies that create a **fake peace between offices**. Street name: ***misfile***. Enforced by Administratum audit, Arbites when blood follows paper, Ministorum when the lie touches baptismal or burial books.  
  - **Rabble version:** Selling forged ribbons / stealing slips — survival crime that still reads as Throne-theft.  
  - **Common version:** “Correcting” a cousin’s death date, padding a concordance, quiet erasure — the sin that kills trust between floors.  
  - **Upper version:** House-sponsored false concordance to win tithe hearings — worst when it collapses a Schedule K wall or a treaty room; careers end in vault exile or worse.
- **Legatus public read — the Caliper:** Mortals name **Henric Kessler** as **the Measured Line** / **the Caliper** — the governor who fires as if the Arbites stand behind every muzzle, who quotes Lex when civilians are in the arc, who treats collateral as a lawsuit against the Emperor’s optics. Surface only: **measured fire** and **legal geometry**. Not gene-seed talk; not sealed *Strategium* minutes.

## 1. Military (mortal)

Castra-Vetera’s mortal force culture is **corridor law**: PDF and hive militia hold stacks; Arbites own the ministry atria and appeal-floor peace; Imperial Guard billets rotate through as tithe-debt and Shared Sweat partners. When the Wall is late, **rabble die first** in overflow riots and scrub-tunnel collapses; **common** die second as draft and shaft-security; **upper** die last unless a confraternity wires demo to **Schedule K** voids and forces everyone into the same arithmetic (Hive Dominus texture — civilians remember that grey plate can refuse spray).

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Levy fodder, atrium pickets, unpaid “volunteer” corridor shields when windows close violent. Know Arbites batons better than Guard doctrine. Shared Sweat contact = being shouted out of a kill-lane or handed a ribbon that means *evacuate this floor*.
- **Common:** Career PDF stack-guards, ministry security with docket badges, Guard draft as family wound and sometimes pride. Train on **friend–foe discrimination** drills that sound like paperwork: who is cleared, who is schedule-listed, who is not. Respect Batavi **measured fire** when it saves a chapel wall; fear it when silence in the stairwell means someone is choosing angles.
- **Upper:** Officer houses, Arbites liaison patrons, PDF command blood. War is optics and liability. Prefer Kessler’s Caliper reputation to Chapters that erase habs for one sigil. Ribbon Houses supply advocate-officers who can read both Lex and crowd geometry.

**Worked examples**

- **Draft board as appeal window:** Common families treat conscription notice as a docket — amendments, medical concordance, advocate delay. False medical concordance = local mortal sin with military teeth.
- **Riot at closed overflow:** Rabble press a shuttered window; PDF fires warning; Arbites decide whether the ministry “deserves” a baton lesson. Batavi almost never appear for pure queue riot — absence is itself a civic fact.
- **Shared Sweat surface:** Joint corridor clearances; Guard learning not to spray Schedule K; civilians learning that *Secunda* stalker angles mean **assigned targets**, not wrath. Mortals do not receive *Axiom* lectures — they receive **hold** and **no fire on that facade**.

## 2. Culture

Amenities are **queue comfort** and **optical calm**: autumn gardens, low-glare atria, leaf holography, tea-stalls licensed for window-bands. Aesthetic is late-imperial consistory — iron humor, ribbon saints, seals kissed instead of rings (*triple stamp kiss*). Leisure is scarce: licensed autumn promenades, advocate-gallery spectating, rationed holograph theaters. Forbidden leisure includes unlicensed “concordance parties” (forged stamp drinking games) and any rite that opens a vault seal for thrill.

**Non-Mass rites:** *autumn vow* under falling holograph leaves; *triple stamp kiss* as iron joke that became real etiquette; memorial walks past the *ribbon saint* plaque (archivist who refused to alter a death date); penance queues where amended forms are pinned to atrium rails like offerings.

**By class — include Sound**

- **Rabble:** Amenities = dry alcove and a ribbon that has not been cut. Leisure = watching Upper promenades through grillwork; illegal ink-stick races. **Sound:** clipped Low Gothic; stamp curses; laughter that stops when a clerk looks. Shame = dropped slip in public. Success = being called by number before close.
- **Common:** Amenities = guild tea under canopy, clean badge polish, one autumn promenade per cycle. Leisure = docket clubs, advocate-gallery tickets, hymn-of-form practice (not the author Hymn — local procedural chants). **Sound:** exhausted precise nouns; dry jokes about departments that hate each other; *rain-closed* cousin from Noviomagus mocked as wrong weather. Shame = missed hour-band. Success = seal-clean concordance without advocate.
- **Upper:** Amenities = private gardens on muted orange, house holograph cycles, sealed galleries. Leisure = vow ceremonies, concordance theater, patronage of Ministorum autumn liturgy. Ribbon Houses host quieter rooms where common advocates may stand without being eaten. **Sound:** High Gothic cooled with iron humor; longer sentences; *triple stamp kiss* said with teeth. Shame = waiting in a common window on purpose and being photographed. Success = compelled hearing that ends with three stamps and a rival’s silence.

## 3. Society

Rank is **readable in ribbon, floor, and wait**. Humiliation is public voiding of a name, closed-window exile, or forced scrub duty in vault waste. Street law favors **form over fist** until fists protect forms; then Arbites arrive. Local governance is ministry-stack and noble tithe-house mesh with planetary governor optics under Chapter macro-shadow. Representation for commons = guild petitions and advocate vouchers; for rabble = overflow noise until batons. Rationing is **seal-issued** from tithe floors. Tax-as-wound is the **docket that never clears** — fees, amendments, and concordance bribes that starve a household while leaving them “legally alive.”

Marriage is **contract-first**; love is **not illegal**, merely **badly documented**. Children are registered to quota and bloodline floors; unregistered births are future *misfile* fuel.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Street kin; ribbon gangs that are half mutual aid; no marriage theater — cohabitation until a clerk demands a form. Humiliation = pink-slip ghosting.
- **Common:** Scribe bloodlines; contract marriages with guild witnesses; advocate fees as dowry-adjacent. Humiliation = public amendment pinning for a relative’s false concordance.
- **Upper:** Tithe-house dynasties; autumn vows as politics; Ribbon Houses marry “down” into mid-scribe talent to keep floor-passes honest. Humiliation = vault-key revocation.

**Friction matrix**

| Pressure | Rabble | Common | Upper |
|----------|--------|--------|-------|
| Who is “forgotten” | Always / voided | By Terra; not by district if seals clean | By Terra; refuse by Castra-Vetera |
| Holy object | Ribbon, slip stub | Triple stamp, docket plaque | House seal, private window, autumn token |
| Ministorum | Useless / dangerous when they steal queue time | Wrong accent, sometimes useful for burial books | Patronage + status mask; autumn liturgy as optics |
| Batavi presence | Watch / fear / dark pride when Caliper holds a facade | Shared Sweat respect; Lex quotes as weather | Necessary shadow; want influence without being owned; prefer Kessler’s optics to forge wrath |

## 4. Religion

**Official creed:** Ecclesiarchy-baptized Imperial cult — God-Emperor language in chapels and on registry headers.  
**Local accent (Porta Nihili / grey-clerk piety):** Holiness is **procedure**. Penance is **amended forms**. Pilgrimage is **appeal windows**. Concordance between hostile departments is the nearest thing to miracle the commons trust. Stamp booth / concordance booth often outranks chapel when a receipt is needed; chapels still own baptism, marriage blessing, and burial books — and therefore own lethal leverage when those books are *misfiled*.

Priests vs laity: priests who cannot read a docket are entertainment or suspect; priests who keep burial dates honest earn *ribbon saint* respect. Holidays that stop work: autumn vow cycles, ribbon-saint memorial, tithe-seal feast days (work stops for Upper; Common gets half-band; Rabble gets closed windows and hunger). Texts in practice: High Gothic collects, local form-litanies, Lex excerpts posted at Arbites doors. Religious punishment: public amendment, chapel denial of burial stamp, referral to Arbites when false concordance touches sacred books.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Chapel = place you are turned away when you smell of vault waste. Faith = holding the slip. Sin language = *misfile* even when they mean theft.
- **Common:** Baptize in grey-clerk cant; prefer concordance booth to ecstatic sermon; fear burial-book errors more than hellfire speeches.
- **Upper:** Fund autumn liturgy; auction piety carefully; Ribbon Houses prefer quiet correct devotion that survives audit.

**Contrast only:** Batavi honor **Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight** — not Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Shared words on seals and aquilas ≠ shared creed. Mortals may call a Batavi “the Emperor’s hand” in awe; that is civilian optics, not Chapter theology, and not a Founding claim.

## 5. Economy

**World function / export:** Judgment, tithe governance, registry storage, noble contract brokerage, legal and archival services for the Central Bastion mesh. Paper-light industry (binders, slate, incense scrubbers) supports the brain; heavy forge output is imported from Noviomagus rumor and sealed routes. Wealth means **vault depth**, **window priority**, **advocate retainers**, and **clean concordance credit** — not scrap tons or silo elevators.

Work runs on **dockets and hour-bands**. Tithe payment is seal-and-table: ministries convert planetary and house obligations into stamped debt; failure smell is **ink-rot and panic incense** in a closed audit hall — clerks speaking too quietly, Arbites at the door, autumn holographs switched to “mourning cycle.” Exemptions: noble treaty rooms, Chapter-adjacent sealed facilities (including ***Depositorium Cataphractae*** sustainment under *Secunda* — mortals do not audit it), certain Ministorum and Mechanicus liaison suites on Schedule K lists. Real markets: ribbon vendors, advocate fees, forged-slip black markets, tea-stall licenses. Ledger fiction: “everyone is filed” — rabble know otherwise.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Cash is ribbon and favor; sell queue standing; fail tithe by never being correctly named. Failure smell = eviction to overflow without a number.
- **Common:** Wages in seal-credit and ration bands; guild dues; advocate mortgages. False concordance for tax relief is the economy’s favorite damnation.
- **Upper:** Tithe-house ledgers, private vault capital, patronage markets. Wealth displayed as **depth of access**, not gold noise. Ribbon Houses broker fees between strata.

## 6. Education

Literacy is **power and liability**. Who learns: scribe bloodlines by inheritance; Common by guild and tithe school; Upper by house tutors and ministry academies; Rabble by street form-running and stolen hour-band scraps — enough to survive a queue, rarely enough to win a concordance.

**Tracks:** guild copyist · house advocate · chapel burial-book · Administratum clerk · PDF Lex primer · (rare) Mechanicus liaison slate for vault climate gear.  
**Curriculum-as-virtue:** exact nouns, death-date honesty, three-department patience, contempt for empty thunder.  
**Cost:** years of eye-strain and lung-scrub from incense; patronage debts; bodies lost to draft when school seals fail. Literacy *does* this: lets you amend before you starve — or forge a **false concordance** and damn a floor.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Oral queue lore; mark recognition; “don’t sign what you can’t read” as survival proverb often broken by hunger.
- **Common:** Tithe schools; scribe apprenticeships; advocate voucher exams. Pride = reading a variance aloud without trembling.
- **Upper:** Academy High Gothic; Lex geometry for officer houses; Ribbon Houses train bilingual floor speech (common docket + noble hearing).
