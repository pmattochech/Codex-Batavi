## B2. Hard edges (do not merge)

1. **Faith:** Populi = Ecclesiarchy-baptized Porta Nihili — God loves **what does not buckle**; confession is **bone scan**; holiness often lives in **strap booth** over chapel. QVARTA = **Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight** — oath, Wall geometry, Analytical Silence. Shared words (*hold*, *seal*, *Throne*, *duty*) ≠ shared creed. Do not baptize brothers into yield-cult or rewrite guild piety as *Axiom*.

2. **Verticals / keys:** Mortals sorted by **load grade** and **strap stamp** (secondary: **knee scripture**). Chapter moves on **parallel keys** — precinct fences, billet seals, relay floors mortals cannot claim. Brothers ≠ Upper by clean knees; shaft magnates ≠ Legatus by registry ink. A Brace House guest pass opens **civil–Chapter rooms**, not Strategium.

3. **Shame ≠ false yield:** Mortal sin = claiming load / scan / strap blessing / marriage contribution you did not carry. QVARTA shame lattice (when it bites) is **rotation debt**, **amber delay contempt**, **seal-line failure**, **treating Castellan absence as victory leave** (Legatus Ring failure-risk) — **not** guild false yield. Street may rhyme “soft knee” at a brother who will not chase; *Strategium* does not file that as civic sin. Do not equate Valestrand’s withheld fire with a forged scan.

4. **Bridge ≠ billet:** **Brace Houses** = named lower-upper band — orthotic contracts, mid-shaft load registries, Shared Sweat kitchens, guest orthotic ink, *face-hold*. Soft power. **Not** Vexilla rank, **not** billet authority, **not** March clearance. Martha’s-kitchen-class continuity (when it appears on Tertius civil floors) is **civil mirror**, not Chapter affection theater.

5. **Xenos / civic hate ≠ Chapter geometry:** Civic Tyranid fear, Auxilia export grief, “the choir goes wrong in the head,” and PDF static-hold pride ≠ friend–foe targeting doctrine, March-edge / March-deep authorization, Genetor / Confessor Vexilli review, or Silence Cohort cult capture. Mortals may hate the swarm as weather; brothers hold **threat geometry** with receipts. No civic “welcome” for xenos retainers; no tavern right to name sealed *Curia* thresholds.

**Surface rhyme lock — March-of-the-Machine:** Allowed in populi mouths as **long siege weather**, **amber rotation gossip**, **held crest rumor**, **missile trades that wait**, Shadow as choir-wrong-in-the-head. **Forbidden** as mortal fact: March-deep clearance law, Shadow-threshold logs, Genetor Vexilli veto text, Versibar / clinical curse taxonomy, Founding stamps.

## B4. Bleed both ways

| Surface | → QVARTA | → populi |
|---------|------------|----------|
| **Heel-click / boot seal** | Civilian two-tap greeting (PDF / guild folk echo of the Warden) migrates into billet etiquette as recognition of seal integrity | Valestrand’s briefing tell (two taps = clearance already live) leaks into street myth: “the Grav Lock listens to ankles” |
| **Shared Sweat** | Guard / PDF / stretcher guilds dig under slide with Wall; class briefly flattens in dust | Plans alongside mortal commanders; hierarchy returns after; civic pride without kinship claim |
| **Yield / receipt culture** | Load bells and stamp piety → brothers’ preference for **missile trades with receipts** and logged rotation | Industrial war tempo under QVARTA reads to clerks as “held hours” — ammo and crest time as planetary weather |
| **Slide choir lock** | Stratum learned in the wait (Rabble stairs / Common stamp windows / Upper sealed galleries) | Weather = logistics; no charity theater; QVARTA priority geometry when fences open |
| **Orthotic / gravitic rhyme** | Brace shops and boot-debt slang → easiest Mechanicus–Chapter handshake language | Mechanicus claims the rhyme as their seal; mortals learn brothers “walk debt” like honest workers |
| **Silence under pressure** | Men of Iron hangover / open-channel quiet near March-edge billets (habit) | Analytical Silence undercuts empty thunder; yield-priests rise, ecstatic missionaries fall |
| **Death map** | Wall-name rumors filed next to boot-debt tables | Separate Chapter vault / Reclusiam law — civic Hold Walls ≠ Astartes crypt |
| **Guest orthotic ink / Brace House** | Soft power into Chapter-civil rooms | QVARTA paperwork on same civil stack — cold; ink ≠ affection |

**Populi → QVARTA (short):**  
Brace cant, heel honesty, and load-bell metronomy teach brothers that **stillness that holds** is local virtue — Valestrand’s withheld fire finds weather that already respects amber. Orthotic clergy and PDF shield pairs make Shared Sweat cheap to start and hard to romanticize. False-yield scandal culture makes QVARTA contempt for forged fitness scans feel native without importing guild courts into *Axiom*.

**Populi → QVARTA (deep examples):**

1. **Boot-debt as shared weather** — Aspirants and Auxilia cadres arrive already speaking ankles. Line sergeants do not invent “boot debt”; they **militarize** a civic phrase into gravitic boot inspection. The bleed is vocabulary ownership: mortals mean next-shift pain; brothers mean seal integrity under 2.8G and trench collapse risk.

2. **Load festival posture** — Brothers billeted near festival plazas absorb the civilian contempt for swaggering carry. QVARTA parade culture stays thin; grey plate that **stands** outranks grey plate that **performs**. This rhymes with semi-auto crest law without teaching civilians the sealed reason.

3. **Slide stretcher guilds** — Civilian orthotic medicae who haul under choir dust teach brothers that mortal courage is **strap-share**, not speech. Shared Sweat after-action praise (when it happens) names the guild, not a Ministorum miracle.

**Vexilla → populi (short):**  
Shared Sweat as dark civic pride; quiet “hours and meters” priests rise; held-crest rumor explains why pursuit feels like sin on the flats; thin Ruhe / March-line gossip without sealed doctrine; parallel keys teach Brace Houses *face-hold* without wolf rank; industrial grey fashion bleeds from billet edges into Common brace chrome.

**Vexilla → populi (deep examples):**

1. **Shingle Warden folk etiquette** — Two heel taps become PDF corridor greeting and guild door habit. Mortals believe they imitate the Legatus; QVARTA treats the taps as **seal check**, not blessing. Fiction fuel: a Rabble porter taps heels at an Arbites gate and gets laughed at — wrong vertical.

2. **Amber rotation gossip** — Long sieges leak “amber” into tavern talk as *the Wall won’t chase today*. Correct as surface rhyme to Valestrand’s flaw (contempt for downslope glory). Wrong if clerks invent March-deep clearance boards as public law.

3. **Receipt fire myth** — Civilians see missile trades that wait and invent holiness: God loves the receipt. Useful Porta Nihili accent; dangerous if they claim to own *Armourium* acceptance ritual. Brothers do not correct theology in the ditch — they correct **geometry**.

Brothers who mock soft knees in mortal hearing risk collapsing hard edge #3 if they file civic sin as Chapter shame.

| **Adeptus Administratum** | Load registries vs parallel Chapter keys — two verticals. Hates unlogged Chapter guest traffic on civil floors. Filing false yield as clean tithe is civic sin that can starve Auxilia drafts QVARTA needs as ecology, not as worshippers. |
| **Adeptus Ministorum / Ecclesiarchy** | Accent conflict. Yield-first local creed vs Ancestor-Sovereign. Empty thunder falls when brothers stand silent under dust; baptism records remain useful. Do not merge chapel *face-hold* with *Axiom*. |

| **Auxilia intake / conditioning offices** | World-function flesh export and neophyte-support ecology. Surface contact with Batavi conditioning shadow. Pride and grief braided; no Founding lecture at the intake desk. |
| **Local noble houses / tithe dynasties** | Want fence influence. Peak contact is patronage theater; QVARTA answers with hours and meters. |
| **Criminal / underhive powers** | Scan forgers and strap-theft rings seed false yield. Chapter does not run guild crime courts; Arbites / guilds do. Bleed risk: forged “Warden fitness” papers sold near billet spines. |

**Absent at the seam (still informative):** Adepta Sororitas, Custodes, Officio Assassinorum — no Tertius civic robe that mediates QVARTA contact. Inquisition / other Astartes = sealed rumor only — do not thicken into public March theology.

## B7. Fiction tells

- **Mortal → QVARTA signal:** Who **sweats and stays still** beside them without a sermon — stretcher strap shared under slide-dust; heel seals checked before a speech; a scan receipt asked for before a blessing.

- **Brother → populi signal:** **Strap booth → load grade gate → heel-click threshold**, then civil fence vs precinct seal; hours and meters on the vox; grey plate that waits with receipts while the choir goes ugly.

- **Wrong:**
  - Hive-king Castellan / feast-king Valestrand.
  - Brace House officer as QVARTA rank.
  - False yield = March shame / rotation debt / amber delay.
  - Secundus lamp-mystery or Scorched penal theology as Tertius–QVARTA daily bleed grammar.
  - Dumping March-deep / *Curia* / Genetor veto / gene-seed / Founding into tavern mouths as fact.
  - Collapsing Batavi Ancestor-Sovereign into Ministorum God-Emperor ecstasy.
  - Opaque friend–foe abbreviations; civic “welcome” for xenos retainers.
  - Treating gravitic boots as mortal PDF standard issue rather than Chapter doctrine with civic **rhyme**.

- **Right:**
  - Slide choir lock sorting classes while QVARTA holds crest geometry beyond the fence.
  - Shared Sweat silence under dust; hierarchy returns after.
  - Shingle Warden folk etiquette (two taps) misapplied across verticals for comedy or tragedy.
  - False yield scandal in guild court while brothers refuse downslope glory on amber — parallel, not identical.
  - Orthotic clergy and Mechanicus arguing who owns the ankle-rhyme.
  - Thin Ruhe / held-line rumor: “The Wall held the spine. The choir went wrong in the head. He still wouldn’t chase.” — no sealed taxonomy.
  - God loves what does not buckle (mortal) standing next to *We hold* / Throne-weight (Batavi) without merger.
