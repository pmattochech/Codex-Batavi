## 0. Preamble

### 0.1 Physical frame — seed as budget

**Elysium-Minor** hangs at **surgical distance** from *Sol-Vetera*’s clean light: close enough that Administratum sky-schedules can sell **“true gold hour”** postcards, far enough that forge soot and Black Rain never stain the brochure. Official filing calls it a **paradise world** — flawless **artificial skies**, engineered **beaches**, climate held in perpetual resort mildness. Gravity sits near Terran comfort; the air tastes faintly sweet, then floral, then **pink** at the edge of notice. Locals stop noticing. Off-worlders joke about **honeymoon lungs** until the first week’s headache teaches them the atmosphere is **doing work**.

Citizens think in **leases**, **rest-passes**, **conception tallies**, and **quota windows**. Slow death here is not acid rain or oxygen fire — it is **mask fatigue**: the long quiet knowledge that the beach is a **factory floor painted blue**, and that bodies are the line’s feedstock. Provincial feel is **resort-imperial**: High Gothic on gate plaques, Ministorum chapels with salt-white stone, Host guilds speaking hospitality cant as if leisure were liturgy. Under the smile, **biology is the export** — births measured for Noviomagus grinders and Incus-Gravis hunger, not bolts or grain tons.

**Budget metaphor:** **seed**. Time, food, perfume, surf sound, and soft confession all convert into **audited fertility product**. A clean lease is wealth; a wasted conception outside quota is theft from the Throne’s future labor pool — moralized before it is criminalized.

### 0.2 Fork

**Not Noviomagus:** no Black Rain elevator police, no rust as daily god, no gear prayer as default virtue. Work here is **rest** that must **reproduce**.  
**Not Khan-Bator:** calories arrive as guest trays and nursery paste, not slime barges; oxygen panic is imported gossip, not local law.  
**Not Castra-Vetera Prime:** judgment thrives elsewhere; Elysium sells **absolution-by-vacation** and files the ledger in midwife halls.  
**Porta Nihili accent:** faith of work becomes **rest virtue** — usefulness earns sleep; sleep must **pay back in children**. Outsiders misread **decadence** (wine, surf, soft lighting). Spreadsheets say **strategic incubator**. The fork is **mask liturgy**: paradise as **factory input**.

### 0.3 Sorting border (class police)

- **Primary sorter:** **Rest-pass / lease grade** (who may walk the glass beach as guest vs who scrubs it).  
- **Secondary sorter:** **Fertility ledger status** (quota-clean · quota-debt · host-service · audit-flag).  
- **Felt before a clerk speaks:** perfume density, dome tint, whether a Host greets you with a smile that **counts**, and whether an audit confessor’s soft voice finds your pulse before your name.

| Stratum | Rest-pass / lease exposure | Fertility-ledger rights | Meaning |
|---------|----------------------------|-------------------------|---------|
| **Rabble** | Service corridors, night scrub, duct and pink-filter labor; guest beaches only as invisible hands | Rarely on guest quota; births logged as **host-labor product** or **unpassaged**; high audit-flag risk if caught **wasting seed** in service wings | Paradise is wallpaper they wash; class is the smell of bleach under floral spray |
| **Common** | Time-boxed rest-reward leases (forge/Guard productivity medals, Shared Sweat billets, midwife plaques) | Guest conception windows; midwife-clerk stamps; private chapel offerings with receipts | Leisure is **earned prayer**; a missed quota window feels like a failed Mass |
| **Upper** | Host-house keys, honeymoon-dome ownership, multi-lease brokerage | Audit patronage, midwife-Administratum ranks, soft-confessor dinner rights | Sell the mask; fear spreadsheet truth more than heresy sermons |

**Worked intersections**

1. **Glass-wave promenade at shift change** — Common guests arrive in rest colors; Rabble Hosts-in-training vanish into service doors; Upper Host elders watch from tinted balconies that never fog.  
2. **Midwife hall after two-sun wedding** — Common couples file conception offerings; Rabble scrubbers empty offering bowls; Upper midwife-Administratum signs the hash that feeds System I biomass math.  
3. **Soft confession booth** — blood taken kindly; class learned by whether the needle is apologized for, priced, or forgotten.  
4. **Pink-filter crawl** — Rabble only; Common guests told the air is “blessed rest”; Upper Hosts know duct schedules and never speak them at dinner.

**Fiction tells for the border:** cracked guest badge → midwife plaque → Host smile that opens a dome without asking. Insult: calling a scrubber a **guest**. Mercy: an extra rest-hour or a midwife stamp that clears a false flag.

### 0.5 Local mortal sin & civil / Apothecarion read

- **Local mortal sin — wasting seed:** Any conception, fertility product, or registered reproductive labor diverted **outside audited quota** — unlogged service-wing births sold for black rest-hours, guest conception skipped then faked, pheromone-dosed food diverted to non-quota bodies, midwife stamps forged for barren leases. Enforced by midwife halls, soft confessors (blood and quiet), Host guild shame courts, and Administratum fertility ledgers. **Class-colored:** Rabble — unlogged birth in the crawl; Common — missed window then fake offering; Upper — diversion of nursery product for house prestige or off-world sale.
- **Legatus public read:** **N/A**.
- **Civil / Apothecarion authority (mortal):** The **White Hand’s soft auditors** / rest-house confessors — public face of Drusus’s demographic audit and the lease office that grants paradise. Surface only: blood taken kindly, air that “helps rest,” food that “blesses fertility.” Mortals do **not** receive sealed *Apothecarion* clinical taxonomy as street fact.

## 1. Military (mortal)

Elysium-Minor is not a Wall fortress-world. Mortal force exists to **protect the mask**, **police lease fraud**, and **shepherd rest-reward billets** — including Guard and PDF veterans on productivity medals, and occasional Shared Sweat guests whose presence is pride and fear in equal measure. When the Wall is late elsewhere, Elysium’s first deaths are **corridor** deaths: riot at a denied rest-gate, Host shame beating that becomes Arbites paperwork, nursery panic when an audit flag locks a dome.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Levy fodder for dome perimeter and scrub-riot pickets; first bodies when guest panic turns; used as human buffers at service doors. Rarely trusted with guest-facing arms.
- **Common:** Career PDF of the Rest Cordons — polite armor, soft helmets, real shot for lease fraud and seed-diversion gangs. Shared Sweat billet veterans treated as temporary aristocracy of scars; expected to conceive on schedule like everyone else.
- **Upper:** Officer Hosts and midwife-liaison security houses; private dome guards; soft power over which riots become “incidents.” Prefer Arbites theater that never reaches the brochure.

**Worked examples**

- **Draft:** Productivity medals convert forge and Guard service into rest-passes; refusal to accept a lease can be filed as **ingratitude toward the Throne’s sleep** — social death before legal death.
- **Riot geometry:** Glass promenades are wide for beauty and for **funneling**; service doors lock from guest side. Rabble know the kill-box; Common guests learn it the first time a Host smile hardens.
- **Arbites contact:** Thin on beaches, thick at midwife halls and soft booths — they treat **wasting seed** adjacent to theft. Loud heresy is rarer than quiet diversion.
- **Shared Sweat surfaces:** Batavi billet guests rest in sealed wings; mortals meet them as **quiet statues who earned sleep**. Contact is hospitality trays, medical check-ins, and the rumor that the White Hand watches the same air. No mortal street doctrine of Chapter clinical law — only the felt fact that paradise serves the Wall’s appetite for bodies.

## 2. Culture

Amenities are the product: engineered surf (**glass wave** sound piped under sand), pink-gold artificial dusk, Host smiles timed to lease grade. Aesthetic is **resort-imperial** — white stone, soft lighting, floral spray over bleach. Public shame is a **denied smile** or a midwife flag worn as gossip; public success is a **two-sun wedding** under both visible stars of the triad from a honeymoon dome, or a rest-pass renewed without audit blood.

Leisure is scarce for Rabble, bought for Common, brokered for Upper. Forbidden leisure: black beaches outside lease hours, unlogged conception parties, “true silence” rooms where pink air is filtered out (sold as luxury, prosecuted as seed-waste facilitation when births drop).

Non-Mass rites: Host greeting liturgy, glass-wave watches, conception offerings, pink-saint jokes told only in service corridors, lease-renewal toasts with nursery paste.

**By class — include Sound**

- **Rabble:** Amenities = leftover trays and stolen five minutes of fake surf between scrub shifts. Aesthetic: bleach under floral. Shame = being seen by a guest. Success = a Host-probation nod that moves you off night duct. **Sound:** Short, dark, *pink-saint* jokes; sentences cut like tray tickets.
- **Common:** Amenities = timed beach blocks, chapel salt-white Mass, midwife tea after offering. Aesthetic: rest colors, clean badges. Shame = audit flag; success = two-sun wedding photo and quota-clean stamp. **Sound:** Grateful exhaustion; soft contempt for loud off-world priests; hospitality cant learned like prayer.
- **Upper:** Amenities = private dome weather, audit dinners, Guest-of-Guests lists. Aesthetic: mask perfection — never let a scrubber appear in the frame. Shame = demographic hash that blushes; success = brokerage of whole rest cohorts. **Sound:** Cool hospitality High Gothic; fertility cant under compliments. Host-probation: quieter, less auctioned.

## 3. Society

Rank is **lease-visible**. Humiliation is the Host smile that does not open a door. Street law is Host guild custom until midwife or Arbites stamp arrives. Local governance mixes **Rest-Reward Civil Office** (leases), **Midwife-Administratum** (fertility ledgers), and **Host courts** (hospitality shame). Representation is thin: Common rest-guests have complaint booths that smell like perfume and delay; Rabble have laundry tags.

Rationing hits nursery paste, pink-filter cartridges, and guest-hours. Tax-as-wound arrives as **demographic tithe** — bodies owed — felt as shame when a window is missed, not only as coin.

Marriage/contract: **two-sun wedding** as prestige form; contracts often include conception windows and midwife witnesses. Host-probation houses marry for smile-discipline and liaison desks. Divorce is ledger partition first.

Death/name geography: marble nurseries for Upper losses; Common names on chapel walls beside rest-pass numbers; Rabble names on laundry tags and duct graffiti. Wall rumors arrive as guest gossip — civic memorial, not *Reclusiam* crypt law.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Rank = which service door you may open. Humiliation = guest laughter. Street law = Host beatings that never reach Arbites if the mask holds.
- **Common:** Rank = plaque + window. Humiliation = public midwife flag. Governance felt as stamp booths more than councils.
- **Upper:** Rank = dome keys and audit dinner seats. Humiliation = soft confessor who takes blood at the wrong party. Governance = they *are* the smile the world sells.

**Friction matrix**

| Pressure | Rabble | Common | Upper |
|----------|--------|--------|-------|
| Who is “forgotten” | Always — brochure-blind | By Terra; remembered by midwife ledger | By Terra; refuse to be by System I procurement |
| Holy object | Scrub kit, duct mask | Rest-pass, midwife stamp, offering bowl | Dome key, clean demographic hash |
| Ministorum | Useful when soft; dangerous when loud | Wrong accent if anti-rest; useful for Mass cover | Patronage tool + status mask |
| Batavi presence | Watch / fear / dark pride when billets arrive | Shared Sweat respect; rest beside giants | Necessary shadow; want influence without admitting the farm |

## 4. Religion

Official creed remains Ecclesiarchy-baptized: God-Emperor on the chapel wall, Mass in salt-white stone, feast days that stop guest work when the Rest Office allows. Local accent is **rest virtue** — *“the Throne grants sleep to the useful.”* Private chapels quietly take **conception offerings** (coin, blood-prick, named vow) with receipts. Guilt and glory share a bed: leisure is holy when it **reproduces**; leisure that refuses the ledger is suspect.

Priests vs laity: public preachers compete with **audit confessors** who speak softly and take blood. Stamp booth vs chapel: midwife halls often win when holiness needs a receipt — *“God saw the seal.”* Holidays: two-sun wedding seasons, rest-renewal vigils, pink-saint joke-days in service wings (never on guest calendars). Texts in practice: lease catechisms, midwife manuals, Mass primers. Punishment: public denial of smile, chapel penance that includes a new conception window, Arbites when diversion is proven.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Chapel when forced; real liturgy is scrub-before-dawn. Pink-saint jokes as anti-hagiography — first fertility martyr as bitter humor, not guest devotion.
- **Common:** Mass for cover and comfort; conception offering as the prayer that counts; soft confessors feared and trusted.
- **Upper:** Chapter-house patronage of Ministorum where useful; audit dinners as parallel holiness; loud anti-incubator preaching treated as threat to the mask.

**Contrast only:** Batavi honor **Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight** — not Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Population still uses chapel language; the **feel** is rest-and-seed first. Shared words ≠ shared creed.

## 5. Economy

**World function/export:** **Biology** — audited births and rest-stabilized labor returned to forge and Guard grinders. Secondary: hospitality brokerage, midwife training export, soft medical tourism under demographic cover.

Work runs on Host shifts, midwife halls, Rest Office windows, pink-filter maintenance, nursery lines. Tithe payment is **bodies on the hash** timed to System I demand. **Failure smell:** empty nursery wards, midwife flags blooming on guest badges, soft confessors appearing at dinner, Host smiles that stop opening doors, floral spray turned up to hide bleach panic.

Exemptions: medically sealed barren leases (rare, expensive, often temporary), Host-probation service credits, Apothecarion audit wards that freeze a dome for “rest health.” Real markets: black rest-hours, forged stamps, diverted nursery paste. Ledger fiction: paradise postcards, “voluntary” conception piety, productivity medals that erase the farm language.

Wealth under this sky: **renewable lease**, **quota-clean blood**, **dome weather you control**. Coin without a stamp is decoration.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Paid in tray rights and filter cartridges; wealth = a day off the duct; failure smell = bleach without floral cover.
- **Common:** Paid in rest-hours and midwife clearance; wealth = renewed pass; failure smell = flag on the badge.
- **Upper:** Paid in brokerage fees and audit favor; wealth = other people’s leases; failure smell = hash that blushes in Noviomagus eyes.

## 6. Education

Who learns: Host academies teach smile-discipline and lease law; midwife colleges teach stamps, blood, and quota math; Common guest catechism teaches rest virtue in three days; Rabble learn duct maps and when to vanish. Who is kept dark: full pheromone and stimulant doctrine stays **soft rumor** — Common know the air “helps”; Upper Hosts and midwife chiefs know schedules; Rabble feel headaches and joke about pink saints.

Tracks: Host guild · midwife-Administratum · chapel · Rest Office clerk · duct/service apprenticeship. Curriculum-as-virtue: usefulness earns sleep; sleep repays in children; the mask must not slip. Cost: years of Host-probation smile work; bodies lost to audit flags; patronage for dome schools. Literacy *does* lease law, midwife hashes, and complaint forms that never succeed without a smile sponsor.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Apprenticeship by bruise; literacy optional; moral curriculum = don’t be seen.
- **Common:** Guest catechism + midwife literacy for stamps; virtue = clean window.
- **Upper:** Host academy + audit etiquette; virtue = sell paradise without saying **farm**.
