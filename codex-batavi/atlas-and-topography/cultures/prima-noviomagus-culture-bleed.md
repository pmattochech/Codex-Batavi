## B2. Hard edges (do not merge)

Ratified locks — **do not soften** in fiction or wiki summary.

1. **Faith:** Populi = Ecclesiarchy-baptized on record + **throughput theology** (seals, stamps, logged torque). PRIMA / Batavi = **Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight** — oath, Wall geometry, *Axiom* discipline — **not** Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Shared words (“seal,” “hold,” “duty,” “we hold”) ≠ shared creed. A brother who taps a duty belt after forge cant is borrowing friction habit, not converting to booth orthodoxy; a stamp clerk who whispers *We hold* is rhyming with the Wall, not joining *Reclusiam*.

2. **Verticals / keys:** Mortals are sorted by **Black Rain** exposure / filter grade and **elevator rights** (denied → shift plaque → house key → Solis bridge pass). Chapter precincts = **parallel keys** — a second vertical that does not sit “above” upper class. Brothers are **not** peak elevator magnates; magnates are **not** Legatus by money or guest ink. Wrong-car boarding is civilian shame; wrong Chapter floor is trespass or death — different police, different meaning.

3. **Shame ≠ unlogged scrap:** PRIMA **obligation field** / rectification charge (Valerian / Malakor debt the *Alabarda* lattice carries — *“Debt we carry — not a crime you committed”* per [vexilla §2.2](../../personae-command-index/intro-and-heraldry/vexilla-by-vexilla-design.md)) is **not** mortal **unlogged scrap** or **missed torque**. Populi sin is ledger failure under rain. Chapter shame is sealed continuity geometry under humiliation. Thin street rumor may say “the first seat broke; the Wall still holds” — it must **not** teach Versibar catechism, *Lupercal* naming, or equate obligation with scrap diversion.

4. **Bridge ≠ billet (Solis ≠ Vexilla rank):** **Solis family / Solis-tier households** = Phase A **lower-upper bridge band** — soft power via kitchen continuity, Shared Sweat *face-hold*, guest ink, bridge passes into Chapter-**civil** rooms. Martha’s kitchen = Chapter soft door (Triumvirate / Alaric Visage-law space elsewhere documented). Solis band is the **civil mirror**, not the same institution as Vexilla billet, Legatus staff, or PRIMA line. Bridge passes open outer galleries — they do **not** own Strategium floors or wolf rank.

5. **Xenos / civic hate ≠ Chapter geometry:** Civic **Ork-hate**, Guard/PDF Shared Sweat, and wet-shaft scrap wars are daily mortal weather. Chapter threat geometry (friend–foe discrimination under auspex law, kill-box priority, specialty-cohort tasking) is **not** the same file. No civic “welcome” for Eldar retainers or xenos “specialists.” Mortals who sweat beside brothers against Orks still misread if they claim kinship or import Chapter kill doctrine as street catechism.

## B4. Bleed both ways

### Surface matrix

| Surface | → PRIMA (what sticks on brothers) | → populi (what sticks on mortals) |
|---------|--------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| **Gear prayer** | Common shift rite (three knuckle taps) migrates into Batavi duty-belt friction as borrowed habit — not booth creed | Seen as Wall manners that “rhyme” with seal piety; mortals over-read kinship if they treat the tap as shared Mass |
| **Shared Sweat** | Plans alongside Guard/PDF; ditch flattens rain class briefly; hierarchy returns after dry gallery | Civic pride and dark pride; primary contact with brothers without sermon; misread risk = “we are kin now” |
| **Ammo / yield / clocks** | Industrial war tempo under Corbec; seal-as-prayer becomes queue discipline and acceptance trials | Ammo queues, embarkation tallies, line-replacement pressing Third Shift Concord bands; calibration seat feels like **clocks**, not parade |
| **Storm lock** | Weather = logistics; priority geometry; no charity theater | Stratum learned in the wait (filter → plaque → car); when PRIMA present, Chapter priority reads as second weather beside rain |
| **Guest ink / bridge passes** | Solis ink into Chapter-civil rooms; Watch / PRIMA paperwork may share civil stack — cold, not affectionate | Soft power without shaft ownership; *face-hold* taught by proximity to parallel keys — still mortal |
| **Death map** | Wall-names enter mortal rumor; PRIMA capital crypt stays Chapter | Separate Chapter-supreme vault; “wolf’s floor” taboo; do not file brothers’ dead as district open-rain weather |
| **Forge cant / receipts** | Cant and receipt silence feed Analytical Silence + tally culture under Corbec | Quiet priests rise; empty thunder falls; Warden Gauge myth strengthens when clocks stay honest |
| **Exhausted loyalty** | Weather Corbec’s continuity answers — discipline over comfort through Vigil-century shadow | *Rain-closed face* as civic virtue; loyalty that does not need audience |

### Populi → PRIMA (short + deep)

**Short:** Forge cant and receipt holiness train brothers toward **silence + tallies**; gear prayer migrates onto duty belts; *limitanei* “we built the wall” **rhymes** with wall-as-instrument (rhyme only — not progenitor proof); exhausted loyalty is the weather Corbec’s continuity answers; stamp-booth contempt for empty speech undercuts missionary theater that would waste ammo time.

**Deep examples:**

1. **Receipt → tally:** A Third Shift Concord stamp clerk who will not bless a cargo without a clean seal teaches the same moral shape Corbec uses when another Legatus disputes *Armourium* queue priority — numbers over shout. Brothers do not become clerks; they inherit the **anti-theater** pressure of a forge capital.

2. **Gear prayer migration:** Three knuckle taps begin as common shift orthodoxy. On PRIMA plate they become friction habit before embarkation or belt check — **borrowed**, never owned as Ministorum rite. *Reclusiam* may tolerate the gesture as discipline; it does not baptize it.

3. **Limitanei rhyme:** Urban “we built the wall” pride on Noviomagus flatters brothers who hear Shared Sweat as partnership. Doctrine still forbids reading that pride as Founding genealogy or civic ownership of Castra-Lupus.

4. **Rain as logistics teacher:** Storm-lock waits train civilians in stratum. Brothers operating on the forge lip learn that **charity theater** in open Black Rain is waste — weather is a queue problem, matching Corbec’s intolerance of heroic ammunition waste.

### PRIMA → populi (short + deep)

**Short:** Shared Sweat becomes civic pride without kinship deed; quiet priests rise where empty thunder falls; calibration seat = ammo queues not parade; thin Valerian gossip allowed without sealed doctrine; parallel keys teach Solis *face-hold* without wolf rank; Warden Gauge myth locks Corbec as clocks, not feast.

**Deep examples:**

1. **Shared Sweat as pride, not kinship:** PDF canopy barracks and stamp booths in the Third Shift Concord forge-lip band muster with PRIMA line-replacement tempo. Mortals who sweat without sermon read correctly. Mortals who claim “the Wall adopted us” misread — hierarchy returns with the next dry gallery.

2. **Quiet over thunder:** Batavi Analytical Silence and Corbec’s few-word tallies make loud Ministorum imports look like stolen shift time. District chapels that dig in Shared Sweat keep face; ecstatic idle that ignores rain schedule loses it.

3. **Calibration as queue weather:** Other Vexilla’ needs press through Noviomagus as ammo and acceptance trials. Common bands feel PRIMA as **tempo on the booth**, not as Castellan theater. Upper magnates who try to own that tempo as patronage fail the hard edge on verticals.

4. **Thin Valerian aftermath (allowed rumor vs seal):** See worked lock **B4.2** below — street may know fracture and hold; street must not know sealed names or clinical Versibar law.

5. **Parallel keys → Solis manners:** Lower-upper bridge houses learn how to enter Chapter-civil outer galleries without claiming shaft or billet. That education is bleed, not promotion into PRIMA.

### B4.1 Worked lock — district overlap (*Third Shift Concord* / forge-lip billet)

**Draft lock (ratified):** PRIMA line-replacement and ammo acceptance trials press the **Third Shift Concord** middle-stack band (Phase A common class). Solis **bridge passes** open Chapter-civil rooms on the **Castra-Lupus outer galleries**, not Strategium floors. Overlap zone = stamp booths + PDF canopy barracks within one rain-schedule district of the forge lip — Shared Sweat musters here; peak magnate cars do not.

**Fiction use:** Open on rain schedule slate + plaque window; show who boards which car; put brothers and PDF in the same ditch **without** kinship speech; close with dry-gallery hierarchy restored. Wrong beat: a magnate car “hosting” the muster as patronage feast.

**Hard edge reminder:** No sealed names, no Versibar catechism, no *Lupercal* in mortal mouths as fact. Obligation field ≠ unlogged scrap.

**Class Sound at contact (bleed cue):**

- **Rabble** near brothers: short verbs, dark jokes, “we don’t leave the pipe” — may go silent when plate arrives; silence ≠ understanding.  
- **Common:** exhausted loyalty, receipt clauses; soft contempt for speech-makers in the ditch; quiet respect that still says *sir* wrong if it becomes kinship claim.  
- **Upper peak:** cool High Gothic; measure silence like yield; risk auctioning piety at the wrong door.  
- **Solis:** quieter than magnates; sentences that leave room for the Wall without naming ownership — *face-hold* as bleed skill.

## B7. Fiction tells

- **Mortal → PRIMA signal:** **Who sweats beside them** without a sermon; then filter grade → plaque → which car; then stamp booth vs chapel; then whether they claim kinship after the dry gallery returns.

- **Brother → populi signal:** **Filter → plaque → car** reading of the mortal; then stamp booth vs chapel priority; then whether the ditch is Shared Sweat or patronage theater; then Warden Gauge clocks (queues) vs feast speech.

- **Wrong:**
  - Hive-king Castellan / feast-king Corbec  
  - Solis as PRIMA officer or Vexilla billet  
  - Obligation / rectification field = unlogged scrap or missed torque  
  - Shared Sweat = permanent kinship  
  - Batavi creed collapsed into Ministorum God-Emperor worship  
  - Founding / progenitor “proof” from street wolf marks or *limitanei* pride  
  - Opaque friend–foe letter-codes in mortal mouths  
  - Magnate ownership of ammo clocks via guest ink  
  - Sealed Valerian / Versibar / *Lupercal* doctrine as street fact  
  - Peak elevator car “hosting” forge-lip Shared Sweat as patronage feast  

- **Right:**
  - Storm lock — mortals queue by stratum; PRIMA on priority geometry when present  
  - Shared Sweat only when the ditch is one; hierarchy returns dry  
  - Warden Gauge = clocks and tallies, not parade  
  - Throughput theology at the booth; Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight on the Wall — shared words, split creed  
  - Solis *face-hold* and kitchen soft door without wolf rank  
  - Thin Valerian rumor (“first seat broke; Wall holds”) without sealed names  
  - Parallel keys ≠ upper class; shame lattice ≠ scrap sin  
  - Chronological blindness — rumor ≠ *Strategium* seal  
  - Forge-priest handshake on ammo clocks without creed merge  
  - Third Shift Concord overlap = booths + PDF canopy, not magnate cars
