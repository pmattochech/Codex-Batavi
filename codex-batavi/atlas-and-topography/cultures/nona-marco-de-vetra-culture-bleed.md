## B2. Hard edges (do not merge)

1. **Faith:** Populi = Ecclesiarchy-baptized **release-schedule mysticism** (God loves the useful hand that does not shout early). *Nona* = **Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight** — oath geometry, not Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Shared words (“silence,” “seal,” “hold,” “Throne,” “window”) ≠ shared creed. Delay clergy are **not** Chaplains; liturgical cadence on Vetra’s channel is **signal discipline for scared crews**, not Mass.

2. **Verticals / keys:** Mortals sorted by **transmit clearance** + **vitra-string / laser-house lineage**. Chapter = **parallel keys** on array decks, farm-spine watches, and civil rooms that touch Shouter logistics. Brothers ≠ upper class by clearance; vitra elders ≠ Legatus by trunk ownership. Guest ink and *window-hold* soft power do **not** open Strategium or *Alabarda* plant rights.

3. **Shame ≠ premature cry:** Populi wound = **premature cry** (unbudgeted outbound — panic ping, mis-stamped window, patronage leak, illegal silhouette paint that becomes a target paint). *Nona* shame lattice = **cheap expendability** and **seconds unpaid** — making the bait-moon’s sacrifice theatrical, overcommitting kill ratio while losing antenna integrity, false-escalating hash into a shout that paints the farm, or treating Castellan / suppression weather as jamming and waking the wrong Tier. **Silent Hours** (140.M42) is **debt the lattice carries** (field 4: time-and-signal victory, twenty-seven brothers) — **not** a civilian premature-cry prosecution, and **not** mortal license to narrate sealed pulse contents.

4. **Bridge ≠ billet:** **Vitra-string laser houses** = named lower-upper / soft power (alignment patronage, release-schedule influence, *window-hold*). They are **not** Vexilla rank, **not** Watch honor, **not** Shouter billet ownership. Shared Sweat galleries and Chapter-civil rooms are **handshake floors**, not promotion ladders into *Nona*.

5. **Xenos / civic hate ≠ Chapter geometry:** Mast-foot Ork-hate, pirate-cutout rage, Navy “broadcast habit” insults, and Guard sweat ≠ *Nona* threat geometry (layered delay, EM budget, deny-intact-capture, Threshold dyad with *Octava*). No civic “welcome” for xenos retainers; no civilian briefing that collapses friend–foe discrimination into elegant auspex faith. Mortals learn **who stops transmitting**; brothers plan **who may spend shells on the moon**.

**Station-IX edge (do not absorb into Marco identity):** Station-IX = **monastic receiver** (listen-only, cold vow, laser to Vitreus). Marco = **bait and shout** (delay-and-deny until the cry flies or the moon burns). Import gossip (*listen marriage*, *sidus silence*) may color Marco speech; **do not** collapse the two into one silence cult in bleed scenes.

### Populi → *Nona* (short + deep)

**Short:** Schedule piety, strap watch, mute-runner chalk, and exhausted loyalty to **on-time silence** become the weather *Nona* fights inside — not sermons to convert.

**Deep examples:**

1. **Delay knuckle → plant check.** Common three-tap on the stamp booth before shift rhymes with Vetra’s habit of checking manual vox before trusting the relay alone. Brothers do not take the knuckle as rite; they take it as **proof the civilian spine still fears early heat**.
2. **Signal wolf as civilian cant.** Allowed farm insult for premature cry. *Nona* may hear “wolf” and must **not** dump Wolf’s Curse taxonomy into the corridor. Correct bleed: treat it as **outbound discipline failure**, answer with mute geometry, not clinical lecture.
3. **Vitra-string patronage pressure.** Lower-upper houses want release influence and fear being the leak. Bleed into command as **political noise on the schedule** — not as billet rights. Vetra’s known flaw (hard delegation of Shouter perimeter) bites here: executive captains burn when houses treat access as affection.
4. **Post-scorch riot law.** Families of reclaim dead vs delay clerks at Coupling Bay Seven teach *Nona* that denial product has a **mortal invoice**. Perimeter presence that ends argument without sermon is Shared Sweat surface — not Ministorum victory.

### *Nona* → populi (short + deep)

**Short:** Shared Sweat silence, quiet officers, ammo/EM clocks under the Warden, and parallel keys teach *window-hold* without granting wolf rank; Silent Hours enters street talk as thin pride, not sealed pulse catechism.

**Deep examples:**

1. **Once-per-cycle rotation line.** Mortals notice Vetra does not repeat slogans that echo wrong in hash. Common *hash-closed face* rises in status; loud missionaries fall. Bleed effect: **silence priests / delay clergy** gain against empty thunder — still Ecclesiarchy-accented, not Ancestor-Sovereign conversion.
2. **Scorch ordered twice.** Civic rumor turns the double order into folklore (“the Warden says it twice so the farm cannot pretend it misheard”). Correct: discipline against panic and against intact-capture buyers. Wrong: treating double order as beam-wedding romance or God-Emperor ecstasy.
3. **Sustained-pin clocks.** Boltstorm / heavy bolter rotation (barrel heat under EM damp) bleeds into PDF career pride as “we held the alley clock.” *Limitanei*-style “we built the wall” **rhymes** with bait-moon expense — rhyme only, not Chapter kinship.
4. **Civil rooms ≠ upper class.** Parallel keys visible at trunk-adjacent galleries teach Solis-analogue *face-hold* as Marco *window-hold*: keep house face calm when the farm wants to scream — **without** claiming *Nona* decks.

- **Farm-spine / array-deck dry galleries** — post-hash Shared Sweat silence-as-courtesy; civilian alignment crews beside brothers; class returns when shutters lift.
- **Trunk-gate stamp booths** — civilian holiness receipt beside parallel Chapter keys; do not merge booth into *Alabarda* plant authority.

## B7. Fiction tells

- **Mortal → *Nona* signal:** A hand hovering over a transmit stud, then dropping — or a delay stamp pressed hard enough to bruise paper while hash rises — **and** who sweats silent beside them at mast foot without a sermon.
- **Brother → populi signal:** Check the **manual vox spool** before trusting the pretty relay; scorch said **twice**; perimeter watch still walking when the wire is quiet; liturgical line spoken **once** per cycle.
- **Wrong:** Hive-king Castellan on a bait-moon; vitra-string house as *Nona* officer track; premature cry = Silent Hours debt; collapsing Marco into Station-IX cold-vow monks; Warden as God-Emperor ecstasy preacher; open-vox hero speeches as virtue; Founding / progenitor / clinical curse stages in farm mouths; friend–foe abbreviations; Solis kitchen geography pasted onto Marco.
- **Right:** Schedule piety under bait-moon pride; clearance felt before speech; Shared Sweat silence as courtesy; seconds bought so the shout behind stays clean; shame = cheap expendability / unpaid seconds on the Chapter side, premature cry on the mortal side; Station-IX kept as gossip/wing stub, not Marco identity.

|------------------------------|-------------------------------------|
