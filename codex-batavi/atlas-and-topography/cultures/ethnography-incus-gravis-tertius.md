## 0. Preamble

### 0.1 Physical frame — yield as budget

**Incus-Gravis Tertius** is a hyper-gravity **super-Earth** at crushing **~2.8G**. The surface reads as endless **flats**: adamantium-hard crust broken into **shingle seas** — fields of interlocking stone plates that **slide**, **stack**, and **sing** when wind or flare-pressure moves them. Rain here is not Noviomagus soot-fall; it is **shingle slide**. Districts learn weather by **sound**: a soft choir means drift; a hard choir means avalanche. Children who cannot name their district’s slide-tone are considered half-blind.

Urbanism is **inverted**. Hive cities are carved **down** into crystalline adamantium crust — galleries, load shafts, compression halls — because building *up* is vanity that gravity bills in corpses. “Sky” for most citizens means the **lamp grid** of the shaft above their assigned tier, not the red-dwarf dusk of **Incus-Gravis-Prime**. Surface work exists (sensor spines, auxilia drill flats, shingle harvest corridors), but **life’s ledger** lives underground, where weight is engineered into architecture and every stair is a tax.

**Slow death** is **microfracture**. Knees, ankles, lumbar plates, and strap-points fail first. Bone scans are as ordinary as ration stamps. Orthotics are not luxury medical theater — they are **civic clothing**. A body that “walks clean” without straps is either Upper (bought distance from true load) or lying. Citizens think in **yield points**, **boot debt**, **strap hours**, **compression shifts**, and **load festivals**. The provincial feel is **highland-anvil barbaricum** wearing Imperial forms: High Gothic on tithe boards, Porta Nihili work-creed in every brace shop, and a stubborn belief that God loves **what does not buckle**.

**Sphere note (jurisdiction only — not this file’s ethnography):** Vexilla QVARTA seat jurisdiction covers the **Incus-Gravis sphere** — Tertius capital ethnography here; **Incus-Gravis Secundus** and **Incus-Gravis-I** Phase C stubs live on [worlds-crucible.md](worlds-crucible.md) § **QVARTA wing** — do **not** treat this document as their culture dump.

### 0.2 Fork

**Not Noviomagus.** Noviomagus sorts by **Black Rain** and **elevator keys**; Tertius sorts by **load grade** and **strap stamp**. Forge capital thinks in rust and throughput; the Super-Earth thinks in **posture under crush**. A Noviomagus clerk’s insult is *unlogged scrap*; a Tertius clerk’s insult is *false yield*.

**Not Secundus.** Secundus is perforated catacomb light-theology — lamps, crystal scribes, echo marriage. Tertius is **horizontal miracle** and **weight guild** law. Outsiders who call both “inverted hive worlds” earn contempt: one lives by **trace**; the other lives by **yield**.

**Not Incus-Gravis-I.** The Scorched runs penal rotation and heat index. Tertius may *export* auxilia bodies toward dish labor and *import* sentence fodder rumors, but its daily grammar is **compression**, not **flare prayer**.

**Porta Nihili accent:** God loves **what does not buckle**. Holidays are **load festivals** — carry, don’t sing. Confession is **bone scan**. Speech is cheap; a clean orthotic seal is holy. Men of Iron hangover appears as **boot-check silence** and hatred of unlogged motion on open channels near March-edge billets — habit, not doctrine lecture.

**What outsiders misread:** They hear “high-G world” and invent berserk glory culture. Locals prize **stillness that holds**. They see storm shields and gravitic boots on Batavi and assume mortal PDF copies Chapter theater — mortals copy **ankle discipline** and **receipt fire**, not power-armor myth.

### 0.3 Sorting border (class police)

- **Primary sorter:** **Load grade / strap stamp** — which compression tier a body may enter without escort, and which orthotic allotment the guild will renew.
- **Secondary sorter:** **Knee scripture** — the visible (or scan-readable) microfracture pattern that proves a body has *actually* carried; fake-clean knees without Upper exemption read as **false yield**.
- **Felt before a clerk speaks:** How a body **stands** under 2.8G — ankle set, strap hang, breath timing — and whether their boots make the **honest heel click** of maintained seals.

#### Load grade / strap stamp

| Stratum | Exposure | Rights | Meaning |
|---------|----------|--------|---------|
| **Rabble** | Chronic crush work; scrap orthotics; surface shingle gangs | Service shafts only; strap renewals by hour-debt | Class is the grind in the joints; “promotion” is a better brace, not a title |
| **Common** | Managed compression halls; guild straps; scheduled bone scans | Shift-tier keys; compression marriage load-share logs | Virtue = held yield on the bell; sin = false yield on the ledger |
| **Upper** | Bought distance: sealed low-G galleries, imported cushions, private scan houses | House load registries; guest strap ink; Brace House bridge passes | Clean knees can be purchased — and must be *performed* as piety so Common does not riot |

#### Knee scripture (secondary)

| Stratum | Exposure | Rights | Meaning |
|---------|----------|--------|---------|
| **Rabble** | Patterns read by orthotic clergy in alley clinics | None — scripture is evidence you worked, not a petition right | Palmistry of bone; pride and doom in the same scan |
| **Common** | Guild clinic liturgy; marriage scans; draft fitness | Scan stamps gate PDF draft and guild rank | The body is the receipt |
| **Upper** | Private Apothecarion-adjacent clinics (mortal) | Clean-file exemptions; sealed “rest years” | Buying distance from scripture while claiming to honor it |

#### Worked intersections / fiction tells

1. **Slide choir lock** — when shingle seas roar, shaft gates close by load grade; Rabble wait in service stairs; Common argue stamp windows; Upper already inside sealed galleries.  
2. **Load festival plaza** — all strata meet; sorter is who carries *what* in public and who only *sponsors* the carry.  
3. **Boot seal inspection** — PDF / Arbites corridor habit; class learned in whose heels fail first.  
4. **Brace House pattern** — covered routes between guild halls and Chapter-adjacent civil floors without owning apex shafts.

**Fiction tells:** scrap strap → guild stamp → house registry ink. Insult: *soft knee* / *false yield*. Mercy: a shared strap hour or guest orthotic ink.

### 0.4 Fiction strata lock

**Labels:** Rabble · Common · Upper *(standard — no rename this pass)*  
**Bridge band:** **Brace Houses** — named lower-upper houses that own orthotic contracts, mid-shaft load registries, and Shared Sweat liaison rooms without owning apex galleries.

#### Rabble

- **Who:** Shingle scrapers, reclaim strap-stitchers, unstamped shaft-porters, failed draft washouts, bone-broke beggars trading scan time for food, underhive compression squatters.
- **Mood premises map:** Duty without audience = holding the lowest plates so upper shafts do not shear. Forgotten, not faithless = never on tithe boards worth naming; oath is *don’t cut a brother’s strap*. Weathered service = hands that still buckle a brace. Loss as weather = missing porters filed as slide casualties.
- **Sound:** Short sentences, dry jokes about ankles. Proto–*We hold* as “knees don’t quit.” Humor is crush-gallows.
- **Holy object:** Shared strap, scrap knife, honest heel.
- **How “forgotten” works:** Pre-forgotten — if the slide takes you without a guild stamp, your name does not climb the shaft.

#### Common

- **Who:** Weight guilds, compression marriage households, stamp clerks, orthotic clergy assistants, PDF career cadres, Auxilia intake families, mid-hive load accountants.
- **Mood premises map:** Duty without audience = the load bell *is* the prayer. Forgotten, not faithless = Nihilus-edge known; baptize in brace shops, not cathedral ecstasy. Weathered service = logged yield, on-time bone scan. Loss as weather = Wall-name rumors filed next to boot-debt tables.
- **Sound:** Exhausted loyalty. Metronomic shift talk. Soft contempt for loud missionaries who cannot read a load slate.
- **Holy object:** Strap stamp, compression marriage log, guild plaque.
- **How “forgotten” works:** District remembers; Imperium may not — and that is the wound they manage with seals.

#### Upper

- **Who:** Shaft magnates, house load-registry elders, Administratum liaison blood, Ministorum patrons of load festivals, Brace House bridges.
- **Mood premises map:** Duty without audience = audience is local (tithe, Chapter shadow, rival houses). Forgotten, not faithless = status anxiety; over-invest in clean scans and “correct” devotion. Weathered service = feed the Auxilia machine; fear useless piety and false yield scandals equally. Loss as weather = names in marble still filed as cost.
- **Sound:** Cooler sentences; High Gothic over brace cant. Brace Houses quieter than apex magnates; less auctioned piety.
- **Holy object:** Chapel + clean audit + house load key.
- **How “forgotten” works:** Refuse to be forgotten by Tertius; fear being forgotten by Terra.

### 0.5 Local mortal sin & Legatus read

- **Local mortal sin — false yield:** Claiming a load share, bone-scan result, strap blessing, or compression-marriage contribution you did not carry. Enforcement: weight guild courts first; orthotic clergy second; Administratum/Arbites when tithe or draft fraud is proven. **Class-colored:** Rabble — strap theft / borrowed stamp; Common — misfiled load-share or “rest day” fraud on the bell; Upper — bought clean scans and guest ink sold as earned scripture. Used in Religion (confession as scan), Economy (tithe failure smell), and later Phase B shame≠sin hard edge.
- **Legatus public read:** Mortals name Orin Valestrand **the Shingle Warden** — also **Grav Lock** in PDF cant. Surface myth: he taps heel seals twice before clearance; he speaks in hours and meters; he hates “downslope glory.” No sealed March taxonomy in tavern mouths — only that QVARTA holds when the choir gets ugly.

## 1. Military (mortal)

Mortal force on Tertius is organized around a single civilian truth: **movement is expensive**. PDF doctrine teaches **static corridor hold**, choke geometry, and boot-seal discipline long before it teaches pursuit. Draft feeds **Auxilia** labor cadres and neophyte-conditioning support lines as much as rifle companies. When the Wall is late, the first to die are **shingle pickets** and **shaft-mouth rabble** holding plates that Upper galleries treat as “outer weather.”

**Arbites posture:** Present at tithe spines, draft halls, and load-festival plazas — less street theater than Noviomagus, more **boot and stamp** enforcement. Riot geometry follows inverted hives: control the **load shafts**, and the underhive starves for air and exit. Corridor suppressions use gravitic boot trainees and shield walls that *rhyme* with Batavi storm-shield habits without claiming Chapter kit.

**Shared Sweat surfaces (civilian only):** Joint Auxilia–PDF trench drills on surface flats; civilian stretcher guilds under shingle slide; Brace House kitchens near Chapter-adjacent civil floors; load festivals where Batavi shadow is watched, not worshipped. Mortals learn QVARTA as **held crest** rumor — heavy fire, melta on choke, missiles with receipts — not as armory manual. March-of-the-Machine texture reaches civilians as **static hold weather**: long sieges, amber rotation gossip, Shadow-in-the-Warp fear as “the choir goes wrong in the head,” without sealed *Curia* taxonomy.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Levy fodder, shingle pickets, shaft porters pressed into riot walls. Die first when slides open or when a seal line breaks. Pride is surviving a draft season with both ankles. Fear Batavi as weather that walks.
- **Common:** Career PDF, Auxilia cadre families, guild militia that holds compression halls. Draft fitness = bone scan + strap stamp. Soft pride in Shared Sweat without speeches. Contempt for “runners” who treat high-G as sport.
- **Upper:** Officer houses, Brace House staff for civil–Chapter liaison, sponsors of load-festival honor companies. Prefer to **fund** holds rather than stand them; still must appear at festivals carrying a symbolic load or risk Common insult.

**Worked examples**

- **Draft hall of Shaft Tertius-Seven:** Common youth scanned; Rabble washouts stamped for Auxilia haul; Upper sons buy “rest certification” and risk false-yield prosecution if a rival house leaks the clinic file.
- **Slide riot, District Choir-Nine:** Shingle avalanche blocks three exits; Arbites seal upper gates by load grade; Rabble crush in service stairs; PDF holds mid-shaft with shield pairs; Brace House opens one guest corridor — mercy as politics.
- **Shared Sweat stretcher line:** Civilian orthotic clergy and PDF medicae haul under slide-dust while QVARTA grey plate anchors a crest beyond the civil fence — contact surface is sweat and silence, not catechism.

## 2. Culture

Amenities are **brace shops**, **compression baths** (short low-load relief rooms — expensive), **load-festival plazas**, and **choir listening posts** that publish slide forecasts like weather liturgy. Aesthetic is **industrial grey**, strap-leather, orthotic chrome, inverted-shaft lamp amber — March-edge billet grey bleeding into civil fashion without claiming Chapter plate. Public success is **held yield** displayed without swagger; public shame is **soft knee** proven on a clinic slate. Leisure is scarce: gambling on slide-choir timing, compression-marriage feast hours, illegal low-G dens (Upper, policed), and forbidden “downslope races” that Arbites break because they kill draft stock.

Non-Mass rites: **strap blessing** (orthotic clergy), **knee scripture reading**, **load festival carries**, **heel-click greeting** among PDF and guilders (two taps — civilian echo of the Warden’s tell, now folk habit).

**By class — include Sound**

- **Rabble:** Amenities = scrap brace stalls and shared compression squat corners. Leisure = dark jokes, knife games, listening to choir for free warning. **Sound:** short, metal-dry; “ankles singing” = debt; humor that would scandalize a missionary. Success = still walking. Shame = dying in open slide without a name tag.
- **Common:** Amenities = guild halls, scheduled baths, festival practice yards. Leisure = compression marriage dances that look like load drills, stamp-booth gossip, quiet Shared Sweat pride. **Sound:** metronomic; exhausted loyalty; *slide-closed faces*. Soft contempt for empty thunder. Success = clean stamp at season end. Shame = false yield on the marriage log.
- **Upper / Brace Houses:** Amenities = sealed galleries, private clinics, sponsored festivals. Leisure = High Gothic recitals timed *after* load bells so Common cannot call them thieves of yield. **Sound:** cooler sentences; brace cant under Gothic; Brace Houses quieter, more face-hold than auction. Success = clean audit + festival patronage. Shame = leaked soft-knee file.

## 3. Society

Rank is felt as **load grade** before it is filed as title. Humiliation is being forced to **stand the scan** in public after a rival claims false yield. Street law favors those who **share strap** in a slide; it kills those who cut straps to escape. Local governance mixes **weight guild councils**, **orthotic clergy courts** (for body-and-brace disputes), Administratum load registries, and Planetary Governor spines that live in Upper galleries. Representation for Rabble is thin — festival petition hours and draft riots. Rationing follows compression tier: protein and orthotic lubricant first to stamped workers. Tax-as-wound is the **yield tithe** — bodies and straps billed as planetary product.

**Compression marriages** define Common life: pair (or triad) for **load-share**, logged to guild and Administratum. Divorce is **misalignment of yield** — legal fiction that can hide abuse; clergy and guild arbiters watch. Upper houses marry for registry merger; Rabble “brace up” without stamps and are vulnerable to draft seizure.

Death geography: memorial plaques in mid-shaft **Hold Walls** for stamped dead; Rabble slide dead often become **choir stories** without marble. Wall rumor (Batavi names) is filed next to boot-debt tables — civic grief, not *Reclusiam* law.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Street law is strap-share or starve. No real representation. Rations are leftover. Tax wound = pressed haul quotas. Marriage = informal brace-up.
- **Common:** Guild vote and marriage log are civic spine. Humiliation = public scan. Tax wound = yield tithe on the bell. Pride = children who pass draft scan without false yield.
- **Upper:** Governance owners. Tax exemptions bought as “strategic rest houses,” hated by Common. Marriage = registry. Humiliation = festival without a carried load.

**Friction matrix**

| Pressure | Rabble | Common | Upper |
|----------|--------|--------|-------|
| Who is “forgotten” | Always — slide-erased | By Imperium, not by guild | By Terra; refuse to be by Tertius |
| Holy object | Shared strap, honest heel | Stamp, marriage log, plaque | Chapel + clean audit + house key |
| Ministorum | Useless / dangerous if they delay scans | Wrong accent; useful at festivals | Patronage tool + status mask |
| Batavi presence | Watch / fear / dark pride | Shared Sweat respect; Warden rumor | Necessary shadow; want influence without being owned |

## 4. Religion

Official creed is Ecclesiarchy-baptized Porta Nihili: the Emperor is paid in **held yield**. Local accent: God loves **what does not buckle**; the **flat god** folk image (miracle = horizontal hold) is syncretic color clergy tolerate when it does not become beast-cult. Priests who cannot read a bone scan are entertainment. Orthotic clergy (brace-blessers) often outrank chapel priests in daily holiness. Holidays that stop work: **Load Festival** seasons (district carries), **Choir Silence** days after major slides (no lies while the seas still settle — custom), and Imperial feast days translated into **strap renewal** markets.

Texts in practice: load slates, scan printouts, High Gothic collects mumbled under brace cant. Religious punishment: public scan, strap confiscation, pilgrimage carries across shingle (dangerous), and — for false yield — guild excommunication that equals economic death. **Stamp booth vs chapel:** bone-scan clinic and strap booth win when holiness needs a receipt; chapel wins when Upper needs *face-hold* for tithe inspectors.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Alley orthotic blessers; chapel as rumor. Confession = showing knees. Prayer = buckle the strap.
- **Common:** Guild chapel + scan liturgy. Holidays = load festivals. Sin = false yield. Soft respect for priests who stand a carry.
- **Upper:** Chapter-house patronage, sponsored festivals, clean private confession. Use Ministorum to launder rest exemptions as piety.

**Contrast only:** Batavi honor **Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight** — not Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Shared words (*Throne*, *hold*, *seal*) ≠ shared creed. Mortals still use chapel language; the **feel** is yield-first. Do not collapse Batavi faith into Ecclesiarchy ecstasy in this ethnography.

## 5. Economy

**World function:** Auxilia labor backbone, neophyte-conditioning support ecology, high-G training flats, orthotic/brace manufacture, shingle-mineral and adamantium-crust harvest corridors, export of **hardened bodies** and **compression components** into the Crucible mesh. Tithe is measured in **yield bodies**, **strap production**, and **held-line hours** as much as ore.

Work runs on **compression shifts** and **load bells**. Failure smell of tithe: clinics full of soft-knee scandals, guild stamps delayed, Auxilia drafts underfilled, shingle choirs unanswered because porters fled — Administratum calls it “shortfall”; locals smell **false yield** and **boot debt** cascading. Exemptions: Upper rest houses, Brace House liaison staff, certain orthotic clergy, and sealed “strategic clinic” bands — all hated when visible. Real markets: strap parts, scan time, guest ink, compression bath tokens. Ledger fiction: clean yield that never met a shaft.

Wealth under this sky: **distance from crush** plus **authority to assign crush to others**. A rich man who cannot stand a festival carry is only half-rich.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Sell hours of body; buy scrap straps; black market scan forgeries (false yield seedbed).
- **Common:** Guild wages, marriage load-share pools, PDF pay, Auxilia family remittances. Tithe wound is personal.
- **Upper:** Registry rents, clinic ownership, festival sponsorship as political insurance. Export contracts with Noviomagus brace-steel and Secundus crystal tooling (trade link, not ethnography theft).

## 6. Education

Who learns: stamped Common children in **guild schools**; Upper in house tutors and Administratum academies; Rabble in alley brace-lore and “choir schools” that teach slide-tones for survival. Who is kept dark: unstamped Rabble — literacy limited to load marks and danger signs unless a Brace House charity hour intervenes.

**Tracks:** weight-guild apprenticeship; orthotic clergy novitiate; Administratum load-registry clerk track; PDF cadet academies; Ministorum seminary (thin, Upper-tilted); rare void-charter scholarships off-world (envied, suspect).

**Curriculum-as-virtue:** posture drills, boot-seal maintenance, basic bone-scan literacy, High Gothic enough to read a tithe board, Men of Iron silence drills near March-edge rumor zones (habit: don’t chatter on open channels). Virtue is **held attention under weight**, not eloquence.

**Cost:** years of microfracture, patronage debts for clinic school seats, bodies lost in apprentice carries. Literacy *does* this: lets you contest a false-yield accusation, read a marriage log, and survive an Arbites corridor inspection. Illiteracy keeps you haul-only forever.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Oral choir-lore; scrap arithmetic; occasional charity stamp-literacy. Cost = children lost to slides during “lessons.”
- **Common:** Guild school spine; PDF academies; orthotic assistant tracks. Cost = apprenticeship injuries accepted as tuition.
- **Upper:** Full Gothic, registry law, clinic science, off-world polish. Cost = patronage wars; fear of Common accusing soft education as soft knee.
