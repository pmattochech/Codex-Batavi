## B2. Hard edges (do not merge)

1. **Faith:** Populi = Ecclesiarchy-baptized record + Porta Nihili **caloric honesty** (God is **fed**; Throne receipt = silo seal / Atlas weigh-in). *Tertia* = **Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight** — oath and Wall geometry, **not** Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Shared words (*Throne*, *seal*, *Wall*, *hold*) ≠ shared creed. Mortals hearing *We hold* as “keep the barge floating” are **rhyming**, not converting.

2. **Verticals / keys:** Mortals sorted by **elevator rights** (primary) and **oxygen grade** (secondary). Chapter moves on **parallel keys** — chem-seal hatch authority, inspection-floor cordons, *Alabarda* plant geometry. Brothers are **not** silo nobility; house elevator keys do **not** buy Legatus nod. Chart-Grace bridge passes open **civil / inspection** floors — never Strategium, never *Reclusiam* crypt law.

3. **Shame ≠ diversion:** Mortal **diversion** = grain, starch, fertilizer, or Atlas load leaving the approved graph (theft from the Throne’s mouth) — includes unlogged spoilage, false bloom write-downs, torch-without-permit as chemistry cover. *Tertia* failure / shame lattice ≠ diversion charge. Chapter failure-risk (Legatus Ring): treating **inversion as purely biological plume geometry**; early-career **overpressure** shortcuts that fire remembers; burn that **destroys harvest window** instead of opening it. A blushing chart is **civic sin weather**. A brother who sparks without seal discipline is **theater failure**, not a diversion hearing.

4. **Bridge ≠ billet:** **Chart-Grace houses** = named lower-upper soft power (debt grace, Atlas-civil liaison, Shared Sweat inspection continuity, quiet *face-hold*). They are **not** Vexilla rank, **not** Chapter billet, **not** Martha’s kitchen (Solis band belongs to Noviomagus / *Prima* seat — do not import). Guest ink into civil–Chapter inspection rooms ≠ wolf affection.

5. **Xenos / civic hate ≠ Chapter geometry:** Bloom riot, spore panic, Ork-adjacent barge hate, diversion-syndicate beatings = **civic** friend–foe discrimination (*who holds a licensed torch*; *who smells like diversion*). *Tertia* threat geometry = **thermal breach**, **lying air**, monoculture bunker fire, harvest-window seconds vs tons. Civic hate does **not** license civilians into kill-box mathematics; Chapter geometry does **not** rewrite diversion law into *Axiom*.

**Additional seat hard edge — fire paradox (do not collapse):** On Khan-Bator, open flame without permit is **heresy with chemistry**. *Tertia* fields flamers, meltas, pyreblaster-class burners as **modal lean**. The edge is **licensed, sealed, timed burn under chem-cert** vs **foreign spark / incense / diversion torch**. Mortals who call every Marine burn “foreign spark” are **wrong**; brothers who treat spark curfew as cowardice are **wrong**.

**Populi → *Tertia* (deep examples):**

1. **Breath budget as tempo:** Brothers billeted in silo stacks inherit mortal timing — mask changes, spark curfew bells, elevator windows — as logistics weather. Theron’s theater already reads **lying air**; common-class monitor juniors supply the civic half of that sensor culture without becoming Apothecarion.

2. **Exhausted loyalty weather:** Hymn-mapped common sound (*chart-closed face*, soft contempt for loud missionaries) is the climate *Tertia* continuity answers. Controlled-heat jokes in briefing rhyme with barge dark humor; ice on the trigger rhymes with spark law — **rhyme only**.

3. **Atlas / third-strand gravity:** Cable-guild mute climb and *cut-day* terror teach Shared Sweat rope kin to treat unbalanced load as moral failure. *Tertia* plant sites respect cable tension rumor without owning Golden Grain ethnography (Phase C wing: [worlds-central-bastion.md](worlds-central-bastion.md) § **III *Tertia* wing**).

4. **Diversion as false friend:** Mortals try to file Chapter cordon seizures as “anti-diversion piety.” Brothers who accept that filing risk confusing **civic chart law** with **threat geometry**. Hard edge B2.3 holds.

**Vexilla → populi (deep examples):**

1. **Seal Flame as civic weather:** Unannounced audits, hatch cordons, chem-cert drill smell. Upper houses want influence without ownership; Chart-Grace sell *face-hold*; rabble learn fear first, dark pride second.

2. **Quiet priests rise:** Empty thunder (incense processions) fails oxygen-monitor orders. Shared Sweat–adjacent clergy who bless valves and paste outrank silk missionaries — surface effect of Batavi silence, not conversion to Ancestor-Sovereign.

3. **Thermal as necessary monster:** After bunker-fire years and Tyranid-adjacent spore mixes (Legatus rise theater — chronology access sealed for street), mortals accept that some fires are **Wall fires**. They still prosecute **foreign spark**. The distinction is lived, not preached as gene-seed.

4. **Chief Victualler shadow:** Mesh knows Khan-Bator feeds forge stacks and war-stomach. Civilians feel it as endless tonnage demand and draft-vs-harvest clash — not as titled Chapter office in barge mouths.

## B7. Fiction tells

- **Mortal → *Tertia* signal:** Mask / filter grade → plaque → which elevator car boarded without looking → whether a torch is licensed → whether the chart is already smoothed → whether bodies freeze for a hatch-nod before the burn-mask moves.

- **Brother → populi signal:** Parallel keys vs house elevators; chem-cert wax smell; O₂ *auspex* tick before hatch; harvest-window timers spoken in tons; thermal weapons under spark curfew without carnival; silence where missionaries shout.

- **Wrong:**
  - Treat Khan-Bator / *Tertia* as **Noviomagus forge culture** or import *Prima* obligation / broken-cog shame as this seat’s lattice.
  - Collapse Batavi into Ministorum God-Emperor worship; make incense processions “normal” under oxygen law.
  - Equate **diversion** with Chapter shame / Furor / *Axiom* failure.
  - Treat **Chart-Grace** as Vexilla officers or as Solis / Martha’s kitchen.
  - Invent **Founding** number or progenitor Chapter; dump gene-seed or Strand labels into barge mouths.
  - Call every Marine burn *foreign spark*; call every civic spark ban *cowardice*.
  - Absorb **Bator-Secundus / Tertius / Vacuum Harvest / Golden Grain** as full ethnographies here (wing stubs live on worlds-central-bastion § III).
  - Keep obsolete “Fifth Vexillatio” as seat doctrine; opaque friend–foe abbreviations.

- **Right:**
  - Breath-as-budget; **yield / oxygen / elevator / silo** locks held.
  - **Diversion** = local mortal sin; Seal Flame = public Legatus read; Chart-Grace = bridge ≠ billet.
  - Fire paradox held: civic spark taboo ↔ licensed thermal breach.
  - Shared Sweat flattens class briefly; hierarchy returns; kinship claim refused.
  - Inspection weather: mortals smooth charts; *Tertia* reads lying air; two verticals, one corridor.
  - Vigilax / sealed successes stay rumor-thin on the street (see locked example below).
