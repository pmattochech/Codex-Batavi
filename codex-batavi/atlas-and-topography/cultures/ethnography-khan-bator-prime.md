## 0. Preamble

### 0.1 Physical frame — breath as budget

**Khan-Bator Prime** is a dense-atmosphere, **super-oxygen** agri-industrial world: monoculture plains (algorithm green — one crop grammar repeated to the horizon), nutrient-slime oceans (barge routes, **scum readers** as honor profession), and **10 km silo-cities** where citizenship is vertical. Population scale is **trillions** across plains, slime coasts, and stacked silo districts — the caloric engine that feeds forge stacks, void logistics, and the Chapter’s war-stomach without romance.

Citizens think in **yield**, **breath**, **elevator windows**, and **silo seals**. Gravity is Earth-normal enough that off-worlders notice the **sky first**, not their bones: too bright, too rich, lungs that taste like **blue** after a week. Locals pride **blue lung** adaptation — worker physiology as status, not tourism.

**Slow death** here is chemistry, not soot. Open flame that would be festive on a forge world is **heresy with chemistry**. Sparks kill in bulk. Mask laws, spark curfews, and oxygen-monitor red-tags are civic weather. Combustion without permit is not “fun” — it is a mass-casualty attempt dressed as piety when missionaries import incense.

**Golden Grain Station / Atlas Elevator:** the **35,000 km** tether is shared-fate theology made steel. Unbalanced load is sin in dock cant and moral cant both. Cable tension is the planet’s pulse; civilians learn early that **the chart** and **the braid** decide whether a district eats.

**Provincial feel:** not Noviomagus forge-capital ceremony; not Castra-Vetera autumn paperwork. Khan-Bator is **granary empire** — High Gothic tonnage in the mouths of tithe nobility, slime-tide saints in the barges, and a permanent knowledge that **God is fed** when the silo hash seals clean.

### 0.2 Fork

**Not Noviomagus:** rust is not the budget; **breath** is. Saints are harvest curves and calendar floods rewritten as slime tide — not forge masters whose dies never wandered. Stamp booths exist, but the holy receipt is more often a **silo seal** or **Atlas weigh-in** than a torque stamp.

**Not Castra-Vetera Prime:** law arrives as **chart inspection** and diversion charges, not marble judgment theater. Forms save silos or starve districts — grey-clerk piety without the Throne-city’s autumn chill.

**Not Aethelgard:** salt-in-the-ration travels here as mourning bread metaphor and shared-tithe stomach; tide grief does not. Rivalry with Aethelgard is polite until famine is not.

**Porta Nihili accent:** throughput theology translated into **caloric honesty**. Faith of work sounds like yield per hectare, sealed silo hash, Atlas-knee bruises worn as seniority. Speech is cheap; **manifest integrity** is holy.

**Outsiders misread:** oxygen discipline as cowardice; hunger festivals (charts on silo skins) as joyless atheism; fire-devil folktales as primitive dualism; slime-tide saints as water ignorance (they are hydraulic literacy dressed as hagiography).

  - *Common color:* window trading that becomes diversion if manifests diverge; “honest spoilage” that isn’t.

## 1. Military (mortal)

Khan-Bator’s mortal force geometry is **corridor, barge, and silo** — not parade ground romance. Planetary Defense Force and local militia own spark-curfew enforcement, bloom-riot containment, and Atlas approach pickets. Imperial Guard draft windows pull from silo stacks and barge guilds when the Wall is hungry for bodies; Arbites posture hardens when diversion riots threaten tithe integrity. Shared Sweat contact is surface: parallel keys at inspection floors, civilian rope kin in Atlas queues learning helm-still pauses, bargemen knife-flat hand signals for load balance that Batavi sometimes mirror — not Chapter doctrine; full Vexilla bleed in [tertia-khan-bator-culture-bleed.md](tertia-khan-bator-culture-bleed.md).

**Who dies first when the Wall is late:** rabble in slime docks and red-tagged districts; then common PDF canopy and barge militia; upper evacuate by house elevator while Chart-Grace argue guest ink for civil floors. Oxygen monitors die early in fire years — angels of HSE become corpses of chemistry.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Levy fodder, slime-side pickets, illegal climb conscripts for sever drills, first into bloom riots with barge hooks. Friend–foe discrimination on the street is “who holds a licensed torch” and “who smells like diversion.” No prestige; survival is the shift.
- **Common:** Career PDF, barge militia with lung-trial initiation, oxygen-monitor auxiliaries, Shared Sweat pride without speeches. Draft windows hit harvest calendars — missing a window is civic shame second only to diversion. Riot geometry: wet shaft and dock, not marble plazas.
- **Upper:** Officer houses, silo-nobility patronage of PDF canopies, Chart-Grace staff who open bridge passes for civil–Chapter inspection rooms. They fund spark licenses for “chapel defense” and fear Batavi cordon drills more than they admit. Peak magnates treat Guard billets as status; Chart-Grace treat them as continuity insurance.

**Worked examples**

1. **Spark-curfew riot** — missionary incense vs oxygen-monitor red-tag; PDF canopy between; Arbites if the chart spikes toward diversion cover.
2. **Atlas sever drill** — cable guild + civilian rope kin; rumor that *cut-day* is real this time; rabble caught in braid stories.
3. **Unannounced silo audit (Batavi weather)** — mortals smooth charts; elevator cordon; Chart-Grace walk bridge floors; no worship of Marines — only inspection weather.
4. **Diversion chase on slime barge** — scum reader augury vs Administratum hash; militia boards; Arbites if tonnage is Throne-mouth scale.

## 2. Culture

Amenities are scarce, bought, fake, or forbidden under oxygen law. Leisure is licensed torch hours (rare), hunger-festival graph-watching (statistical, not festive), barge song that never carries open flame, and Bottle Fair windows at Golden Grain when load-swap permits multi-world creole. Aesthetic: algorithm green plains, slime sheen, silo skins projecting yield charts, mask chrome, starch-white chapel paste. Public shame is a red-tag on a district’s festal torch or a blushing chart; public success is balanced load, honest spoilage report, Atlas knee bruises as seniority.

Non-Mass rites: **grain oath** (hand in starch paste — shared tithe stomach), **gear prayer** knuckle taps on lift gears (imported from Noviomagus dockers into belt cant), **mute climb days** on the tether, **scum literacy** readings at bloom, **chemical funeral** habits bleeding from Bator-Secundus (cross-link only — not wing ethnography this pass).

**By class — include Sound**

- **Rabble:** Amenities = shared mask time, barge heat, stolen starch. Leisure = dock jokes, bloom watching from illegal climbs. Sound: short sentences, dark humor — *“the chart blushes”* spat between breaths. Proto–*We hold* as “we don’t leave the barge.” Public shame: red-tag on the whole stair. Success: surviving a lung trial rumor without dying.
- **Common:** Guild halls with sealed air, harvest bells, licensed spark for gear blessing only. Leisure = hunger festivals as graph projection — relief when the line goes flat. Sound: exhausted loyalty; *chart-closed face*; soft contempt for loud missionaries; quiet respect for Shared Sweat. Rite: gear prayer on lift gears; scum literacy as weather. Shame: wrong elevator car. Success: seal-clean window + second harvest for stranger’s debt (grain variant of witness debt).
- **Upper:** Sealed galleries, private torch licenses, statistical poetry in High Gothic at hunger festivals (irony and terror both). Sound: cooler sentences; forge envy jokes about Noviomagus until tithe tightens; Chart-Grace quieter, less auctioned piety. Rite: window stamp on house seal / chapel lintel; silo marriage as elevator-key theater. Shame: loud devotion that fails audit. Success: clean Atlas weigh-in + debt grace retained.

## 3. Society

Rank is vertical citizenship first — elevator keys, then oxygen grade, then plaque. Humiliation is public chart blush, red-tag, and diversion hearing. Street law prioritizes **grain truth** (manifest integrity) → **oxygen safety** → Administratum appeal. Local governance: silo councils, barge guild routes, oxygen-monitor orders with real HSE power, chart inspectors who save or starve. Representation is guild window and house key — not democracy. Rationing follows yield graphs; tax-as-wound is diversion fear and elevator window fees. Marriage/contract: **silo marriage** merges elevator keys and spoilage liability; divorce is contract partition first; love is not illegal, merely badly documented. **Route marriage** (rare) ties Navigator-adjacent charter families to downwell slots — sky-touching, sometimes suspect.

Death/name civic geography: chemical funerals and alkali pits after spore panic years; mourning as **hunger silence** (no feast noise during chart spike). Wall-name rumors exist beside yield graphs — memorial is silo plaque and starch sprinkle, not *Reclusiam* crypt law (do not steal).

**By class**

- **Rabble:** No elevator; street law is barge knife and monitor fear. Humiliation = dry-silo insult and open-sky exposure without kit. Contracts = paste oaths and debt to guilds. Forgotten always.
- **Common:** Plaque spine; guild representation; tax wound = window fees and honest-spoilage audits. Silo marriage among shift dynasties. Names remembered on district silo skins during hunger festivals.
- **Upper:** House keys; patronage representation; tax wound = audit survival and debt grace politics. Chart-Grace = bridge band holding civil continuity without peak shaft. Magnates name losses in marble; still treat them as cost.

**Friction matrix**

| Pressure | Rabble | Common | Upper |
|----------|--------|--------|-------|
| Who is “forgotten” | Always | By the Imperium, not by the silo | By Terra; refuse to be by Khan-Bator |
| Holy object | Mask, knife, starch paste | Seal, plaque, harvest bell | Chapel + clean audit + elevator key |
| Ministorum | Useless / dangerous (incense = death) | Wrong accent; paste communion useful | Patronage tool + status mask; spark licenses |
| Batavi presence | Watch / fear / dark pride | Shared Sweat respect; smooth charts | Necessary shadow; want influence without being owned |

## 4. Religion

**Official creed vs local accent:** Ecclesiarchy-baptized on record; Porta Nihili faith of work as **caloric honesty**. God is **fed**. Throne receipt is silo seal, manifest hash, Atlas weigh-in. Visiting preachers who demand procession smoke meet **chemical theology**: smoke without permit is mass murder attempt. Compromise liturgy: blessed pressure valves, communion paste, mute climb days on the tether (silence as holiness when load is holy). Effort doctrine / shovel theology arrives as Auxilia overseer humor when ecstatic imports collide with spark law (see ecclesiarchy-on-the-frontier).

**Priests vs laity:** Local clergy often hold efficiency and HSE-adjacent posts — coveralls under stoles, starch backpacks, valve blessings. Idle silk priests are suspect. Laity prefer seal over sermon when holiness needs a receipt.

**Holidays that stop work:** Hunger festivals (statistical — charts, not fireworks); mute climb / cable blessing days; calendar-saint slime-tide observances that pause barge routes during bloom risk; spark-curfew intensifications that stop open-air labor.

**Texts in practice:** Harvest-curve hagiographies; hue catechisms imported as rumor from Bator-Secundus (cross-link); Administratum graph liturgy projected on silo skins; Fire Devil folktales for children (spark taboo without Mechanicus briefing).

**Religious punishment:** Diversion hearings framed as theft from the Throne’s mouth; torch-without-permit as heresy-with-chemistry; reeducation under effort doctrine for imported ecstasy. Grey-clerk piety respected when seals prevent famine; mocked when forms ignore slime-bloom forecasts.

**Booth vs chapel:** Stamp / seal booth at silo gate often outranks chapel for daily holiness; chapels sprinkle starch on lift gears and hold paste communion. Incense chapels without oxygen permits do not survive monitor orders.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Fire Devil stories; paste oaths; chapel as warmth if sealed. Missionaries with incense are enemies. Forgotten faith = barge held anyway.
- **Common:** Throughput saints; gear prayer; mute climbs; soft contempt for empty thunder; Shared Sweat priests rise.
- **Upper:** Chapter-house patronage of silo chapels; window stamp piety; Chart-Grace quieter devotion that still audits. Loud devotion that fails audit = taboo.

**Contrast only:** Batavi honor **Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight** — not Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Shared words (Throne, seal, Wall) ≠ shared creed. Mortals still use chapel language; the *feel* is yield-first. Do not collapse Batavi into Ministorum in this file.

## 5. Economy

**World function / export:** Caloric factory — algae, slime starch, bulk calories measured in **tons per breath**. Secondary: fertilizer chain from **Bator-Secundus** (Phase C wing stub) — marriage metaphors borrow stable compounds. Tertiary: **Vacuum Harvest Fields** neurochem — dosimetry piety; workers joke *glass tongue*; law treats batch contamination as soul risk. Atlas Elevator / Golden Grain Station is the export bottleneck; Bottle Fair windows hitch to load-swap. Wing: [worlds-central-bastion.md](worlds-central-bastion.md) § **III *Tertia* wing**.

**How work runs:** Monoculture plains on algorithm green; slime barge routes; silo vertical shifts; oxygen-monitor HSE as production religion; chart inspectors as hunger/famine toggles.

**Tithe payment / failure smell:** Tithe is sealed tonnage + Atlas weigh-in hash. Failure smell is **blushing chart**, spoiled starch stench in sealed galleries, red-tag smoke residue, and the social silence of hunger festivals when the line will not go flat. Diversion is the charge when grain leaves the approved graph.

**Exemptions:** Debt grace for audit-survivor houses (Chart-Grace origin story); critical oxygen-monitor and sever-engineer isolation privileges; Chapter inspection windows that flatten civic queues temporarily (surface only).

**Markets vs ledger fiction:** Real markets = Bottle Fair creole, barge skim economies, window trading. Ledger fiction = clean yield graphs that ignore bloom; house books that call diversion “spoilage.” Wealth under this sky = elevator keys, oxygen grade, clean seals, Atlas priority — not decorative gold.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Filter-hour / mask-hour currency; barge skim; unpaid reclaim. Wealth = breath and a place on a barge. Failure smell hits them first.
- **Common:** Guild wages in starch and window rights; scum-reader honor pay; monitor junior stipends. Wealth = seal-clean plaque + lung trial respect. Tithe mechanics lived as harvest calendars.
- **Upper:** Tithe nobility tonnage poetry; house fertilizer contracts; route-marriage downwell slots. Chart-Grace wealth = debt grace + bridge passes. Peak wealth = private cars and private air.

## 6. Education

Literacy on Khan-Bator is **graph literacy** before poetry: yield curves, bloom forecasts, oxygen permits, elevator schedules. Who learns is who is kept useful; who is kept dark is who is kept haulable. Curriculum-as-virtue: honest spoilage reporting, spark taboo, balanced load, caloric honesty. Cost in bodies (lung trials, bloom years), years (apprenticeship on barges and silo lifts), and patronage (guest ink / Chart-Grace sponsorship into civil floors).

**Tracks**

| Track | Who | Virtue taught | Cost |
|-------|-----|---------------|------|
| **Barge / slime guild** | Rabble→common | Scum literacy, lung trial, route cant | Bodies in bloom; years on water |
| **Silo shift / plaque school** | Common | Chart reading, seal liturgy, gear prayer | Years + window patronage |
| **Oxygen-monitor novitiate** | Common (some Chart-Grace juniors) | HSE as holiness; spark law; red-tag ethics | Social fear; early death in fire years |
| **Chapel / paste school** | Common→upper patrons | Calendar saints, valve blessing, effort penance | Soft power; conflict with monitors if incense |
| **Administratum chart college** | Upper / Chart-Grace | Tonnage High Gothic, diversion law, Atlas weigh protocol | Years + house blood |
| **Cable / Atlas initiation** | Bridge + guild | Third-strand oath, mute climb, sever drill literacy | Bruises (*Atlas knee*); isolation of sever engineers |
| **House tutors** | Upper | Statistical poetry, face-hold, silo marriage contract | Patronage only |

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Kept dark of High Gothic; taught spark taboo and barge knife by survival. Literacy that matters = reading blooms like weather. Formal school = rare stamp charity or monitor labor reeducation.
- **Common:** Plaque schools and guild apprenticeships; curriculum-as-virtue = seal-clean yield. What literacy *does*: open elevator windows, survive audits, speak dock creole with Golden Grain crews.
- **Upper:** Chart colleges and house tutors; literacy *does* debt grace, tithe poetry, and influence without owning every shaft. Chart-Grace educate for bridge rooms — civil + inspection continuity — not magnate auction piety.
