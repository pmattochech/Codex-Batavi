## 1. Physical frame — breath as budget

**Khan-Bator Prime** sits under **dense atmosphere** and **super-oxygen**: combustion that would be festive elsewhere is **heresy with chemistry**. Open flame is **controlled**, licensed, timed — sparks **kill** in bulk.

**Surface grammar:** **monoculture plains** (algorithm green — one crop grammar repeated to horizon), **nutrient-slime oceans** (barge routes, **scum readers** as honor profession), **10 km silo-cities** (vertical citizenship — **elevator rights** are **blood**). Sky color reads **too bright** to off-world lungs; locals pride **blue lung** adaptation.

**Golden Grain Station / Atlas Elevator:** the **35,000 km** tether is **shared fate** theology made steel — **unbalanced load** is sin in dock cant and moral cant both.

## 2. Historical stratum

**Pre-Imperial granary logic:** populations learned **yield before eloquence** — saints named after **harvest curves**, not battlefields.

**Imperial mesh:** Castra Vetera consolidation turned Khan-Bator into **caloric engine** for **forge stacks** and **void logistics**. **Administratum** registers replaced oral silo myth slowly; **diversion** became the standard charge when grain left the approved graph.

**Men of Iron hangover (attenuated):** automation scares live in **oxygen discipline** — **mask laws**, **spark curfews**, **listen vow** at elevator inspection windows.

---

## 3. Economy — tons that eat prayers

* **Primary:** algae, slime starch, bulk calories — **factory biology** measured in **tons per breath**.

* **Secondary:** **fertilizer chain** from **Bator-Secundus** (ammonia skies, reagent lakes) — marriage metaphors borrow **stable compounds**; deaths borrow **controlled burn** rites.

* **Tertiary:** **Vacuum Harvest Fields** neurochem — dosimetry piety; workers joke **glass tongue**; law treats **batch contamination** as **soul risk**.

**Porta Nihili creed here:** God is **fed**. **Faith of work** sounds like **yield per hectare**, **sealed silo hash**, **Atlas knee** bruises worn as seniority.

## 4. Social structure — vertical citizenship

**Silo nobility:** families holding **elevator keys** and **priority descent** slots — not always oldest money; often **audit survivors** who survived a bad chart year and inherited **debt grace**.

**Slime bargemen:** guild routes across **nutrient oceans** — initiation includes **lung trial** and **scum literacy** (read blooms like weather).

**Oxygen monitor orders:** masked inspectors with **real HSE power** — respected as **angels who prevent fire-god** — feared when they **red-tag** a district’s **festal torch**.

**Hunger festivals:** **statistical** — charts projected on silo skins, **not** fireworks; relief expressed as **line goes flat**.

## 5. Kinship & marriage

**Silo marriage:** two houses merge **elevator keys** and **spoilage liability** — divorce is **contract partition** first; love is **not illegal**, merely **badly documented** (Castra-Vetera joke recycled locally).

**Grain oath:** hand in **starch paste** — parallel to **salt-in-the-ration** metaphor used in **Aethelgard** funeral bread; here it marks **shared tithe stomach**, not tide grief.

**Route marriage:** rare — **Navigator**-adjacent charter families who own **downwell** slots; viewed as **sky-touching**, occasionally **suspect** when **Administratum** audits spike.

## 6. Religion — three layers

**A. Porta Nihili basal:** **Throughput theology** translated into **caloric honesty**. **Throne receipt** is **silo seal**, **manifest hash**, **Atlas weigh-in**.

**B. Imperial Ecclesiarchy:** visiting preachers sometimes demand **procession smoke** — locals answer with **chemical theology**: smoke **without permit** is **mass murder attempt**. Compromise: **blessed pressure valves**, **communion paste**, **mute climb days** on the tether (silence as holiness when **load** is holy).

**C. Local cult complex:**

* **Calendar saints** — named after **flood metaphors** rewritten as **slime tide** — **not** water ignorance; **hydraulic literacy** dressed as hagiography.

* **Silo blessing** — chapels **sprinkle starch** on **lift gears**; **gear prayer** knuckle taps spread from **Noviomagus** dockers into **belt cant**.

* **Fire devil** folktale — **teaches children spark taboo** without Mechanicus briefing; **misread** by off-world priests as **primitive dualism**.

## 7. Death & mourning

**Burial:** **chemical funeral** culture bleeds from **Bator-Secundus** — **controlled burn** of remains where law allows; **spore panic** years force **alkali pit** interment — **odor theology** merges with HSE.

**Mourning register:** **hunger silence** — **no feast noise** during chart **spike** — parallel to **Sidus silence** attenuated, but keyed to **yield graphs**, not stars.

## 8. Law — diversion is theft from the Throne’s mouth

**Priority:** **grain truth** (manifest integrity) → **oxygen safety** → **Administratum** appeal.

**Heresy:** often filed as **diversion**, **unlogged spoilage**, **torch without permit** — ecstatic **Ministorum** imports that conflict with **spark law** get **reeducated** under **effort doctrine** (see **ecclesiarchy-on-the-frontier**) — **shovel theology** arrives as **Auxilia** overseer humor.

**Grey clerk piety:** respected when **seals** prevent **famine**; mocked when **forms** ignore **slime bloom** forecasts.

## 9. Language — hunger metaphors

**Silo cant:** fast **yield metaphors** — **“the chart blushes”** = spoilage risk.

**Dock creole:** mixes **High Gothic tonnage** with **Atlas braid** slang — mutually intelligible with **Golden Grain Station** crews system-wide.

**Administrative High Gothic:** spoken **cool** by tithe nobility — **statistical poetry** used in **hunger festivals** for **irony** and **terror** both.
