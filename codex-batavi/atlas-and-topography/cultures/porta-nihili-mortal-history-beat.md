## I. Age of Strife — the bottle

**Symptom:** Languages **compression**. Neighbors who survived the same **lane** invented **bottle creole** — numbers, debt, storm names, **feast rails** keyed to scarcity — because **High Gothic** arrived rarely and **eloquence** didn’t patch hulls.

**Symptom:** **Witness debt** ethics — IOUs to the dead who held **quota** beside you — older than **Administratum** receipts; **salt-in-the-ration** and **grain oath** metaphors begin as **hunger diplomacy**, not sermon.

**Symptom:** **Trade marriage** prototypes — keys, ropes, nets merged before **triple stamp** romance existed.

---

## II. Men of Iron aftermath — lane choke

**Symptom:** **Listen vow** and **relay silence** generalized — broadcast framed as **sin** where **automation ghosts** still haunted **relay houses**, **Reef** spines, **elevator** ears.

**Symptom:** **Grey clerk piety** rises — **correct filing** as **salvation** when **routes** die faster than **rhetoric**; **ledger grace** replaces **Absolvitor** fantasy for workers who cannot stop lines.

**Symptom:** **First weld** salvage theology — **claim** rituals on wreck-strewn lanes (**Steel Belt**, tide flats) become **law before law catches up**.

---

## III. Imperial reunification — super-tithe & Batavi altitude

**Symptom:** **Throne receipt** vulgarized — proof of useful output becomes **street challenge** (*prove you paid the species forward*) — **faith of work** aligned with **Administratum** hashes without requiring **cathedral literacy**.

**Symptom:** **Forge-throughput Ecclesiarchy** alliance (**Iron Zeal**) — priests in **coveralls**, communion at **stations** — outside missionaries read frontier **silence** as **atheism**; locals read **noise worship** as **theft of oxygen**.

**Symptom:** **Batavi altitude** reads as **weather** — patrol geometry alters **market layout**, **fair seasons**, **silence festivals** — mortals adapt routes; **proximity substrata** absorbs habit **without** doctrinal pamphlets ([witness-inheritance-chronicles.md](../../chronicles/09-frontier-life/witness-inheritance-chronicles.md)).
