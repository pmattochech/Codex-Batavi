## 1. Physical frame — rust as budget

**Noviomagus Prime** sits at **1.12G**, crust hollowed and ribbed with adamantium, under permanent soot and acid **Black Rain**. ~**120 billion** in stacked hives. Life is **rust management**: filters, gutters, reclaim vats.

**Provincial capital feel:** Imperial forms arrive intact (Administratum, Ministorum where soot allows, High Gothic in law); local identity stays **forge-first**. Citizens think in **shifts**, **district stamps**, **yield**, and **rain schedules**. The Throne is honored when **the line holds** and the **tithe seal** reads clean.

**Fork:** **Provincial Roman** energy — High Gothic law, local forge cant; urban *limitanei* who built the wall themselves. Direct Chapter administrative shadow.

---

## 2. Faith of work (Porta Nihili accent)

* **The Emperor is paid in labor.** Completed shift, logged manifest, child registered to quota, fuse replaced before brownout — prayers with receipts.
* **Speech is cheap; seals are holy.** Ecstatic preachers who cannot audit a cargo slate = entertainment or suspect. Stamp booth over chapel when holiness needs a receipt — *“God saw the seal.”*
* **Silence can be offering** (relay silence, listen-only watches) — Men of Iron hangover as habit.
* Mortals remain **Ecclesiarchy-baptized** on record. Accent: God loves **the useful hand**.
* **Contrast only:** Batavi honor Ancestor-Sovereign / Throne-weight — not Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Population still uses chapel language; the **feel** is work-first.

**Throughput theology:** Saints are forge masters whose dies never wandered. Sin is **unlogged scrap** and **missed torque**.

## 3. Three classes (anthropological strata)

**Locked labels:** **Rabble** · **Common** · **Upper**.  
Not Administratum legal castes — fiction strata. Class is felt first as **filter grade**, then **plaque**, then **which elevator car** a body boards without looking.

### 3.1 Rabble

**Who:** Undercroft reclaim crews, gutter runners, unstamped scrap-sorters, rain-blind beggars between elevator rights, Black-Rain lungers selling filter time by the hour.

| Mood premise | How it functions |
|--------------|------------------|
| Duty without audience | Survival *is* the shift — lowest drains held so stacks above do not drown in rust |
| Forgotten, not faithless | **Pre-forgotten** — never on the ledger worth remembering. Oath: don’t steal a brother’s filter; don’t leave a body in open rain |
| Weathered service | Faith = hands that still move |
| Loss as weather | Missing people are weather reports; grief short, then back to the vat |

**Sound:** Short sentences, dark humor. Proto–*We hold* as “we don’t leave the pipe.”

### 3.2 Common

**Who:** Shift guilds, middle-stack district dynasties (e.g. Guild of the Lower Gutter, Third Shift Concord), stamp clerks, forge hands with **civil ID plaques** — spine of throughput theology.

| Mood premise | How it functions |
|--------------|------------------|
| Duty without audience | The seal *is* the prayer; Wall above must not starve for shells |
| Forgotten, not faithless | Nihilus-edge known; baptize and stamp in **forge cant**, not cathedral ecstasy |
| Weathered service | Virtue = logged torque, on-time shift, rain schedule; sin = unlogged scrap, empty speech |
| Loss as weather | Convoy silence and Wall-name rumors filed next to rust |

**Sound:** Exhausted loyalty. *Rain-closed face.* Soft contempt for loud missionaries; quiet respect for Shared Sweat without speeches.

### 3.3 Upper

**Who:** Stack-elevator magnates, forge-house elders, Administratum liaison blood, Ministorum chapter-house patrons, **lower-upper bridge houses**.

**Solis family (lock):** **Lower upper class.** Bridge household — forge-cant rooms and Chapter-adjacent civil rooms without peak elevator ownership. Soft power = Shared Sweat / kitchen continuity and quiet *face-hold*. Still upper, not mid-common.

| Mood premise | How it functions |
|--------------|------------------|
| Duty without audience | Audience is **local** (Chapter shadow, tithe, rival houses); form keeps the forge legal |
| Forgotten, not faithless | Status anxiety — over-invest in seals and “correct” devotion so outsiders cannot call them fringe |
| Weathered service | Feed the machine; charity as rationed usefulness; fear useless piety as much as heresy |
| Loss as weather | Losses named in marble and ledgers — still treated as **cost** |

**Sound:** Cooler sentences; forge-cant under High Gothic. Lower-upper / Solis: quieter than magnates; less auctioned piety.

### 3.4 Friction matrix

| Pressure | Rabble | Common | Upper |
|----------|--------|--------|-------|
| Who is “forgotten” | Always | By the Imperium, not by the district | By Terra; refuse to be by Noviomagus |
| Holy object | Filter, knife, shared scrap | Seal, plaque, shift bell | Chapel + clean audit + elevator key |
| Ministorum | Useless / dangerous | Wrong accent, sometimes useful | Patronage tool + status mask |
| Batavi presence | Watch / fear / dark pride | Shared Sweat respect | Necessary shadow; want influence without being owned |

## 4. Class shards

Atlas-wide color still applies: *rain-closed face*, *black tooth*, *gear prayer* (common default). Class-specific:

| Class | Slang | Rite | Taboo |
|-------|-------|------|-------|
| **Rabble** | *filter-hour* | *pipe watch* (hand on pipe, hand on brother) | Body in **open Black Rain** |
| **Common** | *seal-clean* | *gear prayer* (three knuckle taps) | **Unlogged scrap** |
| **Upper** | *face-hold* | *window stamp* (house seal / chapel lintel) | **Loud devotion** that fails audit |

**Solis color:** *window stamp* + *face-hold*; kitchen / Shared Sweat as soft power in the lower-upper band.

## 5. Black Rain + elevator rights (class police)

No separate caste ministry required for most of life. **Acid weather** and **vertical keys** sort bodies. The mesh **feels** the border before a clerk speaks.

### 5.1 Black Rain

| Stratum | Exposure | Meaning |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **Rabble** | High / chronic | Gutter and reclaim work; filter-hours as currency; open-rain dead = name erased |
| **Common** | Managed | Canopies, shift tunnels; rain schedule as moral calendar |
| **Upper** | Bought distance | Sealed galleries; Solis band still *knows* the smell; magnates pretend otherwise |

**Filter grade** reads class: cracked rag → guild filter → house cartridge. Shared Sweat crises briefly flatten rain class; hierarchy returns with the next dry gallery.

### 5.2 Elevator rights

| Stratum | Stance | Meaning |
|---------|--------|---------|
| **Rabble** | Denied / service-only | Stairs, illegal climbs; mid-stack catch = beating or reclaim labor |
| **Common** | Shift rights | Plaque + window; wrong car = not *seal-clean*; windows traded like dowries |
| **Upper** | House keys | Private cars; Solis **bridge passes** for civil + Chapter-adjacent floors without owning the shaft |

**Civil ID plaque** at the gate is liturgy. **Guest ink** is patronage currency. Chapter precincts = **parallel vertical** (not “upper class”). Lower-upper houses are the mortals most used to that parallel without claiming it.

### 5.3 Intersections

1. **Wet shaft** — rabble/common meet; upper almost never.  
2. **Dry gallery after shift bell** — common up; rabble turned; upper already inside.  
3. **Storm lock** — priority only; stratum learned in the wait.  
4. **Solis pattern** — covered routes + bridge passes.

**Fiction tells:** filter → plaque → car boarded without looking. Insult: open rain without kit / dead in the rain. Mercy: filter-hour or guest ink.

## 6. Institutional slices (jobs inside classes)

Not a fourth class — robes that change **which holy object** and **which boss**, not rain/elevator rank.

| Institution | Rabble | Common | Upper | Friction note |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|---------------|
| **Forge-priests** | Gutter tech, filter-hour pay | Shift priest by stamp booth | House Magos-liaison | Prefer stamp over chapel |
| **Ministorum** | Rain-side confessors | District chapel vs seal-booth | Chapter-house / *face-hold* | Empty thunder falls; Shared Sweat priests rise |
| **Administratum** | Forgers of windows | Plaque / ledger spine | Audit chiefs, registries | Enforce elevators more than sermons |
| **Guard / PDF billets** | Levy fodder, rain pickets | Career militia, Shared Sweat pride | Officer houses, Solis staff | Closest to Wall sweat without being Chapter; Ork hate daily; xenos “specialists” stay alien |
