## 0. Preamble

### 0.1 Physical frame — pressure as budget

**Station-Maleventum**, called **Ultima Arx** in dock cant and Administratum gloss alike, is a modified *Ramilles*-class fortress grown into the metallic heart of a giant asteroid. It does not “orbit a homeworld”; it *is* the homeworld for most of its civilians — stacked pressure decks, gun galleries, dry docks, and sealed civilian rings facing the **Iron Strait**, the only mined passable route through the **Great Reef**.

Outside the hull, the blue giant **Maleventum** throws coronal mass and ion storms that treat unshielded sensors as jokes and ungrounded armor as scrap. Inside, life is **pressure management**: bulkheads, gasket cycles, mag-boot rights, drill schedules that interrupt births and weddings without apology. Gravity is **station-set** — often light enough that micro-g balance becomes childhood etiquette — and the slow death is not acid rain but **decompression rumor**, **EM hash blindness**, and the knowledge that a wrong hatch kills a corridor faster than any sermon.

Citizens think in **holds**, **salvo windows**, **ground straps**, **pressure tickets**, and **watch rotations**. The provincial feel is **fortress civitas**: Imperial forms arrive (Administratum seals, Ministorum chapels where vibration allows, High Gothic in law), but identity stays **gun-and-seal first**. The Throne is honored when the **strait holds**, the **manifest hash** matches, and the **bulkhead sings dry**.

**Witness Ford** texture (mortal): outward choke beyond the reef maze — convoy triage, chart escort, lost ships, bad emergence, mis-filed transit logs. Horror is **route failure** and **hull truth**, not calendar tricks. Mortals do not speak of the island “bending time”; they speak of **coffin ledgers**, **Insular chart-houses**, and the statistical death of blind warp guesswork (see galactic lock).

### 0.2 Fork

**Not Noviomagus:** no Black Rain elevator theology, no forge-stack rust budget as primary metaphor. Gear prayer exists as import cant; here the holy object is a **sealed hatch** and a **paid current**, not a torque wrench alone.

**Not Tempest-V:** not vibration-monk minimalism. Ultima Arx is crowded, drilled, naval-adjacent, loud with sirens and soft with open-*vox* discipline.

**Not Abyssal-Anchor / Nox:** this file does **not** absorb dock-noir entrepôt culture or black-glass gravity mysticism. Those bodies keep fork stubs in [worlds-threshold.md](worlds-threshold.md) until their own ethnographies are ordered.

**Porta Nihili accent:** Throne is **hold**. Pilgrimage is **watch rotation**. Baptism is **pressure test** — *“sealed like bulkhead.”* Virtue is continuity under siege-normal. Outsiders misread Spartan silence as empty faith; locals hear it as **gun theology** — discipline thick, myth lean, receipts thicker than incense.

### 0.3 Sorting border (class police)

- **Primary sorter:** **Pressure ticket / sealed-deck clearance** (which bulkhead you may cross without escort; which mag-boot lane you own).
- **Secondary sorter:** **Gun-family plaque / strait-house charter** (hereditary battery and route rights) and **mute-runner ink** (courier license that never echoes on open net).
- **Felt before a clerk speaks:** gasket smell on clothes; whether a body clips a **ground strap** by habit; soft speech on open channels; whether boots score mag-tread without looking.

### 0.5 Local mortal sin & Legatus read

- **Local mortal sin — false pressure:** Declaring a compartment, route, hull, or body **sealed / grounded / safe** when gauge, cam hash, ground strap, or Navy/dock slate says otherwise. Class colors: Rabble *borrowed ticket / lied hatch*; Common *misfiled pressure log / open-band echo during hash*; Upper *charter fraud / route marriage without Ultima Arx stamp*. Enforced by dock magistrates, Arbites when blood follows, Navy boarding officers, and social death from mute-runner guilds. Reef accent of the same sin: **ungrounded boast** (see Twin).
- **Legatus public read (mortal):** **the Soft Voice** — open channel quiet; **Static-Lord** — EM weather as office. Mortals say he counts **two seals** before hard orders. Surface only; no gene-seed, no *Axiom* lecture in populi mouth.

## Twin — Great Reef (deep mortal culture)

*Same civilization mesh as Ultima Arx; not a second ethnography file. Reef clans live the strait’s weather; the station files their seals. Holds, arcs, and grounding law belong here in depth.*

### T1. Physical frame — current as weather

The **Great Reef** is the native conductor-rich belt under Maleventum’s electric scourge: asteroids that charge until million-volt arcs write temporary scripture across ungrounded armor. **Static** is weather. **Grounding** is survival. **Lightning** is calendar. Only **mined straits** — chiefly approaches to the **Iron Strait** — are passable; elsewhere the maze hides **Wasp Nest** batteries and the bones of crews who treated ion as scenery.

Mortal habitat is **hold culture**: nickel spines, grounded docks, clan barges, strait-pilot huts, cable shrines. Gravity varies rock to rock; childhood is learning which rail is live. Slow death is **arc flash**, **ungrounded greed**, and **listen-vow** failure on open band.

### T2. Fork & Porta accent

**Storm-barbaricum** with maritime instinct in rock — reef law oral and fast. God is **well grounded**. Virtue is continuity — *“current paid.”* Sin is **unshielded boast** (attracts arc — literal and moral). Not Noviomagus rust; not station citadel paperwork first — though Ultima Arx stamp still owns legal life.

### T3. Social bands on the reef (same labels, reef material)

- **Rabble:** Day-labor on foreign holds; spark-scarred; no clan charter; sell grounding hours.  
- **Common:** Reef clans (route families), strait pilots, iron priests who bless **cables** and **skin**, wasp-vow keepers.  
- **Upper / Strait houses (bridge):** Charter families whose **route marriage** and salvage *first weld* claims file at Ultima Arx; own pilot seats and wasp-adjacent silence rights.

### T4. Holds, arcs, rites (mortal)

| Practice | What it is | Class color |
|----------|------------|-------------|
| **Ground rite** | Clip copper tongue to rail before speech or work | Rabble survival; Common piety; Upper theater + real risk |
| **Spark marriage** | Bond sealed under **controlled** arc | Common/Upper; Rabble watch, rarely afford control gear |
| **Wasp vow** | Silence until battery “speaks” (local signal discipline) | Common gun-adjacent; Upper political silence |
| **Salt in the hull** | Purification — ground the ship’s mood | All; Rabble with borrowed salt |
| **Listen vow** | No shout on open band during storm charge | Men of Iron hangover as civic religion |

**Local sin accent:** **ungrounded boast** = **false pressure** spoken in reef grammar — claiming safe transit, safe hull, or safe tongue without strap, hash, or pilot word.

### T5. Economy-ecology (reef)

Primary: pilot fees, grounded salvage, conductor ore under lethal constraints, battery labor for Wasp-adjacent houses (civilian periphery — not Chapter doctrine). Tithe and legal hash still route through **Station-Maleventum**. Failure smell: ozone and burned gasket; moral smell: a mouth that transmitted when the storm was listening.

### T6. Batavi / Navy surface (reef)

*Octava* void presence reads as **weather that boards** — hull truth over auspex lies in mortal proverb. Navy cutters and chartist escorts buy **pilot seats**; Insular Navigators do not casually walk clan holds without escort etiquette. Shared Sweat appears as joint boarding drills and dead crews counted in the same silence.

## 1. Military (mortal)

Ultima Arx is **siege-normal**. Mortal force is organized to keep the Iron Strait and civilian rings alive until Navy and Chapter geometry arrive — and to die usefully when they are late. PDF and gun-family militias own galleries; Arbites own riot law in sealed corridors; Guard draft eats overflow; Shared Sweat contact is sweat and schedule, not doctrine lecture.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** First into duct pickets and drill overflow; levy fodder when boarding alarms climb; die in outer rings when Wasp or pirate pressure spikes. Learn friend–foe discrimination as **who shoots the hatch you were told to hold** — not as sealed Chapter targeting law.
- **Common:** Career PDF rota, gun-family crews, mute-runner wartime courier corps, Shared Sweat pride without speeches. Riot geometry = corridor choke, mag-boot lanes, pressure doors as crowd control.
- **Upper:** Officer houses, strait-house command of hereditary batteries, Navy liaison desks, magistrate wartime courts. Buy distance from first contact; still lose heirs when the strait truly burns.

**Worked examples**

1. **Salvo drill vs wedding** — Common couple’s *strait ring* ceremony paused mid-vow; priest and gun-sergeant share the same clock; Upper guests already in sealed gallery.  
2. **Arbites corridor riot** — Rabble reclaim dispute; shock mauls in micro-g; Common plaques checked before names.  
3. **Shared Sweat surface** — PDF gallery and Batavi boarding team share a bulkhead for an hour; mortals remember helm silence and soft *vox*, not gene lectures.  
4. **Navy press** — Outer Gate or system patrol needs hands; Common gun plaques become draft grace; Rabble become numbers.

## 2. Culture

Amenities are scarce and drill-shaped: leisure is a **quiet second** between sirens, a licensed spark show on a controlled rail, a bottle-creole song that never hits open band during hash. Aesthetic is industrial grey, gasket black, cable shine — **Spartan** without Earth names. Public success = clean pressure ticket and a child who survived baptism-as-pressure-test. Public shame = **false pressure** proven on cam, or an open-band echo that “called the arc.”

Non-Mass rites: *arx knee* (kneel on grating — rank geography), *strait ring* (wedding band cut from cable), *first salvo child* (born during drill — lucky or cursed, debated), ground rite imports from the reef, mute-runner ink renewal.

**By class — include Sound**

- **Rabble:** Leisure = sleep and borrowed spark-hour; humor short and cruel; Sound: hatch verbs, decompression jokes, soft when sirens teach fear. Success = still breathing after overflow duty. Shame = body left in unsealed duct.
- **Common:** Leisure = guild halls with sealed windows; bottle creole markets; Sound: soft open-*vox*, rain-closed cousins become *hash-closed face*; gear prayer knuckles beside pressure taps. Success = *seal-clean* month. Shame = misfiled log.
- **Upper:** Leisure = citadel quiet rooms, licensed chapel music that yields to drill, Navy dinner geometry; Sound: cooler sentences, gun cant under High Gothic; Strait houses quieter, less auctioned piety. Success = *face-hold* through audit. Shame = loud devotion that fails hash.

## 3. Society

Rank is felt as **which air and which gun**. Humiliation is public ticket revocation, grating knees in drill lock, mute-runner refusal to carry your slate. Street law is corridor law: dock magistrates first, Arbites when blood or tithe fraud climbs, Navy when the hull is theirs. Representation = guild seats and strait-house councils that argue salvage and route rights; Administratum appeal is slow mail through hash.

Rationing follows siege logic — calories and gasket kits both. Tax-as-wound: pressure inspections, charter fees, draft windows; exemptions cling to Navy-critical and Chapter-adjacent billets (surface envy, not doctrine).

Marriage/contract: *strait ring*; *route marriage* when Navigator or pilot rights merge (filed at Ultima Arx); reef *spark marriage* for clan bonds. Death geography: memorial plates on sealed bulkheads; Wall-name rumor for those who died “beyond the ford” — civic grief without *Reclusiam* crypt law.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Unrepresented except as riot risk; marriage informal; names erased by next drill if unpaid.
- **Common:** Guild voice; plaque marriages; mourning silence keyed to watch bells.
- **Upper:** Charter politics; route marriage as ambition and suspicion; marble plates that still read as cost.

## 4. Religion

**Official creed:** Ecclesiarchy-baptized on record — God-Emperor language in chapel ledgers.  
**Local accent:** Faith of work as **hold**, **watch**, **seal**. Stamp booth and pressure desk often outrank idle cathedral time. Priests who cannot read a gauge are entertainment or suspect.

Holidays that stop work: rare — **ground feast** after major ion storm; **salvo saints’** maintenance day; imported Castra-Vetera autumn liturgy truncated by drill. Texts in practice: pressure manuals treated as near-liturgy; High Gothic collects spoken stiff. Religious punishment: public grating knees, ticket revocation, heresy filed as **false pressure** / open-band blasphemy before theology climbs.

Booth vs chapel: booth wins when holiness needs a receipt — *“God saw the seal.”* Chapel wins for Upper *face-hold* and Navy funeral geometry.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Rain-side / duct-side confessors; God = air that stayed; little patience for incense in drill.
- **Common:** District chapel vs stamp booth détente; soft contempt for ecstatic imports.
- **Upper:** Chapter-house patrons; correct devotion as status armor; Strait houses prefer quiet seals.

**Contrast only:** Batavi honor **Ancestor-Sovereign** / Throne-weight — **not** Ministorum God-Emperor worship. Shared words ≠ shared creed. Mortals still use chapel language; the **feel** is gun-and-seal first.

## 5. Economy

**World function:** Iron Strait choke logistics — docking, rearm, repair, pilot brokerage, conductor/salvage hash, Navy and chartist throughput under EM scourge. Export is **passage made survivable** and **guns kept fed**; import is food, gasket chemistry, chart escorts, munitions.

Work runs on watch rotations and drill interruptions. Tithe payment: manifests through Ultima Arx registries; failure smell is ozone, burned insulation, and a clerk’s mild voice asking for **hash match**. Exemptions: Navy-critical crews, Insular chart-house bonded staff, certain gun-family wartime billets — resented as holy favoritism.

Real markets: bottle-creole holds, mute-runner black-slate (illegal echo still happens), reef salvage courts. Ledger fiction: tonnage that existed only on open band. Wealth = **air rights**, **charter slots**, **mag-boot lanes**, and the power to make someone else’s pressure ticket wait.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Sell hours and risk; paid in filter time and leftover calories; theft charged as diversion from the Throne’s passage.
- **Common:** Guild wages, plaque dowries, pilot fees; virtue = honest log.
- **Upper:** Charter rents, Navy contracts, audit immunity purchased with usefulness; *route marriage* as capital merger.

## 6. Education

Who learns: those whose tickets and houses require literacy in gauges, High Gothic forms, and soft-*vox* discipline. Who is kept dark: outer-ring labor taught only enough not to kill a corridor — and sometimes not even that.

Tracks: **gun-family apprenticeship**; **dock guild schools**; **mute-runner ink colleges** (silence as curriculum); **chapel/Administratum** clerk tracks; **Navy cadet feeder** desks; **strait-pilot** oral law (reef) bonded to Arx stamp. Curriculum-as-virtue: seal honesty, hash literacy, friend–foe civilian drills as “who opens which hatch,” not sealed Chapter auspex law. Cost: years of hearing damage, EM exposure, patronage debts, bodies lost to training decompressions.

Literacy *does* this: lets you argue a ticket, marry a route, survive a Navy boarding interview, and prove you did not commit **false pressure**.

**By class**

- **Rabble:** Oral duct lore; stolen primer pages; literacy as escape or danger.
- **Common:** Plaque schools; gauge catechism; bottle creole + stiff High Gothic.
- **Upper:** House tutors; Navy/Administratum polish; Strait-house children learn both grating knees and dinner geometry.
