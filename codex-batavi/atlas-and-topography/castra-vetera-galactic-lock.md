# Castra Vetera — galactic lock & Batavi identity charter

**Status:** Authoritative for **new** atlas, organization, and political prose. **Staff companion (nine-pole roster, CV calendar, MoI rails):** [`foundation-war.md`](../personae-command-index/events-and-chronologies/foundation-war.md).

**Supersedes (retired names):** ***Porta Nihili*** (strategic designation), **Loken Passage** (astrographic funnel). Legacy chronicles may still mention them; new wiki/atlas text uses this lock.

## I. What it means to be Batavi

| Idea | Lock |
|------|------|
| **Historical core** | **Island people** — neither fully of the Imperium’s heart nor of the open void; **essential**, **covenant-bound**, **multi-skilled** |
| **Castra Vetera** | The **island** where they **become themselves** — identity gold, not only material prize |
| **Role** | **Wall against the tide** — Chaos, xenos, renegade void, plague-traffic; **the tide is anything** that would flood **inward** |
| **Passage doctrine** | **Evil does not pass inward**; **need passes** under witness, tithe, and geometry — **selective triage**, not open door |
| **Gene mystery** | **Unknown** Founding and **unknown** progenitor on **every** open layer; chimeric **legion-echo** phenotype possible — the Chapter **accepts** the mystery and **does not** hunt parent-Legion receipts |
| **Ancestor** | **Only the Emperor** counts as ancestor in Batavi mouth — not primarch laundry lists, not template genealogy |
| **Faith (40k prose only)** | **Do not** write the Chapter as Ministorum-style **God-Emperor worshippers**. They **honor the divinity the Emperor carries** — oath, Throne-weight, **Ancestor-Sovereign** — without chapel ecstasy. *(Author scaffold: ancestor-spirit / Orixá-like weight — **never** name Umbanda or real-world religions in `codex-batavi/` text.)* |
| **Imperial Guard** | **Shared Sweat** extends to **war council**: same line of fire, plans offered **alongside** Lord-Militants and regiment command — **respecting** chain of command, **not** bypassing it |
| **Motto** | *We hold.* — inward seal |

### Space Wolves — “proof of Russ blood” (author beat)

When a Space Wolf points at wolf-marked armor as **proof** of Russ lineage, a Batavi may answer along these lines (tone **flat**, not heresy-preaching):

— Look again. If you insist on reading our plate as genealogy, you will find **every old Legion** in us. We are not here to recover a formula. The only ancestor we count is the Emperor. Does your proof still matter?

**Open record stays:** **unknown** — do not confirm Russ, Fenris, or any parent Chapter.

## II. Insula Tenebrarum — where the island sits

| Field | Lock |
|-------|------|
| **Fan subsector name** | **Insula Tenebrarum** |
| **Polity / mesh** | **Castra Vetera** — **Nine Phalanx** (three macro-systems, nine operational worlds) |
| **Segmentum** | **Ultima** |
| **Imperial half** | **Imperium Nihilus** — weak Astronomican, dark-Imperium logistics |
| **Placement** | **North–northeast Ultima Nihilus rim** — **Eastern Fringe limes** (facing the Fringe, **not** inside it). **Distant from Ultramar** — tithe and law pressure without Tetrarch backyard. **Not** Nachmund / Vigilus (**Obscurus** gauntlet). **Not** Jericho, Koronus, Ghoul Stars, or Pacificus as **address** |
| **Outward cheek** | **Mixed fringe void** — Tau, Ork scrap, Drukhari, Necron wake-sign, renegade traffic; **no single** prime invader on the open record |
| **Administratum gloss** | *Insula Tenebrarum — Ultima Nihilus, rim braid on the Eastern Fringe limes; critical filter node, orphan of Ultramar.* |

### Delta (two rivers — Germania + Rome mix)

| Cheek | Outward hunger (“Germania”) | Inward pressure (“Rome”) |
|-------|---------------------------|---------------------------|
| **Void river** | Fringe dead stars, xenos drift, raider ecology | Spies, smugglers, opportunist traffic testing the ford |
| **Storm river** | Regional **warp ribbon** (fan: **Tenebrarum Ribbon**) — cult bleed, renegade craft | Tithe law, Administratum witness, corridor **demand** |

The **island** is the braid between them. Batavi **filter** what may cross toward Imperial consumers.

### Physical ford (outward choke) — **Witness Ford** (locked)

**Canonical:** **Witness Ford** — where convoys earn **witness** and **tithe receipt** before inward routing.

**Alternatives (legacy / regional slang only):** Cordon Ford · Insula Gate · Tenebrarum Reach · Filtered Ford.

### Three marsh channels (triangle warp routes)

English thematic names — one regulated warp channel per system pair; **short warp forbidden** inside the mesh except via these three:

| Systems | Channel name |
|---------|----------------|
| **Central Bastion** ↔ **Crucible** | **Forge Marsh Channel** |
| **Crucible** ↔ **Threshold** | **Tempest Marsh Channel** |
| **Threshold** ↔ **Central Bastion** | **Crown Marsh Channel** |

**Iron Highways** = sublight **marsh roads** between the three islands (congested realspace arteries). **Per-channel** coffin data — delta geometry, not one galaxy funnel.

### Warp travel — **dangerous lanes**, not time anomaly (locked)

| Rule | Lock |
|------|------|
| **Retired** | Subsector-wide **temporal dilation**, **ricochet rewind**, **“fast dome” aging**, **500 yrs inside = decades outside** — **obsolete** in new prose unless a file explicitly preserves **in-universe myth** |
| **Realspace** | Noviomagus and the **Nine Phalanx** run **normal strategic-depth** time |
| **Warp** | **Extremely dangerous** approach, egress, and **marsh-channel** translation — **Void Tides**, ribbon harmonics, pylon feedback, weak Astro. Horror is **lost ships**, **bad emergence**, **mis-filed transit logs** — **not** cells rewinding age |
| **Who may cross** | **Insular chart-houses** — **Navis Nobilite** covenants **bonded to Castra Vetera** with sealed **coffin ledgers** for **Witness Ford** and each **marsh channel**. Generic Nobilite or blind Astro-guesswork = **statistical death** |
| **Batavi practice** | Chapter convoys **embed** house Navigators or buy **chart escort** under ***Lex Ignis*** receipt; Strategium treats **marsh navigation** as **munition**, not ornament |
| **Ordo Chronos** | **Calendar correlation** and **beacon calibration** — explains why **CV annals** and **~M42** tithe scrolls **disagree** without claiming the forge-world **runs fast** |
| **Chronostrife** | Post–Rift **Terran dating** contested; **CV** is the stable **operational** metronome |

**In-fiction line:** *The island does not bend time. It bends routes — and only those who were born to its charts know which bends are survivable.*

## III. Golden ground (two layers)

1. **Identity** — Foundation crucible, **Der Batav**, **Cohors Batavorum** naming; where the Chapter stops being a shadow hunting parentage.  
2. **Material** — Rare ore, industrial and agricultural throughput, fan **maiden** / **wraithbone-grade** bodies — why outsiders **war** for the island.

### Who wants the island (atlas / later eras)

| Faction | Appetite |
|---------|----------|
| **Tau** | Resource pocket for Greater Good |
| **Iron Warriors / Black Legion / Vashtor-aligned** | Forge throughput — arms, armor, munitions |
| **Aeldari** | Maiden / soul-material logic |
| **Chaos** | Storm-ribbon cults + industrial soul |
| **Imperium** | **Cannot lose the filter** — super-tithe + inward seal |

**Foundation War:** antagonist roster unchanged (no Tyranid **fleet** driver). Splinter or rogue xenos **pressure** belongs to **post-crucible** or **rim** atlas, not crucible retcon.

## IV. Imperial tolerance (super-tithe)

Terra tolerates non-*Codex* mass and biological horror because **removing the Batavi filter** opens the inward vector — and because **Insula Tenebrarum** pays **≥15% over** Administratum quota in arms, ore, and cordon intelligence.
