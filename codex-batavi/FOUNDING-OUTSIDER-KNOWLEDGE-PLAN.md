## 1. Design rules

1. **Default Imperial face:** registers show **unknown Founding** / **undisclosed progenitor**; outsiders **infer** from phenotype and gossip.  
2. **No omniscient Administratum file** that quietly says “Sons of Horus” unless **you** write a leak beat — treat that as **Vermilion-tier** damage.  
3. **Inquisition** may **suspect** chimerism or Cawl-era stitching; **proof** is bounded by what audits and samples they actually extracted under treaty.  
4. **Timeline hygiene:** early M42 voices do not use **Strand α/β** labels; sealed briefings after ~048.M42 may, in **appropriate** POV.

## 3. Scene prompts (chronicle-ready)

- **Wrong certainty:** A remembrancer files a **confident** parent-Chapter guess; *Strategium* **declines comment** — record tension without confirming.  
- **Almost-right:** Hereticus savant lists **chimeric indicators**; hits **phenotype** but misattributes **which** archive threads merged.  
- **Watch medicae:** Argues with Chapter liaison over a **Grey-tier** flag — **professional** friction, no theology.  
- **Post-048.M42 sealed briefing:** Only Triumvirate + *Vermilion* scribe — **Strand α/β** allowed; same facts **cannot** be spoken in an Administratum hall without consequence.
