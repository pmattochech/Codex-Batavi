## WIKI: *LEX IMPERIALIS* AND IRON JUSTICE

**I. The law’s invitation (definitive presence)** Unlike many planetary lords and Chapter Masters who fear Adeptus Arbites scrutiny and isolate their worlds, Lord Castellan Alaric von Helis executed the opposite maneuver. He personally requested definitive, expanded Judges on the Castra Vetera frontier. For the Triumvirate, Arbites are not intruders; they are essential gears holding *Lex Imperialis* and stability. Batavi work alongside Magistrates so law is applied not through cruelty but to maximize absolute sector efficiency.

**II. Crime mathematics (failure treatment)** On hive worlds elsewhere in the Imperium, a laborer who faints from exhaustion or hunger and, in falling, drops a raw-material container delaying tithe is summarily beaten or executed by an Arbites as saboteur and traitor. On Noviomagus, Arbites operate under doctrine adapted by *Axiom Rationis* coldness.

They do not beat or kill the weak laborer; they process him. The exhausted individual is collected, fed biomass rations, and treated medically. The Wall does not execute parts that can be repaired. Yet the offense is rigorously logged. The act is filed under **“Gear Negligence.”** Punishment is not death but imposition of extra production quota once health returns. The laborer is also put under constant medical reporting so the Imperium ensures that “gear” is not damaged enough to break the greater machine. Justice feeds the body only so it has strength to pay its debt to the forge.

**III. Joint hunt (Silence Cohort)** If physical failure gets treatment and extra work, real treason and duty evasion get surgical terror. The **Silence Cohort** — Chapter counter-intelligence, ivory right pauldron — works in joint jurisdiction with *Lex Imperialis*. Beyond correlating records and communications, these warriors **patrol the city** specialized in **patterns**: a wharf’s, a shift’s, a district’s *baseline* (how people walk, speak, breathe, fix gaze, sound under strain, smell). They detect **anomalies in the whole** — scent is one channel among several, with **body sounds**, **gait**, **speech**, and **gaze direction**. Against the Alpha Legion, which cultivates “perfect” disguises, ivory doctrine is explicit: **nothing is too perfect**; integral hyper-correction of the role is itself the signature. Against cults and hybrids, they read **micro-dissonance** in the same habit fabric. Preferred method when Chapter biology **does not** trigger *Ruhe* — as with larval cults without Shadow in the **Warp**. In open field they keep the classic role: track, intercept, and capture traitors, deserters, and instigators. A Batavi of this Cohort does not summary-execute in the dark; he cuffs the target and drags him to *Colmeia Justitia* for Imperial Law to consume publicly.

**IV. Submission doctrine (Alaric’s view)** Alaric von Helis presents a governing paradox: he is far more permissive than most Imperial political lords toward population biological weakness and need. He tolerates and repairs fatigue. In exchange he has zero tolerance for deliberate default or any hint of secession. The dogma he imposes through Arbites and his own legionaries is inflexible: *“All is the Imperium, and without it man is nothing.”* Challenging that truth draws total, silent annihilation by Noviomagus’s walls.
