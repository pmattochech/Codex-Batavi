# \[WIKI\] CHARACTER DOSSIER: SOREN RIIS

*(Liaison-Praefect — Deathwatch correspondence / *Medicinae*)*

**Full name:** Soren Riis.  
**Rank:** Apothecary (*Medicinae*).  
**Title:** **Liaison-Praefect (Deathwatch correspondence)** — bearer of ***Pactum Clavis Sanguinis*** vox-primary for the Watch.  
**Chapter:** *Cohors Batavorum*.  
**Administrative file:** Vexillatio I (*Prima*) — **billet fiction** for tithe scrolls; duty is **Chapter-wide** (see [deathwatch-and-batavorum.md](../../political-relations/deathwatch-and-batavorum.md) §5).  
**Reports to:** Chief Apothecary **Drusus** (absolute).  
**Apparent service age:** ~140–170 Terran years (estimate).

---

## 1\. Role and mandate

Riis is the **living seal** between Watch *Apothecarion* curiosity and *Medicinae* secrecy. He does **not** command kill-teams; he **gates** bodies:

* files **certification tiles** on the **aptitude chain** after a completed *Watch cycle* — mandatory paper for **Legatus** and *Decima* tracks ([deathwatch-and-batavorum.md](../../political-relations/deathwatch-and-batavorum.md) §4);  
* clears rotations for **endocrine** and **Wolf’s Curse** stability windows;  
* issues **Tier White / Grey** disclosure packets and **withholds** Black-tier genotype data;  
* travels to **debrief anchors** (Noviomagus, neutral stations, escorted Watch facilities) for return audits and **blood-key** splits;  
* negotiates **compact appendices** with each theater’s **Watch Apothecary designate**.

His presence is **bureaucratic violence** dressed as courtesy: polite diction, **no** yielded slide on sealed archives.

---

## 2\. Appearance and protocol

* **Helm:** Standard *Medicinae* multi-lens rig; **Protocol of Visage** applies — helm **does not** come off in Watch spaces except under **Triumvirate-witnessed** medical necessity.  
* **Markings:** No heraldic vanity; **grey** chassis, **crimson** pauldrons per line; optional **discrete ivory pin** on gorget (Silence-trained **observer** certification — useful when Watch missions touch cult pattern-work; he is **not** Silence Cohort headcount).  
* **Tools:** **Narthecium** configured for **xenos toxin** isolation and **Furor** sedative loads; carries **cryptographic clavis** bands for Watch scanner handshake.

---

## 3\. Personality and voice

* **Temperament:** Dry, impatient with Watch genetors who confuse **mission triage** with **inheritance mining**.  
* **Speech:** Clinical; cites **compact clause** numbers the way Varro cites rites.  
* **Loyalty vector:** Drusus first, **Chapter blood** second, **Imperial utility** third — never inverted.  
* **Field quote (attributed):** *“You may have his wounds. You do not have his ledger.”*

---

## 4\. Narrative hooks

* **Friction:** A Watch Apothecary who pushes for **Black-tier** data mid-battle becomes Riis’s **enemy** without firing a shot — vox silence, delayed clearance, **Drusus** invoked.  
* **Liability:** If Riis is **killed** en route to debrief, *Medicinae* activates **contingency Liaison-Praefect** (name rotates); Watch loses **48-hour** automatic trust window until replacement seals new keys.  
* **Humanity:** Riis has **no** Martha-kitchen softness arc requirement; optional beat — he respects **mortal** medicae who keep mouths shut under audit.
