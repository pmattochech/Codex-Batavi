# \[WIKI\] DOSSIER: THE FORGE BROOD (MARTHA’S CHILDREN)

**Collective archetype:** Survivors of industrial death world **Legacy:** Bearers of the **Pack Warmth** talisman

Unlike children on other Imperial worlds, Martha’s brood was raised under “war geometry” doctrine. The late Thunderwolf Viggo was their mentor, nanny, and first line of defense. Each received an amulet made from Viggo’s bones so “pack warmth” would never leave them.

## 1\. Jonas (The tactician / Firstborn)

**Age:** 34 years (4 years older than Elara Solis).  
**Status:** Married to **Sarah**. They are parents of a 5-year-old boy, marking the start of the fortress’s new civil generation.  
**Current role:** Senior auditor at one of Noviomagus Prime’s factories.  
**Trait:** Alaric von Helis personally tested his capacity and knowledge before appointing him, applying the principle of putting the best resource in the right place. Jonas applies “friction logic” to ledgers to keep productive efficiency.

## 2\. Mara (The provider / Second mother)

**Estimated age:** 30 years.  
**Current role:** Food logistics and central community kitchen management.  
**Trait:** Martha’s right hand; masters the “synthetic meat rite” and serves as emotional anchor for siblings and Service Sector population in crisis.

## 3\. Sofia and Silas (The healers)

**Estimated ages:** 27 years.  
**Current role:** Trauma nurses and triage.  
**Trait:** In training, operating under strict **Chief Apothecary Drusus** supervision. Drusus supports and validates their trades out of deep respect for nurses, whom he considers on the “front-line geometric preservation of biological assets.”

## 4\. Kael (The scavenger / The shadow)

**Estimated age:** 25 years.  
**Current role:** Urban infiltration and logistics supply specialist.  
**Trait:** Grew up moving through ventilation ducts undetected. Today uses those skills to inspect structural faults in restricted areas and supervise material flow in sub-forges.

## 5\. Vanya (The fierce / The blade)

**Estimated age:** 22 years.  
**Current role:** Close defense and civil militia martial training.  
**Trait:** Most aggressive of the brood; internalized doctrine that “pain is error’s correction” (lesson absorbed from Varro). Wields combat blades with lethal precision and acts as civil-tier security supervisor.

## 6\. Thaddeus (The engineer / The mechanic)

**Estimated age:** 19 years.  
**Current role:** Systems maintenance and heavy technical support.  
**Trait:** Inherited aptitude from late father Tobias. Works in sync with Kadmos’s tech-acolytes, treating fortress piping as arteries of an organism that must be kept alive at any cost.

## 7\. Livia (The spark)

**Estimated age:** 16 years.  
**Current role:** Civic coordination assistant.  
**Trait:** Former youngest (before Leo’s birth); protected by Viggo’s tail during acid storms and one of the few who still sleeps without fear in Alaric’s armor’s shadow. Symbol of humanity and hope for exhausted workers.

## 8\. Leo (The little lion / True youngest)

**Current age:** 10 years.  
**Status:** Was newborn at father Tobias’s funeral (570.M42).  
**Trait:** Fearless since childhood; grew up climbing Astartes armor legs. Today left toy soldiers behind and serves apprentice on Noviomagus light assembly lines, with silent respect and protection from the *Cohors Batavorum*. The Wall ensures he is the only child in the whole sector who has never known the terror of the void.
