# [WIKI] CHARACTER DOSSIER: VALDRIC

## 1. IDENTITY (SPIRIT AND FLESH)

**Nomenclature and tactical designations:** The biological asset carries multiple identification layers, reflecting utility and perceived lethality at Imperial space’s edge:

* **Valdric:** Primary name, forged in his homeworld’s ice.  
* **Ice Reaper:** Hunting epithet earned in youth, subduing Aethelgard blizzard superpredators by brute force.  
* **Antipode / Cynic scholar:** Psychological profile tags in tactical archives, denoting organic, paradoxical nature before fortress silence.

**Visual reference:** `assets/valdric-reference.png` — current basal appearance (post-feral period). Adapt: light blond hair instead of dark; remaining features consistent with description below.

**Primary nature and physical topography:** Valdric is an abhuman (*Homo Sapiens Lupus*), bearer of a recessive lethal gene-load native to Aethelgard’s frozen jungles. Strictly mortal, chained to thermodynamics and time’s friction.

Physically he is a 1.95 m organic wall, hyperdense bone and muscle separating him from ordinary humans — and from anything passing for human without effort. Today’s look is not transformation: what he became permanently after childhood’s feral period, when *Homo Sapiens Lupus* biology manifested without civilization’s brake and fixed final form.

Face of predator who learned speech. Jaw wide and firm, subtle projection that does not distort features but anchors them in something not quite human. Sparse beard — blond and dull like his hair — spreads on jaw, less adornment than natural extension of nonstandard anatomy. Disordered blond hair falls over ears; tips lift slightly only when wind pulls or fingers brush — with **claws**. Nails long, black, curved, tough enough to bite metal, not built for show. Not display; simply what Valdric’s hands are.

Canines visibly longer than human norm — not enough to prevent mouth closure, enough to show when he smiles, shouts, or anger nears threshold. Diction unimpaired. Fiction of ordinary person is what they impair.

Eyes green — not leaf green but Aethelgard nocturnal predator amber-green, odd light catch in dark. Analytical, sharp, deeply expressive; most disconcerting thing in his face: not monstrous, but too intelligent for the set they belong to. Valdric’s beauty is subjective in the literal sense — those who find him attractive have a case; those who find him threatening too. Same features. Difference is what the observer chooses first.

**Mind and spirit (mask, mourning, forge):** Valdric’s psyche rejects *Axiom Rationis* mathematical stasis ruling Batavi legionaries. He runs on chaotic frequency structured in three layers:

* **Cynic mask and mourning rift:** Outwardly loud, lightly impulsive, cheerfully cynical personality. Continuous joke, scorn, verbal insubordination as active destabilization tools. Says what he wants, aware of Imperial reprisal, exempt from death terror. Psych audit shows chaotic behavior is barricade for sanity. Beneath swaggering surface and staggeringly erudite intellect lies tectonic melancholy. Valdric is haunted by lost tribal family. When combat stops and he is alone, smile dies; gaze drops, breath becomes rasping sigh carrying silent mourning of survivor of loved ones. He laughs, purely, so memory’s weight does not crush him.  
* **Friction theology (steel soul):** Raised outside Imperial Cult, Valdric reveres Emperor in shamanic optic as “Maximum Ancestor” and storm lord. Faith extends to matter. Where *Adeptus Mechanicus* placates machine spirits with unguents and binary submission, Valdric’s tribe taught practical animism: inanimate objects have soul, but soul only awakens through care. For him maintaining sword, carapace plate, even iron pot is not technical routine; communion. Cleaning, sharpening, tending personal gear infuses life — tools treated as allies repay affection by saving him in war’s friction.  
* **Symmetry of the unbent (parallel with Castellan):** Despite method opposition, Valdric and Lord Castellan Alaric von Helis are reflections of same unbreakable bond. Both **unbent**. Under universe pressure neither yields, sharing atavistic violent loyalty to weak and needy. Divergence is execution: Alaric is structural anvil of law, embracing Imperial tradition to shield humanity; Valdric is chaotic storm. Refuses spine-bend before vain authorities or laws he judges tyrannical, preferring to shatter system gears if it saves an innocent.

## 2. BIOLOGY (MEAT MACHINE)

**2.1. Forge of mortal flesh (entropic sync)**

Valdric is not elevated transhuman, carries no gene-seed granting clinical immortality. Biology is by-product of recessive lethal gene forged in Aethelgard Prime friction. As abhuman (*Homo Sapiens Lupus*), physiology rigidly chained to time flow. Ages naturally, feels gravity and years’ wear, but internal engineering keeps organism at lethal maximum allowed for respective age.

**2.2. Load architecture and thermodynamic collapse**

Tactical foundation: basal hyperdense bone and muscle. True anomaly: extreme reactive regeneration. Under severe structural trauma — deep lacerations or perforations — cells do not merely scar; force violent mitosis rebuilding flesh and bone in real time. Survival exacts grotesque thermodynamic bill. Healing instantly burns caloric reserves, plunging abhuman into deep starvation. To avoid organ failure post-heal, forced to consume colossal biomass immediately.

**2.3. Warg and threshold (breathing barrier)**

Valdric has two states. One described above — what he always is, awake or asleep, war or Martha’s Kitchen. No disguise for what he is. Appearance permanent since living as animal in Aethelgard jungles in childhood, before proper speech or memory he had been human. When civilization returned, returned with this face, these claws, these eyes. No return to before.

Second state is **Warg**.

Warg is not elevated emotion nor intensified version of baseline. Different category of existence: body Valdric inhabits ceases to be his. Transformation hormonal and emotional — rage enough to saturate system, adrenaline without regulatory outlet. Result complete metamorphosis into quadruped-biped beast covered in fur, authentic wolf muzzle, claws aimed at annihilation without cognition. Warg does not reason. Warg does not recognize allies. Warg removes threat and keeps removing everything in range.

Valdric is not blocked from transform by trauma. He **chooses** not to transform; choice costs continuous work.

Tool is **Breathing doctrine** — inherited from father, refined decades by necessity: tactical oxygen and heart-rate control stopping adrenaline crossing transformation threshold. High rage, loss, injustice triggering moral switch — Valdric breathes. Counts. Regulates. Visible to those who know: moment concentrated stillness before action, deeper breath than should be, fingers flexing. When he succeeds, rage converts to intellect-directed kinetic force — efficient, lethal, controlled. When he fails, Warg emerges. No intermediate state.

Weight of knowing what happens when he fails is what maintains discipline. Not psychological barrier — choice Valdric renews every high-intensity situation, alone with nervous system, no written doctrine to consult.

## 3. FRONTIER ARSENAL (MATTER’S BILL)

Valdric’s kit is antithesis of *Adeptus Mechanicus* sterile tech. Built to inflict catastrophic enemy trauma but imposes severe physical and thermal limits on bearer, stopping abhuman from becoming unstoppable military force without paying matching caloric cost.

**Ballistic chassis — “False Mercy” armor**

Valdric rejects servo-armor weight and maintenance, runs modified carapace chassis he named *False Mercy*.

* **Thermodynamics and hypertrophy:** Armor, no Imperial eagle or polish, kept matte gunmetal grey, covers torso, shoulders, legs, deliberately leaves arms bare. Aesthetic “flaw” is biological requirement: dissipates hypermetabolism extreme heat, ensures Warg expansion not mutilated by own metal.  
* **Integrated gauntlets:** Hands and forearms not separate gloves but heavy plasteel manoplates inseparable from armor bracers. On proximal phalanges metal forged into brutal short spikes. When Valdric closes fist, bone density converts armor spikes into rupture tools able to crush ferrocrete and pierce body mesh with surgical lethality. Natural claws exposed above joint — armor cut to accommodate; covering with metal would waste what biology already built.

**Ancestral blade: *Kujhade***

* **Technical designation:** Standard-Aethelgard bastard sword.  
* **Native name:** *Kujhade* — “The Destroyer.”  
* **Metal alloy:** Forged from wreckage of ancient *Space Hulk* that fell from Tempestas-Major magnetic storms onto Aethelgard in past cycles. Valdric fused irradiated superdense plasteel from stellar hulk with obsidian matrices and impact diamonds from his world’s Stone Spines. Result colossal grey-blue near-black blade, vitreous edge texture, terrifying molecular resilience. Rejects energy fields — deliberately, by alloy choice, not flaw.  
* **Penetration biomechanics:** Inert blade does not shear Astartes ceramite without massive force. Efficiency demands thrust as primary motion. Transhuman kinetic impact directed to geological tip shears enemy armor molecular structure under absolute physical friction.

**Primary mid-range weapon — *Wall-Breaker***

* **Technical designation:** Standard-Incus-Gravis heavy autocarbine.  
* **Trauma mechanics:** Rust massive automatic rifle. Fires super-sized tungsten-core cartridges for structural tear and blunt trauma. Kick absorbed by bare dense arm muscle.  
* **Thermal limit:** Restricted to semi-auto or locked three-round bursts. Without liquid cooling, exceeding cadence overheats barrel. Glowing metal sears bare arm skin, triggering reactive mitosis and draining caloric stock. Ignoring weapon limits ignores metabolic limits.

**Secondary armament — “Imperial Diplomacy”**

* **Technical designation:** Standard-Retrobii plasma pistol.  
* **Naming cynicism:** Named in direct affront to Imperial bureaucracy — which Valdric considers volatile, destructive, prone to exploding in user’s face. Magnetically anchored thigh. If magnetic containment fails and plasma leaks, thermal trauma triggers emergency regeneration and same starvation bill.

## 4. EVENTS (FRICTION CHRONOLOGY)

***Kujhade* legacy and Breathing doctrine**

Valdric’s youth forged under hunter legend ghost. Father transmitted ancestors’ story — only ones surviving lethal **early Aethelgard** ambush challenges, xenos extinct to *Cohors Batavorum* five centuries before, stories still war myth among tribes. Valdric inherited *Kujhade* and aberrant *Homo Sapiens Lupus* biology endemic to tribe. To stop pride and rage consuming him early, father taught Breathing doctrine: tactical oxygen control stopping adrenaline crossing transformation threshold. Survival tool, not philosophy.

**Ice Reaper (basal fury)**

At 16, lethality proved without transform. During hunt, group ambushed by *Vorvrak*, Aethelgard blizzard alpha superpredator. Applying absolute heartbeat and breath control, Valdric subjugated and pierced beast’s skull operating strictly in basal lupine appearance. Feat earned *Ice Reaper* epithet and betrothal to village’s most coveted woman, inflating pride to dangerous levels.

**Pride, fall, first Warg**

Structural failure came from ego. Weeks after victory, young rival used dishonorable tactics in duel. Pride wounded before tribe shattered Breathing doctrine. Adrenaline saturated system, crossed threshold — Valdric became Warg first time before everyone. Facing abomination, elders and own father banished him to frozen wilds.

Wilderness period long. Weeks became months. Without civilization brake, without need to regulate biology among people, lupine form persisted. Body learned to run that way. When Valdric returned to language, tools, abstract thought, face from before stayed in woods. What returned is what he is today — and has been since.

Absolute calamity on return. Seeking reconciliation, found tribe dissected alive by Flayer-caste Necron incursion awakened in depths. Seeing xeno constructs tear fiancée before his eyes, regulation collapsed. Warg emerged again — and with no witness to tell enemy from ally, abomination slaughtered Necrons and survivors alike. When fury ceased and body cooled, Valdric stood among dead including mother, siblings, father, who forgave with last breath.

Warg not blocked by that trauma. Valdric knows exactly what it does, with whom, without why. That clarity — not horror but precise cost knowledge — feeds Breathing doctrine. He chooses not to transform. Renews choice every time rage rises past threshold.

**Feral years and cowardice geometry (566–168.M42)**

Valdric wandered forests as hermit two years. At 18, tracked by slaver net using stolen industrial grey armor and false *Cohors Batavorum* heraldry. Unable to subdue him in direct fight, they cornered local nomads and industrial colonists as hostages. To save mortals, Valdric refused fight — absorbing shock traps and neurotoxic sedatives until collapse. Awake in starship hold, same innocents for whom he sacrificed freedom shackled beside him. Broke chains by pure muscle contraction. Silently eliminated guards, took ship.

**Dock math and vanity gold (568–573.M42)**

Docking Noviomagus Prime, tried reporting scheme to *Adeptus Arbites* judges. Blind bureaucracy answered with power maul blows. After retaliating and breaking two magistrates’ necks, sentenced perpetual servitude at planetary docks. There became physical shield for Nork, docile Ogryn under lethal work. Breaking point: Orestian noble whipped Nork during inspection. Valdric threw surgical punch sending aristocrat into ferrocrete — shattering facial topography, fracturing arm. Noble, later rising as heretic antagonist, refused cloning, replaced lost limbs with ostentatious solid-gold cyber armed hidden blades. That night newly promoted Admiral Elara Solis interrogated Valdric in cells. Philosophical clash between structural law and erudite anarchism became fast improvised blade duel; Valdric marked admiral’s face. Bureaucratic retaliation transferred him to Deep Forges.

**Forge massacre and Captain Vorek (post-573.M42)**

Sub-forges invaded by Chaos blood cult. Without tactical arms, facing mortal workers’ execution, moral trigger pushed Valdric to regulation edge. Wielding heavy machinery, began lethal pruning of heretics keeping cognition — barely. Conflict shifted when *Cohors Batavorum* squads dropped from orbit. Recognizing industrial grey and crimson pauldrons — slavers’ colors — trauma overrode analysis. He launched hyperdense mass against **Captain Vorek**. Collision devastating: deep perforations, reactive mitosis cannibalizing caloric reserves, blunt impact cracking Vorek’s helm ceramite lens. Biological tank empty, thermodynamic failure immediate. Captain delivered single abdomen punch, shutting central nervous system. Abhuman collapsed in lethargic coma. Apothecary Drusus isolated him for audit forging new utility.
