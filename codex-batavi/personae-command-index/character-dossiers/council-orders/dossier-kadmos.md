# \[WIKI\] CHARACTER DOSSIER: KADMOS

*(The “Doctrinator” / Heir of Noviomagus / Iron Link)*

**Full name:** Kadmos of Noviomagus.  
**Rank:** Master of the Forge (primary Techmarine).  
**Specialization:** Biomechanical engineering, siege architecture, heavy chassis maintenance.  
**Chapter:** *Cohors Batavorum*.  
**Origin:** Noviomagus Prime (civilized forge world).  
**Estimated age:** ~350–400 Terran years (secular veteran).

**Visual reference (working — AI facsimile, not a geometry lock):** [`kadmos-master-of-the-forge.png`](../../../lore-images/kadmos-master-of-the-forge.png) — Mark III bulk, bronze arm, mecadendrites; dossier §1 for cybernetics detail.

---

## 1\. \[WIKI\] APPEARANCE AND PHYSIOLOGY

*Iron under skin.*

* **Physique:**  
  * Kadmos embodies industrial robustness of his origin. Massive even for Astartes standards, bone structure densified by Noviomagus mineral treatments.  
  * Skin pale greyish, common to those raised under forge soot, marked by chemical burn scars and neural interface grooves.  
* **Cybernetics:**  
  * **Bronze arm:** Left arm shoulder to fingertips replaced by master-grade prosthesis after Noviomagus-I containment campaign. Piece exposed, showing hydraulic pistons and polished bronze gears.  
  * **Ocular interface:** Right eye sapphire infrared lens, linked direct to heavy weapon *spirit-link*, enabling real-time trajectory calculation.  
  * **Gear mantle (mecadendrites):** As Master of the Forge, Kadmos’s form celebrates brutal biomechanics. Industrial mecadendrites snake from back like metallic scorpion tails, operating arc welders, thermal scanners, hydraulic drivers in perfect thought sync.  
* **Power armor:**  
  * Uses **Mark III (Iron Armour)** variant, reinforced with adamantium plates recycled from Logus Ring. Armor shows chapter dark metallic grey with oxidized bronze detail.

## 2\. \[WIKI\] PERSONALITY AND PSYCHOLOGY

*Mind as gearwork.*

* **Temperament:**  
  * Kadmos stoic, methodical, calculating. Does not see war as glory quest but engineering problem needing violent efficient solution.  
  * Infinite patience, able to wait hours in absolute silence until enemy enters weapon “efficiency zone.”  
* **Combat philosophy:**  
  * Deep faith in **Weight doctrine**. Victory guaranteed by fire superiority and unbreakable logistics. Often cites dogma: *“Flesh fails, iron endures, but supply wins.”*  
* **Distinctive trait:**  
  * Unlike other **Batavi**, Kadmos keeps near-religious connection to equipment maintenance state. Speaks to support weapon’s machine spirit as living companion.

---

## 3\. \[WIKI\] SIGNATURE EQUIPMENT

*Kadmos’s teeth.*

* **“Hammer of Noviomagus” (modified heavy bolter):**  
  * Old-pattern heavy bolter recovered from lost forge ruins. Kadmos modified with Noviomagus-V liquid nitrogen cooling.  
  * Fed by high-capacity ammo pack; projectiles forged from Steel Belt recycled steel.  
* **Strip knife:**  
  * Disproportionately large combat knife forged from Ignis-Minor industrial saw. Used for melee and cutting through enemy vehicle armor.

## 4\. \[WIKI\] ORIGIN AND RECRUITMENT

*From soot to ceramite.*

* **Childhood in forges:** Kadmos born Noviomagus Prime lower levels, apprentice cauldron worker in macro-cannon forges. Natural heat and fatigue resistance drew recruiting Chaplains visiting forge world.  
* **Weight test:** During rite of passage, Kadmos not only carried standard ore load but repaired stuck hydraulic elevator mechanism under simulated suppression fire, demonstrating technical calm Chapter values.  
* **Ascension:** Accepted neophyte, sent **Incus-Gravis Tertius**, bone structure pushed to limit under 2.8G gravity, solidifying musculature for heavy weapons.

---

## 5\. \[WIKI\] COMBAT FEATS AND CAMPAIGNS

*Scars on armor.*

* **Bunker-Secundus defense:** During Drukhari pirate raid trying to loot gene-vault **Noviomagus-V**, Kadmos held lone service corridor six hours. Used cold vacuum environment, deliberately overheated heavy bolter to draw xeno heat sensors, then detonated liquid nitrogen lines on them.  
* **Vitreus purge:** On lens world, Kadmos led **Batavi** squad through Reflection Caverns eliminating heretic infiltrator sect. Used crystal refractive properties for calculated ricochet shots, killing targets outside direct line of sight.  
* **Noviomagus-I rescue:** Campaign where Kadmos lost organic arm saving sergeant from saboteur-triggered mine collapse. Held ten-ton support beam with left arm while operating bolter right-handed until rescue arrived.

## 6\. \[WIKI\] RELATIONSHIPS AND CHAPTER POSITION

*Gears in command structure.*

* **Alaric’s counselor:** Noviomagus origin makes Kadmos vital link between Lord Castellan Alaric and Adeptus Mechanicus clergy. Alaric often consults Kadmos on long-campaign logistical viability.  
* **Master of the Forge (primary Techmarine):** Kadmos absolute chief of *Cohors Batavorum* machinery and technology. Not only full Techmarine but commander conclave of all other tech-acolytes, Techmarines, and artificers. Walking Noviomagus factories, followed by own mechanical specialist cohort. His word dictates how steel bends to bear giants’ strength.  
* **Neophyte mentor:** Kadmos known as rigorous instructor on **Steel Belt**, teaching new recruits vacuum suit maintenance matters as much as bolt accuracy.  
* **Survival architect:** Only Batavi with authority and mechanical intellect for continuous ***Providence* chassis** maintenance (Alaric’s *Castra-Lupus* armor). Also directly responsible for “Iron Mitosis” on civilians and abhumans, designing load-bearing exo-spines and bioprosthetics that turned Malakor reject scum into backbone of Noviomagus industry and defense.

## 7\. \[WIKI\] QUOTES AND DOGMAS

*Forge voice.*

* **On strategy:** *“A heroic charge wins a battle; an adamantium supply line wins a crusade. Choose the metal.”*  
* **On maintenance:** *“If Machine Spirit is not honored with oil and prayer, it will betray you the moment void knocks.”*  
* **Personal motto:** *“I am the hammer that strikes, but Noviomagus is the metal that endures.”*

## 8\. \[WIKI\] DESTINY IN FERRUM-LOGOS

*Sentinel among dead.*

* **9th Company bond:** Kadmos currently senior technical consultant to 9th Company on **Logus Ring**. Skill spotting rare archaeotech in scrap vital to preserving Chapter firepower.  
* **Tomb guardian:** Patience and precision make him among few **Batavi** authorized maintenance vigil on stasis sarcophagi of Dreadnoughts at **Bifrons-Ferrum**. Knows each Ancient’s awakening rites by name.  
* **Ad-Mortem mission:** Kadmos coordinates ballistic tests on dead world, ensuring Noviomagus-produced ammunition keeps penetration standard against rising sector threats.

## 9\. \[WIKI\] BRONZE ARM SECRETS

*More than prosthesis.*

* **Integrated tool:** Kadmos’s bronze arm not only weapon; contains integrated maintenance **Narthecium** with micro-tools, structural integrity sensors, short-range palm-hidden plasma cutter.  
* **Direct interface:** Prosthesis has neural direct link to “Hammer of Noviomagus” (heavy bolter). Lets him adjust fire rate and cooling flow by thought alone without leaving trigger.  
* **Noviomagus Prime seal:** Prosthesis shoulder engraved with homeworld Mechanicus seal. Said arm holds small relic: fragment of first anvil used at Chapter’s foundation.
