*(The Descent Ledger / Aethelgard Storm-Master)*

**Seat:** **Aethelgard Prime** — storm assault, tide moons, vertical infrastructure, jump certification hub.  
**Chapter:** *Cohors Batavorum*.

## 2\. Personality and psychology

* **Temperament:** Competitive without braggart tone — treats **closure speed** as the only scoreboard.  
* **Voice:** clipped, timing-coded; uses **countdown** cadence in vox even outside drops.  
* **Flaw:** Impatience with **static** siege holds — *Strategium* pairs him with SEXTA liason officers when QVINTA must **wait** for breach lanes.  
* **Field quote (attributed):** *“If you can see the landing zone, you’re already late.”*

## 3\. Combat and doctrine

* ***Alabarda* head — Type Descensus (bardiche, forward chain beard):** **chainaxe** with **long bardiche beard pointing forward** — not a Primus three-face polearm; **no impaler**. **Long shaft** for standoff cleave. **Belt chainsword** for the close band; **power gladius** for the quick death. Dual-wield (bardiche + chainsword) when relay is planted or borne by escort — [alabarda-head-groups.md](../../../arsenal-and-logistics/alabarda-head-groups.md) §7.  
* **Theater fit:** [vexillatio-operational-armory.md](../../../arsenal-and-logistics/vexillatio-operational-armory.md) — QVINTA **storm assault**: jump packs, **chainsword-primary** line melee, power axes, boltstorm descent.  
* **Melee doctrine:** QVINTA is the only Vexilla where **pure line** treats **chainsword** as **default** saw-mass — Aethelgard brutality and **Wild Hunt** closure when massed enemies spike assault tempo. **Shadows** under **Silence** keep **knife → gladius** ([cohort-standard-kit.md](../../../arsenal-and-logistics/cohort-standard-kit.md) §§2, 6).  
* **Command style:** Suppression trains **rapid closure**; Ruin carries **melta** for post-landing hull work; Siege is the **least** populated division — QVINTA prefers **motion** to trench math. Sturmwahl **descends into** the fight first: **Type Descensus** eats assault meat at shaft range, **belt chainsword** takes the closers, **power gladius** ends what must die fast — then **plants** the haft (butt spike) in the **hole**, relay live, and **points** the **failed pressure joint**; QVINTA assault **spreads** from that **puncture** through the army like **contagion** (closure speed as disease vector).  
* **Foundational echo:** Aethelgard was the **Foundation War** crucible; Sturmwahl’s briefings still reference **jungle verticals** as pedagogical myth.
