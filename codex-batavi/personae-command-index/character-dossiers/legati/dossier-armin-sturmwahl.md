# \[WIKI\] CHARACTER DOSSIER: ARMIN STURMWAHL

*(The Descent Ledger / Aethelgard Storm-Master)*

**Full name:** Armin Sturmwahl.  
**Rank:** **Legatus**, 5th Vexillatio (*Quinta*).  
**Seat:** **Aethelgard Prime** — storm assault, tide moons, vertical infrastructure, jump certification hub.  
**Chapter:** *Cohors Batavorum*.  
**Bearer of:** *Alabarda Vexillum* (*Quinta* lattice). **Vexillum register:** four-field panel (§3) — [vexilla-by-vexillatio-design.md](../../intro-and-heraldry/vexilla-by-vexillatio-design.md).  
**Apparent service age:** ~210–260 Terran years (estimate).

---

## 1\. Appearance and physiology

*Gravity is a suggestion he invoices.*

* **Physique:** Dense core strength; **jump-pack** hours show in calf hydraulics wear patterns (maintained, not romanticized).  
* **Armor:** Highest **assault Primaris** and Inceptor-class adjacency in personal escort; crimson trim often **scuffed** from hull kisses and gantry scrapes.  
* **Tell:** Sub-audible **grav-comp** hum check before every drop — ritual, not superstition; if the hum is wrong, the slate delays.

---

## 2\. Personality and psychology

* **Temperament:** Competitive without braggart tone — treats **closure speed** as the only scoreboard.  
* **Voice:** clipped, timing-coded; uses **countdown** cadence in vox even outside drops.  
* **Flaw:** Impatience with **static** siege holds — *Strategium* pairs him with *Sexta* liason officers when *Quinta* must **wait** for breach lanes.  
* **Field quote (attributed):** *“If you can see the landing zone, you’re already late.”*

---

## 3\. Combat and doctrine

* ***Alabarda* head — Type Descensus (three-point, chain beard):** power impaler and power hammer retained; **axe cheek** is **chainaxe** with long bardiche beard; **belt chainsword**; certified **dual-wield** with pole when relay is planted or borne by escort — [alabarda-head-groups.md](../../../arsenal-and-logistics/alabarda-head-groups.md) §7.  
* **Theater fit:** [vexillatio-operational-armory.md](../../../arsenal-and-logistics/vexillatio-operational-armory.md) — *Quinta* **storm assault**: jump packs, **chainsword-primary** line melee, power axes, boltstorm descent.  
* **Melee doctrine:** *Quinta* is the only Vexillatio where **pure line** treats **chainsword** as **default** saw-mass — Aethelgard brutality and **Wild Hunt** closure when massed enemies spike assault tempo. **Shadows** under **Silence** keep **knife → gladius** ([cohort-standard-kit.md](../../../arsenal-and-logistics/cohort-standard-kit.md) §§2, 6).  
* **Command style:** Suppression trains **rapid closure**; Ruin carries **melta** for post-landing hull work; Siege is the **least** populated division — *Quinta* prefers **motion** to trench math.  
* **Foundational echo:** Aethelgard was the **Foundation War** crucible; Sturmwahl’s briefings still reference **jungle verticals** as pedagogical myth.

* **074.M42 | Dreadwolf Red Tide (Moraine-Nine):** Recovery mission for **V** *Quinta* dead after World Eaters prison hulk; found **Lieutenant Einar** alive at massacre epicenter — **withheld cull** after salute and ***Quinta* cadence**. * **121.M42 | Gutgrind Descent:** Led **V** *Quinta* jump clearance of Ork scrap-spire on Selene-Black; Warboss **Gutgrind** killed; certified *Alabarda* **field 4** (Foundation War **Aethelgard opening** **not** used for fourth panel). Narrative: [gutgrind-descent.md](../../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/gutgrind-descent.md).

## 5\. Relations

* **Alaric:** Rare direct vox from Sturmwahl — when it happens, it is **permission to spend jump fuel** at unsustainable rates; answers are usually *yes* or *no*, not lectures.  
* **Gervas Bifronte (6th):** Friendly rivalry — Sturmwahl mocks **plod**; Bifronte mocks **bounce**. Joint ops are efficient bloodbaths.  
* **Corbec Ardens:** Respects Corbec’s “boring numbers” enough to let logistics set assault boundaries in sealed anti-inversion planning; their method differs, objective does not.  
* **Peer Legati:** **Go-to** for vertical relief and **tide-moon** emergencies.  
* **Einar Dreadwolf:** Closest line-brother; **074.M42** Moraine-Nine — Sturmwahl raised the cull blade, withheld it after the ***Quinta* cadence** salute. Transferred survivor to elite cadre under perpetual **Varro** watch.
