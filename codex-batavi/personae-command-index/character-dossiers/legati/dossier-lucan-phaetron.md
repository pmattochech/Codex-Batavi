## 3\. Combat and doctrine

* ***Alabarda* head — Type Lamina (monomolecular precision):** mono-edged primary cheek, mono spike, small insulated hammer on **heat-shielded haft** — [alabarda-head-groups.md](../../../arsenal-and-logistics/alabarda-head-groups.md) §9. **One clean plane** on seal iris, conduit flange, and lens-forge fault lines — **no chain vibration** (mono lattice contamination risk). **Insulated hammer** for non-cutting seal tasks only.  
* **Pole doctrine:** Plant at **manifold node, vacuum-port spine, or lens-array choke** for **vent geometry accounting** — relay carries **Legatus tick** while Ruin runs **sequential underburn** along the spine. **Two-hand pole** default in active containment; **one-hand** only for brief **relay actuation** (vent-window sign-off, IFF slice) while the pole stays planted — not dual-wield like *Quinta*. Cloth may stay **furled** under photonic hash and glare.  
* **Dual seal:** **No breath without Legatus and Techmarine** sign-off **per node** — human relay tick and machine auspex tick; Kadmos’s rite in the chain even when Kadmos is not on the plate.  
* **Theater fit:** [vexillatio-operational-armory.md](../../../arsenal-and-logistics/vexillatio-operational-armory.md) — *Septima* **energy stress**: plasma incinerators, las fusils, neo-volkite where contract allows; bolters as **failure backbone** (short bursts, assigned arcs — no tracer vanity that blinds spotter glass).  
* **Command style:** Ruin overlaps **plasma AT**; Suppression holds perimeter volume; Phaetron personally signs **vent windows** on critical days; **ritual silence** on overcharge windows.  
* **Training philosophy:** *Septima* **punishes** careless plasma more harshly than other Vexillationes — one bad vent can blind a cohort. Rage vented wrong; the line pays in **restraint**, not hero overcharge.
