# \[WIKI\] CHARACTER DOSSIER: LUCAN PHAETRON

*(The Glass Burn / Vitreus Voltaic)*

**Full name:** Lucan Phaetron.  
**Rank:** **Legatus**, 7th Vexillatio (*Septima*).  
**Seat:** **Vitreus** — thermal and photonic stress; glass seas, lens forges, plasma vent discipline.  
**Chapter:** *Cohors Batavorum*.  
**Bearer of:** *Alabarda Vexillum* (*Septima* lattice). **Vexillum register:** four-field panel (§3) — [vexilla-by-vexillatio-design.md](../../intro-and-heraldry/vexilla-by-vexillatio-design.md).  
**Apparent service age:** ~190–240 Terran years (estimate).

---

## 1\. Appearance and physiology

*Light hurts twice if you’re lazy.*

* **Physique:** Lean, **heat-tolerance** conditioning visible in vascular efficiency under med-scan; minor **retinal filtering** augments (approved *Medicinae*).  
* **Armor:** **Extra vent routing** on backpack; plasma incinerator certification marks on both pauldrons.  
* **Quirk:** Keeps a **charred** lens fragment on a chain inside chestplate — memento from a forge collapse he survived; not superstition, **mnemonic** for vent protocols.

---

## 2\. Personality and psychology

* **Temperament:** Bright, sharp humor **off** the line; on the line, **ritual silence** during overcharge windows.  
* **Voice:** Slightly metallic edge from old smoke scarring; modulates down for **las-fusil** spotter teams.  
* **Flaw:** Contempt for **Mechanicus** liaisons who treat Batavi plasma doctrine as “primitive” — has been disciplined **once** for vox tone; still believes he was right.  
* **Field quote (attributed):** *“The glass forgives nothing. Be sharper than the lens.”*

---

## 3\. Combat and doctrine

* **Theater fit:** [vexillatio-operational-armory.md](../../../arsenal-and-logistics/vexillatio-operational-armory.md) — *Septima* **energy stress**: plasma incinerators, las fusils, neo-volkite where contract allows; bolters as **failure backbone**.  
* **Command style:** Ruin overlaps **plasma AT**; Suppression trains **disciplined overcharge** under *Armourium* rite — Phaetron personally signs **vent windows** on critical days.  
* **Training philosophy:** *Septima* **punishes** careless plasma more harshly than other Vexillationes — one bad vent can blind a cohort.

---

## 4\. History (in-universe — chronology access)

* **Watch proof:** **Certified *Watch cycle***; includes xenos engagements where **EM and thermal** signatures were the primary intelligence channel.  
* **Rise:** **Lieutenant** fame from holding a **lens dock** against Drukhari raiders with **controlled** plasma bloom — *Strategium* noted **zero** catastrophic vent chain.  
* **112.M42 | Null-Vent Covenant:** Led **VII** *Septima* containment of plasma/photonic cascade on Vitreus orbital plate Seven; manifold bled to vacuum ports; nineteen *Septima* battle-brothers killed; allied skitarii dead redacted under Mechanicus treaty; certified *Alabarda* **field 4**.
