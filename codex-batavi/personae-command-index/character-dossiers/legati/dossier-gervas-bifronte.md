# \[WIKI\] CHARACTER DOSSIER: GERVAS BIFRONTE

*(The Twin-Gate Warden / Greater Mason / Father of Walls)*

**Full name:** Gervas Bifronte.  
**Rank:** **Legatus**, SEXTA.  
**Chapter offices:** **Siege Master** of the *Cohors Batavorum*; ***Maior Caementarius*** (**Greater Mason**); ***Pater Murorum*** (**Father of Walls** — Guard and mortal mesh coinage).  
**Seat:** **Bifrons-Ferrum** — dual-world gulf fortifications, stasis-adjacent vault war, siege core of the Crucible macro-system.  
**Chapter:** *Cohors Batavorum*.  
**Bearer of:** *Alabarda* + Vexilla (SEXTA lattice). **Vexillum register:** four-field panel (§3) — [vexilla-by-vexillatio-design.md](../../intro-and-heraldry/vexilla-by-vexillatio-design.md).  
**Apparent service age:** ~240–290 Terran years (estimate).

## 1\. Appearance and physiology

*Two horizons, one ledger.*

* **Physique:** Broad, optimized for **Gravis** and **Indomitor** load — models heavy-plate thermal limits so brothers under him know the cage is survivable, not myth.  
* **Armor — *Typus Sextus — Bifronte* (Legatus configuration):** **Terminator-only** office plate — Noviomagus **artificer Tartaros-pattern Tactical Dreadnought Armour**; co-designed with **Kadmos** (*rite of the block*); seat reads **twin-gate siege**, **synchronized breach**, and **teleport reserve** — not line Mk X, assault descent, or Lex optics. ~**90% industrial grey** chassis. **Chest aquila** (Legatus law). **Purity seals** on gorget joins and homer housing.  
  * **Cowl — Tartaros (*Typus Sextus* helm family):** **Artificer** **Tartaros** cowl — mobile siege read. **No** transverse crest — ***Sextus* exception:** **ornate worked iron halo** on cowl (heavy filigree rank ring — distinct from Corbec’s forge halo and Sturmwahl’s pack spine). **Orange lenses**.  
  * **Dorsal:** **Visible teleport homer hardpoint** on cowl / back plate — breach-slate rite (**GREEN** commit · **AMBER** hold · **RED** extract); never opening move.  
  * **Left forearm:** **Relay cogitator** on vambrace — **no** integral storm shield.  
  * **Right vambrace + gauntlet:** **Crimson**; breach-weapon routing (theater modal — storm bolter / power fist / melta).  
  * **Pauldrons:** **Both crimson**; **left** — **white** frontal-wolf + **white fillet**; **right** — **Crux Terminatus** (**Corbec-pattern Mk X inset**, **worn**) over **sculpted relief** (not paint): **twin pillars / breach frame**, **SEXTA** faixa.  
  * **Greaves / sabatons:** Reinforced for **Type Malleus** threshold plant.  
  * **Epithet origin:** *Strategium* scribes coined **Bifronte** from orders that never split the world cleanly — **Gate A / Gate B** as **single equation**. ***Maior Caementarius*** and ***Pater Murorum*** post-date reformation (~087.M42): masonry supremacy over the **Nine Phalanx** capital mesh, confirmed after **Cage of Bifrons** survival.  
  * **Explicit negatives:** No Mk X office plate · no crest · no integral storm shield · no Watch marks · no PRIMA chains · no jump pack · no Corvus / Errant / Mk V–VII bowl families.
* **Visage protocol:** Cowl sealed outside Triumvirate-authorized exceptions.

## 2\. Personality and psychology

* **Temperament (*Vigil-first*):** Treats waiting as **active work** — survey, tether math, thermal ledger, homer slate. Enemies read stillness as hesitation; allies read it as coldness. Both are wrong until the mark. Methodical, almost **monastic** patience between detonations; switches to **zero-latency** command the second charges arm.  
* **Architect fuse (Dorn-adjacent / Perturabo-adjacent — temperament only):** **Discipline, duty, unyielding will** filed as **Friction Geometry obedience** — hold because the math holds, breach because the math demands it. Contempt for **stupid haste**, not for allies: he will **object with auspex** in joint council when a plan dies for pride ([`remonstratio-docens.md`](../../doctrine-and-organs/remonstratio-docens.md)). The **Perturabo strand** is **building as proof** — every wall is an argument; every flaw he finds **before** the enemy is a receipt that excellence was not wasted. He is **not** Imperial Fists, **not** Iron Warriors, **not** any Primarch’s son — only the Batavi answer to siege specialists.  
* **Voice:** Pre-breach — low, minimal, **construction litany** (*thermal check · tether seal · homer color*). Breach minute — **staccato counts and colors** (*Three. Two. One. Mark. Green.*). Council with Alaric — **one line per unreconciled weak point**; **Iron Protocol** over oratory. *The time for speeches is done.*  
* **Inner life:** The battlefield **inside** the brother — **Furor harness**, command vanity, trust in brother-officers — must be **conquered before** the first charge arms. Drusus would approve the coefficients; Varro would approve the silence.  
* **Flaw:** Underestimates **political** speed — trusts **walls and plans to outlast liars**, assumes treachery will eventually **present a breach face**. Worked in vault war; dangerous in ministry and spire politics (SECVNDA’s lane). *Reclusiam* sometimes assigns adjutants for **civilian narrative** control.  
* **Penance ethic:** After **Valerian Heresy** guilt (§4), he treats **siege tempo** as **non-forgiving** — pain sustains the next **plan observed**, not self-pity.  
* **Field quotes (attributed):** *“The vault does not hurry. Neither do we — until we do.”* · *“Vigilance is not waiting. It is building the strike while the enemy thinks you sleep.”*

## 3\. Combat, doctrine, and Chapter office

### 3.1 Legatus combat (SEXTA)

* ***Alabarda* head — Type Malleus (impact-first):** dominant power maul / forge hammer face; reduced axe edge; reinforced haft for breach plant — [alabarda-head-groups.md](../../../arsenal-and-logistics/alabarda-head-groups.md) §8.  
* **Theater fit:** [vexillatio-operational-armory.md](../../../arsenal-and-logistics/vexillatio-operational-armory.md) — SEXTA **siege and vault war**: largest **Indomitor** pool, plasma cannons on heavy chassis, cyclone support, storm shields on line brothers.  
* **Twin-phase commit (Bifronte grammar):** **Pre-breach** — TDA in assault boats, slow tide, thermal checks. **Breach minute** — **Gate A / Gate B** on **one mark** (*Three. Two. One. Mark.*). **Ingress** — **mission-variable** fast wave (Land Raiders, bikes, jump elements — mix per theater) clears the hole and plants homer. **Reserve** — homer **GREEN** → Bifronte + terminator wedge **teleport in**; homer **RED** (pre-authorized on slate) → **extraction** if geometry closes — not panic vox. Opposite of Sturmwahl: Gervas **opens** the puncture and **commits** mass after evidence, he does **not** race the first meter.  
* **Command style:** Siege division is **cultural heart**; Suppression trains **breach escort** and **interior lockstep** rather than open skirmish vanity.  
* **Stasis logistics:** Coordinates with *Armourium* on **Dreadnought-adjacent** supply — vocabulary overlaps with Kadmos’s forge priests without merging chains of command.

### 3.2 Siege Master — mesh architecture (Chapter-wide)

**Scope:** Gervas is the Chapter’s **Siege Master** and **architect of walls** for the **Nine Phalanx** mesh. He does **not** supersede Legati in battle command; he holds **supreme drafting authority** on **static defense geometry**, capital fortresses, gulf vaults, and **redundancy mesh** against siege specialists ([`friction-geometry.md`](../../doctrine-and-organs/friction-geometry.md) §1).

## 4\. History (in-universe — chronology access)

Three acts — staff map to *Vigilance* register (Foundation build · brother-war wound · counter-siege endurance). Full mesh filing: [`master-chronology.md`](../../events-and-chronologies/master-chronology.md).

### Act I — Apprentice mason (~015–045.M42)

* **Foundation / early mesh:** Apprentice siege officer under early **Friction Geometry** — learns that **infrastructure is weapon**: refineries, silos, and hive ribs become **purified cages** and kill channels. Helps draft first **Legatus-seat** fortifications across the crucible mesh as strength returns from ignorance toward **Der Batav** identity.

### Act II — Valerian Heresy wound (~065–086.M42)

* **Rank:** **Captain** / senior siege cadre on **Bifrons-Ferrum** gulf coupling.  
* **Wound:** During **Valerian Heresy** rot, Gervas **trusted** a **Valerius-aligned gate crew** on a **tether hab coupling** he had certified — **Gate A held**, **Gate B opened** under **loyal-seeming** orders until Alaric’s geometry **saved the mesh** but **late receipt** cost brothers. He **never** splits **Gate A / Gate B** again without **independent witness** (Silence cohort seal or Arbites observer).  
* **Emotional lock:** *Penance lasts until duty is served* — brother-trust converted to **procedure**, not nihilism. Valerius remains **ghost in blueprints**, not spoken name in open council.

### Act III — Cage of Bifrons and Korvus (~092–098.M42)

* **~092.M42 | Cage of Bifrons opens (*Ferrum Cage*, sealed register):** **Iron Warriors**–aligned siege elements (**IV Legion throughput appetite** per [`castra-vetera-galactic-lock.md`](../../../atlas-and-topography/castra-vetera-galactic-lock.md)) press the **Bifrons vault mesh** for years. Public tithe scrolls file **undifferentiated heretic Astartes**; sealed *Strategium* names **Iron Warriors** doctrine mirrored and **out-engineered** on Batavi **redundancy mesh** — planned withdrawal where each meter **taxes** the attacker. Gervas commands **defensive redundancy** as **Greater Mason**; pain sustains **each plan observed**.  
* **Watch proof:** **Certified *Watch cycle*** completed before SEXTA Legatus confirmation — includes at least one **void-station** siege rotation (file sealed).  
* **Rise to Legatus:** **Captain** during earlier **dual-anchor** skyhook crisis; *Strategium* read his after-action as **textbook** gulf *Friction Geometry*. **Siege Master** and ***Maior Caementarius*** confirmed post-reformation (~087–092.M42) during vexilla commission rebuild.  
* **098.M42 | Korvus twin breach (Cage climax):** Led **SEXTA** **simultaneous** assault on **Korvus-A** outer vault and **Korvus-B** inner spine within one Strategium tick — **counter-breach** that **breaks** the **Ferrum Cage** strangle; tether preserved; 44 battle-brothers killed or incapacitated; certified *Alabarda* **field 4**. Narrative: [korvus-twin-breach.md](../../../chronicles/vexillum-chronicles/korvus-twin-breach/chapter.md) · [aar-vex-vi-korvus-breach.md](../../../chronicles/vexillum-register/aar-vex-vi-korvus-breach.md).

## 5\. Relations

* **Alaric:** Not friendship — **load-bearing trust**. Alaric does not inspire Gervas; he **authorizes audits**. Gervas does not advise; he **names unreconciled meters**.  
* **Kadmos:** **Block and gate** partnership — Kadmos forges the tooth; Gervas places the jaw. Occasional **priority fights** over plate allocation — resolved in minutes, not grudges.  
* **Corbec / PRIMA obligation:** Respects PRIMA **rectification field**; never jokes about shame panel — builds **debt geometry** into Noviomagus plans.  
* **Valerius *(expunged)*:** The **brother-trust** ghost — reason for dual-gate **witness law**.  
* **Iron Warriors (enemy):** Professional hatred — **mirror he refuses to become**; redundancy mesh exists **because** he studied their doctrine in blood.  
* **Armin Sturmwahl:** Rivalry of **tempo**; joint ops blend **drop** and **breach** with high casualty budgets — both men audit the math afterward. In the Legatus Ring, Bifronte refuses to confuse **breach evidence** with **command inversion**.  
* **Peer Legati:** First call when a world must be **opened** or **fortified** without pretending either will be pretty. They retain **full battle command**; Gervas owns **the wall they fight from**.
