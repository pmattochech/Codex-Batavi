# \[WIKI\] CHARACTER DOSSIER: EINAR DREADWOLF

*(The Red Tide / Under the Deathwolf’s Jaws)*

**Full name:** **Einar Dreadwolf** — brother-given honor name after **Moraine-Nine**; shore registry before capture cited **Einar** of **Needles** cadre (Aethelgard native).  
**Rank:** **Lieutenant** — assault line, **V** *Quinta*; attached to **Legatus** Sturmwahl’s elite closure cadre after recovery.  
**Chapter:** *Cohors Batavorum*.  
**Vexillatio home:** **V** *Quinta* (Aethelgard Prime).  
**Apparent service age:** ~100–120 Terran years (estimate); **~century** in managed **Furor** only — **never** Exanimus before the Nails.

---

## 1\. Appearance and physiology

*What the Needles made; what the ship tried to unmake.*

* **Physique:** Broad-shouldered assault build; **digitigrade** conversion **not** present — flat foot, *Quinta* jump-line anchor intact.  
* **Furor (pre-capture — sole stage):** Flame-pool irises, slit pupils, perpetual exhaustion mask, **Type I — Pride** engraved state (Aethelgard shore competence). **No** Exanimus protocol: no lip-piercing canines, no predatory mane, **no** crimson gauntlet filing — he held the line under *Axiom* for a century without crossing.  
* **First Exanimus (074.M42 — Nails + ship + Moraine-Nine):** Forced across the threshold during implantation and massacre — jaw hypertrophy, mane, **red hands**, near-snarl lock; **irreversible** per curse law. What Sturmwahl almost culled was **new** stage geometry, not an old wolf.  
* **Post-capture stigmata:** **Butcher’s Nails** analog — conscious implant bundle; **cable** and **neural loom** tissue **merged** into the **new** mane along nape and jaw line (visible when helm open). Not a separate *Wolf’s Curse* stage — **foreign architecture** riding Strand β pressure; Varro files as **second beast** bound inside the first.  
* **Field kit (authorized):** **Partial armor** only — chest, waist, thigh plates; **bare** limbs in assault heat; **open** helm or no helm in sanctioned hunts. **Dual chainaxes** (ship salvage pattern, re-blessed by *Armourium*). Chapter does **not** treat the loadout as vanity — **distance geometry** for inner focus.

---

## 2\. Personality and psychology

* **Temperament:** Before capture — **pride** as fuel (Aethelgard shore: prove the tide did not own you). After capture — **duty** as welded replacement (same vector as Triumvirate **Type III**, but **line** rank, not stabilized).  
* **Voice:** Low, tide-flat; in recovery debrief, one sentence that became *Quinta* law: — **They were gone.** — **I gave them the gift to die as Batavi before the enemy could bite at their souls.**  
* **Distance:** Keeps clearance from pack warmth — not contempt; **focus**. Brothers honor him; he does not accept mess-hall proximity when Varro’s watch interval is active.  
* **Self-request:** Has asked **Armin Sturmwahl** to **cull** him if duty geometry fails — Armin has **not** filed assent; Varro has **not** forbidden the request.  
* **Refusal:** Will **not** train neophytes — *Strategium* accepts (precedent hazard).

---

## 3\. Combat and doctrine

* **Role:** **Traitor-hunt** and **Versibar** recovery specialist under **mandatory Judex** presence. Theaters filed include **Armageddon**-class splinter actions (traitor Astartes + cult mass) — always with **Varro** on mission slate.  
* **Style:** **Wulfen-adjacent** closure — not *Versibar*, not sanctioned **Wild Hunt** depth without *Curia* record; personal geometry of **chainaxe** mass and **post–Moraine-Nine Exanimus** strength (he learned the stage in blood, not in decades of prior filing). Slaughters **World Eaters** and **Ork** ferals with equal throughput when the **Red Demon** (Nails + Khorne bleed) is not steering.  
* **Gene-seed:** Harvested on death **only** if tissue passes **Vermilion** audit; if corruption is confirmed, **destroyed**. If he falls in lawful battle, **Versibar funeral rites** apply — same as any brother who dies in **Execratio** geometry (he is **not** Versibar while living).  
* ***Quinta* cadence:** When steadiness returns after slaughter, speaks the assault answer to **Iron Protocol** — *We hold, as the Needles hold the cold tide.* (See [GLOSSARY-EN.md](../../../GLOSSARY-EN.md).)

## 4\. History (in-universe — chronology access)

* **Rise:** Needles shore tribes → *Quinta* assault line; **century** of service held at **Furor** only — rare long-run containment without Exanimus promotion.  
* **Closest bond:** **Armin Sturmwahl** — jump-qualified friendship; Sturmwahl’s only recorded **hesitation** before a cull stroke.  
* **074.M42 | Shepherd’s Hook → *Kzorn’s Maw* → Moraine-Nine:**  
  * **Capture:** **V** *Quinta* squad (twelve) overrun at tide relay during Khorne-leaning bleed; **days** aboard World Eaters prison hulk. Narrative: [dreadwolf-the-maw.md](../../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/dreadwolf-the-maw.md) · recovery: [dreadwolf-red-tide.md](../../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/dreadwolf-red-tide.md).  
  * **Conscious Nails:** Brothers implanted **awake**; **Einar** **last**; cable loom at nape; **tripartite bind** (wolf vs **Red Demon** / nail-song) — marine **holds chains**, cage **almost** broken.  
  * **Mercy on ship:** Eleven brothers **gone** in cage (Versibar or soul-empty); Einar **gift-killed** before Brass could **bite** souls.  
  * **Slaughter in Maw:** Subject kills WE surgeons, guards, remaining ship crew; hulk crashes feral Moraine-Nine — **forced first Exanimus** on impact.  
  * **Recovery:** **Armin** + **Varro** recovery element; epicenter slaughter vs WE remnants + **Orks**; Sturmwahl **raised blade** for cull; salute + ***Quinta* cadence**; **not** culled.  
  * **After:** Transferred to Sturmwahl elite; **Varro** on **every** mission; **Vermilion** file — *In watch. Under the Deathwolf’s jaws. No soul-name. No precedent. No cult.*  
* **Present (Silent Vigil onward):** Chapter-wide **knowledge**; open honor **Dreadwolf**; Judex **withholds** soul-name taxonomy.

* **Varro:** **Deathwolf** watch — not affection; **bind**. Varro **refuses** to name the soul in liturgy; **witnesses** the salute at Moraine-Nine as first proof the **two-beast leash** held in open war.
