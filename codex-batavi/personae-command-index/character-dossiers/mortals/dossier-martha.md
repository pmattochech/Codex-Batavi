# \[WIKI\] DOSSIER: MARTHA

**Title:** Matriarch of the Kitchen / Keeper of the Fire.  
**Other titles:** Law of the Stove, Iron Mother, Queen of the Clean Zone.  
**Archetype:** Civil authority / emotional stability.  
**Current age:** ~65–70 Terran years.

## 1\. PSYCHOLOGICAL AND SOCIAL PROFILE

Martha is living proof that power on Noviomagus Prime is not always measured in ceramite or bolter calibers. She is matriarch of a brood of eight biological children, but her role extends to the whole fortress.

* **Civil courage:** Martha is possibly the only unaugmented human in the Aethelgard system who shows no fear before the Lord Castellan. She sees him beyond Exterminator armor, treating him as the man he was before the weight of the world crushed him.  
* **Domestic anchor:** While Alaric and Varro keep order through war and law, Martha keeps order through nourishment and civility. Her kitchen is the only place in the fortress where time seems not to be dictated by battle chronometers but by the rhythm of pots.

## 2\. JURISDICTION: THE KITCHEN (CLEAN ZONE)

Within her kitchen’s bounds, military rank is secondary to Martha’s orders.

* **Clean rule:** Not even the Lord of the Wall is exempt. Entering Martha’s kitchen requires boot-clean protocol and weapon systems deactivated. The “Clean Zone” is a sanctuary of domestic order amid industrial chaos.  
* **Meat tax:** Viggo, the late Thunderwolf, was Martha’s silent enforcer. He kept recruits and servants in line for scraps of synthetic meat. After the wolf’s death, silence in the kitchen became her most lasting tribute.

## 3\. ARSENAL AND AUTHORITY (DOCTRINE OF THE SPOON)

Martha carries no firearms, but her authority is exercised with tools of her trade that became symbols of power.

* **Industrial wooden spoon:** A tool of hardened synthetic oak, short-club sized. Martha uses it not only to stir ration cauldrons but to gesture orders and, occasionally, apply “disciplinary corrections” to giant wolves’ muzzles or legionaries’ arm plates who try to steal food early.  
* **Viggo’s talisman:** As matriarch, she received from Alaric one of the runic talismans forged on Fenris. The amulet, carved from Viggo’s bones, channels **Pack Warmth**. For Martha, it is the guarantee that her kitchen’s fire and her children’s safety will never be extinguished by Noviomagus winter.

## 4\. RELATIONSHIP WITH THE IRON VENUS (ELARA)

Martha is Elara’s maternal figure. She cared for the girl while Alaric was sunk in Silent Vigil silences. Martha taught Elara that diplomacy and leadership begin with understanding the basic needs of those you serve. While Alaric taught Elara to be iron, Martha taught Elara to be the fire that shapes it.
