# \[WIKI\] DOSSIER: APOTHECARY DRUSUS

**Title:** Chief Apothecary of the *Cohors Batavorum* / biological logistics.  
**Other titles:** Iron Surgeon, Seed Reaper, White Hand.  
**Archetype:** Clinical pragmatism / trench medic.  
**Estimated age:** ~580 Terran years (Foundation War veteran).

## 1\. PSYCHOLOGICAL AND OPERATIONAL PROFILE (MEAT MATH)

While most Adeptus Astartes Apothecaries nurture deep brotherhood with patients, Drusus reflects Noviomagus Prime’s sterile industrial environment. He does not see “wounded brothers”; he sees “biological assets” and “viability margins.”

* **Ruthless triage:** In a siege, resources cannot be wasted on sentiment. If a legionary falls and fast-repair odds fall below tactical margin, Drusus offers no false hope or comfort words. He administers the Emperor’s Peace, extracts gene-seed, and moves to the next target. Chapter survival always outweighs individual life.  
* **Biometric confessor:** As Foundation War veteran (015.M42) and clinical eyewitness who cataloged the Lord Castellan’s first stabilized *Execratio*, he is the only one in the Chapter who fully grasps the extent of Alaric’s biology and psychological fracture. Drusus used auspex sensors to prove wolf Viggo’s heart was the “biological anchor” stopping the Castellan’s brain waves from collapsing on the Adamant Throne. As creature of logic and biology, Drusus is the scientific voice Alaric trusts to separate reality from madness.

The Chief Apothecary’s face, perpetually hidden by his multi-lens helm, is final proof hostile biology can be tamed by scalpel. At **Exanimus**, Drusus retains the **ocular stigmata fixed at his Furor rupture** (amber–fire slit-pupil irises — the “flame-pit” eyes of the first stage, which do not revert when the jaw and extremities cross into Exanimus morphology) alongside severe mandibular hypertrophy and long lower canines that escape lip closure. He is not “still in Furor”; the earlier stage’s signature persists **cumulatively** under the later stage’s reconstruction. No melanin variation or sickly pallor in his skin; his dermis shows standard transhuman health.

Drusus’s recorded state is **Type III — Duty**, precipitated during the Foundation War’s first quarter. The Venator-Alpha Enclave was an enemy the *Apothecarion* had no tools to catalog: collapse biology was new, death patterns new, and every falling brother was data lost forever for lack of reference model. Furor came not from loss pain — Drusus already treated deaths as data — but from clinical impossibility of *naming* what was destroying the ranks. Being sole responsible for a whole Chapter’s biological diagnosis and lacking the diagnosis: that was the emotional shock the *Axiom* did not contain. What burned in was compulsion to let no datum leave uncataloged. The *“visceral intolerance of imprecision and waste”* he projects outward is the visible surface of that state; the biology beneath is urgency of a medic who once lost patients because he did not know what he was seeing — and decided in that moment it would never happen again.

In continuous act of technical asepsis, Drusus keeps blond hair and beard perfectly trimmed, shaving thick neck fur until skin is absolutely smooth. He restructured lip and facial musculature himself via relief incisions and tendon reattachment. Exanimus keratin claws are trimmed monthly with surgical tools — his and Varro’s. Unmanaged, progressive growth in confined gauntlet space causes chronic pressure that amplifies stage instability; Drusus documented this clinically and treats trimming as mandatory maintenance like any *Apothecarion* procedure. Consequently Drusus does not growl or struggle to articulate High Gothic. His speech is exact, cold, clinical, accompanied only by a light peculiar accent from air passing his large canines. Exanimus instability is present in Drusus — he simply suppresses it through control effort he himself cataloged and calibrated clinically. The state burned in at his founding-collapse threshold projects as visceral intolerance of imprecision and waste: when an asset is mismanaged or a diagnosis ignored, compulsion to intervene with cold cruelty is biological before doctrinal. The “infinite patience of a machine” Drusus projects outward costs, in real biology, more than the exterior suggests. He retains amplified strength and sharpened senses of the monster; what he lacks is unrestricted volatility — and that absence is discipline, not nature.

## 2\. ARMOR AND HERALDRY (CLINICAL CAMOUFLAGE)

* **Hybrid chassis:** Unlike traditional Apothecaries in all-white armor (easy targets), Drusus keeps armor base **industrial grey** for camouflage and visual integration with Batavi war machine in *Zone Mortalis*.  
* **Trade distinction (clinical white):** Only his right arm (mounting dangerous Narthecium), heavy med-pack full of stabilizing fluids, and helmet are painted traditional surgical white.  
* **Pauldrons:** Crimson, right pauldron edge painted pure white.  
* **Surgical helm and feeding protocol:** Tactical-design helmet with asymmetric multi-lenses for auspex and high-precision biometric sweep. Drusus refuses to expose face to contamination or emotional attack. Lower frontal plate — section covering chin, lower jaw, and mouth — has pneumatic rails embedded in ceramite: when triggered, lock releases internal pressure with **dry hydraulic sound** and thin **decompression vapor cloud**, then plate **splits at center and slides laterally** to helm flanks. Result is direct mouth access compartment — industrial, functional, no aesthetic concession. No delivery tubes or automatic jaws; panel only opens so food is brought manually to mouth, by Drusus’s own hand or server. On close, plate returns to rails with hermetic pressure and **dry magnetic click**, restoring helm’s clinical isolation. In Martha’s Kitchen, it is the only sound he makes before eating — and after.  
* **Stage markings (red code):** Furor crimson stripe runs horizontally across surgical helm’s asymmetric multi-lenses. Exanimus marking is applied on **left** arm and gauntlet (industrial grey), preserving functional surgical white on right arm (*Narthecium*). In combat, red left gauntlet is the only visible part of the code — enough.
* **Viggo’s talisman:** Hung on flask belt, amulet carved from old Thunderwolf bones. Runic relic channels **focus and wisdom**, honoring silent patience of predator who watches life and death without blinking.

## 3\. ARSENAL AND MEDICAL DOCTRINE (THE HARVEST)

Drusus is not a front-line fighter by choice, but cutting through the enemy is non-negotiable prerequisite to reach the fallen in Noviomagus hell.

* **Batavi Narthecium:** Drusus’s med-gauntlet is not delicate; it is industrial repair gear. High-rpm drills to pierce ceramite, brutal bone saws, plasma cauterizers, heavy automatic coagulant injectors. Where others suture, Drusus staples and welds.  
* ***Reductor* (harvest blade):** Lethal pneumatic tool designed to pierce fallen Astartes chest or throat and extract progenoid glands in seconds. Drusus’s most important weapon, securing Chapter future.  
* **Standard Primaris bolt rifle:** Used methodically for calculated shots, opening safety corridors and temporary exclusion zone around wounded.  
* **Surgeon’s duality (close arsenal):**  
  * **Astartes chainsword:** Heavy work and mechanical obstruction. Drusus saws through damaged ally armor or dismembers dense organic mass enemies blocking patient access. His “tactical amputation” tool.  
  * **Force gladius (second clinical blade):** Absolute precision weapon. Unlike noisy saw, gladius used for surgical thrusts to elite enemies’ vital points. Drusus applies anatomical knowledge to paralyze or kill instantly any threat trying to interrupt gene-seed extraction.

## 4\. RELATIONS AND CHAPTER POSITION

*Bridle of the triad.*

Triumvirate internal philosophy rests on three functions: Alaric is blade, Varro anchor, Drusus bridle. If Alaric cannot tell biology from madness, Drusus draws the line. If ranks melt — physically or psychologically — Drusus decides who still has repair value and who must be released.

* **Alaric von Helis:** The Chapter’s most complex relationship. Drusus holds Alaric’s full biometric file: every *Das Erbe* cycle, every rebound, every micro-infarct documented since Foundation War. He does not serve the Castellan from affective loyalty; he serves because Alaric is the Subsector’s most valuable biological asset, and he has monitored for centuries an organism the galaxy still cannot replicate. Alaric’s trust in Drusus is not emotional — functional, like a general trusting the only man who will tell truth about his own collapse.
* **Varro:** Clinical respect, doctrinal tension. Varro handles invisible — soul, discipline, **Wolf’s Curse** spiritual weight. Drusus handles measurable — chemistry, telemetry, viability rates. The two rarely disagree on diagnosis; they disagree what to do with it. Varro accepts death as geometry. Drusus resists death while repair cost does not exceed loss cost. That productive tension keeps the Chapter functional.
* **Elara Solis:** Drusus tracked Elara’s growth with same clinical interest he would a rare specimen. She is not Astartes, but weight carried and function exercised produce unusual biometric profile for unmodified human. He checks her health periodically unasked — and never explains beyond *“preventive maintenance of command asset.”*
* **Martha and the Kitchen:** Martha is the only living being Drusus treats with something like spontaneous courtesy. Not affection — courtesy, as a surgeon treats instruments. Drusus recognizes Martha’s Kitchen fulfills no pharmacological protocol can replicate: Castellan’s affective anchoring. He visits regularly, opens helm panel with dry magnetic lock click, eats in silence. Martha never tried to start conversation; Drusus never showed he needed one.
* **Batavi:** For ranks, Drusus is the figure who appears when you are falling. Not to save — to calculate. Veteran Batavi know the moment Drusus tilts head left: calculation just tipped wrong.

## 5\. RECORDED FEATS

*Vermilion file — Triumvirate access only.*

* **015–045.M42 | Foundation War:** Veteran of full conflict. First clinical record of Furor and Exanimus stages in field — beginning with his own biometric data, documented with same clinical cold applied to any other specimen. Personal Furor collapse in conflict’s first quarter is archived in personal records as *“Type III Rupture Event — Primary Sample”*: impossibility of cataloging enemy destroying ranks became emotional shock the *Axiom* did not contain. What emerged was recorded state defining everything after: irrevocable compulsion not to lose unnamed data. Cataloged in real time Alaric’s collapse to Execratio and will-forced return — only documented case to date. His report founded *Axiom Rationis* protocol as biological containment tool, not philosophy.
* **046.M42 | Jagd War (first documented axis):** Ork invasion of Skarjaw was first event where Drusus precisely recorded ranks’ collective endocrine response *before* contact — testosterone and growth hormone spikes two days before impact, regardless of tactical order. He provisionally named phenomenon *Jagd* in personal records. Formal definition waited two years and second field proof.
* **048.M42 | Noviomagus Secundus purge (second axis — Ruhe):** While 4th Cohort fought Tyranids in mining catacombs, Drusus monitored biometric telemetry of eight hundred warriors entering simultaneous limbic collapse no prior model predicted. Descended personally post-battle to document survivors in task loop. Later report named and defined *Ruhe* as second axis — and **Das Erbe** term appeared first as concept, still incomplete.
* **050.M42 | Malakor V incident (third axis — Blutrecht) and *Codex Das Erbe*:** Castellan’s execution of Valerius supplied third dataset: rebound was neither physical exhaustion nor limbic collapse but purpose collapse — deep systemic depression after elimination of focused hate object. Drusus cataloged state as *Blutrecht* (replacing provisional *Heavy Rains* — internal term retired, no formal record). Analysis showed trigger is not “generic Chaos” but **recognition of traitor gene-seed** — specifically signature of corrupted Astartes sharing lineage root. Intensity scales on two vectors: corruption depth and proximity to Horus Lupercal line. Valerius case hit top tier by stacking all vectors: fallen Batavi, corrupted Loken lineage, claim to *Lupercal* title. With three axes mapped and distinction between Blutrecht and amplified *Axiom Rationis* (response to generic Chaos without recognizable gene-seed) formalized, Drusus compiled ***Codex Das Erbe*** — full biological triangle synthesis of *Cohors Batavorum*. Most important biological text in Chapter history, remains *Vermilion* — Triumvirate only. Same Malakor V report contains archived entry **“Inverted Stability Case”** — no subject name, no ID number. Doctrinal conclusion: absence of progression in **Wolf’s Curse** stages is not protective factor; in at least one documented case it was risk factor. Marine never forged by rupture never built structures rupture forces. *Apothecarion* since monitors long biologically stable marines with same attention as confirmed Furor marines.
* **Ca. 560.M42 | Viggo anchoring:** Used auspex sensors to prove Thunderwolf Viggo’s heartbeat worked as biometric anchor for Castellan brain waves during five hundred years of Silent Vigil. Report classified immediately. Proof only materialized after wolf’s death, when Alaric’s collapse retroactively confirmed data.
* **Ca. 562.M42 | Iron and Blood Tribunal (*Bloodcaller*):** Only recorded breach of Drusus’s Visage Protocol in Chapter history. Aboard cruiser *Bloodcaller*, before Commander Dante, Mephiston, and a Batavi neophyte, Triumvirate removed helms in calculated maneuver demonstrating *Axiom Rationis* dominance over **Wolf’s Curse**. Drusus coordinated moment logistics — lighting angle, removal order, neophyte posture — for maximum demonstration impact. Helms were replaced before any photograph could register.
* **568.M42 | Sigma Moon battle — Integration anomaly:** Drusus ran mobile *Apothecarion* during Alaric’s single combat against Daemon Primarch Angron. Monitored Castellan telemetry in real time while *Castra-Lupus* was systematically destroyed over fifty-three minutes. After Alaric’s collapse, coordinated field extraction and began emergency protocol for multiple lethal damage: right arm lost below elbow, four implanted organs failing, fractures in forty-two percent of left-side bones. Initial estimate six months recovery with surgical reimplant. Third week of regenerative coma, sensors registered what Drusus later classified **Integration anomaly**: gene-seed, after five and a half centuries progressive fusion with stabilized Execratio organism, had ceased being implant set and become host’s **master cellular blueprint**. Lost organs regrew from basal tissue with precise architecture — no surgery, no reimplant. Arm regenerated to wrist in ninety days. Alaric woke day ninety-two with biometrics superior to pre-battle. Full Integration anomaly report classified *Vermilion* immediately; Drusus considers it most significant biological discovery in Chapter since 031.M42 Execratio itself — and possibly most important *Apothecarion* finding in Segmentum Pacificus since Tenth Legion’s foundation.
