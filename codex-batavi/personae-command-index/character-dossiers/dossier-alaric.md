Alaric embodies “pragmatic grimdark.” He is not a speech-giver but a presence. Authority radiates from war geometry: belief victory is an equation of positioning and endurance.

* **Anchor motto:** In combat he uses open vox protocol to say calmly: *“We hold.”*  
* **Behavior:** Extremely economical with words. Fixed point where enemy tide shatters. During Silent Vigil (his deep clinical depression), sanity anchored by stabilizing presence of Viggo, whose heartbeat stopped Castellan collapsing on Adamant Throne.
* **Recorded state (Type III — Duty):** Alaric’s Furor collapse came Foundation War first quarter — not wounded pride nor fear but accumulated crush of enemy wholly outside doctrinal model. *Codex Astartes* did not predict Venator-Alpha Enclave: every protocol failed, every position fell, chimera gene-seed predator biology challenged by force hunting as superior predator. What burned in when *Axiom* failed was not rage at defeat nor terror of extinction — duty. Irrevocable compulsion not to retreat. Fixation on point not yet fallen. Recorded state amplified fixation into permanent biological vector: Alaric does not explode when opposed; he anchors. Greater external pressure, slower and more fixed he becomes. Physiological mechanism that became *“We hold”* — not chosen motto but verbal expression of biological state. Drusus categorized as Type III in *Codex Das Erbe*: rarest and most sustainable recorded state, because it anchors rather than accelerates.

### 1.1 THE PHYSIOGNOMY OF ALPHA ANOMALY

Alaric von Helis’s face and human body map Foundation War scars and living proof **Wolf’s Curse** can be bent by will. Unlike official records simplifying his condition, Alaric is not pure Execratio (**Versibar**), nor at Exanimus stage. Forcing his own mutation to recede after falling into abyss, biology locked at undocumented intermediate stage.

He inherited muscular density, traction strength, and aberrant size of **Versibar** (colossal 3.5 m height) but kept biped structural integrity of transhuman biology. Face is geometric extrapolation, most brutal *Exanimus stage* seen in his generals. Alaric shows maxillary hypertrophy still more severe and massive than Varro and Drusus, widened bone structure built for crushing. From that foundation project long thick lower lupine canines escaping lip containment. Yet he imposes absolute military asepsis: grey hair and beard rigorously shaved, face carries millimetric scars of tension-relief micro-surgeries ensuring High Gothic articulation with icy perfection. Weathered face reads ~40 Terran years maturity. Free of Silent Vigil lethargy weight, Castellan dermis regained healthy hard transhuman forge pigment, fully shedding tomb-pallor and deep eye hollows, though heavy neural interface ports remain driven direct into skull and jaw. Framing this perfectly tamed abomination, eyes work as inhuman signature of his stasis: deep crimson iris with greened edges and permanently slit pupils.

Beyond anatomical changes, Alaric von Helis’s intermediate-stage stabilization produced command anomaly *Apothecarion* classifies Alpha Frequency. Not traditional **Warp** psychic manifestation but bio-telepathic sub-vocal resonance inherent chimera seed (Loken/Russ) atavistic dynamic.

**Versibar control:** Execratio stage turns fallen Batavi into 4.5 m lupine abominations, irrational monsters wholly immune standard tactical doctrine. Through Alpha Frequency, however, Alaric operates these biological weapons. Using low-frequency growls, physical traction postures, and pure predator-gaze pressure, he imposes direct orders corrupted **Versibar** biology obeys instinctively.

**Crown maintenance (physical challenge):** Submitting these beasts is not definitive algorithm; volatile biological hierarchy. As in nature, leadership needs continuous maintenance. When new Batavi succumbs to curse becoming **Versibar**, or Lord Castellan goes long cycles without exercising visual authority over fallen herd, abominations’ instincts drive them to test Alpha. Lethal challenges occur. To restore control and prevent beast mutiny, words useless. Alaric forced subjugate monsters using only metric they understand: brute physical force application, beating until structure yields and submission recognition re-established.

**Silent command (line ranks):** Alpha Frequency utility extends perfectly to Batavi keeping tactical stability. In critical engagements Alaric can command **Vexillationes in field** (or **Tenth** when isolated) non-verbally through *vox*. Using only dry clicks, coded growls, breath modulations, transmits complex tactical guidelines. For common ranks those sounds translate instinctive obedience. Unlike **Versibars**, sound legionaries need no violent dominance reaffirmation; minds armored *Axiom Rationis* accept Alpha frequency instantly, converting animal instinct into absolute military efficiency.
