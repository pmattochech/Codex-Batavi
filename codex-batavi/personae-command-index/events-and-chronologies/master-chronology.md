# Master chronology: the iron legacy

* **Zero point:** Opening of the Cicatrix Maledictum (ca. 999.M41).  
* **Enemy:** Time and entropy.  
* **Operational calendar start:** 015.M42.

**Addendum: Castra Vetera paradox and broken time** Noviomagus’s geographic isolation works in symbiosis with chronological isolation. Brutal Immaterium storm pressure against our Blackstone Pylon network dilates time. For Holy Terra, the Great Rift opened scant decades ago; inside Noviomagus’s dome, five hundred years of clinical pain unfolded in full. Yet that anomaly exacts a thermodynamic toll on those who dare cross the veil.

**Temporal ricochet mechanics (void toll):** Outsiders leaving Castra Vetera suffer *temporal decompression*. Crossing the exit fold, physical reality hauls their “individual time” backward. Cellular biology rewinds violently in extreme physical agony — bones crack to readjust density, organs spasm to purge accelerated cells. The mind, however, preserves every second of trauma. Above all, time forgives age, but iron does not forgive failure: atavistic mutations driven by lethal **Wolf’s Curse** and endocrine wear from *Das Erbe* are **not** healed by temporal ricochet. They are permanent.

**Root elasticity (bond of origin):** Entropy recognizes the cradle. Individuals whose temporal origin lies *inside* the frontier hyper-time — such as Iron Venus Elara Solis, or any Batavi legionary — carry accelerated time as their “zero point.” They are immune to decompression. When a native leaves for normal space, “temporal elasticity” occurs: the **Warp** stretches their time perception to sync them with Noviomagus. An absence of tens of days outside reflects as a variable but bearable lapse inside the frontier. The anchor does not break.

**Immaterium fluctuation and Ordo Chronos:** The flow is not linear math but fluid anarchy the Adeptus Mechanicus calls “Void Tides.” At high tide, one day outside may mean a decade inside; in calm, a month may equal two. That non-linear lethality drew the elusive Inquisition *Ordo Chronos*. Operating in the *Silent Belt*, they map safe decompression routes where **Warp** dilation is staged so imperial tithe fleets and allies transition without becoming biological dust. Invaders who flee without exact coordinates often end pulverized inside their own suits by gravitational and temporal whip.

## I. Foundation era and myth (015.M42 – 045.M42)

*Establishing the frontier, discovering biology, breaking the Codex by necessity.*

* **015.M42 | Predator War (liberation of Aethelgard):**  
  * *Initial status:* Chapter lands *Codex-compliant* (~1,000 Astartes).  
  * *Conflict:* Thirty-year guerrilla war against *Venator-Alpha* Enclave in swamps and jungles.  
  * *The curse:* Stress of being “prey” awakens gene-seed. Furor and Exanimus stages manifest.  
  * *Alaric’s miracle:* At climax, Alaric reaches Execratio (Versibar) and returns by will, stabilizing at intermediate Execratio (3.5 m).  
  * *Cost:* Reduction to only 500 battle-brothers.

## II. Post-war and expansion (045.M42)

*The decision that defined the Chapter’s illegal structure.*

* **Chapter expansion (non-*Codex* strength):** Alaric concludes 1,000 men are insufficient. Orders expansion to **5,000 riflemen**.  
* **Super-Tithe (Badab lesson):** Institutes **Shared Sweat doctrine** to push industrial output 15% above quota, buying political immunity with economic efficiency.

## III. Golden Projection era (045.M42 – 050.M42)

*Drusus’s tactical calibration and tragedy.*

* **046.M42 | Jagd War:** *Predatory instinct* activation against Waaagh! Skarjaw.  
* **048.M42 | Ruhe defense:** *Perfect soldier* activation under **Shadow in the Warp** of **Hive Mind** (tactical immunity to swarm-scale psychic veil). *Later doctrinal note:* Tyranid threats **without** that aggregate signature — e.g. hidden Genestealer cults — cannot rely on *Ruhe* as detector; weight **falls on** the Silence Cohort. **IV** *Quarta* **field 4** reference hold: [ruhe-defense.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/ruhe-defense.md) · [aar-vex-iv-ruhe-defense.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/aar-vex-iv-ruhe-defense.md).

Ratified by Roboute Guilliman as direct response to expansion to 5,000 marines — the Imperium ignores illegal structure in exchange for biological transparency and a shield in the void.  
* **050.M42 | Malakor V incident (Silent Vigil begins):** Alaric is forced to execute Valerius (Onyx Wolf), his Legatus and traitor brother — first Batavi to fall to Undivided Chaos, taking the title *Lupercal*. ## V. Noviomagus rebirth (550.M42 – 565.M42)

*End of depression and humanization through Elara.*

* **550.M42 | Parapet encounter:**  
  * *Event:* Alaric finds abandoned infant **Elara Solis** in the rain. The child’s cry breaks Silent Vigil emotional catatonia.  
  * *Change:* Alaric adopts the child and begins visiting “Martha’s kitchen.” Shared Sweat evolves from forced labor to social integration.  
* **555.M42 | Incus-Gravis crisis (nobility purge):** Alaric executes corrupt aristocracy hoarding supplies. The population sees the “monster” protect the small.

## VI. Gate war and ascent (565.M42 – present)

*Elara’s maturity and Throne validation.*

* **568.M42 | Battle of Sigma Moon (Angron):**  
  * *Theater:* Sigma Moon — mining satellite system. No civilian population; only soldiers, convicts, and servitors when invasion hit.  
  * *Enemy:* Angron (Daemon Primarch) and World Eaters.  
  * *Action:* Alaric uses regenerative biology to hold Angron in single combat. **Integration anomaly** — ability to regrow Astartes organs without reimplant — first documented in full.  
  * *Clean-up:* **Grey Knights** purge the moon after battle. Elara Solis, present as civilian observer, is not informed of operation nature — a frontier Inquisitor supplies official version (corruption too deep for containment); Alaric confirms only half-truths. Elara spends years reconstructing what really happened.  
* **350.M42 | Vigilax incident:** Marines Malevolent run unauthorized purge against civilians under Castra Vetera mandate on Vigilax Prime. Varro applies *Crozius* (reeducation mode) to enemy captain, eliminates two Astartes who aim weapons, destroys Malevolent battle banner before troops. Drusus documents second violence focus in Marna-Down sector. Later hearing with Inquisitor and Black Templars witnesses — Malevolent Chapter Master insults Alaric referencing Valerius execution; Castellan kills him bare-handed. Lasting tension between Malevolent and Alaric’s presence through Silent Vigil. *See:* [vigilax-incident.md](../../chronicles/12-vigilax/vigilax-incident.md); **III** *Tertia* field 4 lens: [vigilax-triangulation.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/vigilax-triangulation.md); AAR: [aar-vex-iii-vigilax-triangulation.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/aar-vex-iii-vigilax-triangulation.md).  
* **575.M42 | Captain-General visit (calibration):**  
  * *Event:* Trajann Valoris descends personally to Noviomagus Prime — not to validate, to audit. Triunvirate receives him with full transparency: descent to Black Cells, full briefing on Wolf’s Curse, Versibar capabilities and vulnerabilities, Testament protocols.  
  * *Result:* Trajann does not free oversight. Increases permanent Custodes presence in subsector. Hands Tyvar *Nihili Protocol* — last-instance seal that, if activated, cascades Blackstone Pylons in temporal implosion, consuming entire frontier so traitors inherit nothing.  
  * *The name:* *Administratum* registers **Castra Vetera subsector and Loken Passage** as ***Porta Nihili*** — not as title granted the Chapter, but as geographic designation of full strategic corridor. The name is warning: if the gate falls, the void consumes it.  
* **580.M42 (present) | Gate consolidation:**  
  * *Status:* Alaric rules as Lord Castellan. **Elara Solis (age 30)** commands the fleet. Inquisitor Vane politically neutralized.
