# Master chronology: the iron legacy

* **Zero point:** Opening of the Cicatrix Maledictum (ca. 999.M41).  
* **Enemy:** Time and entropy.  
* **Operational calendar start:** 015.M42.

**Chronology addendum (Strategium / *Ordos* filing):** Primary annals use **Castra Vetera (CV) reckoning**; **~M42** tithe dates are **approximate** cross-filings — **Chronostrife** and **warp-leg** variance mean off-world scrolls rarely match corridor math one-to-one. **Realspace** on Noviomagus runs **strategic-depth normal**; **time “wrongness”** concentrates in **Warp** approach and egress (**translation hazard**, **Void Tides** in the subsector mesh). *Ordo Chronos* maintains presence for **calendar correlation** and **routing**, not because Noviomagus ages faster under a dome. **Frontier-native** Batavi carry **bond-of-origin** doctrine in recruitment and Steppenwolf files — without **ricochet** as the backbone of service-age fiction.

* **CV y13–y29 | Tidal + machine endgame:** Incus / Vitreus cluster; **Alaric** hits **Execratio** (~**CV y19**; ~**031.M42** *approximate* off-mesh filing) and returns — [`chapter-24-incus-still-burns.md`](../../chronicles/00-foundation-war/chapter-24-incus-still-burns.md), [`chapter-25-the-beast-chapter.md`](../../chronicles/00-foundation-war/chapter-25-the-beast-chapter.md). ***We hold*** as command seal [`chapter-35-we-hold-anyway.md`](../../chronicles/00-foundation-war/chapter-35-we-hold-anyway.md). **Der Batav** shout [`chapter-30-tide-names.md`](../../chronicles/00-foundation-war/chapter-30-tide-names.md). **Cohors Batavorum** ratified [`chapter-37-der-batav-council.md`](../../chronicles/00-foundation-war/chapter-37-der-batav-council.md); coda [`chapter-38-instrumentum-solum.md`](../../chronicles/00-foundation-war/chapter-38-instrumentum-solum.md).

## II. Post-crucible bridge (~045.M42)

*Forges and tithe — **without** yet breaking *Codex* headcount.*

* **Shared Sweat / super-tithe (Badab lesson):** Hard-commits **Aequalitas Machinae** labor and pushes industrial output **~15%** above quota — city-scale proof the mesh can **feed** more plate **before** the Triumvirate risks illegal strength. Rational line stays **~500** until **Malakor V**; **mass** expansion toward **~5,000** is **not** declared in this interval.

## III. Golden Projection era (045.M42 – 051.M42)

*Drusus’s calibration, Valerian Heresy, Primaris reinforcement — **then** the ~5,000 decision and collar.*

* **046.M42 | Jagd War:** *Predatory instinct* activation against Waaagh! Skarjaw.  
* **048.M42 | Ruhe defense:** *Perfect soldier* activation under **Shadow in the Warp** of **Hive Mind** (tactical immunity to swarm-scale psychic veil). *Later doctrinal note:* Tyranid threats **without** that aggregate signature — e.g. hidden Genestealer cults — cannot rely on *Ruhe* as detector; weight **falls on** the Silence Cohort. **IV** *Quarta* **field 4** reference hold: [ruhe-defense.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/ruhe-defense.md) · [aar-vex-iv-ruhe-defense.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/aar-vex-iv-ruhe-defense.md).  
* **050.M42 | Malakor V incident (Silent Vigil begins):** Alaric is forced to execute Valerius (Onyx Wolf), his Legatus and traitor brother — first Batavi to fall to Undivided Chaos, taking the title *Lupercal*. The wider **Valerian Heresy** (**I** *Prima* rot) costs **~300** battle-brothers as **KIA** or **Versibar**; **loyal Versibars** under **Blutrecht** fixation and **Alpha** command contribute to **traitor** destruction — first **documented** **battlefield** ***Adversarii at Portas*** correlation (distinct from **terminal** Black Cell release; clinical: [`projection-aurea-wolfs-curse.md`](../../biological-encyclopedia-bestiary/projection-aurea-wolfs-curse.md)). Post-crisis **Firstboot** count **~200** rational line. Execution triggers the most intense **Blutrecht** response yet recorded in the Chapter. Trauma plunges Alaric into deep depression. Narrative: [malakor-v.md](../../chronicles/06-silent-vigil/malakor-v.md).
* **050.M42 (post-return) | *Prima* *Legatus* seat — Noviomagus re-seat:** *Strategium* **registers** **1st Vexillatio (*Prima*)** *Legatus* **primary anchor** on **Noviomagus Prime** (Central Bastion), ending the First Legatus **exclusive forward** posture on **Threshold / Cicatrix-adjacent** campaign bodies (e.g. **Malakor V** theater). **Purpose:** forge-law alignment, tithe clarity, **symbolic severance** from traitor map coordinates; **VIII**/**IX** absorb primary **rift-mouth** habit in the modern grid. *Prima* **deploys** across the mesh as ordered — the seat is **not** a deployment cage. *See* [vexillatio-operational-armory.md](../../arsenal-and-logistics/vexillatio-operational-armory.md) §3 (*Prima*).
* **050.M42 (late) – 051.M42 | Primaris mass reinforcement:** *Indomitus*–era uplift lands **after** the Heresy bloodletting — **Primaris** tranches, **Torchbearer** / Segmentum logistics, and replenishment **re-open** forge and *Medicinae* bandwidth the **~200** **Firstboot** spine cannot fake. **Only with those bodies and templates on the table** does the Triumvirate treat illegal scale as **executable**.
* **051.M42 | Non–*Codex* expansion ratified (~5,000 ceiling):** Alaric **commits** the mesh to **~5,000** battle-brothers — the strategic decision **follows** reinforcement, not the crucible’s end: Castra Vetera’s mandate is judged **unholdable** on fraternal math alone, but **unforgivable** to enlarge **before** the Throne’s gene-work and hulls arrive.
* **051.M42 | Collar treaty (*Limes Lupus*):** Alaric accepts permanent Adeptus Custodes oversight (Shield-Captain Tyvar) in exchange for full operational autonomy on the frontier. Roboute Guilliman ratifies as **direct response** to the **~5,000** posture and super-tithe — the Imperium tolerates illegal Chapter strength in exchange for biological transparency and a Custodian shield in the void.
* **051.M42 | Deathwatch career lattice:** *Strategium* codifies **mandatory ≥1 certified *Watch cycle*** for any **new** **Legatus** appointment and for **Vexillatio X** (*Decima*) admission (supreme posts exempt). ***Clausula retentionis antecedentis*:** Legati and *Decima* brothers **already lawful** at the seal **retain** post **without** retroactive Watch — see [deathwatch-and-batavorum.md](../../political-relations/deathwatch-and-batavorum.md) §4.2. **Valerius** never operated under this rule — it is a **post–First Legatus** lattice.  
* **051.M42 | *Justice vexillum* commission (*Decima*):** *Armourium* completes Castellan escort standard — **feral wolf slain by gladius** (dominant charge); *Reclusiam* oath alignment; first bearer logged. Narrative: [decima-justice-vexillum.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/decima-justice-vexillum.md); annex: [register-decima-malakor-justice.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/register-decima-malakor-justice.md); sentence: [malakor-v.md](../../chronicles/06-silent-vigil/malakor-v.md).

## IV. Silent Vigil (050.M42 – 150.M42)

*One hundred years of isolation, glacial efficiency, silence. The Chapter runs as pure *Instrumentum Solum*.*

* **060.M42 – 148.M42 | Ice Wall:** Alaric rules from the Adamant Throne, never removing armor. Noviomagus becomes an industrial utopia without joy. Inquisitor Vane tries to prove heresy but is blocked by bureaucracy and the Super-Tithe.  
* **078.M42 | Hive Dominus compliance (Castra-Vetera Prime):** **II** *Secunda* executes **Administratum**-mandated purge of **recidivist guild–cult** holding **Ministry of Tithe Lex** spires; victory metric = **zero** structural breach of listed **sanctae** facades. *Strategium* cites as **measured-fire** reference for *Alabarda* **field 4**. *See:* [hive-dominus-compliance.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/hive-dominus-compliance.md) · [aar-vex-ii-hive-dominus-compliance.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/aar-vex-ii-hive-dominus-compliance.md).
* **096.M42 | Helbrecht at Loken Passage (first meeting):** Black Templars crusade transits corridor with imperial authorization; opportunistic xeno attack in funnel (Drukhari and satellites). Defensive coordination with Batavi. Helbrecht and Alaric measure each other in transit chamber and void; tension between Templar faith and Batavi geometry; tacit recognition of duty without affection confession. Narrative echo in **165.M42** (*wolf-daughter.md*). *See:* [helbrecht-first-loken-passage.md](../../chronicles/08-audit-diplomacy/helbrecht-first-loken-passage.md); *VIII* *Octava* field 4 lens: [loken-funnel-octava.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/loken-funnel-octava.md); AAR: [aar-vex-viii-loken-passage-intercept.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/aar-vex-viii-loken-passage-intercept.md).
* **098.M42 | Korvus twin breach (Bifrons-Ferrum gulf):** **VI** *Sexta* **simultaneous** assault on **Korvus-A** outer vault and **Korvus-B** inner spine — **heretic Astartes** warband filed **undifferentiated** in public register. Single coordinated clock; *Strategium* certifies **field 4**. *See:* [korvus-twin-breach.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/korvus-twin-breach.md) · [aar-vex-vi-korvus-breach.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/aar-vex-vi-korvus-breach.md).
* **110.M42 | Vigilax incident:** Marines Malevolent run unauthorized purge against civilians under Castra Vetera mandate on Vigilax Prime. Varro applies *Crozius* (reeducation mode) to enemy captain, eliminates two Astartes who aim weapons, destroys Malevolent battle banner before troops. Drusus documents second violence focus in Marna-Down sector. Later hearing with Inquisitor and Black Templars witnesses — Malevolent Chapter Master insults Alaric referencing Valerius execution; Castellan kills him bare-handed. Lasting tension between Malevolent and Alaric’s presence through Silent Vigil. **Before** Elara’s parapet encounter. *See:* [vigilax-incident.md](../../chronicles/12-vigilax/vigilax-incident.md); **III** *Tertia* field 4 lens: [vigilax-triangulation.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/vigilax-triangulation.md); AAR: [aar-vex-iii-vigilax-triangulation.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/aar-vex-iii-vigilax-triangulation.md).  
* **112.M42 | Null-Vent Covenant (Vitreus):** **VII** *Septima* contains **lens-forge plasma** and **photonic** cascade without **forgeship** or primary hive **detonation**; *Armourium* adopts as **vent discipline** exemplar for *Alabarda* **field 4**. *See:* [null-vent-covenant.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/null-vent-covenant.md) · [aar-vex-vii-null-vent-covenant.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/aar-vex-vii-null-vent-covenant.md).
* **121.M42 | Gutgrind Descent (Selene-Black, Aethelgard system):** **V** *Quinta* **jump** assault clears vertical **Ork** scrap-spire; **Warboss Gutgrind** killed; moon secured for **Navy** orbital fire solutions. Certified **post–mesh** triumph for *Alabarda* **field 4** (**Foundation War** opening on Aethelgard **not** used for **field 4** certification — predates **public** fourth-field register). *See:* [gutgrind-descent.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/gutgrind-descent.md) · [aar-vex-v-gutgrind-descent.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/aar-vex-v-gutgrind-descent.md).
* **140.M42 | Silent Hours (Marco de Vetra):** **IX** *Nona* holds **relay** and **laser farm** through **Maleventum**-class EM storm; **Shouter** traffic passes **integrity** thresholds; enemy denied **intact capture** of array. *Strategium* certifies *Alabarda* **field 4**. *See:* [silent-hours-marco-de-vetra.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/silent-hours-marco-de-vetra.md) · [aar-vex-ix-silent-hours.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/aar-vex-ix-silent-hours.md).

## V. Noviomagus rebirth (150.M42 – 165.M42)

*End of depression and humanization through Elara.*

* **150.M42 | Parapet encounter:**  
  * *Event:* Alaric finds abandoned infant **Elara Solis** in the rain. The child’s cry breaks Silent Vigil emotional catatonia.  
  * *Change:* Alaric adopts the child and begins visiting “Martha’s kitchen.” Shared Sweat evolves from forced labor to social integration.  
* **155.M42 | Incus-Gravis crisis (nobility purge):** Alaric executes corrupt aristocracy hoarding supplies. The population sees the “monster” protect the small.

## VI. Gate war and ascent (165.M42 – present)

*Elara’s maturity and Throne validation.*

* **165.M42 | *The Wooden Sword* (Guilliman, Calgar, Helbrecht):** Yard inspection and Elara’s public step before the Regent — full chronicle [*wolf-daughter.md*](../../chronicles/07-iron-venus/wolf-daughter.md). **Synchrony protocol** / rewarming of relations per [legislative-chronology.md](legislative-chronology.md).  
* **168.M42 | Battle of Sigma Moon (Angron):**  
  * *Theater:* Sigma Moon — mining satellite system. No civilian population; only soldiers, convicts, and servitors when invasion hit.  
  * *Enemy:* Angron (Daemon Primarch) and World Eaters.  
  * *Action:* Alaric uses regenerative biology to hold Angron in single combat. **Integration anomaly** — ability to regrow Astartes organs without reimplant — first documented in full.  
  * *Clean-up:* **Grey Knights** purge the moon after battle. Elara Solis, present as civilian observer, is not informed of operation nature — a frontier Inquisitor supplies official version (corruption too deep for containment); Alaric confirms only half-truths. Elara spends years reconstructing what really happened.  
* **175.M42 | Captain-General visit (calibration):**  
  * *Event:* Trajann Valoris descends personally to Noviomagus Prime — not to validate, to audit. Triunvirate receives him with full transparency: descent to Black Cells, full briefing on Wolf’s Curse, Versibar capabilities and vulnerabilities, Testament protocols.  
  * *Result:* Trajann does not free oversight. Increases permanent Custodes presence in subsector. Hands Tyvar *Nihili Protocol* — last-instance seal that, if activated, cascades Blackstone Pylons in temporal implosion, consuming entire frontier so traitors inherit nothing.  
  * *The name:* *Administratum* registers **Castra Vetera subsector and Loken Passage** as ***Porta Nihili*** — not as title granted the Chapter, but as geographic designation of full strategic corridor. The name is warning: if the gate falls, the void consumes it.  
* **180.M42 (present) | Gate consolidation:**  
  * *Status:* Alaric rules as Lord Castellan. **Elara Solis (age 30)** commands the fleet. Inquisitor Vane politically neutralized.
