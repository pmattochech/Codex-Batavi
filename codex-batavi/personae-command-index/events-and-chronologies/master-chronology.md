# Master chronology: the iron legacy

* **Zero point:** Opening of the Cicatrix Maledictum (ca. 999.M41).  
* **Enemy:** Time and entropy.  
* **Operational calendar start:** 015.M42.

**Chronology addendum (Strategium / *Ordos* filing):** Primary annals use **Castra Vetera (CV) reckoning**; **~M42** tithe dates are **approximate** cross-filings — **Chronostrife** and **warp-leg** log variance. **Realspace** on Noviomagus runs **strategic-depth normal** — **no** subsetor **time dilation** or **ricochet** physics. **Warp** in/out of **Insula Tenebrarum** is **extremely dangerous** without **Insular chart-houses** (Navis covenants with sealed routes for **Witness Ford** and marsh channels). *Ordo Chronos* = **calendar correlation** and beacon calibration, not “fast dome.” Atlas: [`castra-vetera-galactic-lock.md`](../../atlas-and-topography/castra-vetera-galactic-lock.md).

## II. Post-crucible bridge (~045.M42)

*Forges and tithe — **without** yet breaking *Codex* headcount.*

* **Shared Sweat / super-tithe (Badab lesson):** Hard-commits **Aequalitas Machinae** labor and pushes industrial output **~15%** above quota — city-scale proof the mesh can **feed** more plate **before** the Triumvirate risks illegal strength. Rational line stays **~500** until **Malakor V**; **mass** expansion toward **~5,000** is **not** declared in this interval.

## III. Golden Projection era (045.M42 – 051.M42)

*Drusus’s calibration, Valerian Heresy, Primaris reinforcement — **then** the ~5,000 decision and collar.*

* **046.M42 | Wild Hunt War:** *Predatory instinct* activation against Waaagh! Skarjaw.  
* **048.M42 | March defense:** *Perfect soldier* activation under **Shadow in the Warp** of **Hive Mind** (tactical immunity to swarm-scale psychic veil). *Later doctrinal note:* Tyranid threats **without** that aggregate signature — e.g. hidden Genestealer cults — cannot rely on March of the Machine as detector; weight **falls on** the Silence Cohort. **IV** *Quarta* **field 4** reference hold: [ruhe-defense.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/ruhe-defense.md) · [aar-vex-iv-ruhe-defense.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/aar-vex-iv-ruhe-defense.md).  
* **050.M42 | Malakor V incident (Silent Vigil begins):** Alaric is forced to execute Valerius (Onyx Wolf), his Legatus and traitor brother — first Batavi to fall to Undivided Chaos, taking the title *Lupercal*. The wider **Valerian Heresy** (**I** *Prima* rot) costs **~300** battle-brothers as **KIA** or **Versibar** across the **Malakor** theater and **containment** geometry (**III** / **V** and corridor tax — not solely *Prima* roster arithmetic); **five** *Prima* brothers remain **lawful witness** core. **Loyal Versibars** under *the Cull* fixation and **Alpha** command contribute to **traitor** destruction — first **documented** **battlefield** ***Adversarii at Portas*** correlation (distinct from **terminal** Black Cell release; clinical: [`projection-aurea-wolfs-curse.md`](../../biological-encyclopedia-bestiary/projection-aurea-wolfs-curse.md)). *Medicinae* **Reading B** filing: **conflict-hour** **Versibar** manifests carry **separate** lineage from **pre-crisis** hold cohorts so rot-surge statistics do not absorb older cage data. Valerius’s expunged **First Sword**, **Cassian Vorn**, is the **second documented lucid Versibar** (adjunct **Warp** latch; weaker than Valerius). Post-crisis **Firstboot** count **~200** rational line. Execution triggers the most intense *the Cull* response yet recorded in the Chapter. Trauma plunges Alaric into deep depression. Narrative: [00-malakor-v.md](../../chronicles/06-silent-vigil/00-malakor-v.md).
* **050.M42 (post-return) | *Prima* *Legatus* seat — Noviomagus re-seat:** *Strategium* **registers** **1st Vexillatio (*Prima*)** *Legatus* **primary anchor** on **Noviomagus Prime** (Central Bastion), ending the First Legatus **exclusive forward** posture on **Threshold / Cicatrix-adjacent** campaign bodies (e.g. **Malakor V** theater). **Purpose:** forge-law alignment, tithe clarity, **symbolic severance** from traitor map coordinates; **VIII**/**IX** absorb primary **rift-mouth** habit in the modern grid. *Prima* **deploys** across the mesh as ordered — the seat is **not** a deployment cage. *See* [vexillatio-operational-armory.md](../../arsenal-and-logistics/vexillatio-operational-armory.md) §3 (*Prima*).
* **050.M42 (late) – 051.M42 | Primaris mass reinforcement:** *Indomitus*–era uplift lands **after** the Heresy bloodletting — **Primaris** tranches, **Torchbearer** / Segmentum logistics, and replenishment **re-open** forge and *Medicinae* bandwidth the **~200** **Firstboot** spine cannot fake. **Only with those bodies and templates on the table** does the Triumvirate treat illegal scale as **executable**.
* **051.M42 | Non–*Codex* expansion ratified (~5,000 ceiling):** Alaric **commits** the mesh to **~5,000** battle-brothers — the strategic decision **follows** reinforcement, not the crucible’s end: Castra Vetera’s mandate is judged **unholdable** on fraternal math alone, but **unforgivable** to enlarge **before** the Throne’s gene-work and hulls arrive.
* **051.M42 | Collar treaty (*Limes Lupus*):** Alaric accepts permanent Adeptus Custodes oversight (Shield-Captain Tyvar) in exchange for full operational autonomy on the frontier. Roboute Guilliman ratifies as **direct response** to the **~5,000** posture and super-tithe — the Imperium tolerates illegal Chapter strength in exchange for biological transparency and a Custodian shield in the void.
* **051.M42 | Deathwatch career lattice:** *Strategium* codifies **mandatory ≥1 certified *Watch cycle*** for any **new** **Legatus** appointment and for **Vexillatio X** (*Decima*) admission (supreme posts exempt). ***Clausula retentionis antecedentis*:** Legati and *Decima* brothers **already lawful** at the seal **retain** post **without** retroactive Watch — see [deathwatch-and-batavorum.md](../../political-relations/deathwatch-and-batavorum.md) §4.2. **Valerius** never operated under this rule — it is a **post–First Legatus** lattice.  
* **051.M42 | *Justice vexillum* commission (*Decima*):** *Armourium* completes Castellan escort standard — **feral wolf slain by gladius** (dominant charge); *Reclusiam* oath alignment; first bearer logged. Narrative: [decima-justice-vexillum.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/decima-justice-vexillum.md); annex: [register-decima-malakor-justice.md](../../chronicles/vexillum-register/register-decima-malakor-justice.md); sentence: [00-malakor-v.md](../../chronicles/06-silent-vigil/00-malakor-v.md).

## V. Noviomagus rebirth (150.M42 – 165.M42)

*End of depression and humanization through Elara.*

* **150.M42 | Parapet encounter:**  
  * *Event:* Alaric finds abandoned infant **Elara Solis** in the rain. The child’s cry breaks Silent Vigil emotional catatonia.  
  * *Change:* Alaric adopts the child and begins visiting “Martha’s kitchen.” Shared Sweat evolves from forced labor to social integration.  
* **155.M42 | Incus-Gravis crisis (nobility purge):** Alaric executes corrupt aristocracy hoarding supplies. The population sees the “monster” protect the small.

## VI. Gate war and ascent (165.M42 – present)

*Elara’s maturity and Throne validation.*

* **165.M42 | *The Wooden Sword* (Guilliman, Calgar, Helbrecht):** Yard inspection and Elara’s public step before the Regent — full chronicle [*wolf-daughter.md*](../../chronicles/07-iron-venus/wolf-daughter.md). **Synchrony protocol** / rewarming of relations per [legislative-chronology.md](legislative-chronology.md).  
* **168.M42 | Battle of Sigma Moon (Angron):**  
  * *Theater:* Sigma Moon — mining satellite system. No civilian population; only soldiers, convicts, and servitors when invasion hit.  
  * *Enemy:* Angron (Daemon Primarch) and World Eaters.  
  * *Action:* Alaric uses regenerative biology to hold Angron in single combat. **Integration anomaly** — ability to regrow Astartes organs without reimplant — first documented in full.  
  * *V Quinta terminis:* **Lieutenant Einar Dreadwolf** KIA on outer perimeter — ***Spina Cineris*** molecular terminis; Judex witness (**Varro**); **Legatus Armin Sturmwahl** present; gene-seed **null** (annihilated). Closes **074.M42** Judex watch file. Narrative: [dreadwolf-terminus.md](../../chronicles/14-vexillum-chronicles/dreadwolf-terminus.md).  
  * *Clean-up:* **Grey Knights** purge the moon after battle. Elara Solis, present as civilian observer, is not informed of operation nature — a frontier Inquisitor supplies official version (corruption too deep for containment); Alaric confirms only half-truths. Elara spends years reconstructing what really happened.  
* **175.M42 | Captain-General visit (calibration):**
  * *Event:* Trajann Valoris descends personally to Noviomagus Prime — not to validate, to audit. Triunvirate receives him with full transparency: descent to Black Cells, full briefing on Wolf’s Curse, Versibar capabilities and vulnerabilities, Testament protocols. *See:* [call-to-the-throne.md](../../chronicles/13-apotheosis/call-to-the-throne.md).
  * *Result:* Trajann does not free oversight. Increases permanent Custodes presence in subsector. Hands Tyvar ***Nihili Protocol*** — last-instance seal that, if activated, cascades Blackstone Pylons in **network overload** / **warp sterilization**, consuming the border mesh so traitors inherit nothing. Staff physics = **pylon cascade**, not time anomaly. *See:* [seal-of-the-portal.md](../../chronicles/13-apotheosis/seal-of-the-portal.md).
  * *Administratum gloss:* **Insula Tenebrarum** / **Castra Vetera** mesh filed as critical **Nihilus** filter node; outward choke = **Witness Ford**. *(Retired astro names in new prose: *Porta Nihili*, *Loken Passage*.)*  
* **180.M42 (present) | Gate consolidation:**  
  * *Status:* Alaric rules as Lord Castellan. **Elara Solis (age 30)** commands the fleet. Inquisitor Vane politically neutralized.
