## 1. Summary

The **Valerian Heresy** is the Chapter’s **brother civil war** — not a sudden infection arc, but **~40 Imperial years** of **two-power** mesh command (Castellan **Alaric** / First Legatus **Valerius**) while strength returns toward **~5,000** battle-brothers. **No *Alabarda* vexilla cloth** exists during the war — only **two hosts**. **PRIMA–NONA** and DECIMA **justice vexillum** are **post-heresy** institutions: anti-concentration reform + specialty Legati grown from field squads.

**Terminal crisis (~086.M42):** **Malakor V** — execution of **Valerius** (*Lupercal* / lucid **Versibar** / **Daemon Prince** ascent in endgame). Opens **Silent Vigil** (**~086–186.M42**). Loyal victory imposes **island debt**: Castra Vetera and mortal mesh **almost** (not quite) prioritized over open Administratum narrative — Witness Ford, Shared Sweat, receipt culture hardened.

**Corbec Ardens** and a **small lawful core** = internal resistance inside rot-era PRIMA; post-Malakor → **Castellan vigil** (Contingency Axiom Lupus chain) — **not** distrust; **will not lose another great leader to a finished “why.”**

## 3. Fall mechanism (locks)

### 3.1 Broken heart (credit asymmetry)

- Mesh mortals and parade narrative favor **Alaric**; Valerius’s excellence is **operational**, not legible at distance.
- Alaric **does** file trust and credit for Valerius — **insufficient** against elite audiences and scroll compression.
- Arc: **grief** → **resentment** (“warrior became politician”) → **hate** (“stole my future / the succession mortals already see”).

### 3.2 Warp link — questions only

- **Not** possession rewriting loyalty; **not** “infection made him traitor.”
- Tainted contact (author scaffold: **Word Bearers** sorcerer or bound entity in **artifact**) plants one question in many shapes: **why?**
- Strong will **answers itself** over decades.

### 3.3 Final seal (implicit Horus — do not name in register)

Valerius’s private completion (paraphrase lock):

> *I see it now. It all makes sense. That is why **he** did it. Now I see why. Now I know why. That is also why **I** have to do it. And why I will **succeed** — unlike him.*

### 3.4 Endgame theology (Valerius POV)

- **Harvest** progenoid glands from **Alaric’s** dead — parallel stock, “purity” delusion.
- **Sacrifice / ascension** = **purify** Chapter + **free Castra Vetera** (mortals + Astartes) from Imperium that **abandoned** the island — not nihilism.
- **Versibar + Daemon Prince** only in **ending years** of heresy — not mid-era Furor.

### 3.5 Apex dialogue (Malakor confrontation)

- **Valerius:** *Why?*
- **Alaric (truthful):** wall holds; **we** are the wall; **you** are the tide that breaks and fails — pack geometry vs Valerius **above** the pack.

## 6. Loyalist epilogue (why they hold the island)

Victory after **brother-war** → **debt** to mortals and mesh Valerius claimed to liberate:

- **Shared Sweat** / witness routes hardened.
- **Almost** Castra Vetera over Administratum parade — **not** secession; **filter-node** survival instinct.
