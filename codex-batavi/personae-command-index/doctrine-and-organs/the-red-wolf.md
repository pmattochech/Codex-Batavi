# the Red Wolf — Castellan breach (sealed doctrine)

*Triumvirate + Legatus access. Open Administratum rolls do not carry this file.*

**Always** **the Red Wolf** — capital **R** and **W**, proper epithet in *Reclusiam*, *Strategium*, and corridor speech. **Not** Crimson Wolf, ***rubor thermicus***, RTC, or thermal predator nomenclature.

**Not** **Execratio**. **Not** **Versibar**. The Castellan does **not** grow, does **not** shed *Castra-Lupus*, does **not** advance curse stage. What fails is **battle rhythm** and **friend–foe discrimination** under predator motor debt borrowed from the genome he forced back at the abyss.

---

## 1. Mechanism (stabilized Execratio hazard)

Lord Castellan Alaric von Helis is the sole recorded case to reach **Execratio** and **stabilize** without **Versibar** morphology. Stabilization **blocks** stage ratchet — but **predator motor programs** can still discharge when **engagement rhythm**, **objective collapse**, and **command load** misalign.

**No visual armor change:** *Castra-Lupus* silhouette, heraldry, and plate colours **do not** shift in the window — **no** thermochrome flush, **no** animal posture, **no** quadrupedal gait. Witnesses who know Chapter **red code** must not misfile the event as Furor or Exanimus advance.

**Locomotion:** **Upright** Astartes violence at **~2.85 m** — still Alaric’s chassis, still Terminator mass — but movement reads **savage**: closing speed, shoulder checks, **Second Blade** brutality without *Instrumentum* spacing.

---

## 2. the Red Wolf behavior

**Trigger:** Loss of control over **engagement rhythm** and **target discrimination** — never a planned order. Most often after prolonged **Wolf stance** pressure, objective-collapse (primary enemy dead while predator motor programs remain loaded), or post-execution moral shock (**Malakor V** filing).

**Cap:** **Once per engagement** *when the failure occurs*. Not a tactic the Castellan can repeat by choice after cooling.

**Weapons in the window:** **Aethelgard claws** and **power-fist strikes** primary — ripping, punching, *Sentence* thrusts if the blade is still in hand. Combi-melta is not the signature of the window; **close slaughter** is.

**Friend–foe discrimination failure:** Auspex and vox record **targeting lattice failure** — the Castellan does **not** reliably distinguish brother from enemy under load. **Priority matches Versibar function without Versibar form:** **nearest threat first**; only after that lane is empty does attention move to **allies who closed into his geometry**. Brothers who **hold distance** are less likely to be hit. If the enemy is already destroyed, behavior can match **Versibar** surplus violence: **search-and-rip** until nothing moves in range.

**Strategic rider (post–055.M42):** Because Alaric can operate **Alpha-frequency** command pressure over corrupted assets, any sustained Red Wolf state coupled with command inversion is treated as extinction-grade risk. Contingency chain law: [`contingency-axiom-lupus.md`](contingency-axiom-lupus.md).

**Return:** Not guaranteed by biology. Witnessed recoveries involve **external bind** (Varro) and/or **Alpha-frequency** self-recapture. After return, the Castellan **remembers** thought and sensation — shame, tactical distance, **cold duty** weight (see [`dossier-alaric.md`](../character-dossiers/triumvirate/dossier-alaric.md) §8.4). **Not** human-depression catatonia.

**Abbreviated window (Type B — staff filing only):** Short breach — predator debt discharges with **limited** friend–foe collapse; **mnemonic shame dominant**. **First** Type B on record: **Garmr’s Shelf**, **~055.M42** — Castellan **self-recaptured** at a **non-combatant** pup after Patriarch vector slaughter; origin of **Viggo** imprint as **shame debt** and **protector** obligation, not doctrine. Chronicle: [`viggo-saga.md`](../../chronicles/silent-vigil/viggo-the-heat-of-the-stone/viggo-saga/chapter.md).

## 4. Varro containment — Death Wolf and *ictus coercitionis*

Only **Varro** is trained and spiritually positioned to **close** on the Red Wolf without breaking. ***Reclusiam*** idiom: Varro walks the **Death Wolf** face of the curse — the skull that remains after the predator dies — because he stabilized **Exanimus** on **Type III Duty** and accepts being the **necessary monster** who can meet monsters without flinching.

**Versibars** in the pits **fear** Varro as headsman. **Alaric** in the window does **not** fear him — **Alpha** hierarchy still reads — but a **half-second hesitation** (brother, not prey) has been telemetered before the bind ends.

**Tool — *ictus coercitionis* (restraint stroke):** Varro’s ***Crozius Arcanum*** (*Iron Verdict*) in **Sentence-calibrated** mode, field inverted for **penetration** through ***Castra-Lupus*** and **Black Carapace** interface — **not** to kill the Castellan. Effect: **servo seizure**, joint lock, forced dump through the weapon’s heat sink. Varro fights **defensively**: intercept charge lines, **counter-strike** to limbs and shoulder trunnions, **deny** the kill lane until friend–foe discrimination returns. Lethal Sentence against Alaric remains **possible** in theory; Varro’s office treats that as **Chapter extinction** event and has never authorized it.

Other Chaplains, Custodes **Tyvar**, and line Battle-Brothers are briefed to **clear the geometry** — not to “help” in the pit.
