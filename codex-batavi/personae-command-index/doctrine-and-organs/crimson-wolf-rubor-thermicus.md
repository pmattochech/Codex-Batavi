# Crimson Wolf / *Rubor thermicus* — Castellan thermal bleed (sealed doctrine)

*Triumvirate + Legatus access. Open Administratum rolls do not carry this file.*

Dual naming is deliberate: **Chaplain Varro** and ***Reclusiam*** use liturgical language; **Chief Apothecary Drusus** uses clinical language. Same phenomenon, different obligations.


| Layer                  | Name                                                    | Speaker                 |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Spiritual / Judex      | **Crimson Wolf** (*Lupus ruber*)                        | Varro, senior Chaplains |
| Clinical / *Medicinae* | ***Rubor thermicus*** (RTC — *rubor thermicus cascade*) | Drusus, *Apothecarion*  |


**Not** **Execratio**. **Not** **Versibar**. The Castellan does **not** grow, does **not** shed *Castra-Lupus*, does **not** lose gene-seed viability to stage progression. What fails is **thermal budget**, **battle rhythm**, and **friend–foe discrimination** under predator motor debt borrowed from the genome he forced back at the abyss.

---

## 1. Mechanism (forge + curse red code)

***Castra-Lupus*** was built for torque the standard Terminator mold cannot survive. Servos and *Dreadnought*-class structural members convert **Execratio-density** muscle into motion that reads as Tacticus agility until the suit’s **waste heat** outruns passive venting.

**Red code echo (visual, not a new stage):** On line marines, **Furor** marks the **helm**; **Exanimus** marks **hands and forearms**. On the Castellan, pauldrons and forearms are **already crimson** by heraldry. Under overload, **thermochrome binders** and **heat-bloom** make **grey plate flush red** along joints, ribs, and vent seams — as if the armor were **catching up** to the Chapter’s curse markings. Witnesses who know red code often misfile the event as “the Wall advanced a stage.” Drusus’s counters: **no** stage advance; **RTC**.

**Operational bands (no public gauge):**

1. **Stable — Bastion band:** Passive dissipation; ***Breakwater*** planted acts as primary **radiator** (heat into shield face and stake mass; proximity burn to closing infantry; allies behind the wall).
2. **Tactical overload — Wolf band:** Sustained *Wolf stance* pressure; vapor at vents; *The Sentence* may sink waste heat into the blade (incandescent edge). Rising debt.
3. **Critical — *Rubor thermicus*:** **Failure**, not doctrine. See §2.

Integrated **Noviomagus combi-melta** (right power fist housing) adds heat to the ledger when fired; it is **not** a spammable delete button.

## 2. *Rubor thermicus* behavior (the Crimson Wolf)

**Trigger:** Loss of control over **heat**, **engagement rhythm**, and **target discrimination** — never a planned order. Most often after prolonged **Wolf band** work, combi-melta venting, or objective-collapse (primary enemy dead while predator motor programs remain loaded).

**Cap:** **Once per engagement** *when the failure occurs*. Not a tactic the Castellan can “choose” to repeat after cooling.

**Locomotion:** Quadrupedal **Versibar-adjacent** gait — knuckles and boot-toes, spine low, *Castra-Lupus* mass carried like a hunting animal, not a Dreadnought. Still **~2.85 m** at the shoulder line; still **Alaric’s** chassis.

**Weapons in the window:** **Aethelgard claws** primary — adamantium tips **white-hot**, cauterizing tears. *The Sentence* and *Morkai’s Bite* may be dropped or ignored. Combi-melta is not the signature of the window; **claw slaughter** is.

**Discrimination failure:** Auspex and vox record **IFF collapse** — allies, auxilia, and empty corridors read as **residual prey** until the window ends. If the enemy is already destroyed, behavior matches **Versibar** surplus violence: **search-and-rip** until nothing moves.

**Strategic rider (post-055.M42):** Because Alaric can operate **Alpha-frequency** command pressure over corrupted assets, any sustained Crimson Wolf state coupled with command inversion is treated as extinction-grade risk: one wolf in command directing the pack against the Imperium. Contingency chain law: [`contingency-axiom-lupus.md`](contingency-axiom-lupus.md).

**Return:** Not guaranteed by biology. Witnessed recoveries involve **external bind** (Varro) and/or **Alpha-frequency** self-recapture. After return, the Castellan **remembers** thought and sensation — shame, distance, and accelerated **Silent Vigil** weight (see `[dossier-alaric.md](../character-dossiers/triumvirate/dossier-alaric.md)` §8).

**Abbreviated RTC (*rubor thermicus* Type B):** Short window — predator debt discharges without full IFF collapse or Varro bind. **Caloric debit minor**; **mnemonic shame dominant**. **First** Type B on record: **Garmr’s Shelf**, **~055.M42** — Castellan **self-recaptured** at a **non-combatant** pup after Patriarch vector slaughter; origin of **Viggo** imprint as **shame debt**, not doctrine. Chronicle: `[viggo-saga.md](../../chronicles/silent-vigil/viggo-the-heat-of-the-stone/viggo-saga/chapter.md)`.

## 4. Varro containment — Death Wolf and *ictus coercitionis*

Only **Varro** is trained and spiritually positioned to **close** on the Crimson Wolf without breaking. ***Reclusiam*** idiom: Varro walks the **Death Wolf** face of the curse — the skull that remains after the predator dies — because he stabilized **Exanimus** on **Type III Duty** and accepts being the **necessary monster** who can meet monsters without flinching.

**Versibars** in the pits **fear** Varro as headsman. **Alaric** in RTC does **not** fear him — **Alpha** hierarchy still reads — but a **half-second hesitation** (brother, not prey) has been telemetered before the bind ends.

**Tool — *ictus coercitionis* (restraint stroke):** Varro’s ***Crozius Arcanum*** (*Iron Verdict*) in **Sentence-calibrated** mode, field inverted for **penetration** through ***Castra-Lupus*** and **Black Carapace** interface — **not** to kill the Castellan. Effect: **servo seizure**, joint lock, forced **thermal dump** through the weapon’s heat sink. Varro fights **defensively**: intercept charge lines, **counter-strike** to limbs and shoulder trunnions, **deny** the kill lane until IFF returns. Lethal Sentence against Alaric remains **possible** in theory; Varro’s office treats that as **Chapter extinction** event and has never authorized it.

Other Chaplains, Custodes **Tyvar**, and line Battle-Brothers are briefed to **clear the geometry** — not to “help” in the pit.
