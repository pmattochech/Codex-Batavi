## I. Thesis

The **Castra Vetera** populations do not “convert” the *Cohors Batavorum* through missions, sermons, or cultural-export programs. **Proximity converts.** Decades of garrison, forge detail, trench adjacency, and auxilia logistics import **cadence, idiom, debt-language, and grief tempo** into the Chapter faster than any lexicon committee could approve.

This document names the mechanism so Strategium staff stop mistaking borrowed habit for **cosplay** — and so Reclusiam staff stop burning habit that is actually **load-bearing**.

---

## II. Mechanism — silent adoption by proxy

**Witness inheritance** is the Chapter’s working term for culture absorbed **without** a charter:

* **Shared hazard:** The same tide, quota, hive breach, and forge shift teach Batavi and mortals the same **names for entropy**. What the population calls the hour before a stampede or a furnace flare becomes launch-pad slang — not because theology demanded it, but because **shared clocks** beat louder than *High Gothic* in the ribcage.

* **Administrative invisibility:** Most substrata never receives a seal. It lives in **threshold speech** — hangar murmurs, salt-hand gestures, forge-floor counting — until someone tries to export it off-world and discovers it **does not travel** without context.

* **Debt, not disguise:** Substrata enters as **witness debt** paid to people the Wall refused to bury wholesale — not as mask-theft. Where substrata reads as rival-Chapter trophy theater, the Judex lattice intervenes (see §V).

## IV. Neophyte increment (recruitment as cultural input)

Neophytes drawn from **Noviomagus** and **Castra Vetera** spillways arrive as **dialects of panic** — salt-speech shards, feast residues, District oaths — compressed by elevation and surgery into Batavi cadence across decades.

**Doctrinal shorthand (training cadre):**

* ***Apothecarion*** trims **biology** (compatibility, Furor vectors, seed stress).
* **Judex lattice** trims **lyric** — what survives repeated induction without spiking **Furor** / **Exanimus** precursors becomes **new normal**, not folklore performance.

This is how substrata **drifts** without a reform council: each intake **nudges** Chapter speech the way a forge **wanders** if you never recalibrate the die — except here the die is **Varro’s silence law**, not a Magos calendar.

## V. Judex filtration (Varro office — elevate vs burn)

**Elevate** (toward friction-rite or formal annex) when the habit:

* stabilizes **silence discipline** or **anchoring** under *Das Erbe* pressure;
* binds **auxilia** oath without softening the Wall’s refusal to negotiate with defeat;
* gives mortals a **predictable grief tempo** Batavi can **support** without performing **cheap hope**.

**Burn** (suppress in open cadre) when the habit:

* spikes adrenaline curves wrong for **Furor** hygiene (crowd euphoria, unmanaged chorus);
* hands **Ecclesiarchy** or **rival Chapters** a clean insult vector — “they pray like prey” — **without** tactical offset;
* replaces **Axiom** vocabulary with consolation theater — *the Wall does not cosplay relief*.

Burning is not **shame** of origin. It is **firebreak**: the Chapter cannot afford poetry that softens geometry.

## VI. Engine — Shared Sweat (*Pax* §3)

The **Doctrine of Shared Sweat** is the **highest-bandwidth** channel for witness inheritance. Shoulder-to-shoulder labor with civilians imports substrata faster than parade chapels because **fatigue shares dialect** — not doctrine handouts.

Strategium implication: any policy that **isolates** Batavi from forge floors to preserve “purity” **starves** substrata intake and can **increase** ego detachment the *Pax* was written to prevent.

## VII. One-line issuing phrase (briefings)

> **Castra Vetera did not teach us their myths on purpose; we learned the names they use for the same gravity — and those names became another layer of armor.**

Use as **interior** ethos — not allied *vox* theology.
