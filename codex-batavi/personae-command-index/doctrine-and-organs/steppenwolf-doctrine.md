# Steppenwolf — detached brothers (doctrine note)

*Slang adopted into Chapter usage; *Strategium* treats the status as **ordnance classification**, not scandal.*

---

## 1. Etymology

The word entered Batavi *vox* after a **White Scars** exchange officer glossed a brother’s file with a **Chogoris** field comparison: a predator that **ranges off-pack** — not driven out, but **ill-meshed** with the herd’s tempo or **choosing** distance because the herd’s geometry **wrong-fits** the animal’s stride.

The Chapter kept the loanword **Steppenwolf** (plural **Steppenwolves**) as barracks shorthand. *Reclusiam* and *Medicinae* files use dry notation; line brothers use the name **without** exile-tone — **respect** and **neutrality** are the default registers.

---

## 2. What it means (thematic load)

A **Steppenwolf** is a battle-brother for whom the **Vexillatio lattice** — cohort rhythm, parade *vox*, constant *Axiom* friction with neighbors — does **not** settle the **beast within** the way doctrine predicts. For most Batavi, that lattice **damps** *Das Erbe* oscillation and **channels** *Furor* into formation law. For a Steppenwolf, the **same** structure can **amplify** wildness: too much proximity, too much synchrony, wrong kind of pressure.

**Detached operation** — long-range kill-geometry, sparse resupply, alien tempo — often reads on his biologis feed as **more stable** than garrisoned life. The Wall does **not** read that as treasonous “preference.” It reads it as **tooling**: one Marine, one **different appliance**.

The old Terran literary shadow (**the stranger between two worlds**) is allowed to **hum** under the slang: he remains *Cohors Batavorum*, oath-bound, gene-debt intact — but his **felt** pack is sometimes the **empty band** between Noviomagus’s lattice and the mission’s nowhere. **He is not a pariah** and **not** administratively exiled; he is **reassigned** to a geometry the Triumvirate can justify on the tallies.

---

## 3. Status and tallies

Steppenwolves **remain** on **Chapter strength** and count toward the **5,000** ceiling (see [military-hierarchy.md](military-hierarchy.md) §4.1). They do **not** live “off the books.” *Strategium* maintains a **detached registry** — billet codes vary by theater (Watch fortress, rim cutter, *Cartographica* anchor) while gene-seed and oath stay **Batavi**.

**Exception to Watch rotation numbers:** For **Deathwatch** and *Strategium* **throughput accounting**, Steppenwolf **heads** sit on the **exception ledger** — they **do not** consume slots from the **capped voluntary rotation pool** that limits ordinary line secondments ([deathwatch-and-batavorum.md](../../political-relations/deathwatch-and-batavorum.md) §4). Their **perpetual or long-detached** billets are **negotiated separately** in the Watch–Chapter appendix.

**Frontier birth and chronology:** **Every** Steppenwolf is **of the frontier** — recruited, implanted, and sworn **inside** the Castra Vetera mesh (the Chapter does **not** file **Steppenwolf** status for non-native transfers). They carry the same **bond-of-origin** filing as other **frontier-native** Batavi: **CV** / Strategium annals are **authority** for service biography; **off-world tithe dates** and **warp-leg** duration may **disagree** without imposing **dome-time aging** or **exit ricochet** on the marine — see [master-chronology.md](../events-and-chronologies/master-chronology.md) (**Chronology addendum**).

## 4. Modal patterns (not exhaustive)

**Perpetual Watch (common archetype).** The brother **does not** rotate back into a home Vexillatio cell as his primary home. He holds **open** Deathwatch assignment (or serial extensions under treaty), returning to Noviomagus **only** on **fixed intervals** to **brief command** and to complete the **Rites of Participation and Regulation** — *Medicinae* and *Reclusiam* protocols that re-seat him in the Chapter’s witness and discipline fabric before he ships out again. When the rites close, he returns to the **long watch**.

**Rites of Participation and Regulation — relation to Doctrine of Shared Sweat.** There is **no** separate dossier titled only for these rites; the name denotes a **Triumvirate-scheduled bundle** on each return. **Participation** is **re-entry into the civil mesh**: *Strategium* brief plus **condensed Shared Sweat** blocks — foundry, haulage, or infrastructure labor beside *Auxilia*, restoring the **Drusus** (endocrine burn) and **Varro** (mortal-anchor) effects that daily garrison life normally supplies ([pax-batavorum.md](pax-batavorum.md) §3). While detached, a Steppenwolf **does not** clock ordinary Shared Sweat; the rites **pay down** that absence in focused intervals. **Regulation** is *Medicinae* and *Reclusiam* clearance — clinical *Das Erbe* / *Furor* telemetry and *Axiom* audit so detached geometry does not become unsupervised drift.

**Rim and Halo Stars kill-teams.** Small detachments operating **outside** the Castra Vetera frontier, extreme autonomy, long *vox* silence. **Shadows Cohort** brothers appear disproportionately in these files — infiltration, stalk-geometry, and deniable corridor work match the Steppenwolf profile — but the status is **not** cohort-exclusive.

Deathwatch is a **frequent** shell for Steppenwolves; it is **not** the **definition** of the term. Any Triumvirate-sanctioned detached posture that matches §2–§3 can carry the classification.

---

## 5. Career lattice (short cross-note)

Steppenwolf service is a **branch**, not a punishment detail. It is **not** a substitute path to **Legatus** or *Decima* eligibility unless *Strategium* separately certifies the **closed** prerequisites (including, where applicable, a full **certified *Watch cycle*** as defined in [deathwatch-and-batavorum.md](../../political-relations/deathwatch-and-batavorum.md) §4.1 — perpetual detached files are **audited**, not assumed equivalent). Perpetual Watch Steppenwolves are budgeted under **Watch–Chapter** agreements; liaison and disclosure still run through *Medicinae* ([deathwatch-and-batavorum.md](../../political-relations/deathwatch-and-batavorum.md) §5).
