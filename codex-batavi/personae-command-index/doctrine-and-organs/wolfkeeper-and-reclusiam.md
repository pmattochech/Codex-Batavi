**Doctrine line (unchanged):** Mortivigils keep the brothers’ **bodies in death**; Wolfkeepers keep their **souls in life**.

**Why Wolfkeeper:** Custody of living oath, *Axiom*, silence, and licensed rite — **keeper**, not soft confessor and not Librarium seer. **Wolf** marks Batavi soul-geometry and Executor livery, not a Space Wolves rank copy (*Wolf Priest* remains their word).

**Do not confuse with:**

* **Confessor Vexilli / Confessor Primus** — banner-court *conduct* seats (penance and marks), not the class name for every crozius-bearer.  
* **Death Wolf** — *Reclusiam* idiom for Varro’s curse-face when closing on **the Red Wolf** / headsman silhouette; not the line class title.  
* **Haruspex / Blindgate** — *Librarium* (Otho); warp telemetry and mind-terrain, not soul-law.

Helm hardware, crypt pipeline, and ***licentia lupina*** detail remain in [mortivigil-and-reclusiam-helm-law.md](mortivigil-and-reclusiam-helm-law.md). This page names the **office** and its duties.

## II. What a Wolfkeeper is for

A Batavi Wolfkeeper is a **soul-warden of the living Wall**: priest of **maintenance and ending**, not preacher of glory.

### Core responsibilities (line office)

1. **Soul in life** — hold *Axiom* discipline; stop spiritual fracture before it becomes curse-weather.  
2. **Silence geometry** — teach and enforce when speech feeds the rift (**Closed Door** hygiene).  
3. **Licensed liturgy** — run [Tenets of the Grey Wolves](../../lexicon-and-litanies/tenets-of-the-grey-wolves.md) on **low measured lines** and **silent liturgy** (thurible, incense, crozius mark, stillness).  
4. **Crozius office** — reeducation against own brothers drifting toward structural failure; sentence against enemies and irredeemable traitors (apex calibrations: Varro’s *Iron Verdict*).  
5. **Useful terror / audit** — presence as judgment pressure; faith treated as **compliance and data**, not Ministorum frenzy.  
6. **Remonstrance boundary** — objection before seal is maintenance; after seal, obedience ([remonstratio-docens.md](remonstratio-docens.md)).  
7. **Affect / soul-law teaching** — no demand for Human-readable “proof” faces; bind shame/story hazards the Warp eats ([batavi-affect-kinesics-and-mortal-literacy.md](batavi-affect-kinesics-and-mortal-literacy.md)).  
8. **Judex filtration (lattice)** — elevate useful proximity habit into rite, or burn it as Furor / Ecclesiarchy hazard ([proximity-substrata-and-witness-inheritance.md](proximity-substrata-and-witness-inheritance.md)).  
9. **Induction and rite stack** — *Axiom* induction; friction rites; spiritual close where funeral doctrine calls (Mortivigils lead body / Wall-fusion craft).  
10. **Banner-court conduct lane** — when seated as **Confessor Vexilli**: penance and marks, parallel to Legatus / Genetor.

### With ***licentia lupina*** only

Far-field leash on ***Amissi*** and **Steppenwolf** registries: keep them **Batavi**; deny cult, guest-Chapter echo, personal legend, Brass drift. One seal covers both pools ([mortivigil-and-reclusiam-helm-law.md](mortivigil-and-reclusiam-helm-law.md) §IV).

### Not the line Wolfkeeper’s job

| Lane | Owner |
|------|--------|
| Crypt, bone, Wall-fusion, first rites of the dead | **Mortivigil** |
| Warp telemetry, Haruspicium, Blindgate | **Librarium** (Otho) |
| *Anima speculum*; Death Wolf projection; Red Wolf bind; Hell Cell apex | **Judex** (Varro) — see §III |
| Soft pastoral comfort-confession | **Not Batavi doctrine** |

## III. Judex apex — Varro (Master Wolfkeeper)

Varro remains **Judex**: silence before sentence; audit, not rant. Titles in force: Master Chaplain (Imperial gloss) / **Master Wolfkeeper** / Judex / Master of the Judiciars (Judiciar track TBD).

**Signature tools (locked elsewhere, summarized):**

* ***Anima speculum* (soul-mirror):** *Reclusiam* judgment sense — reads a warrior’s **soul-shape** under curse and law. Not sanctioned battle-psykercraft; not Haruspicium.  
* **Death Wolf face:** The skull that remains after the predator dies. Used when Varro must meet **the Red Wolf** and as the headsman silhouette Versibars fear ([the-red-wolf.md](the-red-wolf.md)).  
* **Projection (combat / judgment show):** The Judex may **show** the Death Wolf soul-face outward — penitent / ending wolf, not god-radiance. Enemies with warp-habit may feel **dread or despair**, not Ministorum holy terror. Line Wolfkeepers do not claim this as class kit; **Death Wolf Measure** in the Tenets is Judex-only.  
* ***Ictus coercitionis* / Hell Cells / Malakor oath:** As in [dossier-varro.md](../character-dossiers/triumvirate/dossier-varro.md).

Upstream immaterial seat: twin-shadow bind-law compressed into licensed liturgy for the lattice ([council-and-librarium.md](council-and-librarium.md) §5–§6).

## IV. Faith cut (open record)

Wolfkeepers honor **Ancestor-Sovereign** weight — oath and Wall — not Black Templar God-Emperor theater. On the open Imperial record, Batavi Founding / progenitor remain **unknown / redacted**; wolf marks are phenotype and heraldry, not a gene-seed receipt.
