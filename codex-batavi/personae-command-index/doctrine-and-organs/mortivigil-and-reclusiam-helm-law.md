## II. Mortivigil

**Definition:** *Reclusiam* novice on the Wolfkeeper path — High Gothic office name **Mortivigil** (death-vigil). Not a Primaris **Judiciar**; not a magistrate. Judiciar office under Varro is a **separate** track (placeholder — evolve later).

**Duties:**

* Guard fallen brothers’ **crypts, bones, and names**.
* Perform **funeral rites** (pyre, armor strip, remnant recovery) and first handling of the dead.
* **Wall-fusion:** fuse the fallen’s **name** into the foundation of the place he died defending — any city, fort, station, or wall-segment in **Castra Vetera**. He ceases to be the Wall’s guard and **becomes the Wall itself**. Bones do **not** stay in that wall (see §VI–§VII).
* Shipboard **first rites** (≥3 Mortivigils per recovery-capable hull) before bone repatriation.

**Stations:**

* **Mortivigil HQ:** Castra-Lupus precinct, **Reclusiam** building (Noviomagus Prime) — gate into Chapter-supreme vault and name-index.
* **Nine capital crypts:** one per Legatus seat world (see §VII).
* **Ships:** temporary ossuary custody until transfer.

**Promotion:** Mandatory service years as Mortivigil before wolf-skull / full **Wolfkeeper**. **Varro** may skip the years for a brother already “Wolfkeeper-shaped” in mind — **almost impossible**, **very rare**.

**Prohibitions:** **Never** hold ***licentia lupina***. No Executor wolf-skull. No far-field *Amissi* / Steppenwolf leash authority.

## III. Wolfkeeper helm law (post–CV y13)

* **All full Wolfkeepers** wear the **Executor wolf-skull** template with **red** lenses — shared cast, shared ego-death.
* **Mortivigils** remain on **human death-skull** + **orange** lenses until raised.
* **Pre–CV y13:** Wolfkeepers (including Varro; then filed as Chaplains on Imperial paper) wore ***Codex* death-skull**; transition dated to *Exanimus Choir*.

---

## IV. ***Licentia lupina*** (recycled meaning)

**Not** “permission to wear the wolf-skull” (that is now default Wolfkeeper livery).

**Is** Varro’s written sanction naming a **full Wolfkeeper** to lead and keep **Batavi** brothers on **detached / far-field** geometry — specifically **The Lost (*Amissi*)** and **Steppenwolf** registries — often far from Castra Vetera. Principal job: prevent identity stray (cult, guest-Chapter echo, personal legend, Brass drift).

* **One license** covers **both** pools.
* Internal ranking and capability evaluation chain the **better** licensed Wolfkeepers toward ***Amissi*** (small pool).
* *Amissi* mission slate still requires Wolfkeeper presence — Varro or a ***licentia lupina*** bearer ([amissi-the-lost.md](amissi-the-lost.md) §III). Legacy stamp ***licentia amissorum*** is **absorbed** into this seal.

## V. Varro — Judex extras (helm + pack)

Varro’s wolf-skull is the **same family** as line Wolfkeepers, plus **Judex-only** additions — **always on**:

* **Jaw exhaustors:** **Incense** plumes venting **past the mandible** into open air (Asmodai-adjacent / hellhound silhouette; *audit already running*).
* **Will-throttle:** plume density and eye intensity under Varro’s will (scare / weight dial) — system does not shut off.
* **Eyes:** **no red lenses visible** — orbits as **fire-orbs without free flame**; Furor stripe still crosses the orbital plane.
* **Sound:** low **furnace** undertone.
* **Pack:** **thurible** at iron-halo **position** (pack apex); **candles** flanking the thurible on the backpack sides — **not** a candle-halo ring; **no** formal iron halo.

**Offices locked:** Master Wolfkeeper / Judex (Imperial gloss: Master Chaplain) **and** **Master of the Judiciars** (Judiciar doctrine **TBD**).

**Office voice (example):** — The moment I entered this world’s noosphere, the audit and judgment were already in course. Pray it does not end in execution.

Full kit prose: [dossier-varro.md](../character-dossiers/triumvirate/dossier-varro.md) §2.

## VI. Death pipeline

| Step | Who | Action |
|------|-----|--------|
| 1 | Field | Marine dies |
| 2 | *Medicinae* / *Reclusiam* | **Gene-seed extraction / autopsy** and **funeral rites** — order by situation (clean recovery vs ruined theater) |
| 2a | Mortivigil / Chaplain | **Funeral pyre** — soft tissue burned; **skeleton** recovered with **relics and cybernetics**; armor retrieved for *Armourium* |
| 3 | Mortivigil | **Name on the Wall** — fused into the foundation of the place he died defending (any Castra Vetera settlement / fort / station) |
| 4 | Mortivigil | **Bones shipped** to the correct crypt (§VII) — always home capital when a home vex exists |

Wall = **name**. Crypt = **bones**. They are not the same place.

## VII. Crypt map

### Castra-Lupus precinct (Noviomagus Prime)

One named precinct, **three primary buildings** (Triumvirate architecture):

| Building | Seat | Role |

**Repatriation rule:** bones **always** ship to the **home** capital crypt when a home vex exists — not the nearest crypt of convenience.

### Chapter-supreme bone vault (Castra-Lupus / *Reclusiam*)

Holds skeletons (and sealed remnant cases) for:

* **Triumvirate** and designated **heroes**
* **The Lost (*Amissi*)**
* Brothers with **no Castra soil-home** — e.g. **Grey Hounds** era (*Slate Cohort*) survivors, Foundation-war brothers never filed to a vex homeworld, other pre-mesh orphans

Noviomagus also keeps a **name-index** (ledger) of Wall-fusions across the mesh so Varro can audit without nine pilgrimages. Frontier Wolf **plaque** chain remains a **Castellan Execratio / Versibar register** (root + iron plaque pairs on *Castra-Lupus* armor cord) — related honor, **not** the Astartes bone crypt and **not** the Wall-name itself; full split table: [frontier-wolf-rite.md](frontier-wolf-rite.md) §II addendum.

## VIII. Sacred offense — Batavi dead

Among Batavi, the greatest crimes are:

1. **Treason**
2. **Desecration of *their* dead** — Batavi bones, Wall-names, crypts, pyre remnants, and funeral custody

Batavi **respect** the dead of other Chapters, auxilia, and mortals as civilized war requires. Those dead are **not sacred** in the same register. Only **Batavi** dead carry this absolute.
