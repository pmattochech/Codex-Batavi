# THE FRICTION CHAIN (CHAPTER MILITARY HIERARCHY)

The *Cohors Batavorum* war machine is a mathematical aberration sustained by survival thermodynamics. To run a 5,000-warrior contingent on the lethal **Insula Tenebrarum** / **Witness Ford** frontier, the Chapter ignored *Codex Astartes* bureaucracy and adopted a command chain built purely to kill logistical bottlenecks. The hierarchy below governs how our **Friction Geometry** applies from trench melee to macro-orbital obliteration.

## 1. RAW STOCK AND STANDARD TOOL (BASE OF EACH VEXILLA)

**The neophyte (ore in the forge):** The recruit is not yet a gear; he is a block of mortal flesh being brutally machined. The neophyte endures continuous lethality stages on Aethelgard and Incus-Gravis, surviving extreme atmospheric and gravitational pressure. His biology seethes endocrinally, fighting to tame gene-seed’s first shock. They operate as scouts and heavy labor, burning aggression through physical effort so Wolf’s Curse terror shatters their minds before they receive ceramite plates.

**The battle-brother (the Batavi):** Line infantry and the mathematical base of our war. A single Batavi is the perfected transhuman tool: ego-stripped, vanity-immune, focused on calculated kills. Under the *Axioma Rationis*, his mind works as a cold dam against his own biology’s horrors. In *Zone Mortalis* they use the *second blade* (armored body as blunt weapon) and march in *Analytical Silence* — no battle cries or futile fanaticism. The mass of these warriors, the Batavi, forms the impassable Iron Wall.

## 2. TACTICAL EXECUTORS (KILL MATH)

**The sergeant (squad calculator):** Direct tactical lead for a 5–10 Batavi cell. The sergeant does not inspire with glorious shouts; he sets fire rhythm, cover angles, and ammo efficiency for his cell. They are the front line’s pressure regulators. When chimeric predisposition fuel rises toward **Wild Hunt** or **March of the Machine** depth, the sergeant holds formation to **authorized** harness bands and enforces rotation cues from the ***Curia Vexilli*** relay — not uncontrolled metabolic burn.

**The lieutenant (*Zone Mortalis* executor):** Maestro of short-range slaughter. Commanding half a sub-cohort (strict fifty-warrior fraction), the lieutenant is the officer who descends into the abattoir. While upper chain draws the siege, the lieutenant applies **Friction Geometry** millimeter by millimeter in choking corridors and mud trenches. He translates macro-strategy into direct execution and advance orders, ensuring enemy blood spills in optimized patterns.

## 3. MACRO-COMMAND AND WELL GOVERNORS (VEXILLA STRUCTURE)

**The captain (macro-strategist):** Commander of a full sub-cohort (100 battle-brothers). Directly subordinate to the Legatus, the captain runs planetary operations theater. He orchestrates bombardment vectors, adamantium supply routes, and kill-zone overlap. His job is to ensure before the **Vexilla** physically hits the threat, the enemy is already logistically mauled by trench positioning.

**The Legatus / Elder-Master (system pillar):** Decentralized military governor and supreme authority over one of the Nine Phalanx’s nine worlds / super-systems (3×3 geographic block — do not confuse with the tactical **Vexilla**). A Legatus commands one **Vexilla** (indivisible force of 500 Batavi). Filed as ***vir in acie*** — **the man in the line** — sovereign among his five hundred, identity in the ***Alabarda*** and command geometry, **not** in Castellan-class artificer armor ([`castra-lupus-doctrine.md`](../../arsenal-and-logistics/castra-lupus-doctrine.md) §1.1; per-Legatus register: [`legati-operational-register.md`](../../arsenal-and-logistics/legati-operational-register.md)). He bears the ***Alabarda* + Vexilla**: a **halberd** (combat polearm — planted blade, strike head, hook geometry per *Armourium* pattern) **carried together** with the unit’s **battle cloth** — the **same heraldic charges** as the formal seal (`[intro-and-heraldry.md](../intro-and-heraldry/chapter-identity.md)` — Legatus battle standard), in a **field-practical** format (typically **narrow vertical** panel or **pennant**, waves and gladius **abbreviated** so the wolf reads at distance; **furled** against the haft in void, storm, or heavy EM where cloth would lie). **Critical:** Alabarda and Vexilla are **not permanently fixed** — the cloth **may detach** and be **ground-pinned separately** while the Alabarda stays free for strike or butt-plant ([alabarda-head-groups.md](../../arsenal-and-logistics/alabarda-head-groups.md) §1). Brothers name the seat in **LATIN-CAPS** only (**PRIMA**…**NONA**; **DECIMA** for the Castellan anchor) — unit and cloth share the word **Vexilla**. The haft integrates a **command relay** that pushes merged tactical *auspex*, **Genetor Vexilli** clearance flags, and biological-failure telemetry to orange-lensed troop visors. *Strategium* does **not** file the banner as vanity: under *Analytical Silence* and broken vox, **visible Chapter mark + Legatus position** are **orientation instruments** — the five hundred close on a **single** spar and a **single** lattice ID. **Panel register:** **four** vexillum fields — chapter achievement, **LATIN-CAPS** ordinal (**PRIMA–NONA**), anchor/specialty icon, **greatest success**; **PRIMA** substitutes an **obligation / remembrance** fourth field (**debt the lattice carries**, not inherited personal guilt — induction and friction policy: [vexilla-by-vexillatio-design.md](../intro-and-heraldry/vexilla-by-vexillatio-design.md) §2.2). The Legatus is the cold, autonomous extension of Triumvirate will. Where he plants the blade — or pins the cloth — retreat is mathematically nullified.

***Alabarda* head and haft:** The **head group** (and certified **haft modes** where issued) varies by **Vexilla** theater — *Armourium* **Types** **Primus** through **Fulmen**: [alabarda-head-groups.md](../../arsenal-and-logistics/alabarda-head-groups.md). **Runtime** actuation — Alabarda plant, length mode, **mount / detach / ground-pin**, furled cloth, head use in contact — is **sole Legatus authority**; **no** subordinate or *Strategium* **approval** is doctrine for those choices. *Armourium* certifies **equipment safety** at manufacture/refit only.

**Deathwatch prerequisite (Chapter law):** Promulgated **after the fall of the First Legatus** — **Valerius** eliminated at **Malakor V (050.M42)** and struck from the rolls; the prerequisite was **not** in force during his tenure. *Strategium* codified the rule ***ca.* 051.M42** as part of the post-traitor command lattice. No Marine may be **nominated or confirmed** as **Legatus** unless *Medicinae* and *Strategium* certify **at least one full certified *Watch cycle*** (Deathwatch deployment from Chapter embarkation through **return audit** — definition and liaison seals: [deathwatch-and-batavorum.md](../../political-relations/deathwatch-and-batavorum.md)). **Pre-decree incumbency (*clausula retentionis antecedentis*):** Legati who already held the *Alabarda* when the **051.M42** seal took effect **retain** command **without** retroactive Watch service — *Strategium* refuses to **vacate** lawful governors to satisfy a **new** proof. **Replacement** appointments must satisfy the prerequisite. **Triumvirate unanimous waiver** may void the requirement for a named successor only in **documented extremis** (*Vermilion* entry required).

**The DECIMA and the Lord Castellan (anchor guard):** The absolute apex of our machine. Unlike PRIMA–NONA garrisoning frontier systems under their Legati, the **DECIMA** operates **without intermediate Legatus**: lethal personal escort to the Lord Castellan, symbiotic with the incumbent’s ***Castra-Lupus*** artificer armor (current configuration: ***Castra-Lupus — Helis***, Alaric von Helis). ***Castra-Lupus*** is the **office name** for the Castellan’s one-of-one armor commission — parallel to a Legatus’s unique ***Alabarda* head group**, not a relic suit passed between commanders; ***Caput Ferreum Castellani*** (*Iron Head*) is **regalia** inherited with the title. Full law: [castra-lupus-doctrine.md](../../arsenal-and-logistics/castra-lupus-doctrine.md). Its members are elite in psychological stability and friction efficiency; **veteran instructors** from the Tenth deploy across the other nine Vexilla to train **specialty cohort** contingents (see [vexillationes-and-visual-summary.md](../../arsenal-and-logistics/vexillationes-and-visual-summary.md)). **Battle standard:** DECIMA flies the **justice vexillum** — **feral wolf slain by sword** (register of **Malakor V** and First Legatus treason ended) — not a tenth *Alabarda* duplicate; detail [vexilla-by-vexillatio-design.md](../intro-and-heraldry/vexilla-by-vexillatio-design.md) §4.

**DECIMA entry requirements (summary):** veteran status or isolated heroism is insufficient. The candidate must **(1)** complete **at least one full certified *Watch cycle*** (same definition as Legatus prerequisite — [deathwatch-and-batavorum.md](../../political-relations/deathwatch-and-batavorum.md)); **(2)** complete **proven minimum time in line infantry** across Suppression, Ruin, and Siege divisions — “tool” before “elite function”; **(3)** serve **in each of the four specialty cohorts**, in organic fifty-warrior detachments per Vexilla, for **at least one campaign cycle** (or *Strategium*-equivalent interval) **per cohort**, with aptitude files signed by home Legatus and Tenth instructor; **(4)** rank **at the top of his pool** (logistical precision, documented kill ratio, zero *Axiom of Reason* discipline failures in audit windows); **(5)** endure the **Batav Wolf trial** — protocol confrontation with a *Canis Batavorum* specimen in a containment chamber, imposing **absolute dominance** on the beast **without** unnecessary death **and without** losing **Furor** containment (species + trial law: [batav-wolf-canis-batavorum.md](../../biological-encyclopedia-bestiary/batav-wolf-canis-batavorum.md); symbolic parallel — silent dominance without spectacle). **Legacy scene citation (do not use):** [diplomacy-aethelgard.md](../../chronicles/outdated/space-wolves-alaric-relations/diplomacy-aethelgard/chapter.md). **Exemption:** the five **supreme posts** in the Tenth (Lord Castellan, Chief Apothecary, Master Chaplain, Master of the Forge, Chief Librarian) are **not** subject to the Watch prerequisite — ancient precedent of foundation and *Strategium* primacy. **All other** battle-brothers admitted to DECIMA (pure line, cohorts, organic support) **must** satisfy **(1)**. The same ***clausula retentionis antecedentis*** applies: brothers **already** on DECIMA’s roll at **051.M42** **retain** standing **without** retroactive Watch; **admissions** after the decree must certify **(1)**. Final approval remains **unanimous Triumvirate**; no appeal.

**Note:** The neophyte witnesses Triumvirate vulnerability and duty geometry at the *Iron and Blood Tribunal* ([incus-gravis-campaign.md](../../chronicles/incus-gravis-tribunal/incus-gravis-campaign/chapter.md) · guest-Chapter scene prose **legacy**) — moral preparation, **not** a substitute for the Batav Wolf trial.

Above them all rests the Triumvirate — the Wall (Alaric), the Scalpel (Drusus), and the Judge (Varro) — the three minds that keep the beast chained to the forge.

### 3.1 The *Curia Vexilli* (banner court) and organ delegates

Each **Vexilla** PRIMA–NONA maintains a **banner court** (*Curia Vexilli*): **Legatus** (war geometry), **Genetor Vexilli** (*Medicinae* clearance lane), **Confessor Vexilli** (*Reclusiam* conduct lane). The three review deployment and discipline depth **in parallel** — fenced domains; neither subordinate comments on the partner’s chart. **Legatus** schedules after risk briefs; **Genetor Vexilli** holds **hard veto on clearance**; **Confessor Vexilli** holds **hard power on penance and marks**. **DECIMA** does not duplicate the lattice — **Triumvirate** authority applies directly (§3 above).

## 4. DEPARTMENTAL ORDERS AND THE 5,000 CEILING

### 4.1 Counting principle

The Chapter’s **5,000** warriors are **every** Astartes with **gene-seed** and *Cohors Batavorum* status — **no** “extra” reserve off-books. **Line**, **specialty cohorts**, **Triumvirate**, **Master of the Forge**, **Chief Librarian**, and the full networks of Apothecaries, Techmarines, Chaplains, and campaign Librarians **count toward this ceiling**. There is no parallel legion of “invisible specialists”: anything not in stasis, exile, or capital sentence **consumes** one of five thousand slots.

### 4.4 What the 5,000 excludes

**Neophytes** in the pipeline (not yet full armor and gene-seed sealed as brother) may operate at variable numbers **outside** the combat grid until elevation — *Strategium* policy, not ceiling inflation. **Servitors**, **Auxilia**, **listed abhumans**, and allied **Skitarii** **do not** count in the 5,000.

### 4.5 Operational adjustments (without changing the frozen table)

- **Noviomagus ↔ line rotation** for Techmarines (the **4** per Vexilla hold; *Strategium* rotates who fills each slot).  
- **Formal liaison** between each **Batavian Haruspex** and the **Nullity Cohort** (psychic *vox* channel and *blank* support protocol).  
- **Decadal review** of *Standard Order of Battle* — only by unanimous Triumvirate decree (extreme friction war, irrecoverable losses, or structural psychic threat shift).
